## 三、核心问题（按顺序问，逐条记答复）

> [!tip] Q1 最关键 —— 先问这个
> "In MyIntealth I can see a **'Begin Application'** option for a new Application for ECFMG Certification. **Is starting a brand-new application the correct way to have PUMC recognized as my medical school of record** — rather than trying to fix my rejected applications?"
>
> （中文：MyIntealth 里有「Begin Application」按钮 → **重开一份新申请、把学校填成协和**，是不是把 school of record 改对的正确办法？还是该走别的途径修旧的？）

**Q2 —— 新申请里具体怎么填**
> "When I begin the new application, should I enter **Peking Union Medical College** as the school that issued my **final medical diploma**, with degree title **'Doctor of Clinical Medicine'** and **graduation year 2012**?"

（中文：新申请里，final medical diploma 的学校填协和、学位称号填 Doctor of Clinical Medicine、毕业年填 2012 —— 对吗？）

**Q3 —— 会不会和被拒旧申请冲突 / 被当 irregular**
> "I have **two previously rejected applications** that listed a different school (West China/Sichuan). Will submitting a **new application with a different school of record** cause any conflict, or be flagged? **How do I make sure this is handled correctly?**"

（中文：我有两份被拒旧申请填的是川大；现在新申请换成协和，会不会冲突 / 被标记 irregular？怎么确保正确处理？—— 这条最怕踩 irregular behavior，务必问清。）

**Q4 —— 博士到底算不算 final diploma**
> "Per ECFMG's **Reference Guide**, 'Doctor of Clinical Medicine' from **PUMC, graduation year 2012**, is listed as an acceptable final medical diploma. **Does that mean my doctoral degree qualifies as my final medical diploma** for certification?"

（中文：《Reference Guide》把协和 2012 "Doctor of Clinical Medicine" 列为可接受最终文凭 → 那我的博士就能当 final medical diploma 用，对吗？）

**Q5 —— 要交什么材料 + 是否直接向协和核验**
> "**What documents** will I need to provide, and will ECFMG **verify them directly with PUMC**?"

（中文：要交哪些材料？ECFMG 会不会直接找协和核验？—— 确认我的英文成绩单口径必须和协和留档一致。）

**Q6 —— 时间线（为排 Step 2）**
> "Roughly **how long** does it take for a new application to be **accepted**? I'm trying to plan my **Step 2 CK** registration."

（中文：新申请大概多久能 accepted？我要据此排 Step 2。）

**Q7 —— 那封跟进邮件**
> "I also sent a **follow-up email on June 24** about this. Has it been received? Should I **wait for a reply, or go ahead** and begin the new application now?"

（中文：6/24 发的跟进邮件收到没？我该等回复还是直接现在 Begin 新申请？）

## 五、红线（说什么 / 不说什么）

> [!danger] 诚实但不自损
> - ✅ 学位称号口径统一：**"Doctor of Clinical Medicine"** = CDGDC（China Academic Degrees and Graduate Education Development Center，中国学位与研究生教育发展中心）官方英译 **"Professional Doctor Degree of Medicine"（field: Clinical Medicine）**，同一学位。
> - ⚠️ **不主动提**临床轮转量偏少（病理路径）——但**被直接问绝不撒谎**（成绩单 ECFMG 直接向协和核，对不上 = irregular behavior = 可能永久禁申）。临床见习我 2024 邮件已如实披露，以协和成绩单为准。
> - ⚠️ **别在电话里答应/确认任何与协和留档不一致的表述**。

> [!warning] 行动顺序提醒（电话只是第 2 步）
> 1. 先把**协和博士英文成绩单**改对（标题 Doctor 非 Master，见 [[ECFMG认证资格_问题记录]] §四-补步骤二）——解锁整条链的前置
> 2. **这通电话**：确认 Begin 新申请填协和的正确性 + 不冲突
> 3. 两件都 OK 后，**本人** Begin Application + 填写 + 提交（高风险动作，想清楚再做）
