# 高频 RF / MCC / MIPF 钥匙速查 (Buzzword → Answer Reflexes)

> [!info] 用途与来源
> CK 大量"**最常见 / 最重要 / 首位危险因素**"型单句反射题——题干给一个场景，正确选项就是某个固定"钥匙"。把 Demi (Divine Intervention) RF 大全里按系统压成一句话反射表，**盖右列默写**。机制不展开，深挖去对应分科 / `*_HighYield速查总览`。

> [!note]- 缩写对照（先记这套，全表通用）
> - **RF** = risk factor 危险因素 ｜ **#1 RF** = 首位危险因素
> - **MC** = most common 最常见 ｜ **MCC** = most common cause 最常见病因
> - **MCCOD** = most common cause of death 最常见死因
> - **MIPF** = most important prognostic factor 最重要预后因素
> - **MSC** = most serious complication 最严重并发症 ｜ **MLCP** = most likely complication 最可能并发症
> - **ML** = most likely 最可能 ｜ **MLM** = most likely mechanism 最可能机制
> - **NBSIM** = next best step in management 下一步处理

> [!note]- 临床缩写对照（表内简写一次性给全称）
> **CVS**：MVP=mitral valve prolapse 二尖瓣脱垂｜MR=mitral regurgitation 二尖瓣反流｜AS=aortic stenosis 主动脉狭窄｜AAA=abdominal aortic aneurysm 腹主动脉瘤｜CAD=coronary artery disease 冠心病｜ED=erectile dysfunction 勃起障碍｜CVD=cardiovascular disease 心血管病｜HTN=hypertension 高血压｜LVH=left ventricular hypertrophy 左室肥厚｜IE=infective endocarditis 感染性心内膜炎｜HOCM=hypertrophic obstructive cardiomyopathy 肥厚型梗阻性心肌病。
> **呼吸/内分泌**：COPD=慢性阻塞性肺疾病｜ARDS=acute respiratory distress syndrome 急性呼吸窘迫综合征｜OSA=obstructive sleep apnea 阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停｜MEN=multiple endocrine neoplasia 多发性内分泌腺瘤病。
> **GI**：GERD=胃食管反流病｜PSC=primary sclerosing cholangitis 原发性硬化性胆管炎｜CF=cystic fibrosis 囊性纤维化｜NAFLD=non-alcoholic fatty liver disease 非酒精性脂肪肝｜HCC=hepatocellular carcinoma 肝细胞癌｜EATL=enteropathy-associated T-cell lymphoma 肠病相关 T 细胞淋巴瘤。
> **Heme-Onc**：SCD=sickle cell disease 镰状细胞病｜HIT=heparin-induced thrombocytopenia 肝素诱导血小板减少｜GVHD=graft-versus-host disease 移植物抗宿主病｜TACO=transfusion-associated circulatory overload 输血相关循环超负荷｜DIC=disseminated intravascular coagulation 弥散性血管内凝血｜ALL=acute lymphoblastic leukemia 急性淋巴细胞白血病｜TNM=肿瘤分期系统。
> **肾/泌尿**：CKD=慢性肾病｜ESRD=end-stage renal disease 终末期肾病｜ADPKD=常染色体显性多囊肾｜FSGS=focal segmental glomerulosclerosis 局灶节段性肾小球硬化｜MCD=minimal change disease 微小病变病｜ATN=acute tubular necrosis 急性肾小管坏死｜RCC=renal cell carcinoma 肾细胞癌｜VHL=von Hippel-Lindau 综合征｜BPH=benign prostatic hyperplasia 良性前列腺增生｜UVJ=ureterovesical junction 输尿管膀胱连接处｜SCC=squamous cell carcinoma 鳞状细胞癌｜SAH=subarachnoid hemorrhage 蛛网膜下腔出血。
> **OB/GYN/Neuro/风湿**：AFP=alpha-fetoprotein 甲胎蛋白｜hPL=human placental lactogen 人胎盘催乳素｜SIDS=sudden infant death syndrome 婴儿猝死综合征｜RB=retinoblastoma 视网膜母细胞瘤｜PID=pelvic inflammatory disease 盆腔炎｜MS=multiple sclerosis 多发性硬化｜CJD=Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease 克雅病｜CMV=巨细胞病毒｜HSV=单纯疱疹病毒｜EM=erythema multiforme 多形红斑｜TEN/SJS=中毒性表皮坏死松解症/Stevens-Johnson 综合征｜SLE=系统性红斑狼疮｜CAP=community-acquired pneumonia 社区获得性肺炎｜CHF=congestive heart failure 充血性心衰。
