## §一 Pediatric Chronic Illness Disclosure 全流程

> [!info] 反射钩
> 儿童 raise concerns → "Tell me what you understand about..." 开头（先 assess understanding）→ 再 empathic respond + 后续 collaborative plan with parents。
> 不能 ① defer 给 parents ② encourage 患儿独自 broach ③ evasive 撒谎 ④ abrupt 直接 raise difficult topic。

> [!info] 深度理解 — 儿童疾病 disclosure 为什么"先评估理解再说"
>
> **场景**：一个 8 岁的孩子患 perinatal HIV / 白血病 / CF 等严重慢病。某次随诊，孩子直接问医生："我是不是要死了？" / "为什么我得吃这么多药？" / "我朋友都没生病为什么我要？"
>
> **直觉答法（错）**：
> - "你父母会跟你说"（defer — 推给 parents 独自承担 + 错过 trust window）
> - "你别担心，没事的"（evasive 撒谎 — 短期安抚 / 长期破坏 trust）
> - "你得了 HIV，这是绝症，你可能..."（abrupt 直接给 worst case — child 心理崩溃）
> - "你自己问你父母吧"（encourage 患儿独自 broach — 让 child 独自承担 emotional burden）
>
> **正确答法核心 = 先问"你已经知道什么 / 担心什么"**：
> ```
> "Tell me what you understand about your illness."
> "What worries you the most?"
> ```
>
> **为什么这样设计 — pediatric ethics 三方平衡**：
> 儿童 chronic illness 有三方利益：
> 1. **Child** — 有权 understand 自己身体（developmentally appropriate）+ 表达 wishes
> 2. **Parents** — 法律 decision-maker + 长期 caregiver + 最了解 child 心理承受力
> 3. **Physician** — medical expertise + objective info + advocate for child's evolving capacity
>
> 任何一方被 bypass 或独自承担 burden → 三方平衡破裂：
> - Physician 独自 disclose without parents → parents 失去 disclosure 主导权 + 不知道孩子知道了什么
> - Parents 独自 disclose without physician → 信息可能错误 + parents 心理崩溃
> - Child 独自承担 → 没有 emotional support
>
> 所以 4 阶段全流程的本质是 **保持三方协作 + 不让任何一方独自承担**。
>
> **阶段 1 "先 assess understanding" 为什么如此关键**：
> Child 问的问题往往不是字面意思 — 他们用 simple 语言表达**深层情绪**。
> - "我会死吗" 真实含义可能是 "我害怕 / 我不想离开 mom" 不是字面询问 prognosis
> - "为什么我要吃药" 真实含义可能是 "我恨这些药的副作用" 而不是询问机制
> - 先问 "tell me what you understand"：
>   - ① 避免医生 over-disclose（给比 child 想知道的更多 → 心理 overload）
>   - ② 避免 under-disclose（给比 child 已经知道的少 → patronizing）
>   - ③ 识别 misconception（child 可能从 sibling / school 听了错误信息）
>   - ④ 给 child 控制感（"你来引导这个对话"）
>
> **特殊场景 — perinatal HIV disclosure 给 adolescent**：
> Late childhood / early adolescence (10-13 岁) 是 typical disclosure window — 此时 child 开始问 specific 问题 + 能 understand 慢病概念 + 进入 sexual development 期需知道传播风险。disclosure 越早，长期 outcome 越好（adherence ↑ / 心理健康 ↑ / disclosure-related 焦虑 ↓）。
>
> **Adolescent advance care planning — 反向直觉点**：
> 直觉："讨论 advance care 会 demotivate adolescent / 加重 anxiety"
> **真相反向**：CF deteriorating / B-ALL induction 失败的 adolescent 提前 family-centered advance care planning → ↓ anxiety + ↑ adherence + 避免 crisis decision + ensure preferences honored。
> 为什么：不知道 = 失控感；知道 + 参与 = 控制感 + dignity。
>
> **CK 怎么考**：
> - 题眼 1：Child raises concern → "Tell me what you understand about..."（不是 "your parents will tell you" / "don't worry")
> - 题眼 2：Disclosure timing for chronic illness adolescent → late childhood / early adolescence + with parents collaboratively + 一起 deliver
> - 题眼 3：Adolescent advance care planning + chronic life-limiting illness → encourage parents to include adolescent（不是 bypass）
> - 钓饵：① "Defer to parents" ② "Encourage child to ask parents alone" ③ "Wait until child is older (18)" ④ "Reassure child everything is fine"。

### Disclosure 4 阶段全流程

```
阶段 1：先 determine patient's current understanding（baseline）⭐
    │      ※ 关键 — 不能 defer / 不能 evasive / 不能直接 raise 死亡
    ▼
阶段 2：基于 understanding，empathic 提供 timely comfort + support
    │
    ▼
阶段 3：基于 patient understanding，与 parents collaboratively plan
    │      ※ Timing / approach / extent of disclosure
    │      ※ Joint participation（不让 family 独自承担 burden）
    ▼
阶段 4：joint disclosure（family + physician 一起 deliver）
           ※ Follow-up support + adherence monitoring + 性教育（如 HIV）
```

### Pediatric ethics 三方平衡

| 方 | 原则 | 操作 |
|---|---|---|
| **Patient（儿童）** | Primary duty + 知情权按发育水平 + emerging autonomy | 患者 raise 的 concerns 不能 defer 不能 dismiss |
| **Parents** | Authority to make decisions + respect concerns | 不 overtly 讨论 "negative"（暂时遵守），但同时 collaboratively 推进 plan |
| **Physician** | Balance + honesty + collaborative | 不撒谎 / 不 evasive / 不单方面 unilateral disclose |

### 4 大常见陷阱

> [!warning]
> 1. **Defer 给 parents**（"ask permission to share with parents"）→ 把 burden defer + 没回应 patient 当下 concerns
> 2. **Encourage 患儿独自 broach**（"encourage patient to talk to parents"）→ 让 11 岁独自承担 difficult discussion
> 3. **Evasive 撒谎安慰**（"will likely feel better after another round of chemo"）→ medical 不诚实 = 损 trust（induction 失败时不知道下轮会好）
> 4. **Abrupt 直接 raise difficult topic**（"inquire about death and dying perception"）→ 太早 jump 到 deep topic without 先 understand baseline

### Disclosure 之前 vs 之后好处对比（Perinatal HIV 特化 — Q19132）

| 因素 | 不 disclose 风险 | Disclose 后好处 |
|---|---|---|
| **Patient trust** | 知 truth 时 trust 受损 | Build patient-physician trust + 长期 relationship |
| **Medication adherence** | 不知 why 服药 → adherence ↓（adolescence 加重）| ↑Adherence（理解 why） |
| **Transmission prevention** | Sexual exploration + 不知 HIV → inadvertent transmission to partners | ↓Inadvertent transmission + 学会 safe sex / disclosure to partners |
| **Psychosocial** | Anxiety / 怀疑 / 偶然 self-discover 创伤 | Adjusted with support |

### Perinatal HIV 特殊（Q19132 锚定）

> [!tip] Adolescence 之前必须 disclose（3 大理由）
> 1. **Fosters trust** + autonomy
> 2. **↑Medication adherence**（理解 why 服药）
> 3. **↓Inadvertent transmission**（adolescence 是 sexual exploration 期）

### "Respect family" ≠ passive

> [!danger] 核心 reframe
> 真正 respect = 与 family **一起 plan**（give timeline + joint participation），**不是把 burden 全推给 family 独自承担**。
> 3 个 not-good 模式：① 只强调 risks of delay（instill distrust）② passive 推 burden 给 family（not collaborative）③ unilateral partial disclose without consent（不 respect family）。

### Parent Wishes to Withhold Information — Collaboration-First Approach ⭐（UW 做对题 — v1.4.5 新增）

#### 反射钩

> [!info]
> Parent 因 concern for adverse effects 想 **withhold information**（诊断 / 病情 / risks）from adolescent → 反射 **collaboration-first**：
> ① **Educate parent** about age-appropriate health care participation
> ② **Explain rationale** for involving adolescent（具体一句话 — 如 "knowing his diagnosis could help him be aware of potential symptoms"）
> ③ **Emphasize collaboration** with "let's first talk"
> ④ 与 parent 一起 establish detailed plan：timing / approach / extent of information sharing
> ⑤ Throughout: **validate + respect** parent's concerns
> ❌ 不 express disapproval / 不 fear-based tactics / 不 delay（当有 real risk 时）/ 不 bypass parent 直接 go to adolescent。

#### 核心 framework — 3 个 baseline 前提

```
全 disclosure 是医患关系基石（respect autonomy + informed decision-making）
    │
    ├─ ① Minor 的医疗决策 default deferred to parents
    │      ※ 例外：reproductive health / mental health（adolescent 自己 consent — §六）
    │
    ├─ ② Adolescent involvement gradient ⭐
    │      ※ Cognitive + physical + psychosocial maturity 增加
    │      ※ → 渐进式增加 medical decision-making 参与
    │      ※ → Foster individuation + health maintenance responsibility
    │
    └─ ③ Parent 仍是 legal decision-maker
           ※ 即使支持 adolescent participation，仍尊重 parent authority
           ※ → 解决 parent's withholding wish 必经 collaborative route
```

#### 应对 Parent Wishes to Withhold 4 步 SOP

```
Parent 表达："I don't want my child to know about [diagnosis/risk]"
    │
    ▼
① Validate concerns（不 dismiss / 不 disapprove）
   "I understand you want to protect [child name]"
   ※ Validation 是 collaboration 的入场券，不是终点
    │
    ▼
② Educate about age-appropriate participation
   "Children at this age can typically understand X / participate in Y"
   ※ Parent 常 underestimate adolescent capacity
   ※ 用 developmental milestones 客观 frame
    │
    ▼
③ Explain rationale for involvement（具体一句话 buy-in）⭐
   "Knowing his diagnosis could help him be aware of potential symptoms"
   "Understanding the medication helps him take it consistently"
   ※ 不抽象 "autonomy is important" — 用 concrete 好处说服 parent
    │
    ▼
④ Propose collaborative plan ⭐ 核心
   "Let's first talk about how and when to share this with him"
   • Timing: 几个小时后？下次随诊？过几天？
   • Approach: parent first 单独？parent + physician 一起？physician 单独?
   • Extent: 哪些 details 现在分享 / 哪些后续?
   ※ Joint disclosure：family + physician 一起 deliver
   ※ 计划允许 parent **"rehearse" difficult discussion**
```

#### 4 大反 pattern（钓饵选项）

| 反 pattern | 例 | 为什么错 |
|---|---|---|
| **① Express disapproval of parenting decision** | "I'm concerned you would be doing more harm by not telling him" | Damage rapport + 增 parent reluctance |
| **② Fear-based tactics** | "If you don't tell him about these deadly symptoms..." | 即使 risk 真实，fear-based 仍 damage rapport + 让 parent defensive |
| **③ Delay conversation when real risk exists** | "Let's revisit this in 6 months" | 当有 small but real risk of sudden death / acute deterioration → delay 不 ethical |
| **④ Shut down + bypass parent** | "Adolescents can manage their own health, I'll talk to him alone" | Adolescent 可参与 ≠ unilateral bypass；不 collaborate + 不 address parent's concerns + 损 family alliance |

#### 与既有 §一 内容衔接

| 触发场景 | 适用框架 |
|---|---|
| **Child raises concerns first** | §一 4 阶段全流程（先 assess understanding → empathic → collaborative plan → joint disclosure）|
| **Parent wishes to withhold** ⭐ 本节 | 4 步 SOP（validate → educate → rationale → collaborative plan）|
| **Adolescent + chronic life-limiting + deteriorating** | §一 Advance Care Planning（family-centered participant model）|
| **Perinatal HIV adolescent disclosure timing** | §一 Q19132 framework（disclosure before adolescence — fosters trust / ↑adherence / ↓transmission）|

> [!tip] 一句话锁
> 「**Validate + Educate + Rationale + Collaborate plan**」4 步 — parent 想 withhold 时永远 collaborative-first，绝不 disapprove / fear-based / delay-when-risk / bypass。

### Advance Care Planning for Adolescent with Chronic Life-Limiting Illness（Q21658 锚定）⭐

> [!info] 反射钩
> Adolescent + **chronic life-limiting illness**（CF / HFrEF / 神经退行性 / cancer 等）+ **deteriorating** → **family-centered advance care planning**：patient 作 **participant alongside parents**。
> ❌ 不 defer 到 18 岁 ❌ 不 wait until stable ❌ 不只 parents 决定 ❌ 不 defer 到 PCP（急性恶化中不能拖）。

#### 核心原则：Patient 是 participant 不是 bystander

| 维度 | ❌ 错误 model | ✅ Family-centered model |
|---|---|---|
| **Adolescent 角色** | 被动 — wait until 18 才有发言权 | **Active participant** alongside parents |
| **Parents 角色** | 唯一 decision-maker | **Co-participant**（保留 legal authority + 与 adolescent 联合 discuss）|
| **Physician 角色** | 仅 transmit info to parents | **Facilitate joint discussion** + 教育 + 建立 trust |
| **时机** | Wait until stable / wait until 18 | **Early + 主动**（chronic life-limiting illness 一旦 deteriorating signal 出现）|

#### 启动信号（不能 defer — 必须立即启动 advance care planning）

```
Chronic life-limiting illness（CF / HFrEF / 神经退行性 / cancer 等）
    │
    │ 出现 deteriorating signal：
    ├─ Functional measure 显著下降（如 CF FEV1 <40% predicted）
    ├─ 反复住院 / exacerbations 频率增加
    ├─ 治疗 response 减弱
    ├─ 新并发症(如肺动脉高压 / 营养不良 / 心律失常）
    ▼
★ 立即启动 family-centered advance care planning ★
    （不 wait stable，不 wait 18，不只 parents 决定）
```

#### Family-centered 5 大好处（反向直觉 — USMLE 必考）

> [!success] 反向直觉点
> 直觉以为"讨论 advance care 会 demotivate adolescent / 加重 anxiety" → **反向真相**：

| 好处 | 机制 |
|---|---|
| **↓ Anxiety** | Adolescent 知道 wishes 被听见，不怕被忽视 |
| **↑ Maintain quality of life** | Preferences honored — 治疗与价值观一致 |
| **↑ Adherence** | Adolescent 理解 + 参与决定 → 更愿意 follow |
| **避免 crisis decision-making** | 提前 plan → 急性 decompensation 时不必 family panic 决定 |
| **Ensure preferences honored** | 若 status decline → 已知 patient preferences（防 lose opportunity）|

#### 关键陷阱（Q21658 触发）

> [!danger] "Minor = parents 全权" 反射陷阱
> Parents 确实保留 legal authority for minors，**但** chronic life-limiting illness 的 adolescent 有 **maturing capacity to appreciate medical implications** → physician 应 **encourage parents to include minors in treatment decisions**，不是 bypass adolescent。
> **不是 binary "成人/儿童"** — adolescent 是连续 spectrum，chronic life-limiting illness 触发 special participation status。

#### 与 §二 衔接（advance care planning → end-of-life decision）

```
adolescent 还有 capacity + chronic life-limiting illness deteriorating
    │
    ▼
§一 Family-centered advance care planning（Q21658）
    │  patient 是 participant，preferences 记录在案
    │
    │ 真到 incapacitated（severe decompensation）
    ▼
§二 End-of-life 3 philosophies（Q21627 NEOT）
    │  已有 advance directive → 按 patient wishes
    │  没有 / partial → surrogate 用 substituted judgment + shared values
```

## §二 End-of-Life Care 5 档 + DNR 范围 + Advance Directive 完整 SOP

> [!info] 反射钩
> End-of-life care 不是 binary "全力救 vs 撤一切" — 共 **5 档 philosophies**，按 family 心理 + medical situation 选档。
> **DNR ≠ Do Not Treat** — 仅 prohibit CPR + defibrillation in cardiopulmonary arrest，其他 standard of care 全开。
> **Living will overrules surrogate**（即使 conflict）— Advance directive 优先于 next of kin / POA 决定。
> Surrogate 决定 = **substituted judgment + shared values**，不是 simply "don't feel guilty"。

> [!info] 深度理解 — End-of-life 的 3 大概念为什么这样设计
>
> 这一节是 ethics 里**最容易被钓饵**的章节，因为 5 档 / DNR / Advance directive 都有**精确的窄定义**，CK 故意用 "听起来差不多但定义错"的选项考你。
>
> ## 概念 1：为什么是 "5 档" 而不是 "全救 / 全撤" 二选一？
>
> **临床现实**：终末期患者的家属心理 + 病情演化是渐变的。
> - 突然从"全力救"跳到"全撤" → family 心理无法承受（guilt / 自责 / 长期 grief）
> - Care team 也需要中间档让"已经在做的不撤、新的不加"
>
> 所以美国 end-of-life 体系发展出 5 档**阶梯式 de-escalation**：
> 1. **Full code** — 全力（默认）
> 2. **DNR** — 仅心肺停时不复苏，其他都做
> 3. **DNI** — 不插管，但 BiPAP / 其他可
> 4. **NEOT** — "已有的继续做，新的不加"（**最被忽略的档** — Q21627 考点）
> 5. **Comfort care only** — 全撤，纯舒缓
>
> 每档对应不同的 family 心理阶段 + 病情阶段。CK 题给 stem "family reluctant to withdraw but prognosis poor" → 答 **NEOT**，不是 comfort care（family 还没准备好）也不是 full code（医学无意义）。
>
> ## 概念 2：为什么 DNR ≠ Do Not Treat？(USMLE 最经典钓饵)
>
> **DNR 全称**：Do Not **Resuscitate** — 仅指**心肺复苏** (CPR + defibrillation + 紧急插管)。
>
> **窄定义的历史**：1970-80 年代 CPR 普及后，临床发现末期患者反复 CPR = 痛苦无意义，所以引入 DNR 让患者**选择拒绝这一项特定干预**。但 DNR**从未**意味"放弃所有治疗"。
>
> **常见反直觉**：
> - 直觉：DNR 患者 = "末期 / 放弃治疗 / 进入临终" → 那 acute stroke 还能给 tPA 吗？
> - **真相**：能。DNR 患者突发 acute ischemic stroke → 仍 tPA candidate（如果其他 criteria 满足）→ tPA **不是** CPR，DNR 不限制。
> - 真相：DNR 患者突发 sepsis → 仍给 IV 抗生素。Cancer pain → 仍给 opioid。GI bleed → 仍输血。**所有"非 resuscitation"的 standard of care 全开**。
> - DNR 只在**心肺停的那一刻**触发 — 此时不 chest compression / 不 defibrillation / 不紧急插管 → 让 natural death 发生。
>
> **CK 钓饵模板**：题干给"DNR patient 来 ED with acute stroke / MI / sepsis" → 钓饵选项 "不给 tPA / 不给 PCI / 不给抗生素 because patient is DNR" → **永远错**。正解永远是 "按 standard of care 给 indicated treatment"。
>
> ## 概念 3：Advance Directive 的优先级 — Living will > POA > Next of kin
>
> **本质**：这三个都是"患者自己 incapacitated 时谁来代为决定"，但**优先级不同**：
>
> | 层级 | 内容 | 为什么优先级这么排 |
> |---|---|---|
> | **Living will**（最高）| Patient 本人**提前 written 指示**："我不要 intubation / 不要 feeding tube..." | 这是 patient **自己 explicit wishes** — 最接近 autonomy 完成形态，权威最高 |
> | **POA / Health Care Proxy**（次高）| Patient **提前 designate** 一个代理人 | 不是 patient 自己 wishes 但是 patient 自己**选**的代理人 → 法律推定该代理人最了解 patient values |
> | **Next of kin**（最低）| Default 按法定亲属顺序（spouse → adult child → parent → sibling）| 不是 patient 自己选的 → 仅 fallback |
>
> **核心反直觉 — Living will overrules POA**：
> 直觉："Patient 既然 designate 了 POA，那 POA 说了算"。
> **真相**：如果 Living will 里**明确 cover 当前情境**（如 "I do not want intubation"），即使 POA 想 intubate → **按 Living will**（不 intubate）。POA 只在 Living will **没 cover** 的情境（或没 Living will）才发挥决策权。
>
> **为什么**：Living will 是 patient **自己的字** → 直接表达 autonomy；POA 是"代理"→ 仅在 patient 自己无法表达时启动。Patient 自己已 explicit 表达过的事项，不需要代理人重新决定。
>
> **CK 怎么考**：
> - 题眼 1：DNR 患者 acute intervention（stroke / MI / sepsis）→ "按 standard of care"（不是"because DNR 不给"）
> - 题眼 2：Living will refuse intubation + POA wants intubation → **Living will wins**
> - 题眼 3：Family reluctant to withdraw + 升级 futile → **NEOT**（不是 comfort care）
> - 钓饵集锦：① "DNR = withhold all aggressive treatment" ② "POA overrides Living will" ③ "Comfort care 是唯一 ethical option when prognosis poor"。

### §2.1 End-of-Life Care 5 档对照表 ⭐（v1.4 扩展自 3 philosophies）

| 档位 | 含义 | **不做什么** | **仍做什么** | 适用场景 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Full code** | 全力救 | — | 一切 | 患者明确 wishes 救 / capacity 不明 default |
| **DNR**（Do Not Resuscitate）⭐ | **仅** cardiopulmonary arrest 时不复苏 | CPR / defibrillation | **其他所有 standard of care**（thrombolysis / 抗生素 / 手术 / 输液 / 化疗）| Patient wishes 不要 CPR 但仍要 active treatment |
| **DNI**（Do Not Intubate）| 不插管 | 气管插管 / 机械通气 | **Non-invasive vent (BiPAP) 仍可** + 其他 treatment | 患者 wishes 不要 intubation |
| **NEOT**（No Escalation of Treatment — Q21627）| 设 upper limit，不再升级 | 新加 dialysis / 新加 vent / 新加 invasive | **已有 care 继续（不撤）** + supportive care | Family reluctant withdraw + prognosis 差 + 再升级 futile |
| **Time-Limited Trial** | 限期尝试 | 草率 withdraw | 暂时 escalate 看反应（2-3 days） | Diagnosis 不明 / 反应不明 / family 需 closure |
| **Comfort care only / Withdrawal** | 完全舒缓 / 主动撤 | Curative interventions / 撤 vent / 撤 pressor / 撤 dialysis | Pain control + 喂养 + 输液（按需） | Family + patient wishes 同意 + futile prolongation |

### §2.2 DNR 范围限定（USMLE 高频钓饵）⭐（Q107705 锚定 — v1.4 新增）

> [!danger] DNR 是窄定义不是宽定义
> **DNR 仅 prohibit cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR + defibrillation) in event of cardiopulmonary arrest** — 不等于 "Do Not Treat"。其他 therapeutic intervention 仍按 standard of care。
> 患者 incoherent → 不影响 DNR 解读，但需走 substitute decision-maker（按 [[完整笔记/专题笔记/_衍生_Ethics精简框架]] §二 hierarchy）consent for treatment。
> Metastatic cancer / terminal illness **不是** thrombolysis / 急性 intervention 禁忌；quality-of-life-impacting 干预仍 indicated。

#### DNR 适用 vs 不适用情境

| 情境 | DNR 适用？ | 处理 |
|---|---|---|
| **Cardiopulmonary arrest**（心脏停 / 无脉性 VT/VF / 呼吸停）| ✅ DNR 适用 | 不 CPR / 不 defibrillation / 不插管 |
| **Acute stroke + tPA candidate** | ❌ DNR 不适用 | 按 standard of care 给 tPA |
| **Sepsis + 需 IV 抗生素** | ❌ DNR 不适用 | 给抗生素 |
| **Acute MI + 需 PCI / fibrinolysis** | ❌ DNR 不适用（除非 cardiac arrest）| 给 reperfusion |
| **GI bleed + 需输血** | ❌ DNR 不适用 | 输血 |
| **Cancer pain + 需 opioid** | ❌ DNR 不适用 | 给 pain control（hospice 标配）|
| **DNR 患者 thrombolysis 后 → 心跳停** | ✅ DNR 触发 | 不 CPR |

> [!tip] 一句话锁
> 「**DNR 仅 arrest 时触发；当前 intervention 不是复苏 → standard of care 全开**」

### §2.3 Advance Directive 完整 SOP ⭐（v1.4 新增 — 之前 TODO 兑现）

> [!info] Advance Directive = legally binding instructions for own health care
> Definition：legally binding instructions regarding patient's own health care + considered determinative for giving consent/refusal + **takes priority over family's/friends' preferences**。
> Requirements：① Patient is **competent at time directive is prepared** ② Enacted **only when patient lacks capacity** for decision-making。

#### Advance Directive 两大 Types

| 类型 | 内容 | 限制 |
|---|---|---|
| **Living will** | Written document 提前 specify 同意 / 拒绝特定 services（intubation / 透析 / artificial nutrition / CPR）| 不能 cover all potential scenarios |
| **Health care proxy / Durable POA** | Designate 一位 surrogate decision maker（**legally appointed by patient**）| 由 proxy 在 patient incapacitated 时按 substituted judgment 决定 |

#### Advance Directive 决策树（含 conflict 处理）⭐

```
Patient lacks capacity（incapacitated）
    │
    ▼
有 Living will 明确 cover 当前情境？
    │
    ├─ ✅ 有 → **按 Living will**（优先于 POA + family 的意见）⭐
    │      ※ Conflict 时 Living will overrules health care proxy
    │      ※ 例：Living will refuse intubation → POA 想 intubate → 不 intubate
    │
    └─ ❌ 无 / partial → 走 Health care proxy（POA）
            │
            ├─ ✅ 有 POA → POA 按 substituted judgment 决定
            │
            └─ ❌ 无 POA → 走 next of kin hierarchy（[[完整笔记/专题笔记/_衍生_Ethics精简框架]] §二）
                   ↓
            （仍 → substituted judgment + shared values）
```

> [!success] 关键 reframe
> **Living will > POA > next of kin** — Living will 是 patient 自己 explicit wishes，权威最高；POA 是 patient 之前 authorized 的代表（次高）；next of kin 是 default fallback。
> "有 POA → 一切 POA 说了算" = ❌ — Living will 仍 supersede POA。

### §2.4 VSED（Voluntarily Stopping Eating and Drinking）⭐（v1.4 新增）

> [!info] VSED = 患者主动停饮食 signal symbolic readiness for death
> Goals of patient care in terminal illness = preserving patient autonomy + prioritizing patient comfort。VSED 可能 increase physical suffering + hasten death，但 also increase psychological comfort by promoting autonomy over dying process。
> 一般 falls within patient autonomy + 不 differ in principle from other patient refusals（如 DNR）。

#### Physician 处理 VSED request 的 4 步

```
Patient request VSED
    │
    ▼
① Confirm cognitively intact + voluntary + not influenced by mental illness
   ※ 排除 reversible depression / 急性 mental illness
    │
    ▼
② Counsel about possible consequences
   - Severe thirst + associated discomfort
   - 加速 dying process
    │
    ▼
③ Discuss alternatives
   - Continuing fluids only（拒食物 / 接受液体）
   - Decreasing rather than eliminating oral intake
    │
    ▼
④ Discuss + record preferences for managing discomfort
   - Small sips of fluids / oral swabbing / ice chips
   - Why: delirium / cognitive impairment may occur as VSED progresses
   - 提前 record 防 lose voice
```

#### VSED vs DNR 类比

| 维度 | VSED | DNR / refusal of treatment |
|---|---|---|
| **法律 / ethical 性质** | Patient autonomy 行使 | Patient autonomy 行使 |
| **Beneficence 制约** | 不超越 competent + informed autonomy | 同 |
| **Physician 角色** | Verify capacity + voluntary + counsel + record preferences | 同 |

> [!warning] 不需 ethics consultation（如果 cognitively intact + voluntary + hospice）
> Ethics committee 仅在 intractable disagreement 时启动；VSED + cognitively intact + voluntary + hospice setting = 明确 autonomy 行使，不需 consult。

### §2.5 NEOT 与 3 Philosophies（保留原有内容 — Q21627 锚定）

#### "Withdrawing life support ≠ withdrawing care" 关键 reframe

> [!success] NEOT 不是放弃
> "**Withdrawing life support ≠ withdrawing care**" — NEOT 是**继续提供 supportive care**（pain control + comfort + 已上药物 + IV fluids + 抗生素）+ 仅**限制新 escalation**。
> 这是 family 怕 guilt 时的关键 reframe — 不是放弃，是停在已尽 reasonable interventions 的 line 上。

#### Surrogate decision 指南

> [!tip] Substituted judgment + shared values
> Surrogate 不应按"自己想让 patient 接受什么治疗"决定，应按 **"patient would have wanted"（substituted judgment）+ shared familial / cultural values** 决定。
> Simply advising "don't feel guilty" 无效 — 没解决 family 的核心 conflict；要给 surrogate **decision framework** 不是情绪 reassurance。

#### 帮 Distressed Surrogate 3 大技巧 ⭐（Q23113 锚定 — v1.4.6 新增）

> [!danger] Surrogate guilt 解决靠 framework 不靠 reassurance
> Surrogate decision-makers 常 experience stress / grief / conflict — 特别 end-of-life withdraw 决策（"I feel like I'll be a bad daughter if I give up on her"）。"Don't feel guilty" / "I trust you" 都是**passive abandonment** — 把 burden 全推给 surrogate。Physician 必须 active 提供 framework + recommendation。

| 技巧 | 内容 | 反射句式 |
|---|---|---|
| **① Refocus on patient** ⭐ | 问 patient was like before illness → bring attention to patient's goals/values + 建立 physician-surrogate trust | **"Help me understand what your mother was like before she got sick."** |
| **② Help apply knowledge** | 帮 surrogate 把 patient goals 应用到 current situation using objective clinical info（prognosis / complications）| "Given her values, how would she weigh continued dialysis vs comfort?" |
| **③ Emphasize shared decision-making** | Offer close empathetic guidance + recommendations based on medical condition → reduce burden + alleviate guilt | **"We can decide together what course is most appropriate."** |

> [!warning] 5 个 passive 反 pattern（USMLE 钓饵）
> - **"Sometimes better to let loved ones go"** → empathy OK 但**不 refocus on patient** + paternalistic
> - **"I trust you to put mother's best interests first"** → passive abandonment + burden 全推给 surrogate
> - **"You're helping her achieve peaceful death"** → empathy + 转 narrative，但**仍 focus on daughter emotion** 而非 patient values
> - **"You're a good daughter as long as you honor what your mother would want"** → 引入 substituted judgment 概念但 **places pressure**（好坏 conditional on her decision）
> - **"Don't feel guilty"** → reassurance only，没 framework

> [!success] 一句话锁
> 「**问 patient was like before illness = 进入 substituted judgment 的钥匙**」+「**shared decision-making：we decide together**」— 不要 "I trust you" / 不要 "better to let go" / 不要 "good daughter if honor wishes"。

#### 关键陷阱

> [!warning]
> - "Reassure family not to feel guilty"（A 类）→ 没给 surrogate decision framework，只是情绪 reassurance
> - "Ethics committee"（B 类）→ 用于 intractable disagreement（active 冲突），不是 family reluctant
> - "Quality of life argument"（D 类）→ Quality of life **主观 + 文化差异大**；physician 不单方面 judge
> - "Respect family wishes + continue escalation"（E 类）→ Patient deteriorating + family 实际 reluctant（不是 firm commitment）+ futile → frank discussion 才负责任
> - **"DNR = 不要任何治疗"（Q107705 类）** → ❌ DNR 仅 arrest 时触发 ⭐ v1.4 新增
> - **"Metastatic cancer = thrombolysis 禁忌"（Q107705 类）** → ❌ Terminal illness 仍接受有显著 quality-of-life impact 的干预 ⭐ v1.4 新增

### §2.6 Code Status Discussion Framework — How to Talk About DNR ⭐（UW 做对题 — v1.4.5 新增）

#### 反射钩

> [!info]
> Severe / progressive disease + 住院 + 可能 cardiorespiratory failure → 必须 candidly discuss code status preferences。
> **核心 4 大原则**：① **Reassure** decisions 不 alter treatment / quality of care ② **Broader discussion** of goals of care（不窄 yes/no）③ **Comprehensive intervention**（不 a-la-carte 逐项问）④ **Neutral terminology** + 避 leading questions（双向 — 不 push toward FULL CODE 也不 push toward DNR）。

#### 4 大核心原则深度对照

| 原则 | 内容 | 操作化 |
|---|---|---|
| **① Reassure care priority** | Patient 常 fear **DNR = 被 care team 降级 priority / 影响整体 care** → 必须 explicitly reassure | "Your decision about code status will not change how much we care for you or the quality of care we provide. We continue all other treatments fully." |
| **② Broader goals-of-care context** | 不直接问 "do you want CPR yes/no" — 先 establish patient's **overall goals**（活 quality / 与 family 时间 / 避免 prolonged dying / 完成 specific 心愿）| "Before we discuss specific resuscitation measures, tell me what matters most to you in your care right now." |
| **③ Comprehensive intervention** | Resuscitation 是 **comprehensive package**（CPR + intubation + mechanical ventilation + pressors + defib + ICU 升级）；不 a-la-carte 问 | A-la-carte = "do you want chest compression? do you want intubation? do you want vasopressors?" → 让 patient 在不理解 overall trajectory 下分项决定 → confusion + 不 reflect true preferences |
| **④ Neutral terminology** | 避 leading language — **双向** avoidance（既不 push 向 full code 也不 push 向 DNR）| 见下表 |

> [!info] 临床场景实操对照 — 4 大原则 why + 好/坏问法 + CK stem 暗号 ⭐
>
> **前提**：Code Status Discussion 解决 4 个核心难点 — 患者怕被冷落（①）、不知怎么决定（②）、术语 confuse 选出矛盾组合（③）、被措辞 push 偏方向（④）。**4 大原则正是对应这 4 个难点的解决方案**。
>
> **核心 3 个 Code Status**：
> - **Full Code** = 全力抢救（CPR + 插管 + 呼吸机 + ICU + pressors + defib 全套）
> - **DNR** (Do Not Resuscitate) = 心脏骤停**不**抢救
> - **DNI** (Do Not Intubate) = **不**插管（可与 DNR 独立）
>
> ---
>
> **① Reassure Care Priority — 强调 DNR 不降低照护质量**
>
> **Why**：患者最大恐惧 = "选 DNR 后医生会**冷落 / 不认真治 / 让我等死**"。这是**人之常情误解** — 把"DNR = 临终决定"读成"DNR = 整体降级"。**事实**：DNR 只决定"**IF** 心跳停 / 呼吸停时做什么"，**不影响其他所有 active care**（抗生素、疼痛管理、IV 液、营养、家属探视、舒适护理）。
>
> | 问法 | 例子 |
> |---|---|
> | ❌ 不 reassure 直接问 | "So do you want DNR or not?" |
> | ✅ 先 reassure 再讨论 | "**Your decision about code status will NOT change how much we care for you or the quality of care.** We continue all other treatments fully. Code status only addresses what we would do **IF** your heart or breathing fails." |
>
> **CK stem 暗号**：患者犹豫 DNR + 担心被忽视 → 医生**第一句**就是 reassure care continues。
>
> ---
>
> **② Broader Goals-of-Care Context — 先问 goals 再讨论 specific**
>
> **Why**：直接问 "Do you want CPR yes/no" → 患者懵（CPR 是什么？基于什么决定？）。Code status 决定应该 **derive 自患者更深层目标**（人生价值观），不是孤立 yes/no。
>
> | 问法 | 例子 |
> |---|---|
> | ❌ 直接窄问 | "Mr. Smith, if your heart stops, do you want CPR?" |
> | ✅ 先广后窄 | "Before we discuss specific resuscitation measures, **can you tell me what matters most to you in your care right now? What is your hope for the time you have? What concerns you most?**" |
>
> 患者可能答："I want to go home, say goodbye to my granddaughter, and not die with tubes everywhere." → 医生 derive：**DNR + comfort-focused care** 符合患者真实意愿。
>
> **关键**：Code status 是**手段**，goals of care 才是目的。**先理解人，再决定方案**。
>
> **CK stem 暗号**：医生 first task = "**explore patient's goals of care**"，不是 jump 到 CPR yes/no。
>
> ---
>
> **③ Comprehensive Intervention — 不 a-la-carte 逐项问**
>
> **Why**：Resuscitation 不是单一动作，是一整套**连环干预**（CPR → 插管 → 呼吸机 → pressors → ICU → defib）。**逐项问会导致医学不一致的决定**。
>
> **经典矛盾**：患者说"我要 CPR 但不要插管" — 临床现实 = **CPR 成功后几乎都需要插管维持氧合**，单独要 CPR 没意义（"想活下来但拒绝活下来的工具"）。
>
> | 问法 | 例子 |
> |---|---|
> | ❌ A-la-carte 逐项 | "Do you want chest compressions? Do you want intubation? Do you want vasopressors? Do you want defibrillation?" → 患者被术语 confuse + 选出矛盾组合 |
> | ✅ Comprehensive package | "Resuscitation in this situation typically involves **several connected interventions — chest compressions, breathing tube, ventilator, medications, ICU care — as a package**. Given your goals, would you like us to attempt this comprehensive resuscitation, or prefer a **more comfort-focused approach**?" |
>
> **关键**：复苏 = **一整套不能拆零买**。决定 = full code（全套）/ DNR（全不做）/ limited code（**例外**，明确限定范围）。
>
> **CK stem 暗号**：选项里有"ask separately about CPR, intubation, vasopressors" → **错**；选"discuss resuscitation as a comprehensive package"。
>
> ---
>
> **④ Neutral Terminology — 双向避免 leading language**
>
> **Why**：用词带价值判断 → 偏向某一方向 → 不是真实 informed decision。**双向都要避** —— 不能 push 选 full code，也不能 push 选 DNR。详细 leading 用词对照见下方 Neutral Terminology Table。
>
> **CK stem 暗号**：题目两个选项一个用 "**keep you alive**"，另一个用 "**attempt CPR**" → 选**后者**（neutral）。
>
> ---
>
> **一句话钉死 4 大原则 ⭐**
>
> **"DNR 讨论 4 步反射"**：
> 1. **先 reassure** —— "Your decision won't affect care quality"
> 2. **先广后窄** —— 先问 goals of care，再 derive 具体 code status
> 3. **整体 package** —— Resuscitation 是一整套，不 a-la-carte
> 4. **中性用词** —— 用 "CPR / breathing machine"，避 "keep alive" 或 "tubes and machines"
>
> **反射钩**：看到 DNR 讨论 stem 的选项 → 排除 ① 没 reassure 的 / ② 直接问 CPR yes/no 的 / ③ 逐项 a-la-carte 问的 / ④ 用 leading language 的 → 剩下的就是正解。

#### Neutral Terminology Table — 双向 leading language 都要避

| 方向 | Leading 语言（避用）| Why biased |
|---|---|---|
| **Push toward FULL CODE**（pro-resuscitation euphemism）| "Do you want us to **keep you alive**?" / "Do you want us to **keep you from dying**?" / "Do you want measures to **help you live longer**?" | Patient 很难 decline — 谁会说"我不想活"？强迫患者选 full code |
| **Push toward DNR**（negative connotation）| "Do you want us to use **drastic / artificial measures**?" / "Do you want **tubes and machines**?" / "Do you want us to **push on your chest**?" | 听起来 invasive / 暴力 / 痛苦 → patient 倾向 decline |
| **Neutral language**（正解）| "Should your heart stop, would you want us to attempt **cardiopulmonary resuscitation**?" / "If breathing fails, would you want us to use **a breathing machine**?" | 中性医学术语 + 不暗示 outcome 方向 |

#### 完整 Discussion SOP

```
Severe disease + 可能 cardiorespiratory failure
    │
    ▼
① Setting — private + adequate time + 关键 family（如 patient wishes）
    │
    ▼
② Reassure care priority ⭐ 先讲这一句
   "Your decision about code status doesn't change the care we provide."
    │
    ▼
③ Establish overall goals of care
   "Tell me what matters most to you right now."
   "What are you hoping for / worried about?"
    │
    ▼
④ Provide medical context（broad picture，不深入细节）
   Prognosis + likely scenarios + 治疗 options
    │
    ▼
⑤ Discuss resuscitation as comprehensive intervention
   "If your heart stops or breathing fails, the team would attempt CPR — that's a package
    including chest compressions, breathing tubes, mechanical ventilation, and ICU care."
    │
    ▼
⑥ Use neutral terminology + 让 patient express true preferences
   "Given what's important to you, would you want us to attempt this if your heart stops?"
    │
    ▼
⑦ Document + 必要时 reassess（preferences 可随病情变化 — 定期 revisit）
```

#### 为什么 a-la-carte 是钓饵

> [!warning] A-la-carte 问法 = 3 个问题
> - Patient 不理解 individual interventions 在 resuscitation 序列中的位置（"我要 chest compressions 但不要 intubation"= 临床上不连贯，因为 compressions 后多数需 intubation）
> - 让 patient 在 fragmented info 下 decide → preferences 不 reflect overall goals
> - Care team 收到 inconsistent orders → confusion + 不能 honor patient's true intent
>
> 正确：先 establish goals → 然后 patient choose comprehensive package（full code / DNR / DNI + selective treatment / NEOT 等 §2.1 5 档之一），care team 按 package 执行。

#### 4 大反 pattern（钓饵选项）

| 反 pattern | 例 | 为什么错 |
|---|---|---|
| **① Pro-full-code euphemism** | "Do you want us to keep you alive?" | Leading language → patient 难 decline → 不 reflect true preference |
| **② Pro-DNR negative phrasing** | "Do you want drastic measures / tubes and machines?" | Leading language → patient 倾向 decline → 不 reflect true preference |
| **③ A-la-carte 逐项问** | "Chest compressions? Intubation? Pressors?" 分别问 | Removes focus from overall goals + confusion + inconsistent orders |
| **④ Skip reassurance about care priority** | 直接问 code status without first reassuring DNR ≠ less care | Patient 怀疑 DNR = 被 deprioritize → 选 full code 防被 deprioritize（fear-based 而非 value-based decision）|

#### 与 §2.1 / §2.2 衔接

```
本节 §2.6 = "如何 TALK about code status"（communication framework）
    │
    ▼
Patient express preference → 落到 §2.1 5 档之一
    （Full code / DNR / DNI / NEOT / Time-limited trial / Comfort care）
    │
    ▼
Specific 档执行细节 → §2.2 DNR 范围限定（DNR ≠ Do Not Treat）
                  → §2.3 Advance Directive SOP（Living will > POA）
                  → §2.4 VSED（autonomy 行使）
                  → §2.5 NEOT philosophies
```

#### USMLE 经典 stem 暗号

| Stem 元素 | 暗示 |
|---|---|
| **"Severe / progressive disease + acute illness + possible cardiorespiratory failure"** | 必谈 code status |
| **"Patient may wish to avoid intubation / mechanical ventilation"** | Likely DNR/DNI preference — 用 neutral language elicit |
| **"Discuss preferences candidly"** | Code status conversation 必触发 |
| **"Promote alignment with patient's preferences"** | 强调 neutral + reassure care priority |

#### 一句话锁

> [!success]
> 「**Reassure care priority + Broader goals + Comprehensive package + Neutral terminology**」4 件套 — 缺一不算完整 code status discussion。
> 双向避 leading：不 "keep alive" / 不 "tubes and machines"。

### §2.7 Home Hospice Care — Eligibility + Common Misconceptions ⭐（Q21142 锚定 — UW 做对题 v1.4.7 新增）

#### 反射钩

> [!info]
> Advanced terminal illness（cancer with mets / 严重 progressive disease）+ poor prognosis + family 问 "hospice will make him die sooner?" → 反射 **"Hospice is not intended to make people die sooner but helps people die naturally, in their own time"**。
> 核心 reframes：① **Hospice 不缩短 life expectancy**（部分研究显示甚至 small ↑）② Early referral **enhance** palliative care quality（不 delay）③ Life-prolonging tx not allowed 但本患者**未 request** → hospice 仍 appropriate。

#### Home Hospice Care 完整框架

| 维度 | 内容 |
|---|---|
| **Patient selection** | Life expectancy **≤ 6 个月** / Agreement to **forgo curative & life-sustaining treatment** / Family or private caregiver available for daily needs / Current symptoms manageable with home care |
| **Location of services** | Patient home / Assisted living facility / Nursing home |
| **Typical services** | Periodic home visits by experienced personnel（nurse）/ Palliative medications（analgesics, antiemetics）/ Durable medical equipment（hospital bed, wheelchair）/ Physical therapy（and other therapy modalities）/ Grief counseling + spiritual + psychosocial support for patient + family |

#### Hospice 4 大 misconception（CK 高频）

| 误解 | 正解 |
|---|---|
| **① "Hospice makes people die sooner"** | ❌ Hospice **does NOT shorten life expectancy**；部分研究 small **increase** in survival（fewer complications of hospitalization / aggressive tx）|
| **② "Hospice meds (morphine) hasten death"** | Palliative meds **may** unintentionally hasten death（如 morphine → respiratory depression），但 **aim 不是 death** — meds 选择基于 current symptoms + patient consent |
| **③ "Hospice means abandoning hope / waiting to die"** | ❌ Hospice = focus on **natural death + comfort + QoL** + spiritual/psychosocial support；部分 patient 在 hospice 中 outlive expectations |
| **④ "Hospice referral 应 delay until 完全 ready"** | ❌ **Early referral enhances** palliative care quality；不该 delay；可随时 withdraw if patient decides on life-prolonging tx |

#### 4 大反 pattern（钓饵）

| 反 pattern | 例 | 为什么错 |
|---|---|---|
| **① "Continue life-prolonging measures even in hospice"** | A 类 | Patients on life-prolonging tx **NOT eligible** for hospice；可 withdraw from hospice if 决定接受 life-prolonging |
| **② "Hospice meds hasten death but help comfort"** | B 类 | 部分对（morphine 可 unintentionally hasten），**但 aim 不是 death** — particular regimen based on current symptoms + patient consent |
| **③ "Delay referral until ready"** | D 类 | ❌ 损 quality of palliative care；early referral best |
| **④ "Since you want to delay death, discuss other options"** | E 类 | Patient **未 request** life-prolonging tx；hospice 仍 appropriate |

#### 一句话锁

> [!success]
> 「**Hospice 不缩短 life，反而可能 small ↑ survival + 显著 ↑ QoL**」— 多数家属 misconception 是反过来的。
> 「**Early referral > delayed referral**」— 不要 wait until "ready"。

### §2.8 Elderly ESRD — Conservative Kidney Care vs Dialysis Counseling ⭐（Q21210 锚定 — UW 做对题 v1.4.7 新增）

#### 反射钩

> [!info]
> Elderly（≥80 岁）+ ESRD imminent + significant **comorbidities + functional decline** + patient **specifically asks about dialysis** → 反射 **"Although dialysis has its benefits, I would like to discuss the risks associated with it"** → 后续 counsel on benefits + risks + 介绍 **conservative kidney care**（nondialytic option focused on patient comfort）。
> 关键钓饵：patient asked about dialysis specifically → physician **不能 unilateral eliminate** dialysis option → 必 explain benefits + risks 而非 dismiss。

#### Dialysis vs Conservative Kidney Care — Elderly Comorbid Decision

| 维度 | Dialysis（younger / limited comorbidity）| **Conservative kidney care**（elderly + comorbidities + functional decline）⭐ |
|---|---|---|
| **Life expectancy benefit** | Extends in younger limited comorbidity | **Benefit negligible** in elderly comorbid + functional decline |
| **Vascular access** | Required | Not required |
| **Time/transportation** | 显著 burden（每周多次）| Minimal |
| **Hospitalizations** | **Prolonged fatigue + ↑ hospitalizations**（catheter-related infections）| 较少 |
| **QoL** | **Diminished** in elderly comorbid | Focused on comfort |
| **Life expectancy on conservative** | — | **6 months to 2 years** average |

#### Conservative Kidney Care 5 大组件

| 组件 | 内容 |
|---|---|
| **① Diet** | Low Na + low K + relatively low protein（minimize acid accumulation）|
| **② Medications** | Phosphate binder + erythropoiesis-stimulating agents（anemia）|
| **③ Volume/electrolyte/acid-base management** | Ameliorate volume + electrolyte + acid-base disturbances |
| **④ HTN + anemia management** | Continued |
| **⑤ Symptom palliation** | Address ESRD-associated fatigue + nausea + pain |

#### Counseling SOP

```
Elderly + ESRD imminent + comorbidities + patient asks about dialysis
    │
    ▼
① Acknowledge patient's question + 不 unilateral eliminate
    "Dialysis has its benefits..."
    │
    ▼
② Discuss benefits + risks of dialysis（candid + neutral language）
    Benefits: life prolongation in some
    Risks: prolonged fatigue / ↑ hospitalizations / vascular access / time burden / diminished QoL
    │
    ▼
③ Introduce conservative kidney care option
    "Alternately, conservative kidney care is a nondialytic option that focuses on your comfort..."
    │
    ▼
④ Ascertain patient's goals of care
    "What matters most to you in this stage of life?"
    │
    ▼
⑤ Shared decision-making based on patient values
```

#### 4 大反 pattern（钓饵）

| 反 pattern | 例 | 为什么错 |
|---|---|---|
| **① Endorse dialysis without risks**（B/C 类）| "Home peritoneal dialysis will avoid hospitalizations" / "Dialysis will allow you to live longer with good QoL" | Elderly comorbid + functional decline → benefit **negligible** + QoL **diminished**；不真实 |
| **② Unilateral eliminate dialysis**（D 类）| "I don't recommend dialysis given your age, let's talk other options" | Patient specifically asked → physician **必 explain** benefits + risks 让 patient decide；不 independently eliminate |
| **③ Jump to conservative without addressing patient's question**（E 类）| 直接讲 conservative care | Patient asked about dialysis specifically → 先 address dialysis 再介绍 alternative |
| **④ Withhold information**（implicit）| Not discussing risks of dialysis | 违 informed consent — patient need 双面信息 to decide |

#### 一句话锁

> [!success]
> 「**Patient asked X → 必 explain X benefits + risks，不 unilateral eliminate X**」（同 [[完整笔记/专题笔记/_衍生_Ethics精简框架]] §九 directive 但反方向 — 那里 1 safe option directive 推荐；这里 patient asks 必 discuss）
> 「**Elderly + comorbidity + functional decline → dialysis benefit negligible**；conservative kidney care = comfort + 6 mo–2 yr life expectancy」
