## Q25 — Nurse reporting an incident（眼溅血液职业暴露）

**What should the nurse do first? → B (go to another area of the hospital to be assessed)**

原文关键句：
> "You'll have to go <font color=#ff0000>**straight**</font> downstairs to <font color=#ff0000>**occupational health**</font> where they'll <font color=#ff0000>**advise and evaluate**</font> you and possibly take some blood. <font color=#ff0000>**Then**</font> I'll help you fill in a formal <font color=#ff0000>**risk assessment**</font>."

同义替换链：
- **go straight downstairs to occupational health** = go to another area of the hospital（职业健康科 = 医院另一区域）
- **advise and evaluate** = be assessed（评估）

干扰项分析：
- **A** documentation：原文确实有 "fill in a formal risk assessment"，但被 "**Then**"（然后）排在第二步 → 时序词是关键，题目问 **first**
- **C** check patient's history：护士自己说 "I can check"（his notes are with him in recovery），但 manager 没有采纳，直接指示先去 occupational health → 说过 ≠ 指示

> [!tip] 出题套路
> "do first" 题必有 ≥2 个动作都在原文出现，靠 **straight / then / first / before** 这类时序词分先后，不是靠"哪个动作听到了"。

## Q26 — Two nurses talking about a patient（Mr. Lee 扔勺子）

**They agree that the patient is → C (behaving unreasonably)**

原文关键句：
> "Honestly, it does sound like he's <font color=#ff0000>**singling you out**</font> a little. That's <font color=#ff0000>**hardly fair**</font>." — "No, it's not, is it?"

同义替换链：
- **singling you out**（针对你一个人）+ **hardly fair**（很不公平）+ 对方附和 "No, it's not" = behaving **unreasonably**

干扰项分析：
- **A** entitled to complain：他确实一直 moaning（抱怨 bandage too tight、curtains），但两人的共识是抱怨**没道理**，不是"有权抱怨"
- **B** a threat to staff safety：扔了勺子但 "Did it hit you?" — "No, he's <font color=#ff0000>**harmless**</font>, but that's not the point" → 原文直接否定"威胁"

> [!warning] "They agree" 题型
> 答案必须是**两个人都表态**的点。找一人陈述 + 另一人附和（"No, it's not, is it?" 这种 tag question 附和）的交汇处，单方观点不算。

## Q27 — Nurse Manager briefing（Mr. Parks 滑倒骨折）

**What is she doing? → B (stressing the need to follow hospital procedure)**

原文关键句：
> "all this could have been avoided if a minor spillage had been dealt with at once and <font color=#ff0000>**protocol had been adhered to**</font>… I must <font color=#ff0000>**emphasize our policy**</font> of immediate action upon any spillage… is in place for a very good reason."

同义替换链：
- **emphasize** = stress（强调）
- **policy / protocol adhered to** = follow hospital procedure（遵守流程）

干扰项分析：
- **A** informing of injury seriousness：伤情（hip surgery）只是开场背景，不是讲话目的
- **C** outlining consequences：legal action 只一句带过（"worst case scenario"），且是用来支撑"必须守规矩"的论据

> [!tip] "What is she doing" 主旨题
> 别抓第一句（背景），抓 **I must emphasize / the point is / what matters** 之后的内容——说话人自己标记的重点才是功能主旨。

## Q28 — Pharmacist explaining medication（atorvastatin 阿托伐他汀 副作用）

**Why does she give a verbal explanation? → C (to allow the patient to raise any concerns)**

原文关键句：
> "This is all explained in the <font color=#ff0000>**booklet**</font> I'll give you, but I wanna make sure I <font color=#ff0000>**answer any queries**</font> you may have before you're <font color=#ff0000>**discharged**</font>… Is there <font color=#ff0000>**anything you'd like to ask**</font>?"

同义替换链：
- **answer any queries you may have** + **anything you'd like to ask** = allow the patient to **raise concerns**（queries/ask = concerns）

干扰项分析：
- **A** highlight severity：她确实分了 less serious / more serious，但那是内容组织方式，不是"为什么口头讲"的原因
- **B** ensure understanding：诱人，但原文说 "You <font color=#ff0000>**won't need to remember them all**</font>"（不要求都记住/理解），落点是 be aware + 有问题随时问

> [!warning] 本题是"目的题"
> booklet 已经覆盖了"理解"功能（written info），口头讲的**增量价值**= 互动问答。开头 "but I wanna make sure…" 的 but 之后就是答案。

## Q29 — Nurse handover（6 岁男孩 Robbie，left lower lobe pneumonia 左下叶肺炎）

**What must the colleague be sure to do? → B (monitor the patient's vital signs)**

原文关键句：
> "At around 12 a.m., <font color=#ff0000>**you'll need to check**</font> that his <font color=#ff0000>**vitals are stable**</font> and unchanged from now and oxygen saturation hasn't dropped."

主语归属分析（本题核心技巧）：
- **B** vitals → "**you'll** need to check" = 交接对象（colleague）的任务 ✅
- **C** blood culture → "**Dr. Stone** needs to determine if his blood culture is positive" = 医生的任务 ❌
- **A** mobile chest X-ray → "**if** his respiratory status worsens, he'll most likely get you to order…" = 条件句 + 将来可能 ❌

> [!tip] Handover 题型三过滤
> 三个选项通常都在原文出现，靠三个维度过滤：①**主语是谁的任务**（you vs Dr. X）②**确定 vs 条件**（need to vs if…most likely）③**现在 vs 将来可能**。"must be sure to do" = 找 you + need to + 无条件那条。

## Q30 — Physician feedback after consultation roleplay

**The most important aspect of a good consultation is → A (creating a secure environment based on trust)**

原文关键句：
> "A key aspect of our profession is <font color=#ff0000>**confidentiality**</font>… because it gives a <font color=#ff0000>**foundation**</font> upon which they know they can <font color=#ff0000>**rely**</font>. The patient needs to be <font color=#ff0000>**assured**</font> of this… And this is <font color=#ff0000>**paramount**</font> for securing any doctor-patient relationship."

同义替换链：
- **foundation they can rely on** = trust（信任）
- **assured** = secure（安心/安全感）
- 整段逻辑：confidentiality → 患者敢信任 → 敢开口 → 关系建立 = **secure environment based on trust**

干扰项分析：
- **C** explaining patient privacy：字面陷阱——confidentiality 一词确实出现，但它是**手段**（用来建立信任的工具），题目问的"most important aspect"是它服务的目的（trust foundation）
- **B** relaxed atmosphere："helps them **relax**" 也原词出现，但同样只是中间效果，且 paramount 那句落在 relationship/foundation 上

> [!danger] 高级陷阱：手段 vs 目的
> 30 题是 Part B 最难的一类：三个选项的词**全部**在原文出现（confidentiality→C，relax→B，rely/assured→A）。区分靠因果链方向：X because it gives Y → Y 才是核心。**paramount / key / essential** 修饰谁，答案就是谁的同义改写。

## 本组做题技巧总结（5 条）

1. **时序词定 first**（Q25）：straight / then / before / after 决定顺序，别选"听到过"的第一个动作。
2. **共识题找附和点**（Q26）：一人说 + 一人 tag question 附和（"No, it's not, is it?"）才算 they agree。
3. **主旨题抓自标记**（Q27）：I must emphasize / the point is 之后 = 功能答案；开场事故描述只是背景。
4. **任务归属过滤**（Q29）：you'll need to（确定+你）> Dr. X needs to（别人）> if…most likely（条件将来）。
5. **手段 vs 目的**（Q30）：三个选项原词全出现时，看 because / in turn / paramount 的因果链落点，选**目的**不选**工具**。
