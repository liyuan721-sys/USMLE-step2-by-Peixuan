# OET 术语与沟通句库（写作/口语共用）

> 返回 [[00_OET总览]] · 服务于 [[OET_写作_转诊信模板]] + [[OET_口语_role-play模板]]

> [!success] 这是你唯一真正要"背"的东西
> OET（Occupational English Test，职业英语考试）奖励"去术语化"、不是术语量。对病人（Speaking）或写给同行时讲到病情，一律换成大白话。每天 10 分钟、穿插 Step 2 复习里背即可。

| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Myocardial infarction        | a heart attack                                           |
| Hypertension                 | high blood pressure                                      |
| Hypotension                  | low blood pressure                                       |
| Dyspnea                      | shortness of breath / trouble breathing                  |

| Hyperlipidemia               | high cholesterol / high fats in the blood                |
| Benign                       | not cancer / harmless                                    |
| Malignant                    | cancerous                                                |
| Biopsy                       | take a small tissue sample to look at under a microscope |

| Chronic                      | long-term / ongoing                                      |
| Acute                        | sudden / short-term                                      |
| Inflammation                 | redness, swelling and irritation                         |
| Anemia                       | low level of red blood cells / "low blood"               |

| Fracture                     | a broken bone                                            |

## 二、医患沟通功能句库（按场景速取）

| 场景 | 句式 |
|---|---|
| 坏消息铺垫 | "I'm afraid I have some news that may be difficult to hear. Is now a good time?" |
| 给空间 | "Take your time. There's no rush." |
| 鼓励提问 | "Please stop me anytime if something isn't clear." |
| 确认顾虑 | "What's worrying you the most about this?" |
| 表达共情 | "I can only imagine how stressful this must be." |
| 解释下一步 | "Here's what I suggest we do next…" |
| 安全网 | "If things get worse — for example [红旗], please come back immediately." |
| 收尾确认 | "Before you leave, can I just check — is there anything else you'd like to ask?" |
