# OET 口语 — role-play 模板（你的最大战场）

> 返回 [[00_OET总览]] · 配套语料 [[OET_术语与沟通句库]]

> [!info] 怎么考
> 2 个 role-play，每个 ~5 分钟。你拿到 role card（演**医生**），考官演**病人/家属**。评分两组：
> - **语言类**：Intelligibility 清晰度 / Fluency 流利 / Appropriateness 用词得体 / Grammar & expression
> - **临床沟通类** ⭐（你要刻意练）：Relationship-building 建立关系 / Patient's perspective 理解病人视角 / Providing structure 给结构 / Information-gathering 问诊 / Information-giving 解释

## 万能六步框架（每个 role-play 都能套）

```
1. 开场建立关系   Rapport
2. 开放式问诊     Elicit（先听病人说）
3. 共情确认       Empathy（接住情绪）
4. 大白话分块解释 Explain（chunk + check 去术语化）
5. 协商 / 处理抗拒 Negotiate
6. 总结 + 安全网 + 收尾  Summary + safety-net
```

## 每步功能句库（背这些，比背单词管用 10 倍）

**① 开场 Rapport**
- "Hello, I'm Dr [名]. Thanks for coming in today. How are you feeling?"
- "Before we start, is there anything in particular you'd like to talk about?"

**② 开放式问诊 Elicit**
- "Can you tell me a bit more about what's been happening?"
- "When did you first notice this?"
- "How has it been affecting your daily life?"

**③ 共情 Empathy（接住情绪，OET 高度奖励）**
- "I can see this has been really worrying for you."
- "That sounds very difficult — thank you for sharing that with me."
- "It's completely understandable to feel anxious about this."

**④ 大白话解释 Explain（chunk + check）**
- 去术语化："What this means in simple terms is…"
- 分块："Let me explain this in two parts. First… / The second thing is…"
- 检查理解："Just to make sure I've explained it clearly — could you tell me what you've understood so far?"
- 给选择："There are a couple of options we could consider. Would it help if I went through them?"

**⑤ 协商 / 处理抗拒 Negotiate**
- "I understand you're hesitant. May I ask what's worrying you most?"
- "How would you feel about trying… for now, and reviewing it in two weeks?"
- "What matters most to you in deciding this?"

**⑥ 总结 + 安全网 + 收尾**
- "So, to summarise what we've agreed…"
- "If [红旗症状] happens, please come back straight away or call us."
- "Do you have any questions before you go?"

> [!tip] Peixuan 专属提醒
> 你最容易因"病理科背景、直接面诊少"而**跳过共情、直接讲机制**。考官就盯这个。**每个 role-play 强制做满第 3 步（共情）+ 第 4 步的 check 理解**，比你专业知识更影响分数。

> [!info] 大白话替换 + 更多沟通句
> 解释病情时把术语换成患者大白话、各场景功能句（坏消息铺垫 / 安全网 / 收尾确认）见 [[OET_术语与沟通句库]]。

# 考经实战句库（文傲，2026-06-28 入库）

> [!success] 来源
> 来自文傲 OET 考经（源文档在 [[源资料]] 文件夹 `OET 考经.docx`）。这一整套是**比上面六步框架细得多的"逐句可背"句库**——上面是骨架，下面是肉。重点直接背下面这些功能句。

## 审题（3 分钟内先问自己 7 件事）

> [!tip] 拿到 role card 先判断，决定开场和结构
> 1. **病人发生了什么**？初次就诊 / 复诊 / 检查结果出来了？→ 决定 greeting 开场方式
> 2. **setting 在哪**（诊所 / 病房 / 急诊）？→ 决定称呼（first name vs Mr/Ms + 姓）
> 3. **是病人本人还是家属**回答？
> 4. 识别 **information gathering** 部分——要问多个问题就**分开一个一个问**
> 5. 识别 **explain task 和医学术语**——不要照搬术语，**先解释再说术语**
> 6. Recognize task 属于 information gathering / diagnosis / treatment 哪一类——三类之间记得 **signposting** 过渡
> 7. 难词在 3 分钟内**自己先读一遍发音**，防止卡壳

## ① Greeting（按场景选开场）

| 场景 | 开场句 |
|---|---|
| 初次见面 | "Hi [患者 first name]. Nice to meet you. My name is XXX, I'm the doctor on duty today. How are you today?" |
| 再次见面 | "Hi [患者 first name]. It's good to see you again. My name is XXX, I'm the doctor on duty today. How are you today?" |
| On examination 查体 | "Hi Mr/Ms [姓]. Thanks for letting me examine you. I would like to ask you some questions about ……" |
| 拿到结果谈 diagnosis | "Hi Mr/Ms [姓]. It's good to see you again. I'm Doctor XXX. I have received the results, so how are you doing?" |
| Emergency 急诊 | "Hi Mr/Ms [姓]. I understand from the triage nurse that …… I'd like to ask you a few questions about ……" |

> [!warning] 称呼规则（容易扣 Appropriateness 分）
> - **Primary Clinic（社区诊所）→ 用 first name**（"Hi [名]"）；**hospital / emergency center → 用 Mr/Ms + 姓**。
> - **家长替孩子回答**：第一次称呼**孩子的名字**，之后用**亲属关系**称呼（daughter / son），**不要说 girl / boy**。

## ② Information gathering（先开放后具体）

> [!danger] 铁律：NEVER ask the patient anything from the background
> role card 背景里已给的信息**不要再问**（"it's your background information, you need to build on it"）。开放性问题可以问，但别问已知事实。

**开放式起手**
- 初次："What brings you here today?"
- 复诊（病历里有记录）："I can see from the medical chart that …… Could you tell me more about …… in your own words?"
- 通用开放句："Could you tell me more about …… in your own words?" / "What's your concern?"

**具体追问句库**

| 问什么 | 句式 |
|---|---|
| 首次出现 | "When did you first notice ……?" |
| 持续时长 | "How long do they last?" |
| 部位 | "Could you point out which part of your body feels the pain?" |
| trigger 诱因 | "Do you remember what you were doing when it began?" |
| 疼痛评分 | "Could you rate your pain on a scale of zero to ten, with ten being the most painful?" |
| 饮食 | "Do you have a balanced diet?" |
| 运动 | "Could you please tell me about your daily exercise routine?" |

> [!tip] task 不具体、只让你 gather information 时问什么
> 主问四块：**现病史 + medication for the symptoms + prior diagnosis + family history**。

## ③ Empathy（共情，OET 高度奖励 —— 你的最大失分点）

> [!danger] Empathy ≠ Sympathy
> Sympathy = 对别人的不幸感到同情/怜悯；**Empathy = 设身处地"穿上对方的鞋"**（putting yourself in the shoes of another）。OET 要 empathy，不要 sympathy。注意 **Tone（语气）**。
> **两个高发时机**：① 问完症状后 ② 告知 diagnosis 后。

| 情况 | 句式 |
|---|---|
| 患者**没**说负面情绪 | "I understand how you feel. Lots of people in your situation feel the same way. I am here to help you in the best way possible." |
| 患者**说了**负面情绪 | "I am sorry you went through that. I can imagine that must be quite painful / frustrating / unpleasant. Your health is my priority. I will do as much as I can to help you." |

## ④ Signposting（三个过渡场景，给结构 = Providing structure 评分项）

| 从 → 到 | 过渡句 |
|---|---|
| gather info → 谈 **examination results** | "Thanks for answering those questions. I have received the results from XXX / I have done the physical examination. I would like to discuss the results with you. I have good news for you / I am sad to inform you that based on your symptoms and after examination, it's highly possible that you could have ……. Do you have any questions about that?" |
| gather info → 谈 **diagnosis** | "Thank you for sharing the information. Now I want to explain what the problem is and how we can help you get better. ……（同上 good news / sad to inform 句）…… Do you have any questions about that?" |
| diagnosis → 谈 **treatment** | "We have discussed the diagnosis. I would like to move to the treatment part." |

## ⑤ Explain（万能公式 —— 去术语化的核心武器）

> [!success] 文傲自创 explain 万能公式（背下来，现场套）
> > **There is (etiology) in (organ). You will / you may notice you could have …… (表现). So, in medical terms, this is called '____'. + It's curable / manageable / not serious.**
>
> - **etiology 病因五选一**：肿瘤 / 外伤 / 炎症 / 免疫 / 遗传。
> - 想说复杂点：「The condition is treatable and manageable with appropriate interventions.」/「The prognosis for this condition varies, but it is generally not life-threatening if treated promptly.」
> - **套用示范（rhinitis 鼻炎）**："There is inflammation in your nose. You could have nose congestion. In medical terms, this is called rhinitis. It's manageable and not serious."

> [!warning] might vs could（解释因果时用错会显得不专业）
> - 解释某个**危险因素对疾病的促进** → 用 **might**。
> - 解释**可能性 / 因果关系** → 用 **could**（"it could be the result of ……"）。

**常见整句 explain（直接背）**
- SLE（systemic lupus erythematosus，系统性红斑狼疮）："SLE means your body is attacked by your own immune system."
- Asthma（哮喘）："Asthma is a breathing condition caused by airway hypersensitivity."

## ⑥ Management + Checking understanding

> [!tip] treatment 提示要 paraphrase，不要照念，下面是框架

| 情况 | 框架 |
|---|---|
| **一个** management | "I'd highly recommend you do …… + What do you think about that?" |
| **多个** management | "There are a few things I need to tell you about managing your current situation. + Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly + How do you feel about that?" |
| management 太多 | 切割成几个部分分别讲 |
| management 和前面问的生活方式有关 | "Based on what you've told me, (symptoms), I'd recommend ……" |

**Management paraphrase = 解释过程 + 说明作用**
- Bronchoscopy（支气管镜）/ Gastroscopy（胃镜）："using a thin tube connected to a camera to look into your lungs / stomach."
- 各种影像："use X-ray / magnet to obtain detailed pictures of your brain / body."
- exercise："to make XX strong."
- splint（夹板）："two plates to keep XX immobile, which is good for recovery."
- 转介资源（举例）："For stress management, the Stress-Relax Clinic can help you with techniques to manage and reduce stress." / "For smoking cessation, Quitline is a great resource to help you quit smoking and provide the support you need."

> [!danger] 给建议句式（避免 suggest/recommend 的语法错）
> 别说 ~~"suggest sb to do" / "recommend sb to do"~~（语法错）。正确：
> - **suggest**：suggest n / doing；suggest (that) you do
> - **recommend**：recommend doing；recommend (that) you do
> - **advise**：advise someone to do；advise doing
> - **advice（名词）**：my advice is to do

## ⑦ 应对患者回应（16 种高频情况 —— 临场救命）

> [!info] 用法
> 考官（演患者/家属）常抛这些"难题"考你的临床沟通。每条记一个反射句。

| # | 患者抛出 | 你的回应 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 担心换药副作用 | 不要等副作用出现才换。"May it be better if we switch from X to Y due to (side effect)?" |
| 2 | 问某药会不会有副作用 | "Different people can experience different side effects. Based on our experience, some patients may have (mouth ulcers or fatigue), but it really depends on the situation. Don't worry, we'll monitor your progress and adjust as needed." |
| 3 | 对药物过敏 | "I will inform the doctor about your allergy. Before we give you any medication, we will perform a skin test to ensure it's safe for you." |
| 4 | 没钱 | "I will refer you to the social worker to see what support we can provide for you." |
| 5 | 担心工作 / 能否回去上班 | 问工作情况 + 提供 medical certification + 安慰。"Can you tell me more about your work situation? We can provide a medical certification if needed for your employer. I'm sure your colleagues or employer will be supportive and understanding." |
| 6 | 觉得方案执行困难 | "I understand it's difficult to follow, since you have a very busy schedule. But …… is really important for your health / recovery. We can start with low-intensity exercise, like ……, and then increase the intensity gradually, so you can get used to it. How does that sound?" |
| 7 | 提出一个（不靠谱）方案 | "Thank you for sharing your thoughts, but I still highly recommend ……" |
| 8 | 半路插问别的问题 | "That's a good question. Is it okay if I come back to it after (asking a few more questions)? The thing I want to mention is that ……" |
| 9 | 不相信 / 要说服 | 正面（"Proved by rigorous clinical trials and recent guidelines; reduce the risk of future attacks"）+ 反面（不这样做的危害） |
| 10 | 问你不懂的东西 | "I recommend that you consult a specialist in [specialty] for further evaluation and treatment." |
| 11 | 漏听一小段 | "Forgive me, I didn't quite catch the last sentence. Could you please repeat it for me?" |
| 12 | 听清了但没理解 | "Could you explain to me what you mean by XXX?" |
| 13 | 问需不需要手术（你不确定） | "That's a fair question. Whether you need surgery depends on your symptoms and the test results. I'd like to arrange a follow-up and monitor your symptoms, and then we can decide on the best option for you." |
| 14 | 家庭内部矛盾 | 鼓励家庭沟通："I recommend having more communication with ……" |
| 15 | 不会做 aftercare | "Don't worry, the well-trained nurse will teach you how to take care of yourself after the treatment and guide you through the aftercare steps." |
| 16 | terminal 癌症病人消极 | "Fortunately, we caught it early, and there are treatment options available. These treatments can help extend your life and improve your quality of life, allowing you to feel better." |

## ⑧ Conclusion（收尾，有时间就做）

> 格式固定："Do you have any concerns? To summarise our conversation today, we have discussed [condition / diagnosis / treatment plan]. Thank you for seeing me today, I wish you a speedy recovery. Goodbye, please feel free to contact me if you have any questions."
