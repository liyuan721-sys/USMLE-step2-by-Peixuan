# OET 写作 — 高频陷阱速查（Benchmark 批改库提炼）

> 返回 [[00_OET总览]] · 模板见 [[OET_写作_转诊信模板]] · 术语见 [[OET_术语与沟通句库]]

> [!warning] 用法
> 每封信写完逐项扫雷。这是 20 封真实批改信反复出现的失分点——**改掉这些，C → B 最快**（虽然 ECFMG 写作只要 C+，但这些低级错会拖垮 Content/Language 维度）。

---

## 贴墙 Top 10 反射点

1. **单复数**：a married **woman**（非 women）；a 22-**year**-old；exercise / acne / advice **不可数无复数**。
2. **有日期的过去事件 → 一般过去时**：`he visited on 11/01/2018`（✗ has visited）。
3. **介词固定搭配**：presented **with** / complained **of** / suffering **from** / responded **to** / admitted **to** / allergic **to** / concerned **about**。
4. **落款**：具名收信人→Yours **sincerely**；不具名→Yours **faithfully**；别写"Your sincerely"。
5. **Re 行只放姓名+DOB**；年龄/诊断写正文；姓名拼写对齐 case notes。
6. **开头段不堆 social history，结尾不重复诊断**。
7. **既往史用日期排序，禁 firstly/secondly**。
8. **动词换同义词**：别一路 presented → came / attended / reported / complained of。
9. **删主观词**：Unfortunately / pleasant。
10. **控 180–200 词 + proofread**（拼写/大小写/缩写）。超 200 扣分，<180 也扣分。

---

## 时态（最高频）—— 错→对

- he was much **worsening** → had worsened / was much worse
- he **has been seen me** → I **had seen** him
- metformin（二甲双胍）**had changed** to → metformin **was changed** to（被动，非完成）
- he **refusing** feeds, **vomits** once → he **is refusing** feeds, **vomited** once
- 规律：**明确过去日期 → 一般过去时**；今日所见 → 现在时/现在完成。

---

## 介词 / 搭配 —— 错→对

| 错 | 对 |
|---|---|
| presented **tiredness** | presented **with** / complained **of** tiredness |
| suffering **of** distress | suffering **from** distress |
| responded well **for** treatment | responded well **to** treatment |
| admitted **in** hospital | admitted **to** hospital |
| allergic **to cats and hay fever** | allergic to cats; **and has** hay fever |
| diagnosed **since 1994** | diagnosed **in** 1994 / has had … since 1994 |
| family history **notable for RA related to his father** | family history **of** RA（rheumatoid arthritis，类风湿关节炎）**in** his father |
| concern**ing about** her baby | concerned **about** |
| decrease **of 13 kg of her weight** | a 13 kg **decrease in** weight / lost 13 kg |

---

## 冠词 —— 错→对

- referred to **physiotherapist** → referred to **a** physiotherapist
- presented with **acute exacerbation** → with **an** acute exacerbation
- Exophthalmos **had seen** in her eyes → **was seen**

---

## 拼写 / 词形（进默写本）

- women→**woman** ｜ extend→**extent** ｜ cessate→**cease/stop** ｜ Baring→**Bearing** in mind
- lethergy→**lethargy** ｜ althought→**although** ｜ futher→**further** ｜ preceeding→**preceding** ｜ propably→**probably** ｜ compilations→**complications**

---

## 措辞：太口语/啰嗦 → 更好替代

| 别用 | 改用 |
|---|---|
| 缩写 don't / couldn't / u | do not / could not / you（**禁缩写**） |
| firstly / secondly | 用具体日期 |
| Unfortunately, she is a smoker | （删主观词）She is a smoker |
| I would be **appropriated** if | I would be **grateful** if |
| In lieu of / In light of（乱用） | **In view of the above** |
| wooziness | dizziness |

---

## 信息筛选：批改者删了什么 / 漏了什么

- **删（不该写）**：开头段婚育细节；给精神科医生详写 tonsillitis/sore shoulder 等小病（压成 summary）；给急诊医生写过细的非急诊背景；与转诊目的无关的 hobbies。
- **漏（该写没写）**：关键阳性体征/检查结果；儿科信"satisfactory weight gain"；**误读 case notes**（动笔前先读懂病历）。

---

## 被认可的开头/结尾范式

- **转诊（refer 新病人）**：`I am writing to refer Mr X, a [age]-year-old [职业], who has features suggestive of [诊断]. Your further assessment and management would be highly appreciated.`
- **告知随访（update，已治疗后）**：`I am writing to update you regarding Mr X, who is now confirmed to be suffering from…`（区分 refer vs update！）
- **结尾**：`In view of the above, I am referring … for further assessment and management. Should there be any queries, please do not hesitate to contact me.`
