# OET 听力 Part B 实战 01 — 髋关节置换术后陪护安排 (Hip Replacement)

## 一、完整 Transcript

**N (practice nurse, 全科护士)**: *[开场推测] Welcome. I'm your practice nurse. You're here* about your <font color="#ff0000">hip replacement</font>, but I just wanted to check whether you had any questions for me at this stage.

**P (patient, 患者)**: My <font color="#ff0000">post-op arrangements</font> are pretty much <font color="#ff0000">sorted</font>. I <font color="#ff0000">live alone</font>, but I know I'm going to need someone with me when I get out of hospital. I've got five friends who've offered to help out and <font color="#ff0000">live in</font> with me. No one person will be in the <font color="#ff0000">flat</font> for a <font color="#ff0000">lengthy period</font>, so it shouldn't be too <font color="#ff0000">challenging</font> for any of us.

I've been told I'll need to be on <font color="#ff0000">crutches</font> for four weeks. I used them when I broke my leg a few years ago and I know <font color="#ff0000">what a pain</font> they can be, so <font color="#ff0000">getting about</font> with those is a worry. But I may have to have the other side done <font color="#ff0000">a few years down the line</font>, so I'd better get used to it.

*[结尾片段] I know what a pain it is.*

## 二、重点词标注（红字速查）

| 红字词 / 短语 | 中文 | 考点 / 陷阱 |
|---|---|---|
| hip replacement | 髋关节置换术 | 主题词 |
| post-op arrangements | 术后安排（post-operative） | 缩写 post-op = 术后 |
| sorted | （英式口语）安排妥当、搞定 = sorted out | 口语，易漏听 |
| live alone | 独居 | 陪护需求的起因 |
| live in (with me) | 住进来陪护（live-in carer 概念） | 短语动词 |
| flat | 公寓（英式；美式 = apartment） | 同义改写点 flat→apartment |
| lengthy period | 很长一段时间（lengthy = long 的正式说法） | 同义改写点 lengthy→long |
| challenging | 有难度、吃力 = difficult / demanding | 委婉说法 |
| crutches | 拐杖 | 拼写陷阱 c-r-u-t-ch-es |
| getting about | 走动、行动 = moving around | 短语动词 |
| a few years down the line | 几年后、将来 = in the future | hedging，易被当干扰 |
| what a pain | （口语）很麻烦、很烦人 | ⚠️ 双关：指拐杖"很烦"，**不是"疼痛"** |

## 三、可能的选择题考点方向（供盲听自测参考）

> [!question]- Part B 单选可能问什么？（先自己听一遍再展开）
> Part B 常问"患者的主要 concern / 态度 / 说话人想表达什么"。本段患者语气要点：
> - 陪护安排**已搞定**（five friends 轮流 live-in，无人久待）→ 不是问题
> - 真正的 **worry / concern** = **用拐杖行动不便**（getting about with crutches is *a worry*）
> - 对未来（对侧也要做）态度 = **接受、想提前适应**（"I'd better get used to it"）
>
> ⚠️ 干扰项常拿"独居 (live alone)"或"术后安排"当答案 —— 但患者明确说这些 *sorted / not challenging*，真正 concern 是**拐杖**。抓 *a worry* 这个信号词。
