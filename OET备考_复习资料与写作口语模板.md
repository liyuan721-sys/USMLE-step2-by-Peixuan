# OET 备考 —— 复习资料 + 写作/口语模板

> [!success] 核心策略（针对 Peixuan 画像）
> 协和临床博士 + 副主任医师 + 39 篇论文 + MGH/Cedars 多年英文环境 → **听/读大概率已达标**，真正战场是两个产出项：
> - **Speaking 口语**（最该练）：演医生对病人，要共情 + 去术语化（你病理路径、直接面诊少）
> - **Writing 写作**（练体裁）：考 **转诊信 referral letter**，固定格式，论文功底帮不上；但只要 **300 分**，达标不难
> **别背单词表** —— OET 没官方词表，奖励的是"把术语说成大白话"，不是认冷僻词。

## 一、考试结构 + 达标分速查

| 分项 | 内容 | 时长 | ECFMG 达标 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Listening 听 | 医患问诊录音 + 健康讲座 | ~45 min | **≥350（B）** |
| Reading 读 | 医疗文本快读 + 细读 | ~60 min | **≥350（B）** |
| Writing 写 | **1 封转诊信**（据病例笔记写） | 45 min（含 5 min 读题） | **≥300（C+）** ⭐ 最松 |
| Speaking 说 | **2 个 role-play**，你演医生 | ~20 min | **≥350（B）** |

- 必须**一次同场考出**四项达标，不能拼分。
- 形式：北京走 **OET@Home**（详见 [[ECFMG认证资格_问题记录]] §八 A，含设备自检红线）。

## 三、Writing 转诊信模板（referral letter）

> [!warning] 体裁铁律（和论文完全不同）
> 1. **不是写得多就好** —— 考的是**从病例笔记里筛"相关信息"**，无关的写进去反而扣分（Content / Purpose criteria）。
> 2. 字数 **180–200 词**，5 分钟读题先圈"收信人是谁 + 目的是什么"。
> 3. 评分六维：**Purpose（开头一句点明目的）/ Content（选对信息）/ Conformity to conventions（格式）/ Genre & style（正式信体）/ Organisation（分段逻辑）/ Language（语法准确）**。

### 标准骨架（fill-in-the-blank）

```
Dr [收信人姓名]
[职位 / 科室]
[地址]

[日期：写全 e.g. 26 June 2026]

Dear Dr [姓],

Re: [患者全名], [年龄]-year-old [male/female]

[开头句 —— 一句话点明目的]
I am writing to refer Mr/Ms [姓], who [一句话主诉 / 为什么转],
for your further [assessment / management / investigation].

[第 1 段 —— 当前最相关主诉 + 关键阳性发现]
[第 2 段 —— 相关既往史 / 用药 / 检查结果（只选与转诊目的相关的）]
[第 3 段 —— 已做的处理 / 当前状态]

[结尾请求句 —— 你要对方做什么]
I would appreciate your [assessment and ongoing management].
Please do not hesitate to contact me if you require further information.

Yours sincerely,
[Dr 你的名字 / 职位]
```

### 高频功能句（直接套用）

| 功能 | 句式 |
|---|---|
| 开头点目的 | "I am writing to refer… / I am writing to request your assessment of…" |
| 引出病史 | "With regard to his medical history, … / Of note, …" |
| 检查结果 | "Investigations revealed… / On examination, …" |
| 已做处理 | "He was commenced on… / She was managed with…" |
| 转诊请求 | "I would be grateful if you could… / I would appreciate your review of…" |
| 礼貌收尾 | "Please do not hesitate to contact me should you require further details." |

### 自查清单（写完对照）

- [ ] 开头第一句就点明 **目的 + 患者**？
- [ ] 收信人对了吗（GP？专科？急诊？格式随之变）？
- [ ] 病例笔记里**无关信息删干净**了吗？
- [ ] 时间线清楚、分段有逻辑（不是流水账）？
- [ ] 180–200 词？正式信体（无缩写如 don't → do not）？
- [ ] 结尾有**请求句 + 礼貌句 + Yours sincerely**？

> [!danger] 最常见丢分点
> ① 把笔记里所有信息照搬（没筛选）② 字数超标堆细节 ③ 用论文式被动堆砌、不像"给同行写信" ④ 没点明转诊目的 ⑤ 缩写/口语化（it's, can't）。

## 四、Speaking role-play 模板（你的最大战场）

> [!info] 怎么考
> 2 个 role-play，每个 ~5 分钟。你拿到 role card（演**医生**），考官演**病人/家属**。评分两组：
> - **语言类**：Intelligibility 清晰度 / Fluency 流利 / Appropriateness 用词得体 / Grammar & expression
> - **临床沟通类** ⭐（你要刻意练）：Relationship-building 建立关系 / Patient's perspective 理解病人视角 / Providing structure 给结构 / Information-gathering 问诊 / Information-giving 解释

### 万能六步框架（每个 role-play 都能套）

```
1. 开场建立关系   Rapport
2. 开放式问诊     Elicit（先听病人说）
3. 共情确认       Empathy（接住情绪）
4. 大白话分块解释 Explain（chunk + check 去术语化）
5. 协商 / 处理抗拒 Negotiate
6. 总结 + 安全网 + 收尾  Summary + safety-net
```

### 每步功能句库（背这些，比背单词管用 10 倍）

**① 开场 Rapport**
- "Hello, I'm Dr [名]. Thanks for coming in today. How are you feeling?"
- "Before we start, is there anything in particular you'd like to talk about?"

**② 开放式问诊 Elicit**
- "Can you tell me a bit more about what's been happening?"
- "When did you first notice this?"
- "How has it been affecting your daily life?"

**③ 共情 Empathy（接住情绪，OET 高度奖励）**
- "I can see this has been really worrying for you."
- "That sounds very difficult — thank you for sharing that with me."
- "It's completely understandable to feel anxious about this."

**④ 大白话解释 Explain（chunk + check）**
- 去术语化："What this means in simple terms is…"
- 分块："Let me explain this in two parts. First… / The second thing is…"
- 检查理解："Just to make sure I've explained it clearly — could you tell me what you've understood so far?"
- 给选择："There are a couple of options we could consider. Would it help if I went through them?"

**⑤ 协商 / 处理抗拒 Negotiate**
- "I understand you're hesitant. May I ask what's worrying you most?"
- "How would you feel about trying… for now, and reviewing it in two weeks?"
- "What matters most to you in deciding this?"

**⑥ 总结 + 安全网 + 收尾**
- "So, to summarise what we've agreed…"
- "If [红旗症状] happens, please come back straight away or call us."
- "Do you have any questions before you go?"

> [!tip] Peixuan 专属提醒
> 你最容易因"病理科背景、直接面诊少"而**跳过共情、直接讲机制**。考官就盯这个。**每个 role-play 强制做满第 3 步（共情）+ 第 4 步的 check 理解**，比你专业知识更影响分数。

## 五、术语 → 患者大白话对照表（你唯一要背的"词表"）

> [!info] OET 奖励"去术语化"，不是术语量。Speaking/Writing 对病人时一律换成右列。

| 医学术语 | 患者大白话 |
|---|---|
| Myocardial infarction | a heart attack |
| Hypertension | high blood pressure |
| Hypotension | low blood pressure |
| Dyspnea | shortness of breath / trouble breathing |
| Edema | swelling (fluid build-up) |
| Hyperlipidemia | high cholesterol / high fats in the blood |
| Benign | not cancer / harmless |
| Malignant | cancerous |
| Biopsy | take a small tissue sample to look at under a microscope |
| Prognosis | the likely course / what we expect to happen |
| Chronic | long-term / ongoing |
| Acute | sudden / short-term |
| Inflammation | redness, swelling and irritation |
| Anemia | low level of red blood cells / "low blood" |
| Arrhythmia | an irregular heartbeat |
| Fracture | a broken bone |
| Hemorrhage | heavy bleeding |
| Sepsis | a serious infection spreading through the body |
| Antibiotics | medicine that fights bacterial infection |
| Anticoagulant | blood-thinning medicine |
| Lesion | an abnormal area / a spot |
| Metastasis | the cancer has spread to other parts |
| Remission | the disease is under control / signs have gone |
| Diabetes (poorly controlled) | your blood sugar has been running high |

## 六、医患沟通功能句库（按场景速取）

| 场景 | 句式 |
|---|---|
| 坏消息铺垫 | "I'm afraid I have some news that may be difficult to hear. Is now a good time?" |
| 给空间 | "Take your time. There's no rush." |
| 鼓励提问 | "Please stop me anytime if something isn't clear." |
| 确认顾虑 | "What's worrying you the most about this?" |
| 表达共情 | "I can only imagine how stressful this must be." |
| 解释下一步 | "Here's what I suggest we do next…" |
| 安全网 | "If things get worse — for example [红旗] — please come back immediately." |
| 收尾确认 | "Before you leave, can I just check — is there anything else you'd like to ask?" |

## 七、现实备考节奏（别喧宾夺主 Step 2）

> [!warning] 你还在 Step 2 冲刺 → OET 走"短平快"

```
第 0 步  做 1 套官方全真摸底（约 3 小时）
          → 确认听读是否已达标，定位 Writing/Speaking 短板
第 1 阶段（2-3 周，每天挤 30-60 min，穿插 Step 2 中）
   ├─ Writing：练 5-8 封转诊信，至少 3 封要"带反馈"批改
   ├─ Speaking：每天 1-2 个 role-play 出声练，强制做满共情 + check 理解
   ├─ Listening/Reading：各做 2-3 套熟题型即可
   └─ 每天 10 min 背 §五 对照表 + §六 功能句
第 2 步  7 月 OET@Home 考（先跑设备自检！）
          → 考完回归 Step 2 主线
```

> [!success] 一句话
> 以你的英文底子，OET 是"学格式 + 练两个产出项"的短期任务，不是长期工程。**Writing 学转诊信体裁、Speaking 练共情和去术语化** —— 这两件做好，达标稳。
