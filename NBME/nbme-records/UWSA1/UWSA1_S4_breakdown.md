> [!example]- UWSA1 Q121 psych / Alcohol withdrawal initial treatment
> ^Q121
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：住院后数小时内出现 tremor、insomnia、restlessness、tachycardia、hypertension，哪怕 urine tox 对 opiates 阳性，**先按 alcohol withdrawal 处理，立即给 benzodiazepines**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Symptoms begin within 12 hours of admission | Classic alcohol withdrawal timing | 戒酒后 6-24 小时最常见先出现 autonomic hyperactivity |
> | Tremulous hands, insomnia, restlessness | Core early withdrawal symptoms | 这些比单纯焦虑更像戒断综合征 |
> | BP 162/94 mm Hg, HR 120/min | Sympathetic overdrive | 交感兴奋是需要立刻压下去的危险信号 |
> | AST 320 > ALT 152 | Supports heavy alcohol use | `AST > ALT` 是题目在暗示长期 alcohol exposure |
> | Opiate-positive tox screen | Potential distractor | 题目故意让你想到 opioid withdrawal，但真正致命的是 alcohol withdrawal |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Benzodiazepines**
>
> **Core Concept**：Alcohol withdrawal 的初始治疗是 **benzodiazepines**，目的是预防 progression to seizures and delirium tremens。题目里虽然有 enlarged pupils 和 GI symptoms 这些可能和 opioid withdrawal 重叠，但 alcohol withdrawal 的 **hypertension + tachycardia + tremor +时间轴** 更关键，而且危险性更高。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：长期饮酒会增强 GABA、抑制 NMDA。突然停酒后，抑制刹车消失，兴奋通路反跳，出现 autonomic hyperactivity。benzodiazepines 是用来补回 GABAergic brake。
>
> ```text
> chronic alcohol use
> -> adaptive downregulation of inhibitory tone
> -> abrupt cessation
> -> relative CNS hyperexcitability
> -> tremor, insomnia, tachycardia, hypertension
> -> benzodiazepines prevent seizures/DTs
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Situation | Best Drug | 为什么 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Acute alcohol withdrawal | Benzodiazepines | 降低 seizure 和 delirium tremens 风险 |
> | Alcohol abstinence maintenance | Acamprosate or naltrexone | 用于长期 relapse prevention，不是急性戒断 |
> | Acute opioid overdose | Naloxone | 逆转呼吸抑制，不治 alcohol withdrawal |
> | Opioid withdrawal | Buprenorphine or methadone | 可缓解戒断，但通常不致命 |
> | Hepatic encephalopathy | Lactulose | 针对 ammonia，不是本题机制 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"住院后发抖又飙压，先把酒精戒断压下来。"**
> - Alcohol withdrawal 会杀人，opioid withdrawal 一般不会。
> - `AST > ALT` 是酒精线索，不是摆设。
> - tremor + HTN + tachycardia 比瞳孔散大更有判别力。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Acamprosate**：用于 alcohol use disorder 的维持治疗，不处理急性 withdrawal。
>
> **C - Buprenorphine**：如果真是 opioid withdrawal 可以用，但本题最需要优先拦截的是 potentially fatal alcohol withdrawal。
>
> **D - Lactulose**：hepatic encephalopathy 常见 confusion、asterixis、肝硬化背景；本题 albumin/INR 正常，不支持。
>
> **E - Naloxone**：针对 opioid intoxication，不针对 alcohol withdrawal；还可能诱发更明显 opioid withdrawal。
>
> **F - Naltrexone**：用于维持治疗，也会加重 opioid withdrawal。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Buprenorphine。因为题干故意给了 positive opiate screen 和 enlarged pupils，但真正应该先救的是 alcohol withdrawal。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q122 hematology oncology / CLL with smudge cells
> ^Q122
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：elderly patient + marked lymphocytosis + lymphadenopathy + splenomegaly，外周血涂片最该想到 **smudge cells = CLL**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 68-year-old man | CLL is a disease of older adults | 年龄本身就在给 CLL 加分 |
> | WBC 38,800/mm3 with 92% lymphocytes | Dramatic mature lymphocytosis | 这不是普通感染性白细胞升高 |
> | Cervical and axillary nontender lymph nodes | Chronic lymphoproliferative process | 多站无痛淋巴结肿大很像 CLL |
> | Palpable spleen tip | Splenomegaly from leukemic infiltration | 脾大进一步支持血液系统肿瘤 |
> | Anemia and thrombocytopenia | Marrow involvement or hypersplenism | 说明不是孤立性 lymphocytosis |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Smudge cells**
>
> **Core Concept**：CLL 是 elderly 最常见 leukemia 之一，表现为 **mature-appearing lymphocytosis**、lymphadenopathy、splenomegaly，以及不同程度 cytopenias。smudge cells 是脆弱淋巴细胞在制片过程中被压碎后的典型表现，题目问 smear 看到什么时，几乎就是在点名 CLL。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：CLL 本质是 monoclonal mature B-cell proliferation。细胞数量多，但免疫功能差，所以病人反而容易感染，常见 hypogammaglobulinemia。
>
> ```text
> clonal mature B-cell expansion
> -> marked lymphocytosis
> -> lymph node/spleen infiltration
> -> anemia and thrombocytopenia
> -> fragile lymphocytes on smear
> -> smudge cells
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Smear finding | Most likely disease | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Smudge cells | CLL | 本题完全匹配 |
> | Lymphoblasts | ALL | 急性白血病，看到的是 blasts，不是成熟 lymphocytes |
> | Auer rods | AML | 粒系恶性肿瘤，不会是 92% lymphocytes |
> | Hypersegmented neutrophils | B12/folate deficiency | 会有 macrocytosis，不解释显著 lymphadenopathy |
> | Rouleaux formation | Multiple myeloma | 常见 bone pain、蛋白异常，不是 dramatic lymphocytosis |
> | Teardrop cells | Myelofibrosis | 更偏 marrow fibrosis 图景 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Old patient, loaded with lymphocytes, smear gets smudged."**
> - 老年 + 无痛淋巴结肿大，先想 CLL。
> - CLL 看起来 lymphocyte 很多，但 immune function 并不好。
> - `smudge cells` 是 Step 题眼，不要和 blasts 搞混。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Hypersegmented neutrophils**：对应 megaloblastic anemia，应该是 macrocytosis，不是本题的巨大 lymphocytosis。
>
> **B - Lymphoblasts**：如果是 ALL，外周血更像 acute leukemia，不会强调 mature lymphocytes。
>
> **C - Neutrophils with Auer rods**：Auer rods 属于 AML 线索，细胞系完全不对。
>
> **D - Rouleaux formation**：multiple myeloma 常见骨痛和蛋白异常，白细胞通常不会高成这样。
>
> **F - Teardrop-shaped red blood cells**：提示 myelofibrosis 或严重 marrow distortion，不是 CLL 经典图。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Lymphoblasts。因为很多人看到“白血病”就条件反射选 blasts，但 CLL 的关键词是 **mature** lymphocytes。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q123 感染 / Catheter-related bloodstream infection
> ^Q123
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：post-op day 3 病人出现 confusion、hypotension、tachycardia、leukocytosis，肺尿腹都不像来源时，先按 **catheter-related bloodstream infection** 走：抽血培养并立刻上 antibiotics。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Postoperative day 3 | Timing fits nosocomial line infection | 导管相关菌血症常在放置后 48 小时以上出现 |
> | Confusion + BP 90/50 + HR 110 | Sepsis physiology | 即使没发热，也已经是脓毒症图景 |
> | WBC 21,000/mm3 | Strong infection signal | 明显白细胞升高支持感染而非单纯并发症 |
> | Clear chest x-ray, no bacteriuria | No clear pulmonary or urinary source | 在排除常见来源后要回头看 central line |
> | Line site appears normal | Does not exclude CRBSI | 导管口没有红脓也不能排除菌血症 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Blood cultures and antibiotics**
>
> **Core Concept**：疑似 catheter-related bloodstream infection 时，标准下一步是 **2 sets of blood cultures**，一组来自 catheter，一组来自 peripheral site，然后尽快给 empiric broad-spectrum antibiotics。这里重点不是先把所有罕见诊断排一遍，而是先处理最像、也最危险的 sepsis source。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：nontunneled central line 常因 skin flora 沿导管外壁或接口进入血流，引发 abrupt sepsis。导管口外观可正常，所以不能靠“没有红”来放心。
>
> ```text
> central venous catheter >48 h
> -> skin flora access bloodstream
> -> bacteremia/fungemia
> -> hypotension + tachycardia + altered mental status
> -> obtain cultures
> -> start empiric antibiotics
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Post-op problem | 常见线索 | 本题为何不选 |
> |---|---|---|
> | CRBSI | Sepsis without another source, line in place | 本题最符合 |
> | Hemolytic transfusion reaction | Within minutes to 24 h, fever, flank pain, hemoglobinuria | 输血是 POD1，时间晚了 |
> | Massive PE | Hypoxemia, pleuritic symptoms, maybe RV strain | 本题氧饱和度正常，白细胞高更像感染 |
> | Intracranial event | Focal deficits or isolated confusion | 不能解释 sepsis physiology |
> | Intraabdominal catastrophe | Peritoneal signs, worsening abdominal exam, purulent/bloody drainage | 腹部软，伤口清亮，不支持 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Post-op sepsis with nowhere obvious to point? Point at the line."**
> - 术后病人最容易让人到处找 source。
> - 当 lung、urine、abdomen 都不典型时，central line 很常见。
> - 不要被“afebrile”或“site normal”误导。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Coombs test**：acute hemolytic transfusion reaction 通常更早出现，而且常有 fever、flank pain、hemoglobinuria。
>
> **C - Corticosteroids**：adrenal crisis 会低压和意识差，但不能比 sepsis 更能解释 leukocytosis。
>
> **D - CT pulmonary angiogram**：PE 可致低压，但通常会有更明显 hypoxemia；本题整体更像 infection。
>
> **E - CT scan of the head**：confusion 不是孤立问题，这里更需要先处理 systemic sepsis。
>
> **F - Laparotomy**：腹腔灾难通常会有腹膜刺激征、异常引流或更差腹部检查，本题都没有。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - CT pulmonary angiogram。因为术后确实有 PE 风险，但这里 leukocytosis 加上没有呼吸影像支持，让 CRBSI 更靠前。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q124 pulmonary / Pregnancy PE workup after low-probability VQ
> ^Q124
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：pregnant patient 高度怀疑 PE 时，**只有 normal V/Q** 才能排除；如果只是 **low-probability V/Q**，下一步还要做 **CT angiogram**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 28 weeks pregnant | Pregnancy strongly increases VTE risk | 妊娠本身就把 PE 先验概率拉高 |
> | Sudden chest pain + dyspnea + diaphoresis | High clinical suspicion for PE | 典型急性肺栓塞表现 |
> | HR 120/min, RR 26/min | Physiologic stress from acute PE | tachycardia/tachypnea 是关键支持点 |
> | Normal chest x-ray | Makes V/Q a reasonable initial test | 妊娠且胸片正常时，常先做 V/Q |
> | Low-probability V/Q scan | Not enough to rule out PE when suspicion is high | 题目真正考点就在这里 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - CT angiogram of the chest**
>
> **Core Concept**：Pregnancy with suspected PE 时，initial test 常因正常 chest x-ray 而选择 V/Q scan；但解读 V/Q 结果时必须结合 **pretest probability**。本题临床概率高，所以 `low-probability V/Q` 仍然不够，必须继续做 **CTA** 才能进一步确认或排除。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：V/Q scan 不是单独读结果，而是和临床概率一起配对。高临床概率患者里，只有 `normal V/Q` 才足以把 PE 拉到足够低。
>
> ```text
> suspected PE in pregnancy
> -> estimate pretest probability
> -> normal CXR allows initial V/Q scan
> -> if V/Q is normal: PE ruled out
> -> if V/Q is low/moderate and suspicion remains high: CTA needed
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Scenario | Next step |
> |---|---|
> | Pregnant, normal CXR, suspected PE | V/Q scan is reasonable initial imaging |
> | High pretest probability + normal V/Q | PE excluded |
> | High pretest probability + low-probability V/Q | CTA |
> | Hemodynamically stable but diagnosis uncertain | Confirm diagnosis before anticoagulation |
> | Pregnancy with confirmed PE | Heparin-based anticoagulation, not warfarin |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Low-probability is not no-probability."**
> - V/Q 的 `low probability` 不等于 rule-out。
> - 妊娠患者也一样要看 pretest probability。
> - 正常 chest x-ray 决定先做什么，不决定做到哪一步就能停。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Discharge home with close follow-up**：PE 还没被排除，不能让她回家观察。
>
> **C - Heparin infusion**：如果 hemodynamically stable，最佳下一步仍是先完成确诊路径，而不是直接经验性治疗。
>
> **D - Inferior vena cava filter placement**：只在 confirmed VTE 且不能抗凝时才考虑。
>
> **E - Pulmonary function tests**：评估慢性 obstructive/restrictive disease，不处理 acute PE workup。
>
> **F - Warfarin therapy**：warfarin 在 pregnancy 中禁用。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Heparin infusion。因为大家会担心拖延治疗，但题目特意给的是 hemodynamically stable patient，且下一步考的是诊断算法。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q125 hematology oncology / Hereditary spherocytosis complication
> ^Q125
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：palpable spleen tip + spherocytes on smear = **hereditary spherocytosis**，最该担心的长期并发症是 **pigment gallstones**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Young woman with palpable spleen tip | Suggests chronic extravascular hemolysis | 脾大提示红细胞长期在 spleen 被清除 |
> | Smear shows spherocytes | Signature of hereditary spherocytosis | 小而深染、无 central pallor 是题眼 |
> | No edema, normal exam otherwise | Not a renal or systemic failure picture | 把答案聚焦到 hemolysis complication |
> | Asked for greatest risk condition | Tests downstream complication | 不是问诊断，而是问后果 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Gallstones**
>
> **Core Concept**：Hereditary spherocytosis 由 spectrin、ankyrin 等 RBC membrane proteins 缺陷导致，红细胞变成不易变形的 spherocytes，在脾脏被反复清除，形成 chronic extravascular hemolysis。持续 hemolysis 会增加 bilirubin load，因此最经典并发症就是 **pigment gallstones**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题真正要记的不是“球形红细胞长什么样”，而是 **hemolysis 的代谢后果**。红细胞周转越快，unconjugated bilirubin 越多，胆汁中 calcium bilirubinate 越容易沉积。
>
> ```text
> membrane protein defect
> -> spherocytes
> -> splenic trapping and destruction
> -> chronic extravascular hemolysis
> -> excess bilirubin production
> -> pigment gallstones
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Smear clue | Disease | Classic complication |
> |---|---|---|
> | Spherocytes | Hereditary spherocytosis | Pigment gallstones |
> | Schistocytes | Microangiopathic hemolysis | Thrombosis/organ injury depends on cause |
> | Hypersegmented neutrophils | B12 deficiency | Neurologic deficits, gastritis context |
> | Burr cells | Uremia/renal disease | Renal insufficiency cause, not consequence |
> | Blasts | Acute leukemia | Marrow failure/infection/bleeding |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Sphere in spleen, bilirubin in bile."**
> - spherocytes 会被 spleen 扣住。
> - chronic hemolysis 会把 bilirubin 源源不断送进胆汁。
> - 所以 HS 常考 gallstones，不是 clotting。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Acute leukemia**：应该看到 blasts，而不是成熟红细胞形态异常。
>
> **B - Atrophic gastritis**：对应 B12 deficiency 图景，smear 应该更偏 macrocytosis 和 hypersegmented neutrophils。
>
> **D - Osteoporotic fractures**：不是 HS 的经典长期风险；题干也没有 sickle cell 或骨代谢疾病线索。
>
> **E - Renal insufficiency**：尿毒症相关 smear 更像 burr cells，不是 spherocytes。
>
> **F - Venous thromboembolism**：更像 microangiopathic hemolysis 或强高凝状态题，不符合本题 smear。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Venous thromboembolism。因为很多人一看到 hemolysis 就泛化成“会不会血栓”，但 HS 的高频并发症是 bilirubin stones。

> ^Q126
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：题干不是单纯问“谁做错了”，而是问 **latent error**。看到 last-minute room change + new personnel + brief handoff disruption，答案要落到 **system-level scheduling and workflow vulnerability**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Wrong procedure performed on the correct side | Confirms a serious patient safety event | 不是 side 错，而是 procedure 错，属于 wrong-surgery event |
> | Last case of the day in a busy hospital | Production pressure raises error risk | 忙碌环境和末台手术容易增加疲劳、赶进度和注意力分散 |
> | Last-minute switch to a different operating room and personnel | Classic latent system factor | 临时换房间、换团队是系统流程缺陷，不是单个人瞬间判断失误 |
> | Brief time-out with a new team | Weakens procedural verification | time-out 质量下降，团队对病人和计划手术不熟 |
> | Asking about latent error intervention | Distinguishes system fix from individual blame | 题眼在区分 active error vs latent error |

>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Optimize scheduling to increase procedure spacing and avoid last-minute personnel changes**
>
> **Core Concept**：本题的 active error 是 surgeon 做了 **wrong procedure**；但让这个错误更容易发生的，是 **scheduling pressure、临时换 OR、换 support team、care continuity 被打断** 这些 latent errors。真正有效的 patient safety 改进，要优先修补系统漏洞，而不是只盯着某个个人。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：patient safety 题最常考的就是把错误拆成 `sharp end` 和 `blunt end`。前者是台前的人做错了，后者是系统先把事故轨道铺好了。
>
> ```text
> production pressure / poor scheduling
> -> last-minute room and personnel change
> -> disrupted continuity and weaker time-out
> -> lower team situational awareness
> -> surgeon performs wrong procedure
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Error type | Typical level | Example in this case | Better fix |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Active error | Frontline action | Surgeon released carpal tunnel instead of trigger finger | Immediate containment and disclosure |
> | Latent error | System/process/policy | Last-minute OR and staffing change | Scheduling redesign, continuity protection |
> | Blame-only response | Individual punishment | Discipline surgeon without system review | Usually weak safety improvement |
> | High-quality prevention | Team and system redesign | Stable team + robust time-out | Reduces repeat events |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"If the case setup changed, think system before surgeon."**
> - 临时换房间、换人、延迟开台，都是 latent clues。
> - patient safety 题问 prevention，优先找系统改造。
> - individual retraining 可以做，但通常不是最佳根因答案。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Have patients verify the procedure rather than operating room teams**：patient 可参与 time-out，但不能取代 OR team verification。
>
> **B - Implement disciplinary measures**：这是把问题缩成个人责任，容易形成 blame culture，没处理 latent contributors。
>
> **D - Require outsiders to lead time-out**：time-out 反而需要熟悉 patient 和 procedure 的团队成员来做，不是越陌生越客观。
>
> **E - Require this surgeon to mark exact incision site and correct side in all future cases**：只针对这位 surgeon 的未来 active error 风险，不解决这次暴露出的 scheduling/system flaw。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Require this particular surgeon to mark both exact site of incision and correct side in all future cases。它看起来很“安全”，但本质还是在修个人，不是在修系统。

> -> fewer urgency episodes

>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：older male smoker with atherosclerosis + sudden intense back pain not worsened by movement + hypotension = 先想 **ruptured AAA**，所以背痛来源是 **referred pain**，不是 mechanical back disease。

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Age 68, male, smoker | Strong AAA risk profile | 年龄、性别、吸烟是 AAA 高频危险因素 |
> | CAD history and carotid bruit | Shows systemic atherosclerosis | 进一步支持血管病背景 |

> **正确答案：G - Referred pain**
>

>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：当腹主动脉瘤破裂时，retroperitoneal bleeding 和 aortic wall pain 可向 back/flank 放射，所以病人会先说“腰背痛”，但本质是 vascular emergency。
>
> ```text
> abdominal aortic aneurysm
> -> aneurysm rupture
> -> retroperitoneal bleeding
> -> sudden severe aortic pain
> -> referred back or flank pain
> -> hypotension and tachycardia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Cause of back pain | Typical clue | Why not this case |
> |---|---|---|
> | Muscle sprain / OA | Worse with movement, relieved by rest | 本题 pain not movement-related |
> | Disk disease | Radicular symptoms, reflex or sensory changes | 本题神经查体正常 |
> | Compression fracture | Positional pain, often tender vertebrae | 本题无局部压痛且出现 shock |
> | Epidural abscess | Fever, tenderness, neurologic deficits | 本题没有感染或神经红旗 |
> | Ruptured AAA | Sudden severe pain + hypotension in vascular-risk patient | 本题完全吻合 |
>
> ---
>

> **"Back pain plus shock is aorta until proven otherwise."**
> - 突然、很重、和动作无关。
> - 老年吸烟男性、有动脉粥样硬化背景。
> - 评估中血压掉下来，题目已经在喊 vascular catastrophe。
>
> ---
>

> **A - Epidural abscess**：通常有 fever、局部压痛、神经缺损，和本题不符。
>
> **B - Lumbar disk disease**：要有 radicular pattern 或 reflex change，本题没有。
>
> **C - Multiple myeloma**：骨痛多与 movement/position 相关，也不解释急性 hypotension。
>
> **D - Muscle sprain**：机械痛最怕动作，本题恰恰不受 movement 影响。
>
> **E - Osteoarthritis**：慢性退变性疼痛，不会突然引起 hemodynamic collapse。
>
> **F - Osteoporosis**：若是 compression fracture，常有 positional pain 和 vertebral tenderness。
>
> **H - Spondyloarthritis**：是慢性 inflammatory back pain，不是急性休克性表现。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Lumbar disk disease。因为患者先诉 back pain，但题目随后给出的 hypotension/tachycardia 其实是在把你从 spine 题切到 AAA。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q130 骨科 / OA with effusion needs arthrocentesis
> ^Q130
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：虽然 x-ray 已经证明 osteoarthritis，但 **NSAID 后反而更痛 + warmth + effusion** 时，别急着打 steroid，先做 **arthrocentesis** 查是不是合并 CPP crystal disease 等其他病因。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Knee x-ray shows joint space narrowing and osteophytes | Confirms underlying OA | 题目先给你一个真 diagnosis，但不是最终答案 |
> | Pain worsened despite naproxen | Uncomplicated OA treatment is not enough | 治疗失败说明要想并存病因 |
> | Mild to moderate effusion and warmth | Red flag against simple OA alone | OA 可有轻度 effusion，但 warmth/明显 effusion 要提高警觉 |
> | No trauma, no erythema, creatinine normal | Narrows differential and keeps aspiration safe/relevant | 没有外伤或明显感染外观，但仍需 synovial fluid 分析 |
> | Best next step in management | Diagnostic clarification comes before empiric escalation | 下一步先抽液，不是直接升级药物或手术 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Arthrocentesis**
>
> **Core Concept**：OA 病人如果 conservative therapy 失败，而且关节出现 **warmth、effusion、明显炎症感**，要考虑 **CPP arthropathy** 等共存病因。此时最佳下一步是 **arthrocentesis with synovial fluid analysis**。本页 text layer 的 `Correct answer` 行被截断，但 rendered page 已核对为 **A**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：CPP crystal deposition 很爱叠加在老年人的 OA knee 上。影像即便没明显 chondrocalcinosis，也不能排除，真正能把诊断落地的是关节液。
>
> ```text
> known knee OA
> -> persistent pain despite NSAID
> + warmth/effusion
> -> suspect superimposed CPP crystal disease
> -> arthrocentesis with synovial fluid analysis
> -> targeted treatment after confirmation
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Scenario | Best next step | Why |
> |---|---|---|
> | Typical OA, mild symptoms | Exercise, weight loss, NSAIDs | Conservative first |
> | OA with persistent pain plus warmth/effusion | Arthrocentesis | Rule in/out CPP or other inflammatory cause |
> | Confirmed inflammatory crystal flare in one joint | Aspiration +/- intraarticular steroid | Treatment after diagnostic clarification |
> | Severe OA after prolonged failed nonoperative therapy | Knee arthroplasty | Reserved later |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Warm OA is not just OA."**
> - x-ray 真的可以同时是真的，又不够解释全部表现。
> - effusion/warmth 出现时，先抽液再升级治疗。
> - crystal disease 特别喜欢搭在老年 OA knee 上一起考。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Continuous indomethacin**：继续经验性按 gout 治没有先把病因分清，不是最佳下一步。
>
> **C - Intraarticular corticosteroid injection**：对 OA 或 CPP 都可能有帮助，但本题先要 synovial fluid analysis，不能直接跳治疗。
>
> **D - Knee replacement**：手术要留到更长期、更完整的 nonoperative regimen 失败之后。
>
> **E - Oral colchicine**：也是偏向经验性 crystal treatment，但 diagnosis 还没确认。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Intraarticular corticosteroid injection。因为 effusion/warmth 让人很想直接消炎，但 USMLE 更强调先通过 arthrocentesis 完成 diagnostic clarification。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q131 OB / Placenta previa next step
> ^Q131
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：32 周孕妇出现 **painless bright red vaginal bleeding**，生命体征稳定、没有持续腹痛，先按 **placenta previa until proven otherwise** 处理，下一步是 **transabdominal ultrasound**，绝不能先做 digital cervical exam。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 32 weeks gestation | Antepartum bleeding after 20 weeks | 孕中晚期出血先想 previa 或 abruption |
> | Bright red painless vaginal bleeding | Classic placenta previa pattern | 鲜红、无痛比 abruption 更像 previa |
> | Irregular contractions but no constant abdominal pain | Less consistent with placental abruption | 有宫缩不等于 abruption，题眼是没有持续腹痛 |
> | Stable vitals and no active cervical bleeding | No immediate crash C-section trigger | 目前母体稳定，可先影像定位胎盘 |
> | Unknown placental location | Determines the safe next step | 胎盘位置没确认前，不能把手伸进宫颈口 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Transabdominal ultrasound**
>
> **Core Concept**：Placenta previa 是胎盘覆盖 cervix，典型表现为 **painless vaginal bleeding after 20 weeks gestation**。只要孕周够大又出血、胎盘位置未知，最佳下一步就是先做 **transabdominal ultrasound**；如果怀疑 previa，再进一步做 **transvaginal ultrasound** 精准确认。题干提到 fetal heart tracing exhibit，但当前可见 source 没显示图本体；解释文字明确 tracing 是 reactive，不影响答案判断。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：随着 cervix shortening 和 lower uterine segment remodeling，覆盖 cervix 的胎盘边缘会部分剥离，导致以母体血为主的无痛性出血。
>
> ```text
> placenta overlies cervix
> -> cervical/lower uterine segment change
> -> partial placental separation
> -> painless maternal bleeding
> -> first confirm placental location by ultrasound
> -> avoid digital cervical exam before exclusion
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：antepartum bleeding
> | Diagnosis | Key Clue | Best next step / 区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Placenta previa | Painless bright red bleeding | Ultrasound first; no digital exam |
> | Placental abruption | Painful bleeding, uterine tenderness, fetal distress | Maternal stabilization; consider delivery if unstable |
> | Preterm labor | Regular contractions with cervical change | fFN may help risk-stratify, but not when bleeding etiology is the main issue |
> | Vasa previa | Bleeding after membrane rupture with fetal distress | Emergency delivery; fetal blood loss pattern |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Painless previa: probe first, fingers never first."**
> - 无痛鲜红血，先把 placenta previa 放到前面。
> - unknown placental location 时，ultrasound 比 pelvic exam 更重要。
> - digital exam 最大风险不是不舒服，而是 **massive hemorrhage**。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Digital cervical examination**：最大禁忌项。若胎盘覆盖 cervix，手指进入 cervical canal 可直接诱发大出血。
>
> **B - Emergency cesarean delivery**：只有 active heavy bleeding、maternal instability 或 persistent fetal compromise 才要立刻剖宫产；本题 tracing 解释为 reactive，母体也稳定。
>
> **C - Fetal fibronectin testing**：是 preterm delivery risk tool，不解决“为什么出血”，而且出血本身会造成 false positive。
>
> **D - Kleihauer-Betke test**：更贴近 abruption 或 Rh-negative 母体的 fetomaternal hemorrhage 评估；本题是 AB positive，且临床不像 abruption。
>
> **E - Tranexamic acid administration**：主要用于 postpartum hemorrhage，不是这道 antepartum bleeding 的首选下一步。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Emergency cesarean delivery。看到 32 周出血容易紧张，但 USMLE 先问的是稳定患者的 **next step**，先定位 placenta。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q132 外科 / Blunt thoracic aortic injury workup
> ^Q132
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**high-speed deceleration + seat belt chest sign + widened mediastinum**，而且病人血流动力学稳定，下一步不是观察，也不是直接开胸，而是 **CT angiography of the chest**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | High-speed motor vehicle collision | Classic rapid deceleration mechanism | BTAI 最重要的是受伤机制 |
> | Seat belt imprint across the chest | External evidence of blunt chest trauma | 说明胸部真的吃到冲击，不只是胸壁痛 |
> | Widened mediastinum on chest x-ray | Screening clue for thoracic aortic injury | 题目直接把你往 aortic injury 推 |
> | BP 140/84, no obvious hemorrhage | Hemodynamically stable | 稳定患者先做 confirmatory imaging |
> | No pneumothorax, no effusion, normal heart sounds | Narrows away from simpler explanations | 不是单纯 rib fracture 或 tension physiology |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - CT angiography of the chest**
>
> **Core Concept**：Rapid deceleration 可造成 **blunt thoracic aortic injury (BTAI)**，最常见于 **aortic isthmus**。若病人 **hemodynamically stable**，首选 **CT angiography** 确诊并规划 endovascular vs open repair。若不稳定，才是直接 thoracotomy +/- intraoperative TEE。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：移动度较大的 aortic arch 与固定的 descending aorta 在急减速时受到 shearing/stretching/torsional forces，最容易在 isthmus 裂伤。
>
> ```text
> rapid deceleration
> -> shearing force at aortic isthmus
> -> partial aortic wall tear
> -> widened mediastinum on chest x-ray
> -> if stable: CT angiography
> -> then definitive repair planning
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：suspected BTAI
> | Scenario | Best next step | Why |
> |---|---|---|
> | Stable after deceleration trauma | CT angiography | Best diagnostic confirmation |
> | Unstable with suspected thoracic vascular injury | Immediate thoracotomy +/- TEE | No time to leave OR/ER pathway |
> | Musculoskeletal chest wall pain only | Analgesics/observation | Only if no aortic red flags |
> | Need old invasive test? | Aortography not routine | Slower and more invasive |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Wide mediastinum after whiplash-speed trauma = image the aorta."**
> - mechanism 比 isolated chest tenderness 更重要。
> - stable -> CTA。
> - unstable -> OR。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Analgesics and observation**：若只是 costochondral pain 可以，但 widened mediastinum 让“先观察”变成危险选项。
>
> **B - Aortography**：老方法，不再是常规首选；更慢、更侵入。
>
> **D - Exploratory thoracotomy**：这是不稳定患者路线。本题虽然 tachycardic，但血压和氧合都还稳。
>
> **E - Transthoracic echocardiography**：看 thoracic aorta 尤其 isthmus 不够好；TEE 才比 TTE 更合适，但 stable patient 仍优先 CTA。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Exploratory thoracotomy。看到 life-threatening diagnosis 容易想“立刻开胸”，但 USMLE 强调先按 hemodynamic status 分流。
>

> ^Q133
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**pain with sitting + dyspareunia + tender fluctuant mass at the base of labium majus**，这是标准 **Bartholin gland abscess**，首选 **incision and drainage**，常配 Word catheter。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Excruciating pain with sitting and intercourse | Classic symptomatic vulvar abscess complaint | Bartholin abscess 很爱考这两个痛点 |
> | Tender, fluctuant 3-cm mass | Abscess rather than solid tumor | fluctuant 基本就把你往脓肿拉 |
> | Base of the right labium majus | Classic Bartholin gland location | Bartholin duct opening 在后外侧 introitus |
> | Bulging medially into the vagina | Typical anatomic direction | 位置描写非常标准 |
> | No mucopurulent cervical discharge | Less supportive of cervicitis | STI 风险高不等于经验性先打抗生素 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Incision and drainage**
>
> **Core Concept**：Bartholin duct obstruction 可先形成 cyst；继发感染后变成 **Bartholin gland abscess**。典型体征是 **painful, tender, fluctuant mass at the base of the labium majus**。初始治疗为 **incision and drainage**，并常放置 **Word catheter** 形成持续引流通道，降低复发。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：腺管先堵，再感染，分泌物出不去就形成局部脓肿；疼痛在坐下和性交时最明显，因为该区域受压最直接。
>
> ```text
> Bartholin duct obstruction
> -> retained gland secretion
> -> secondary infection
> -> tender fluctuant vulvar mass
> -> incision and drainage
> -> Word catheter to maintain drainage tract
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：vulvar lesion differentiation
> | Diagnosis | Typical lesion | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Bartholin abscess | Tender fluctuant labial mass | 本题完全符合 |
> | Genital herpes | Multiple painful vesicles/ulcers | 不是单个 fluctuant mass |
> | Cervicitis | Mucopurulent discharge, friable cervix | 本题宫颈表现不支持 |
> | Bartholin carcinoma | Solid fixed lesion in older patient | 年龄和触感都不对 |
> | Vulvar cancer | Pruritic/friable ulcer or plaque | 不是脓肿感 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Sit pain + side-wall fluctuant lump = Bartholin until proven otherwise."**
> - sit 会痛，是因为位置就在 vulvar introitus 受压区。
> - fluctuant 比 sexual history 更关键。
> - 高 STI 风险不代表先经验性按 cervicitis 治。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Acyclovir**：herpes 是 vesicles/ulcers，不是 3-cm fluctuant abscess cavity。
>
> **B - Biopsy of the lesion**：怀疑 Bartholin carcinoma 才做，通常是 older patient + solid/fixed lesion。
>
> **C - Ceftriaxone plus doxycycline**：虽然她 STI risk 高，但没有 mucopurulent cervical discharge、friability 或 postcoital bleeding，不该把主病灶错认成 cervicitis。
>
> **E - Wide local excision**：是 vulvar malignancy 的手术，不用于典型 Bartholin abscess 初始处理。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Ceftriaxone plus doxycycline。很多人会被 sexual history 带跑，但真正决定答案的是 **anatomic location + fluctuant abscess exam**。

> **提示**：**euphoria + agitation + mydriasis + HTN/tachycardia + overnight resolution + post-binge crash**，这不是 primary bipolar disorder，而是 **cocaine intoxication/use disorder**；长期治疗选 **12-step and individual therapy**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Grandiosity, aggression, nonstop movement | Sympathomimetic intoxication can mimic mania | 看起来像 manic，但时间轴很短 |
> | Dilated pupils, tachycardia, hypertension | Cocaine physiologic signature | 生理线索比口头情绪描述更可靠 |
> | Symptoms resolve by next morning | Substance intoxication rather than primary mood episode | 真正 manic episode 不会隔夜就清干净 |

> | Multiple similar ED visits | Established cocaine use disorder pattern | 问的是 long-term treatment，不是急性镇静 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - 12-step and individual therapy**
>
> **Core Concept**：Cocaine intoxication 可表现得像 mania 或 psychosis，但 **rapid spontaneous resolution** 加上 **cocaine crash** 非常关键。对 **cocaine use disorder**，目前没有稳定有效的 pharmacologic therapy；长期管理靠 **psychosocial treatment**，尤其是 **individual psychotherapy** 和 **12-step groups**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：cocaine 阻断 monoamine reuptake，短时间内让 dopamine/norepinephrine 高峰，造成兴奋、妄想样自信和自主神经过度激活；药效退后就进入低谷 crash。
>
> ```text
> cocaine use
> -> dopamine/norepinephrine excess
> -> euphoria + agitation + mydriasis + hypertension
> -> drug wears off quickly
> -> brief dysphoria/depression ("crash")
> -> long-term care = psychosocial treatment
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：substance use disorder treatment
> | Disorder | Long-term treatment pearl | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Cocaine use disorder | Psychotherapy + 12-step; no proven medication | 本题答案 |
> | Alcohol use disorder | Naltrexone, acamprosate, disulfiram can help | 不是 stimulant disorder |
> | Opioid use disorder | Methadone, buprenorphine, naltrexone | 不适用于 cocaine |
> | Primary bipolar disorder | Mood stabilizer/antipsychotic | 需要脱离 substance 才能成立 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Fast up, fast down, no pill proven."**
> - 隔夜恢复的“mania”先想 stimulant。
> - brief depression after binge = crash，不等于 MDD。
> - cocaine dependence 长期管理考点是 **therapy**, not meds。
>
> ---
>

> **B - Disulfiram**：酒精 aversion therapy，不治 cocaine dependence。
>
> **C - Methadone**：opioid maintenance，不是 stimulant treatment。
>
> **D - Naltrexone**：对 opioid/alcohol 有用，对 cocaine 没有一致有效性。
>
> **E - Olanzapine**：急性激越时可暂时帮忙，但题目问的是 long-term treatment。
>
> **F - Sertraline**：crash 后的短暂 depression 是 substance-induced，不代表 primary depressive disorder。
>
> **G - Valproic acid**：若是 bipolar mania 才考虑，但本题没有 independent mood episodes。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：G - Valproic acid。题干故意把 intoxication 写得像 mania，但 **rapid resolution + mydriasis + crash** 才是真题眼。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q136 Biostats_Master / Paired t-test for repeated measures
> ^Q136
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：同一批患者在 **baseline** 和 **week 6** 各测一次 **quantitative score**，核心不是“癌症”而是“**two related measurements**”，所以选 **dependent samples t-test**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | A/CS score is numeric | Outcome is quantitative | 题目比较的是分数均值，不是分类比例 |
> | Baseline and week 6 | Two time-point comparison | 只有两组，不是 >=3 组 |
> | Same 14 patients measured twice | Samples are dependent/paired | 同一个人前后对照，不能当独立样本 |
> | Asking for significant change | Compare means between 2 related groups | 不是相关性问题，也不是分类变量关联 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Dependent samples t-test**
>
> **Core Concept**：当 **dependent variable 是 quantitative**，并且你要比较 **同一对象前后两次测量** 的均值差异时，用 **paired t-test / dependent samples t-test**。本题的 assessment time 只是把同一批人的两次测量分成 baseline 与 week 6。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：paired t-test 的关键在于每个 baseline observation 都能和同一位患者的 week-6 observation 一一配对，统计上分析的是这些“差值”的均值是否偏离 0。
>
> ```text
> same patient measured twice
> -> paired observations
> -> compute within-person difference
> -> test whether mean difference != 0
> -> paired t-test
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：常见统计检验怎么选
> | Scenario | Best test | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 2 related groups, quantitative outcome | Paired t-test | 本题 |
> | 2 independent groups, quantitative outcome | Independent samples t-test | 本题不是两批不同患者 |
> | >=3 independent groups, quantitative outcome | ANOVA | 本题只有 baseline vs week 6 |
> | 2 categorical variables | Chi-square | 本题 outcome 不是 categorical |
> | 2 quantitative variables relationship | Correlation analysis | 本题问 change，不问 linear relationship |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Same patient twice = pair first, test second."**
> - 看到 before/after，先想 paired。
> - 看到 score/level/weight 这类连续值，先往 t-test/ANOVA 想。
> - 只有在“同一人前后”这个条件拿掉时，才转去 independent t-test。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Analysis of variance**：ANOVA 主要比较 >=3 个独立组均值；本题既不是 >=3 组，也不是独立组。
>
> **B - Chi-square test**：chi-square 用于 categorical vs categorical；本题 outcome 是 A/CS numeric score。
>
> **C - Correlation analysis**：相关分析看两个连续变量是否线性相关，不是比较同一变量前后均值。
>
> **E - Independent samples t-test**：如果 baseline 和 week 6 来自两批不同患者才会用它；本题是 repeated measures。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Independent samples t-test。很多人一看到“2 groups”就选它，但这题真正决定检验方法的是 **same participants assessed twice**。

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |

>

>
> ```text
> opioid overdose
> -> central respiratory drive suppression
> -> respiratory rate down + tidal volume down
> -> CO2 retention
> -> respiratory acidosis
>
> opioid effect on gut
> -> decreased motility
> -> decreased bowel sounds
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：opioid overdose 常考体征
> | Finding | Typical? | 考点 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Respiratory rate decreased | Yes | 不能只记 miosis，呼吸抑制才最致命 |
> | Tidal volume decreased | Yes | 不是“慢但深”，而是“慢且浅” |
> | Hypothermia | Yes | 与 stimulant hyperthermia 相反 |
> | Bowel sounds decreased | Yes | GI motility 被抑制 |
> | Normal pupils | Possible | 不能因无 miosis 就排除 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Opioids slow everything."**
> - 呼吸频率慢。
> - 每口气也更浅。
> - 体温低、肠道也慢。
>
> ---
>

>

>
> **D - Respiratory rate >12/min + hyperthermia**：这更不像 opioid，更接近别的 toxidrome。
>
> **E - Respiratory rate >12/min**：即使其他三项像，呼吸频率增高也已经把答案排掉。
>
> **F - Increased bowel sounds**：和 opioid 的 GI slowing 完全相反。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Respiratory rate >12/min, decreased tidal volume, hypothermia, decreased bowel sounds。有人会抓住“tidal volume down”却忽略 opioid 还会让 **rate** 一起下降。

> **提示**：migraine 患者先吃了别人的 migraine med，后来又打 **sumatriptan** 后出现 **severe HTN + neuro symptoms**，最该想到 **triptan + ergotamine** 的叠加血管痉挛。

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Similar prior headaches, diagnosed migraine | Baseline diagnosis is straightforward migraine | 题目不是在考 SAH，而是在考 treatment complication |
> | Took sister's migraine medication first | Hidden coexposure clue | 真正的陷阱埋在“sister's medication” |

> **机制说明**：本题不是 serotonin syndrome 的经典组合，而是 **two vasoconstrictive migraine abortives** 叠加后把血管收得过头。
>
> ```text
> ergotamine or triptan already taken
> + sumatriptan added
> -> excessive 5-HT receptor agonism
> -> prolonged vasoconstriction
> -> severe hypertension
> -> cerebral/cardiac ischemic complications
> ```
>

>

> |---|---|---|
> | Ergotamine | No | 24 hours 内避免，怕 prolonged vasospasm |
> | Diclofenac | Yes | NSAID，可安全联用 |
> | Metoclopramide | Usually yes | antiemetic adjunct |

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q143 心内 / HFrEF mortality beta-blocker
> ^Q143
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：old MI 后出现 **orthopnea + edema + S3 + displaced PMI**，这是典型 **HFrEF**；题目问长期结局最优的药，记住不是任意 beta-blocker，而是 **metoprolol succinate / carvedilol / bisoprolol**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Prior anterior MI | Common cause of ischemic cardiomyopathy | HFrEF 背景已经给足 |
> | Dyspnea, orthopnea, bibasilar crackles, edema | Congestive heart failure | 标准容量负荷过高表现 |
> | Displaced PMI and S3 | Dilated LV with systolic dysfunction | 强烈支持 reduced EF |
> | Creatinine normal; after optimal diuresis | Patient is ready for guideline-directed long-term therapy | 不是继续处理急性淤血阶段 |
> | Asked for best long-term outcomes | Need mortality benefit drug | 不是只改善症状或住院率 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：G - Metoprolol succinate**
>
> **Core Concept**：stable **heart failure with reduced ejection fraction** 需要上有证据的 mortality-reducing therapy。beta-blocker 里真正有硬结局证据的是 **metoprolol succinate, carvedilol, bisoprolol**。这题选项里只有 `metoprolol succinate` 符合。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：HFrEF 长期被 sympathetic overdrive 推着走。合适的 beta-blockade 能降低心肌耗氧、减轻 maladaptive remodeling，并带来 survival benefit。
>
> ```text
> prior MI
> -> LV remodeling
> -> systolic dysfunction
> -> chronic sympathetic activation
> -> progressive heart failure
> -> evidence-based beta-blocker
> -> improved survival
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：HFrEF 常见药物效果
> | Drug/class | Main role | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Metoprolol succinate | Symptom and mortality benefit | 本题答案 |
> | Digoxin | Symptom control, lower hospitalization | No mortality benefit |
> | Chlorthalidone | BP control, adjunct diuresis | No mortality benefit in HFrEF |
> | Amiodarone | Rhythm control in selected arrhythmias | Not routine survival drug here |
> | Diltiazem/amlodipine | No benefit in systolic HF | Some can worsen due to negative inotropy |
> | Atenolol | Not trial-proven for HFrEF survival | 容易被 "也是 beta-blocker" 误导 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"In HFrEF, not every beta-blocker graduates."**
> - beta-blocker 家族里要背会 3 个毕业生：`metoprolol succinate, carvedilol, bisoprolol`。
> - 问 long-term outcomes = 问 survival，不只是 symptom relief。
> - old MI + S3 + displaced PMI = 先把诊断锁到 ischemic HFrEF。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Amiodarone**：有 arrhythmia 适应证时用，但这题没有明确室性心律失常，也不提高 overall survival。
>
> **B - Amlodipine**：在 systolic HF 里没有明确长期获益。
>
> **C - Atenolol**：这是最大陷阱。虽然也是 beta-blocker，但没有像 metoprolol succinate 那样的 HFrEF trial-level survival data。
>
> **D - Chlorthalidone**：可以帮助控压或辅助利尿，但不是 guideline mortality drug。
>
> **E - Digoxin**：可减少住院、改善症状，但不延长寿命。
>
> **F - Diltiazem**：negative inotropy，对 systolic HF 不合适。
>
> **H - Propafenone**：class IC antiarrhythmic，在 CAD/HF 里反而危险。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q144 endocrine / Hypothyroid amenorrhea with galactorrhea
> ^Q144
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：secondary amenorrhea 再加 **galactorrhea + weight gain + dry skin + bradycardia**，先别急着做 pituitary MRI，先查 **TSH**，因为最像 **primary hypothyroidism causing hyperprolactinemia**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Amenorrhea for 6 months, pregnancy test negative | Secondary amenorrhea workup needed | 先排 pregnancy 后看 endocrine causes |
> | Bilateral scant whitish nipple discharge | Galactorrhea from hyperprolactinemia | 不是乳腺肿瘤型血性分泌物 |
> | Fatigue, weight gain, dry skin, pulse 52/min | Classic hypothyroid slowing | 这是题目最值钱的定位线索 |
> | Mild headaches but no vision changes | Pituitary mass less urgent/less obvious | 不足以直接跳 MRI |
> | Normal BMI, no hirsutism | PCOS less likely | 把你从 testosterone workup 拉开 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Serum TSH level**
>
> **Core Concept**：**primary hypothyroidism** 会让 **TRH** 上升，TRH 不只推高 `TSH`，也会刺激 `prolactin`。高 prolactin 再抑制 `GnRH`，造成 **anovulation -> amenorrhea**。所以这题最优 next step 是先查 `TSH`，不是先做 pituitary MRI。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题的思维顺序是 "amenorrhea + galactorrhea" 先想到 hyperprolactinemia，再往上游追有没有 **hypothyroidism** 这个可逆原因。
>
> ```text
> primary hypothyroidism
> -> low T4
> -> increased TRH
> -> increased TSH and prolactin
> -> decreased GnRH
> -> decreased FSH/LH
> -> anovulation and amenorrhea
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：amenorrhea + galactorrhea 鉴别
> | Cause | Key clue | Next step |
> |---|---|---|
> | Hypothyroidism | fatigue, weight gain, dry skin, bradycardia | Check TSH |
> | Prolactinoma | headaches +/- visual symptoms, isolated hyperprolactinemia | MRI after labs |
> | PCOS | irregular menses, obesity, hyperandrogenism | androgen/metabolic workup |
> | Pregnancy | first rule-out in all amenorrhea | hCG |
> | Breast pathology | unilateral bloody discharge or mass | breast imaging |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Low thyroid can masquerade as a prolactinoma."**
> - amenorrhea + galactorrhea 不等于立刻 MRI。
> - bradycardia + dry skin + weight gain 是 endocrine breadcrumb。
> - 先找 reversible cause，再上影像。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Diagnostic mammogram**：bilateral milky discharge without mass 不像 breast malignancy。
>
> **B - Endometrial biopsy**：用于 abnormal uterine bleeding 风险评估，不是这题的 isolated amenorrhea pattern。
>
> **C - MRI of the pituitary**：确实会让人因为 headache + galactorrhea 想到 prolactinoma，但 hypothyroid clues 更强，且应先做 lab evaluation。
>
> **D - Serum testosterone level**：PCOS 常见于 overweight 或 hyperandrogenic patient，这题都不支持。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - MRI of the pituitary。因为 `amenorrhea + galactorrhea + headache` 很像 prolactinoma，但 **weight gain + dry skin + bradycardia** 其实在大声提示 hypothyroidism。

> ^Q145
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：bilateral hand pain/paresthesia + **positive Phalen test** + splint 2 周效果差，说明是 **carpal tunnel syndrome** 且初始保守失败，下一步选 **local corticosteroid injection**。

> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Pain and paresthesia in both hands | Neuropathic hand symptom pattern | bilateral CTS 很常见 |
> | Symptoms reproduced by full wrist flexion | Positive Phalen test | median nerve compression 经典体征 |
> | Slightly better under warm running water | Common temporary relief pattern | 和 shaking hands 类似，支持 CTS |

> | Wrist splint over 2 weeks with minimal relief | Initial treatment has failed | next step 要升级治疗 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Local corticosteroid injection**
>
> **Core Concept**：**carpal tunnel syndrome** 的初始处理是 **neutral-position wrist splinting**。如果 splinting 效果不够，下一步优先 **local glucocorticoid injection**，通常能更快、更有效地缓解 median nerve compression symptoms。手术保留给 severe 或 refractory disease。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：问题在 transverse carpal ligament 下方的局部狭窄和炎症，所以局部 steroid 比口服药更精准，也比 NSAIDs 更有效。
>
> ```text
> repetitive wrist use
> -> increased tunnel pressure
> -> median nerve compression
> -> pain and paresthesia
> -> splint fails
> -> local steroid reduces tunnel inflammation
> -> symptom relief
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：CTS 治疗阶梯
> | Stage | Preferred treatment | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Mild initial CTS | Night wrist splint | 本题已试过且无效 |
> | Persistent symptoms after splint | Local corticosteroid injection | 本题答案 |
> | Severe weakness/thenar atrophy | Surgical decompression | 本题没有 severe deficit |
> | Oral steroids | Less effective backup option | 不如局部注射 |
> | NSAIDs | Ineffective | 常见误选 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"CTS treatment stays local unless it gets surgical."**
> - 先 splint。
> - splint 不够 -> local steroid injection。
> - 有 thenar atrophy/weakness 或久治不愈 -> surgery。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Oral corticosteroids**：不是完全没用，但效果不如局部注射，而且 systemic side effects 更多。
>
> **C - Oral nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs**：这是高频误选，但对 CTS 并不真正有效。
>
> **D - Physical therapy**：更偏 adjunctive，作为 failed splint 后 primary next step 证据不如 local injection。
>
> **E - Vitamin B12 supplements**：B12 deficiency 常伴 diminished reflexes、vibration/position sense 问题，这题神经查体并不支持。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Oral nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs。因为很多人会把 hand pain 当一般 musculoskeletal pain 处理，但这题核心是 **median nerve entrapment**。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q146 OB / Nifedipine tocolysis adverse effects
> ^Q146
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：`33 weeks` 的 preterm labor，能抑制宫缩又带来 **flushing + headache + hypotension + tachycardia**，先锁定 **nifedipine**，机制就是 **calcium channel blockade**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 33 weeks gestation | Tocolytic choice depends on gestational age | `32-34 weeks` 一线是 nifedipine |
> | Regular contractions with cervical dilation | True preterm labor, not false labor | 需要 tocolysis 来争取胎儿获益时间 |
> | Medication significantly decreases contractions | The drug is acting as a tocolytic | 题目先让你识别药物类别 |
> | Flushing, headache, nausea | Classic vasodilatory side effects | 非常像 nifedipine 的 maternal adverse effects |
> | BP drops, pulse rises | Peripheral vasodilation with reflex tachycardia | 这是 calcium channel blocker 的高频副作用组合 |
> | Poorly controlled gestational diabetes | Makes terbutaline less attractive | beta-agonist 会加重 hyperglycemia |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Calcium channel blockade**
>
> **Core Concept**：preterm labor 在 `32-34 weeks` 的一线 tocolytic 是 **nifedipine**。它阻断 smooth muscle 的 calcium influx，既能让 **myometrium relax**，也会造成 **peripheral vasodilation**，因此出现 flushing、headache、hypotension、tachycardia、palpitations。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是单纯问副作用记忆，而是在考你把 gestational age 和 tocolytic selection 连起来。
>
> ```text
> 32-34 week preterm labor
> -> choose nifedipine
> -> blocks L-type calcium channels
> -> decreased myometrial contraction
> -> peripheral vasodilation
> -> flushing + headache + hypotension + reflex tachycardia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：常见 tocolytics
> | Drug | Best use window | Key adverse effect pattern |
> |---|---|---|
> | Nifedipine | 32-34 weeks | Flushing, headache, hypotension, tachycardia |
> | Indomethacin | <32 weeks | Oligohydramnios, premature ductus closure |
> | Terbutaline | Short-term uterine relaxation only | Tachyarrhythmia, pulmonary edema, worsens hyperglycemia |
> | Magnesium sulfate | <32 weeks for fetal neuroprotection | Somnolence, decreased reflexes, respiratory depression if toxic |
> | Betamethasone | Fetal lung maturity, not tocolysis | Not used to stop contractions |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Nifedipine opens vessels while closing labor."**
> - `33 weeks` 先想 nifedipine。
> - Headache + flushing 是 vasodilation clue。
> - Diabetes 是排 terbutaline 的加分线索。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Beta-adrenergic receptor stimulation**：说的是 terbutaline。它也会 tachycardia，但更常用于短期急救性 uterine relaxation，而且 poorly controlled diabetes 是重要排除点。
>
> **C - Circulation of synthetic glucocorticoids**：betamethasone 用于促进 fetal lung maturity，不负责抑制宫缩，也不解释 flushing/hypotension。
>
> **D - Cyclooxygenase inhibition**：indomethacin 是 `<32 weeks` 的一线，不符合本题 gestational age。
>
> **E - Decreased acetylcholine release at the myoneural junction**：对应 magnesium sulfate。它可有 flushing/headache，但通常不是 `32-34 weeks` 的 routine first-line tocolytic。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Beta-adrenergic receptor stimulation。因为也会 tachycardia，但 `33 weeks + diabetes + classic flushing/headache` 更支持 nifedipine。
>

> | Blue lips and tongue | Central cyanosis, not just peripheral discoloration | mucous membranes 发青是题眼 |
> | Nail beds also blue | More diffuse cyanosis pattern | 支持 systemic hypoxemia |
> | Extremities are warm | Argues against peripheral cyanosis from poor flow | peripheral cyanosis 常见 cool or clammy extremities |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Low arterial oxygen saturation**
>
> **Core Concept**：**central cyanosis** 出现在 lips、tongue、mucous membranes 等高血流组织，说明 arterial blood 里去氧血红蛋白明显增多，也就是 **arterial oxygen saturation 下降**。这和 peripheral cyanosis 完全不同，后者主要是 capillary flow sluggish，arterial saturation 本身仍可正常。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：central 与 peripheral cyanosis 的区分，关键不在“蓝不蓝”，而在“蓝在哪里”和“手脚是暖还是冷”。
>
> ```text
> cardiopulmonary abnormality
> -> decreased arterial oxygen saturation
> -> increased deoxygenated hemoglobin
> -> lips/tongue/mucosa turn blue
> -> central cyanosis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：central vs peripheral cyanosis
> | Feature | Central cyanosis | Peripheral cyanosis |
> |---|---|---|
> | Location | Lips, tongue, mucous membranes | Distal extremities mainly |
> | Arterial oxygen saturation | Low | Usually normal |
> | Extremity temperature | May be warm | Often cool or clammy |
> | Mechanism | Systemic hypoxemia | Sluggish flow with increased O2 extraction |
> | Common exam clue | Tongue is blue | Tongue spared |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Blue tongue means true hypoxemia."**
> - Tongue cyanosis = central.
> - Warm hands/feet = not vasoconstrictive peripheral cyanosis.
> - Feeding intolerance in infant = think congenital heart disease.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Peripheral vasoconstriction**：会造成 peripheral cyanosis，但通常 distal only，而且 extremities 偏冷。
>
> **B - High venous pressure**：可让 capillary flow 变慢、增加 oxygen extraction，仍属于 peripheral cyanosis 逻辑。
>
> **C - Sickling of red blood cells**：新生儿早期有 HbF 保护，6 周大通常不会这样表现。
>
> **D - Polycythemia**：更偏 peripheral cyanosis，而且多在出生后前几天表现。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Peripheral vasoconstriction。因为 nail beds 发青很容易把人带去 peripheral cyanosis，但 **lips + tongue + warm extremities** 已经把答案锁死。

> chronic diabetes
> -> glomerular injury + albuminuria
> -> RAAS activation and hypertension
> -> higher intraglomerular pressure
> -> faster CKD progression
> -> ACEI/ARB blood pressure control
> -> slower nephropathy progression
> ```

>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：diabetic nephropathy 干预优先级
> | Intervention | Benefit | Why not best answer here |
> |---|---|---|

> | Better glycemic control | Helps microvascular disease | Less effective than BP control once nephropathy is established |
> | Smoking cessation | Helpful adjunct | Smaller incremental renal benefit |
> | Protein restriction | Limited routine role | Not standard high-yield primary strategy |
> | Omega-3 | Lipid benefit only | No proven nephropathy benefit |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Diabetic kidneys hate pressure."**

> - The strongest brake on progression is lowering pressure.
> - When CKD + diabetes coexist, think ACEI/ARB.
>

> -> dipstick blood positive

> ```

>

> **Core Concept**：combined OCPs 通过 **suppression of ovulation** 降低 ovarian cancer risk，而且使用时间越长，保护作用越明显。它们也能降低 endometrial cancer risk，但本题选项里真正被问到的是 ovarian cancer。

>
> ```text
> combined OCP use
> -> ovulation suppression
> -> less repeated ovarian epithelial disruption
> -> lower ovarian cancer risk
>
> progestin effect
> -> less endometrial proliferation
> -> lower endometrial cancer risk
> ```

> **A - Choroidal inflammation**：posterior uveitis 常无痛，且有 floaters/炎症背景。
>
> **C - Elevated intracranial pressure**：应见 papilledema 和更弥漫的 ICP symptoms。
>
> **D - Increased intraocular pressure**：acute angle-closure glaucoma 通常是 older adult 的 painful red eye。
>
> **E - Large vessel vasculitis**：GCA 主要在 age >50，且多伴 headache、jaw claudication。
>
> **F - Postictal cortical inhibition**：应有 seizure context 或 visual aura。
>
> **G - Retinal embolism**：会更 sudden、painless，而且常见于有 vascular risk 的 older patient。
>
> **H - Retinal hemorrhage**：fundus 常不正常。
>
> **I - Vascular spasm**：migraine aura 更偏 positive visual symptoms，而不是 this progressive painful optic pattern。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：G。因为同一套上一题刚考 retinal embolism，但这里年龄、疼痛、热诱发和既往 neurologic episodes 全都转向 MS。
