> [!example]- UWSA1 Q041 pulmonary / Septic shock lactic acidosis
> ^Q041
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：发热、warm extremities、严重 hypotension、右下肺 pneumonia 体征，再配上 **pH 7.24 + PaCO2 30**，这是 **septic shock causing lactic acidosis**，本质是 **increased tissue metabolic acid production**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Fever + tachycardia + BP 70/60 | Shock physiology | 先识别这是休克，不只是普通肺炎 |
> | Warm extremities | Suggests distributive shock | warm shock 很符合 early septic shock |
> | Bronchial breath sounds at right base | Pneumonia source | 右下肺感染是最可能感染灶 |
> | pH 7.24 | Acidemia | 明确先从酸碱平衡入手 |
> | PaCO2 30 mm Hg | Low, so not primary respiratory acidosis | 低 CO2 说明在过度通气代偿，而不是 CO2 潴留 |
> | Vomiting only once | Not enough to explain ABG | 呕吐会偏向 metabolic alkalosis，不是本题主因 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Increased tissue metabolic acid production**
>
> **Core Concept**：这题是 **primary metabolic acidosis with respiratory compensation**。septic shock 时组织灌注不足，细胞转向 **anaerobic metabolism**，产生大量 **lactic acid**，所以出现 acidemia；低 PaCO2 只是代偿性 hyperventilation 的结果。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：判断顺序要先看 pH，再看 PaCO2 是否与主问题一致。这里 pH 低而 PaCO2 也低，说明真正问题在代谢端。
>
> ```text
> pneumonia
> -> sepsis
> -> septic shock
> -> poor tissue perfusion
> -> anaerobic glycolysis
> -> lactic acid accumulation
> -> metabolic acidosis
> -> compensatory hyperventilation
> -> low PaCO2
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Choice | Typical Acid-Base Pattern | Why Not This Case |
> |---|---|---|
> | Alveolar ventilation/perfusion mismatch | Often hypoxemia, may cause respiratory acidosis if severe hypoventilation | 本题 PaCO2 低，不是 CO2 retention |
> | RTA type 2 | Non-anion gap metabolic acidosis | 解释不了 septic shock 体征 |
> | Vomiting | Hypochloremic metabolic alkalosis | 与 acidemia 相反 |
> | Starvation ketosis | Mild ketosis | 程度通常没这么重，也不解释感染休克 |
> | Lactic acidosis in sepsis | High-anion-gap metabolic acidosis | 本题完全匹配 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Warm shock + low pH + low CO2 = lactic acidosis, not lung failure."**
> - 看到 warm extremities，要想到 distributive shock。
> - 看到低 pH 配低 CO2，要先想 metabolic acidosis with compensation。
> - sepsis 最常见住院代谢性酸中毒来源就是 lactate。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Alveolar ventilation/perfusion mismatch**：肺炎确实会造成 V/Q mismatch，但那解释的是氧合问题；若主问题是 ventilatory failure，PaCO2 应更容易升高，不会像这里这样下降。
>
> **B - Impaired renal bicarbonate reabsorption**：type 2 RTA 会造成 non-anion-gap metabolic acidosis，但和高热、低血压、肺部感染体征完全不搭。
>
> **C - Increased gastrointestinal chloride loss**：呕吐丢 HCl 会走向 metabolic alkalosis，这是酸碱方向直接相反的错法。
>
> **D - Increased hepatic fatty acid metabolism**：饥饿性 ketosis 可以轻度酸中毒，但不足以解释 septic shock 的严重程度。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Alveolar ventilation/perfusion mismatch。因为题干有 pneumonia，很多人会被肺部病灶带偏，忘了 ABG 的主矛盾是 **metabolic acidosis**。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q042 心内 / Viral myocarditis to DCM
> ^Q042
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：年轻人、viral URI 后 2 周出现新发 heart failure，外加 laterally displaced PMI，优先锁定 **myocarditis progressing to dilated cardiomyopathy**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Upper respiratory infection 2 weeks ago | Postviral clue | recent viral illness 是 myocarditis 经典前驱 |
> | Dyspnea on walking + choking after sleep | Heart failure symptoms | exertional dyspnea + PND 很典型 |
> | Bibasilar crackles + pitting edema | Volume overload | 左右心衰体征都出来了 |
> | PMI at 6th ICS, anterior axillary line | LV enlargement | laterally displaced PMI 指向 ventricular dilation |
> | Age 38 with no prior cardiac history | Makes ischemic disease less likely | 年轻且无长期病史，更支持炎症后心肌病 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Dilated cardiomyopathy**
>
> **Core Concept**：viral **myocarditis** 可导致 myocardial injury，之后出现 **dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM)**，表现为 ventricular dilation、systolic dysfunction 和典型 heart failure 症状。laterally displaced PMI 是题眼，因为它在体格检查上直接提示心室扩大。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：先有病毒相关心肌炎，再因心肌收缩功能受损而心室扩张，最后进入充血性心衰表型。
>
> ```text
> viral infection
> -> myocarditis
> -> myocyte injury
> -> reduced systolic function
> -> ventricular dilation
> -> dilated cardiomyopathy
> -> pulmonary and peripheral congestion
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Condition | Classic Clue | Why Not This Case |
> |---|---|---|
> | Dilated cardiomyopathy | Systolic HF, displaced PMI, often postviral | 本题最匹配 |
> | Mitral valve prolapse | Mid-systolic click, often incidental | 不能解释 postviral HF |
> | Restrictive cardiomyopathy | Diastolic dysfunction from infiltrative disease | 通常不是 young post-URI presentation |
> | HOCM | Exertional syncope, harsh systolic murmur | 与 displaced PMI 和 volume overload 不符 |
> | Constrictive pericarditis | Right-sided failure with rigid pericardium | 更常见 chronic scarring，不是病毒后新发 DCM 模式 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Virus first, ventricle stretches later."**
> - recent viral illness：想 myocarditis。
> - displaced PMI：想 dilated ventricle。
> - dyspnea + edema：说明已经进入 heart failure 表型。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Mitral valve prolapse**：更像结构性瓣膜问题，常见 click 或轻度 regurgitation 线索，不会由 recent viral illness 触发成这种全套心衰。
>
> **C - Restrictive cardiomyopathy**：关键词应是 infiltrative disease、diastolic dysfunction、Kussmaul sign 等，而不是 displaced PMI。
>
> **D - Hypertrophic obstructive cardiomyopathy**：多见家族史、劳力性晕厥或动态流出道梗阻杂音，不是 postviral volume-overload HF。
>
> **E - Constrictive pericarditis**：常和 pericardial scarring 相关，更像 chronic right-sided congestion；而 myocarditis 更能解释本题时间线。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Constrictive pericarditis。因为也会出现 edema 和 dyspnea，但本题真正关键是 **viral prodrome + displaced PMI**，提示 myocardial dilation 不是 pericardial constraint。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q043 derm / Plaque psoriasis
> ^Q043
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：双侧 knees/elbows 的 **well-demarcated erythematous plaques with thick silvery scale**，再加上 extensor distribution，就是 **plaque psoriasis**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Chronic bilateral lesions on knees and elbows | Classic location | extensor surfaces 是 psoriasis 高频定位 |
> | Enlarged progressively over years | Chronic inflammatory dermatosis | 慢性进展比感染更像炎症性皮肤病 |
> | Occasional itching but not painful | Fits psoriasis | 可痒但通常不是以疼痛为主 |
> | Temporary relief with moisturizers only | Barrier care not enough | 保湿只能缓解表面干燥，不能控制炎症 |
> | Frequent kneeling as a plumber | Koebner phenomenon | 局部摩擦/创伤可诱发或加重银屑病 |
> | Rendered image shows silvery scaly extensor plaques | Visual confirmation | 影像上是非常典型的 plaque psoriasis 外观 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：G - Plaque psoriasis**
>
> **Core Concept**：plaque psoriasis 是慢性免疫介导皮肤病，典型表现为 **well-demarcated erythematous plaques with white-silvery scale**，最常见在 **extensor surfaces**。本题图片和病史都非常标准；工作中反复 kneeling 还能用 **Koebner phenomenon** 解释为什么病灶越来越明显。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：关键不是“干燥”，而是表皮角化和炎症信号异常，导致角质形成细胞过快增殖。
>
> ```text
> immune dysregulation
> -> keratinocyte hyperproliferation
> -> thickened epidermis and scale
> -> well-demarcated erythematous plaque
> -> extensor surface predominance
> -> worsens with trauma (Koebner phenomenon)
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical Morphology | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Plaque psoriasis | Silvery scaly plaques on extensor surfaces | 本题完全符合 |
> | Atopic dermatitis | Pruritic eczematous rash on flexural surfaces | 部位不对 |
> | Dyshidrotic eczema | Vesicles on palms/soles | 形态和部位都不对 |
> | Lichen simplex chronicus | Thickened excoriated plaque from scratching | 通常是可及部位单块苔藓样变 |
> | Seborrheic dermatitis | Oily scale on scalp/face | 不在膝肘为主 |
> | Tinea corporis | Annular lesion with central clearing | 没有 ring-like border |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Silver on extensor = psoriasis until proven otherwise."**
> - silver scale：想 psoriasis。
> - extensor knees/elbows：更支持 plaque type。
> - trauma worsens rash：记住 Koebner phenomenon。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Atopic dermatitis**：成人 eczema 更爱 flexural areas，而且通常 pruritus 更突出，病灶也没这么典型的 silvery scale。
>
> **B - Dyshidrotic eczema**：核心是 palms/soles 的 pruritic vesicles，不是膝部厚斑块。
>
> **C - Frictional dermatitis**：摩擦确实会刺激皮肤，但一般不会形成这么经典、边界清楚、覆盖厚银白鳞屑的对称性病灶。
>
> **D - Lichen simplex chronicus**：来自长期搔抓造成苔藓样变，常见 excoriations；本题视觉上更像银屑病斑块。
>
> **E - Mycosis fungoides**：可有 scaly plaques，但临床和图像都没有 classic CTCL 的支持度。
>
> **F - Nummular eczema**：应是 coin-shaped eczematous lesions，不是 extensor silvery plaques。
>
> **H - Seborrheic dermatitis**：偏 scalp、nasolabial folds、eyebrows、ears。
>
> **I - Tinea corporis**：应有 annular border 和 central clearing，本图没有。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Frictional dermatitis。因为题干主动给了“plumber, kneeling”这个诱饵，但这里更合理的用法是 **Koebner phenomenon aggravating psoriasis**，不是摩擦本身解释全部病灶。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q044 GI / Pancreatic cancer jaundice
> ^Q044
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：老年人出现 **painless jaundice + weight loss + cholestatic labs**，先想 **pancreatic cancer obstructing the common bile duct**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Jaundice for 3 months | Chronic obstructive process | 不是一过性肝炎模式 |
> | Decreased appetite and low BMI | Cancer cachexia clue | 食欲差和消瘦提示恶性肿瘤 |
> | Abdominal discomfort without major pain | Fits pancreatic malignancy | 胰腺癌常是 vague discomfort，不一定剧痛 |
> | RUQ fullness | Hepatobiliary obstruction/mass effect | 说明上腹部已有占位或胆道淤积影响 |
> | Total bilirubin 12.5 and alkaline phosphatase 350 | Cholestatic pattern | 重点看 bili 和 ALP，不是单纯 hepatocellular injury |
> | AST/ALT only mildly elevated | Against primary liver parenchymal disease | 转氨酶不够高，提示主问题不在肝细胞本身 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Pancreas**
>
> **Core Concept**：pancreatic head mass 很容易压迫 **common bile duct**，导致 **obstructive jaundice**。所以题目里最有用的不是“黄疸”本身，而是 **黄疸 + weight loss + ALP/bilirubin 明显升高 + vague abdominal discomfort** 这组组合拳。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：胰头部位离胆总管近，所以肿瘤不一定先以疼痛出场，反而常先用胆汁排出受阻来表现。
>
> ```text
> pancreatic head malignancy
> -> compression of common bile duct
> -> impaired bile flow
> -> conjugated hyperbilirubinemia
> -> elevated alkaline phosphatase
> -> jaundice
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Site | Typical Lab/Clinical Pattern | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Pancreas | Painless jaundice, weight loss, cholestatic labs | 本题最符合 |
> | Liver parenchyma | AST/ALT often much higher | 本题转氨酶只是轻中度升高 |
> | Duodenum | Can obstruct but much less common | 作为鉴别可想，但不是首选 |
> | Gallbladder | Less common initial obstructive jaundice | 常见性和模式都不如胰腺癌 |
> | Hemolysis | Unconjugated hyperbilirubinemia, no ALP rise | 本题 ALP 明显升高 |
> | Bone marrow ineffective erythropoiesis | Bilirubin may rise, ALP not cholestatic | 不能解释梗阻模式 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Painless jaundice in an older patient lives in the pancreatic head."**
> - painless jaundice：先想 obstruction。
> - weight loss：再把恶性肿瘤权重大幅拉高。
> - high bilirubin + high ALP：典型 cholestatic lab pattern。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Liver parenchyma**：如果主问题是 hepatocellular injury，AST/ALT 往往会更高；本题是典型 cholestatic dominance。
>
> **B - Duodenum**：理论上也能堵到胆汁出口，但相对 pancreatic cancer 明显更少见。
>
> **D - Gallbladder**：虽然也属胆道附近肿瘤，但作为初发表现的 obstructive jaundice 不如胰头癌经典。
>
> **E - Circulating red blood cells**：hemolysis 可升高 bilirubin，但不会把 ALP 拉这么高。
>
> **F - Bone marrow**：ineffective erythropoiesis 也解释不了 cholestatic enzyme pattern。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Liver parenchyma。因为看到黄疸容易本能想到“肝病”，但这题真正关键是 **ALP/bilirubin disproportionately elevated**。
>

> **提示**：有自杀风险的青少年，突然出现 **severe abdominal pain + hematemesis + green/black stool + shock**，要先想 **acute iron poisoning**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 17-year-old with prior suicide attempt | Overdose risk | 先把 intentional ingestion 放进鉴别 |
> | Anemia history | Possible iron tablet access | 贫血提供了 iron source |
> | Coffee-ground emesis | Upper GI bleeding | 说明胃黏膜受损很重 |
> | Dark green diarrhea | Classic tablet-related stool change | 铁剂崩解可使 stool 变绿黑 |
> | Hypotension, tachycardia, delayed cap refill | Shock | 已经不是单纯胃肠炎 |
> | Diffuse abdominal tenderness with soft abdomen | Toxic mucosal injury more than peritonitis | 更像中毒性 GI injury，而非阑尾穿孔 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Acute iron poisoning**
>
> **Core Concept**：acute iron poisoning 早期先表现为 **direct gastrointestinal corrosive injury**，所以会有 abdominal pain、hematemesis、diarrhea；随后因失液、vasodilation、myocardial injury 和低灌注出现 **shock** 与 **anion gap metabolic acidosis**。题干里的 anemia 史是非常高价值的“药源线索”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：iron 不是单纯“过量补品”，而是能通过 free radical injury 直接伤胃肠和肝脏的毒物。
>
> ```text
> iron overdose
> -> direct GI mucosal injury
> -> hematemesis and diarrhea
> -> volume loss and poor perfusion
> -> lactic acidosis and shock
> -> hepatic necrosis can follow
> -> later bowel scarring/obstruction
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | What Fits | What Does Not Fit |
> |---|---|---|
> | Acute iron poisoning | GI bleeding, green/black stool, shock, suicidal ingestion | 本题完全符合 |
> | Acetaminophen overdose | Nausea/vomiting, delayed hepatotoxicity | 不典型 hematemesis |
> | Appendicitis | RLQ pain, fever | 不会造成 coffee-ground emesis |
> | Narcotic overdose | CNS depression, bradypnea, miosis | 本题是 tachypnea/tachycardia |
> | PID | Lower abdominal pain, discharge, CMT | 不解释 GI bleeding |
> | Ectopic pregnancy | Pelvic pain after missed period | LMP 仅 3 周前，且不致 hematemesis |
> | TCA overdose | Anticholinergic signs, arrhythmia | 应更偏 decreased bowel motility |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Iron burns the gut before it kills the liver."**
> - 先记早期：abdominal pain + hematemesis + diarrhea。
> - 再记中期：shock + metabolic acidosis。
> - 再记后期：hepatic necrosis 和 bowel obstruction。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Acetaminophen overdose**：可以肝毒性，但 GI bleeding 并不是其经典前台表现。
>
> **B - Acute appendicitis**：应以迁移性腹痛、RLQ tenderness、发热更典型；本题上消化道出血完全不支持。
>
> **D - Narcotic overdose**：阿片应有 respiratory depression、bradycardia 或 miosis，而不是 tachypnea。
>
> **E - Pelvic inflammatory disease**：能解释 sexual activity 和腹痛，但解释不了 hematemesis 与休克模式。
>
> **F - Ruptured ectopic pregnancy**：是必须想过的急症，但 LMP 仅 3 周前，而且 ectopic 不会造成 coffee-ground emesis。
>
> **G - Tricyclic antidepressant overdose**：TCA 会有 anticholinergic toxidrome 和心电异常，胃肠道更偏 ileus，不是 diarrhea。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Ruptured ectopic pregnancy。因为青少年、腹痛、低血压会强烈诱导你往 OB 走，但 **hematemesis + green diarrhea + anemia pill access** 把方向拉回 iron toxicity。

> ```text

> -> atrial ectopy/remodeling
> -> atrial fibrillation
> -> dizziness/palpitations
>
> excess thyroid hormone
> -> increased contractility and vasodilation
> -> high systolic and lower diastolic pressure
> -> wide pulse pressure
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Test/Diagnosis | When It Fits | Why Not Here |
> |---|---|---|

> | Carotid sinus massage | Regular SVT | AF 是 irregularly irregular |
> | Tilt table testing | Vasovagal syncope workup | 本题已有 arrhythmia 线索 |
> | Renin/aldosterone ratio | Resistant HTN with hypokalemia | 不解释 AF 和 weight loss |
> | Urine metanephrines | Episodic headache/diaphoresis/palpitations | 本题缺乏 pheo triad |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"AF plus weight loss? Check the thyroid before you chase zebras."**
> - irregularly irregular：先认 AF。
> - weight loss：再问 thyroid。
> - wide pulse pressure：几乎是 hyperthyroid 加分题眼。
>
> ---
>

>

> **正确答案：C - Degree of anaplasia**
>
> **Core Concept**：astrocytoma 预后最核心的是 **tumor grade**。grade 越高，说明 atypia、mitoses、necrosis、neovascularity 越明显，生物学行为越凶，预后越差。**glioblastoma multiforme (grade IV)** 是最差的一端。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：anaplasia 反映的是 tumor 去分化和增殖失控程度，因此比单纯大小或位置更能代表恶性行为。
>
> ```text
> increasing anaplasia
> -> higher tumor grade
> -> more mitoses / necrosis / neovascularity
> -> more aggressive growth
> -> worse prognosis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Feature | Prognostic Value in Astrocytoma | Exam Takeaway |
> |---|---|---|
> | Degree of anaplasia | Highest | 本题答案 |
> | Tumor size | Limited | 影响症状多于生存 |
> | Tumor location | Functional impact | 不如 grade 稳定预测 survival |
> | Distant metastasis | Rare in astrocytoma | 不是主要决定因素 |
> | Vascular invasion | Not the key standard predictor | 真正高价值的是 grade-associated features |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"For gliomas, grade rules."**
> - astrocytoma：先问 grade。
> - grade III：anaplastic astrocytoma。
> - grade IV：GBM，最差。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Size of the tumor**：体积会影响癫痫或占位症状，但不是最稳的 survival predictor。
>
> **B - Metastatic disease**：astrocytoma 多数不是靠远处转移决定结局，更多是局部侵袭和 intracranial progression。
>
> **D - Location of the tumor**：位置确实影响神经功能缺损，但整体预后更受 grade 支配。
>
> **E - Vascular invasion**：容易让人联想到恶性，但标准高频结论仍然是 **degree of anaplasia / grade**。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Location of the tumor。因为脑瘤看起来“位置很重要”，但考试更想考你：**glioma prognosis tracks with grade**。

> ^Q052
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**diarrhea/vomiting + dry mucosa + azotemia + urine Na <20 mEq/L**，这是典型 **hypovolemic hyponatremia due to intravascular volume depletion**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 7-8 watery stools daily and vomiting | Extrarenal sodium and water loss | 先想 volume depletion |
> | Dry mucous membranes, poor skin turgor | Physical signs of hypovolemia | 不是 euvolemic SIADH |
> | BUN 56 mg/dL, creatinine 1.6 mg/dL | Prerenal azotemia pattern | 低灌注支持低血容量 |
> | Serum sodium 122 mEq/L | True hyponatremia | 需要再分 volume status |
> | Urine sodium 8 mEq/L | Kidneys are avidly retaining sodium | 说明丢盐点在肾外 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Intravascular volume depletion**
>
> **Core Concept**：本题是 **extrarenal fluid loss** 导致的 **hypovolemic hyponatremia**。腹泻和呕吐使有效循环血量下降，继而触发 **ADH** 和 **RAAS**。结果是自由水潴留、尿钠降低、血钠下降。最重要的定位线索是 **脱水体征 + urine Na 8 mEq/L**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：低血容量时，身体优先保灌注而不是保渗透压，所以 ADH 会升高，导致水分回收增加，血钠被进一步稀释。
>
> ```text
> diarrhea/vomiting
> -> volume depletion
> -> decreased effective arterial volume
> -> ADH up + RAAS up
> -> water retention + renal sodium avidity
> -> urine sodium low
> -> hypovolemic hyponatremia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Hyponatremia Type | Typical Exam/Labs | 本题是否符合 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Hypovolemic | dry mucosa, azotemia, urine Na <20 if extrarenal | 完全符合 |
> | Euvolemic SIADH | no edema, no dehydration, urine Na usually >20 | 不符合 |
> | Hypervolemic HF | edema, JVD, low effective arterial volume | 不符合 |
> | Intrinsic renal injury | urine Na often >20 | 不符合 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Low sodium with low urine sodium often means the kidneys are doing the right thing."**
> - 尿钠低，不代表没问题；常代表肾脏正在拼命保钠。
> - 先分 volume status，再谈 SIADH。
> - 腹泻患者的 hyponatremia 多半不是肺炎本身造成。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - High plasma osmolality**：高渗性 hyponatremia 常见于 severe hyperglycemia 等；本题 glucose 正常，而且计算渗透压偏低。
>
> **B - Inappropriate antidiuretic hormone secretion**：肺炎确实会让人想到 SIADH，但本题脱水体征和低尿钠更关键。
>
> **D - Left ventricular dysfunction**：会造成 hypervolemic hyponatremia，通常伴 edema 或 JVD，本题没有。
>
> **E - Renal tubular necrosis**：若是 intrinsic renal salt wasting，尿钠通常不会低到 8 mEq/L。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Inappropriate antidiuretic hormone secretion。因为题干先给了 community-acquired pneumonia，但真正决定答案的是 **volume exam + urine sodium**。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定

>

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 7-year-old with sickle cell disease | Functional asplenia risk | 年龄够大，脾功能往往已严重受损 |
> | Fever 39.4 C, BP 70/30, pulse 150 | Septic shock picture | 是 fulminant bacteremia，不是单纯发热 |
> | Sleepiness/lethargy | Toxic appearance | 符合 invasive bacterial infection |
> | Petechiae on both legs | Can occur with DIC in severe sepsis | 不要被它直接带到 meningococcus |
> | No dyspnea, bone pain, or nuchal rigidity | Helps exclude acute chest syndrome, osteomyelitis, meningitis | 题目在帮你聚焦 isolated bacteremia/sepsis |

> **正确答案：E - Streptococcus pneumoniae**

>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：脾脏负责清除被 opsonized 的细菌，尤其是有荚膜的细菌；脾功能没了，pneumococcus 就容易迅速进展为 sepsis。
>
> ```text
> sickle cell disease
> -> repeated splenic infarctions
> -> functional asplenia
> -> poor clearance of encapsulated bacteria
> -> pneumococcal bacteremia/sepsis
> -> fever + shock + lethargy
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Pathogen in SCD | Best Associated Syndrome | 与本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Streptococcus pneumoniae | Bacteremia, sepsis, pneumonia, meningitis | 本题答案 |
> | Salmonella species | Osteomyelitis | 本题无骨痛 |
> | Staphylococcus aureus | Osteomyelitis, septic arthritis | 本题无局部骨关节表现 |
> | E coli | Possible but less common sepsis | 不是首选 |
> | Pseudomonas | Special settings like burns/neutropenia | 不符合 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Sickle spleen shrinks, pneumococcus strikes."**
> - SCD 看到 sepsis，先想 asplenia。
> - Asplenia 看到 bug，先想 encapsulated organisms。
> - 没有骨痛时，别急着选 Salmonella。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Escherichia coli**：能引起 sepsis，但在 SCD 的典型考点里不如 pneumococcus 高产。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q055 Biostats_Master / Higher cutoff lowers sensitivity
> ^Q055
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**raise the cutoff for a positive test** 就意味着更难判阳性，所以 **sensitivity decreases**；同时 healthy people 更不容易被误判阳性，所以 **specificity increases**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Quantitative test with normal range | Cutoff-based interpretation | 这是典型 sensitivity/specificity 移动题 |
> | Upper limit rises from 12 to 13 | Positive threshold moves rightward | 更严格才算 abnormal |
> | Healthy and diseased populations are compared | Overlap creates FP and FN | 说明不能同时把两边都做到完美 |
> | Asks effect on sensitivity and specificity | Pure test-characteristics question | 不要扯 prevalence 或 PPV/NPV |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Sensitivity down; Specificity up**
>
> **Core Concept**：把 cutoff 往右移后，**diseased** 人群里会有更多人掉回 “negative” 一侧，因此 **true positives decrease**，**sensitivity falls**。同时 **healthy** 人群里误判为 positive 的人数减少，**false positives decrease**，因此 **specificity rises**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：cutoff 改变的是“谁被算阳性”，不是疾病真实人数，所以分母通常不变，主要变的是 TP、FP、TN、FN 的分配。
>
> ```text
> higher cutoff
> -> fewer positive tests overall
> -> TP down and FN up
> -> sensitivity down
> -> FP down and TN up
> -> specificity up
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Cutoff Change | True Positives | False Positives | Sensitivity | Specificity |
> |---|---|---|---|---|
> | Higher cutoff | Down | Down | Down | Up |
> | Lower cutoff | Up | Up | Up | Down |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Strict threshold: miss more disease, accuse fewer healthy people."**
> - cutoff 提高 = 更严格。
> - 更严格 = fewer positives。
> - fewer positives simultaneously hurts sensitivity and helps specificity。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Sensitivity up; Specificity up**：几乎不可能靠单次 cutoff 调整让两者同时升高，除非两群完全不重叠。
>
> **B - Sensitivity up; Specificity down**：这是 **lower cutoff** 的效果，不是 higher cutoff。
>
> **C - Sensitivity up; Specificity no change**：只要 cutoff 变动且两群分布重叠，specificity 通常也会受影响。
>
> **E - Sensitivity down; Specificity down**：higher cutoff 不会让 false positives 增加，所以 specificity 不会下降。
>
> **F/G/H/I**：这些 “one side no change” 的选项都忽略了题干所示分布重叠；cutoff 右移会同时改变 diseased 与 healthy 两侧落点。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Sensitivity up; Specificity down。因为很多人只记得 “改 cutoff 会 trade-off”，但方向记反了。
>

> |---|---|---|
> | Pain "coming in waves" | Colicky pain favors mechanical obstruction | 阵发性绞痛比 ileus 更像机械性梗阻 |
> | Distended, tympanic abdomen | Gas-filled obstructed bowel | 腹胀鼓音符合 bowel obstruction |
> | Active bowel sounds | Early SBO can have hyperactive sounds | 早期梗阻常有 peristaltic rush |

> | X-ray with dilated small-bowel loops and air-fluid levels | Classic SBO imaging | 图像与机械性小肠梗阻一致 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Previous abdominal surgery**
>
> **Core Concept**：在高收入国家，**postoperative adhesions** 是 small bowel obstruction 最常见病因。USMLE 常把 “既往腹盆腔手术史” 作为最关键风险因素，优先级通常高于 diabetes、药物副作用或电解质异常。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：机械性 SBO 的核心不是 “肠子不动了”，而是 **某一点被粘连牵拉/卡住，近端肠管继续蠕动并积气积液**。
>
> ```text
> prior abdominal or pelvic surgery
> -> adhesions
> -> focal small bowel narrowing / kinking
> -> proximal dilation with fluid and gas
> -> colicky pain + vomiting + distension
> -> air-fluid levels on x-ray
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Condition | Pain/bowel sounds | Imaging pattern | Best clue |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Small bowel obstruction | Colicky, often hyperactive early | Dilated small bowel, air-fluid levels | Prior surgery, hernia, Crohn disease |
> | Paralytic ileus | Constant discomfort, decreased sounds | Diffuse bowel dilation, less transition pattern | Postop state, electrolyte issue, opioids |
> | Gastroparesis | Nausea, early satiety | No SBO film | Diabetic autonomic neuropathy |
> | Toxic megacolon | Severe colitis, toxic appearance | Markedly dilated colon | C difficile / IBD flare |
> | Peptic ulcer perforation | Sudden severe pain, peritoneal signs | Free air under diaphragm | Not colicky obstruction pattern |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Waves, whistles, and a prior scar = adhesive SBO."**
> - Waves = colicky pain.
> - Whistles = active/hyperactive bowel sounds.
> - Scar = prior surgery, think adhesions first.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Diabetic autonomic neuropathy**：更像 gastroparesis 或 enteropathy，不会急性出现典型 SBO 片子。
>
> **B - Digoxin toxicity**：可有 nausea/vomiting，但不会解释 distension 和 obstipation。
>
> **C - Hypokalemia**：会导致 paralytic ileus，常见 decreased bowel sounds 和持续性不适，不是 colicky pain。
>
> **D - Peptic ulcer disease**：除非 perforation，否则 plain film 不会出现这类多发 dilated small-bowel loops。
>
> **F - Recent antibiotic use**：提示 C difficile colitis；除非 toxic megacolon，否则更偏 colonic process，不是 small bowel obstruction。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Hypokalemia。因为 ileus 和 SBO 都会腹胀呕吐，但 “pain in waves + active bowel sounds + small-bowel air-fluid levels” 更支持 mechanical SBO。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q059 心内 / Doppler pattern of aortic stenosis
> ^Q059
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：apical window 下 **aortic forward flow 在 baseline 下方**；如果下方 trough 比正常更深，说明 through-valve velocity 增高，最符合 **aortic stenosis**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Progressive exertional dyspnea | Symptomatic valvular disease clue | 老年患者慢性活动后气短很像 AS |
> | Apical window Doppler | Orientation matters | 不先定方向就容易把上下 baseline 读反 |
> | Aortic signal below baseline | Flow is away from transducer | apical window 里 forward aortic flow 向远离探头方向 |
> | Deeper aortic trough than normal | Higher velocity through aortic valve | velocity 越高，Doppler 偏移越明显 |
> | Mitral tracing unchanged | Lesion localizes to aortic valve | 排除 mitral stenosis 这类 mitral inflow velocity 问题 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Aortic stenosis**
>
> **Core Concept**：Valvular stenosis 会让血流为了维持前向流量而 **increase velocity across the narrowed valve**。在 apical view，aortic forward flow 记录为 baseline 下方，因此更深的 downward waveform 就代表 **aortic outflow velocity increased**，也就是 aortic stenosis。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题真正考点是 **Doppler direction + velocity interpretation**，不是单纯背 murmurs。
>
> ```text
> apical window
> -> aortic forward flow moves away from transducer
> -> signal plotted below baseline
> -> stenotic valve narrows area
> -> velocity must rise to preserve forward flow
> -> deeper below-baseline aortic waveform
> -> aortic stenosis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Valve lesion | Doppler expectation from apical view | Why not here |
> |---|---|---|
> | Aortic stenosis | Increased below-baseline forward aortic velocity | 本题完全符合 |
> | Aortic regurgitation | Diastolic flow toward transducer, above baseline | 方向不对 |
> | Mitral regurgitation | Systolic backward flow below baseline | 应该出现 mitral-side abnormal signal |
> | Mitral stenosis | Increased mitral inflow above baseline | 本题 mitral tracing unchanged |
> | Mitral valve prolapse | Structural leaflet abnormality, may cause MR | 不是 isolated increased aortic forward velocity |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Apical aortic flow dives down; stenosis makes it dive deeper."**
> - Aortic from apex: down.
> - Mitral from apex: up.
> - Stenosis: faster jet.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Aortic regurgitation**：是 diastolic backflow from aorta to LV，从 apical window 看应朝向探头，应该在 baseline 上方。
>
> **C - Mitral regurgitation**：也会在 baseline 下方，但异常应出现在 mitral-related regurgitant signal，不是 isolated deeper aortic trough。
>
> **D - Mitral stenosis**：会让 mitral inflow velocity 增加，重点应是 baseline 上方的 mitral waveform 改变。
>
> **E - Mitral valve prolapse**：是形态学诊断，若致 MR 才会出现相应 regurgitant flow 图形，本题没有。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Aortic regurgitation。因为同样牵涉 aortic valve，但 regurgitation 的关键是 **flow direction reversal**，不是单纯加深的 forward outflow trough。
>

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：constant epigastric or RUQ pain + fever + leukocytosis，在 obese woman 身上即使 pain radiates to the back，也要先想 **acute cholecystitis**，不是一股脑跳去 pancreatitis。

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 42-year-old woman, BMI 31 | Gallstone risk profile | female + forty + fat 是经典背景 |
> | Constant deep epigastric pain radiating to back | Fits biliary inflammation too | back radiation 不等于只能是 pancreatitis |

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Acute cholecystitis**
>

> **机制说明**：胆囊炎的痛来自 cystic duct obstruction 后胆囊扩张和炎症，不一定非要 textbook 式写成纯 RUQ pain。
>
> ```text
> gallstone obstructs cystic duct
> -> gallbladder distension
> -> inflammation of gallbladder wall
> -> constant RUQ or epigastric pain
> -> fever + leukocytosis + guarding
> -> acute cholecystitis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical clue | Why not this case |
> |---|---|---|
> | Acute cholecystitis | Constant RUQ/epigastric pain, fever, leukocytosis | 本题最匹配 |
> | Acute pancreatitis | Severe epigastric pain to back, elevated lipase | 本题 lipase 正常，酒精线索弱 |
> | Acute viral hepatitis | Marked AST/ALT and bilirubin elevation | 本题仅轻度 ALT 升高 |
> | Cholangitis/CBD obstruction | Jaundice or cholestatic pattern | bilirubin/ALP 正常 |
> | Peptic ulcer disease | Dyspepsia/epigastric pain without inflammatory triad | 发热白细胞不支持 |
> | Pancreatic cancer | Chronic pain, weight loss, jaundice | 急性起病不符 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Back pain can mislead; normal lipase pulls you back to the gallbladder."**
> - Radiation to back 不是 pancreatitis 专利。
> - Fever + WBC 更像 inflamed organ。
> - Normal lipase 是本题纠偏锚点。
>
> ---

>
> **C - Alcoholic pancreatitis**：题干故意给 back radiation 和 vomiting，但 alcohol exposure 很弱，且 lipase 正常。
>
> **D - Common bile duct stricture**：更偏 obstructive jaundice 或 cholangitis，不是这种实验室组合。
>
> **E - Helicobacter pylori infection**：可致 peptic ulcer disease，但 fever 与 leukocytosis 不常见，除非 perforation 且会更毒更腹膜炎。
>
> **F - Medication adverse effect**：某些药会促结石形成，但题干没有典型 culprit drug。
>
> **G - Pancreatic neoplasm**：通常是 chronic course，合并 weight loss 或 hyperbilirubinemia。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Alcoholic pancreatitis。因为 pain radiates to the back 很抓眼，但 normal lipase 和胆囊炎三联征更有决定性。

> ^Q062
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：brief spinning vertigo triggered by rolling over in bed = **benign paroxysmal positional vertigo (BPPV)**，最有效处理是 **head positioning maneuver**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Severe spinning sensation | Identifies true vertigo | 先把 dizziness 分类成 vertigo |
> | Lasts only 30-40 seconds | Favors peripheral positional vertigo | 很短的发作更像 BPPV |
> | Occurs when rolling over in bed or getting up | Classic positional trigger | 这是 BPPV 最标志性的病史 |
> | No focal neurologic clues | Argues against central vertigo | 没有 stroke/cerebellar red flags |
>

>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Head positioning maneuvers**
>
> **Core Concept**：BPPV 由 displaced otoliths 掉进 semicircular canal 引起。最有用的治疗不是吃药，而是 **repositioning maneuvers**，典型就是 **Epley maneuver**，把 otoliths 重新带回去。
>

>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：本题关键不是 vascular risk factors，而是 positional trigger。病因在内耳机械结构，不在血压或血糖。
>
> ```text
> otolith displaced from utricle
> -> enters semicircular canal
> -> head movement shifts otolith
> -> abnormal endolymph stimulation
> -> brief positional vertigo
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Vertigo pattern | Best fit | High-yield next move |
> |---|---|---|
> | Seconds, position-triggered | BPPV | Dix-Hallpike, then Epley |
> | Hours with hearing loss/tinnitus | Meniere disease | Salt restriction, symptom control |
> | Days after viral illness | Vestibular neuritis | Supportive care |
> | Vertigo plus neuro deficits | Central cause | Urgent neuro evaluation |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Roll in bed, room spins, move the crystals."**
> - Bed turning 是 BPPV 经典触发词。
> - 发作短，不是持续整天。
> - 治疗也要 mechanical，不是药物优先。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Head of bed elevation**：不能把 otolith reposition，治标也不稳。
>
> **C - Hypertension treatment**：血压要管，但不是这次 vertigo 的病因。
>
> **D - Tight blood glucose control**：长期有益，但不会立即减少这类 positional vertigo。
>
> **E - Low-dose aspirin**：如果是 cerebrovascular concern 才更相关；本题病史太典型 BPPV 了。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Low-dose aspirin。老年人加 diabetes/HTN 很容易让人跑去想 stroke prevention，但题干核心是 brief positional spinning。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q063 骨科 / humeral midshaft fracture
> ^Q063
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：midshaft humeral fracture 最该联想到 **radial nerve injury**，因为 radial nerve 走在 spiral groove。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | X-ray shows midshaft humerus fracture | Localizes the vulnerable structure | 不是 distal humerus，也不是 elbow injury |
> | Displaced oblique fracture | Increases soft tissue and nerve injury risk | 位移明显，更要想 neurovascular complication |
> | Question asks "high risk of being injured" | Pure anatomy complication question | 这是典型 long-bone fracture anatomy mapping |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Radial nerve**
>
> **Core Concept**：radial nerve runs in the **spiral groove** along the posterior humeral shaft，所以 humeral midshaft fracture 最经典的伴随损伤就是 radial nerve。体检上要查 wrist/finger extension 和 dorsum of hand sensation。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：把神经走行和骨折部位强绑定，这类题就会很快。
>
> ```text
> humeral midshaft fracture
> -> injury near spiral groove
> -> radial nerve vulnerable
> -> weak wrist and finger extension
> -> wrist drop
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Injury site | Structure at risk | Classic deficit |
> |---|---|---|
> | Humeral midshaft | Radial nerve | Wrist drop, dorsal hand sensory loss |
> | Supracondylar humerus | Median nerve, brachial artery | Hand weakness, vascular compromise |
> | Medial epicondyle | Ulnar nerve | Fourth/fifth digit sensory loss, finger ab/adduction weakness |
> | Surgical neck of humerus | Axillary nerve | Deltoid weakness, lateral shoulder numbness |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Midshaft meets spiral groove."**
> - 看见 humeral shaft，先想 radial。
> - Wrist drop 是 radial nerve 最经典的输出。
> - 题目虽问 anatomy，本质也是 fracture neurovascular exam 高频点。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Brachial artery**：更该警惕于 supracondylar region，不是 humeral midshaft 的经典首选。
>
> **B - Median nerve**：和 supracondylar fracture 绑定更紧。
>
> **D - Ulnar nerve**：常在 medial epicondyle 附近受伤，不是 shaft。
>
> **E - Musculocutaneous nerve**：少见损伤，不是此类骨折高频并发症。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Brachial artery。因为它很危险，容易让人高估；但本题问的是 most likely injured structure，位置解剖更支持 radial nerve。

>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：pancreatic head cancer + jaundice + pale stools + severe pruritus + unresectable disease，最有效 palliation 是 **endoscopic bile duct stent**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Mass in head of pancreas | Commonly obstructs the common bile duct | 胰头癌最典型并发症之一就是梗阻性黄疸 |
> | Jaundice, pale stools, bilirubin 15.4 mg/dL | Extrahepatic cholestasis | 这不是单纯肿瘤疼痛，而是胆道梗阻症状 |
> | Severe itching interfering with sleep | Symptom needing palliation | pruritus 是胆汁淤积高频痛点 |
> | Nonresectable metastatic cancer | Goal is palliation, not curative surgery | 题眼在 symptom control |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Endoscopic stent placement**
>
> **Core Concept**：对 unresectable pancreatic cancer 引起的 extrahepatic cholestasis，最有效的 symptom palliation 是 **endoscopic stenting of the CBD**。它比 surgical bypass 更少侵袭，通常先做。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：瘙痒和黄疸真正来源于胆汁出不去，所以最有效处理必须直指 obstruction。
>
> ```text
> pancreatic head adenocarcinoma
> -> common bile duct compression
> -> extrahepatic cholestasis
> -> conjugated hyperbilirubinemia
> -> jaundice + pale stool + pruritus
>
> stent placement
> -> restores bile flow
> -> improves jaundice and pruritus
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Option | When useful | Why not best here |
> |---|---|---|
> | Endoscopic stent | Unresectable malignant CBD obstruction | Best first-line palliation |
> | Surgical bypass | Stent not feasible | More invasive |
> | Antihistamine | Histamine-mediated itch | Cholestatic itch responds poorly |
> | Ursodeoxycholic acid | Some intrahepatic cholestasis states | Does not fix extrahepatic obstruction |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Blocked bile duct? Drain first, medicate later."**
> - Cholestatic pruritus from obstruction needs decompression.
> - Pancreatic head lesion points straight to CBD blockage.
> - Palliative does not mean passive; it means highest symptom relief with lowest burden.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Daily antihistamine therapy**：对 urticaria 一类更合理，对 cholestatic pruritus 不够强。
>
> **B - Daily benzodiazepine therapy**：不解决瘙痒来源，也不解除胆道梗阻。
>
> **D - Surgical bypass procedure**：也能减压，但更 invasive，通常不是 first choice。
>
> **E - Ursodeoxycholic acid**：偏向 intrahepatic cholestasis；本题是 extrahepatic obstruction。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Ursodeoxycholic acid。很多人会记得它和 cholestasis 有关，但这里核心是 mechanical blockage，必须先通路。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q065 pulmonary / malignant pleural effusion
> ^Q065
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：large painless unilateral pleural effusion + weight loss + prior breast cancer，先把 **malignant pleural effusion** 放到最前面。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Progressive dyspnea and dry cough for 3 months | Subacute course | 不是急性感染型时间轴 |
> | Large unilateral left pleural effusion | Favors exudative focal process | 单侧大量积液比 CHF 更像局部病因 |
> | Prior breast cancer plus weight loss | Strong malignancy signal | breast cancer 是 malignant pleural effusion 常见来源 |
> | No JVD, edema, fever, or infiltrate | Makes CHF and parapneumonic effusion less likely | 缺少容量负荷和感染证据 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Malignant pleural effusion**
>
> **Core Concept**：大、单侧、无痛、亚急性 pleural effusion，尤其伴 weight loss 和 cancer history，先想 malignant pleural effusion。确诊要做 thoracentesis。此类积液通常是 **exudative**，常见 low pH、low glucose、high LDH。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：肿瘤侵犯 pleura 或淋巴回流受阻，会让 protein-rich fluid 持续积在胸膜腔内。
>
> ```text
> malignancy involving pleura
> -> increased vascular permeability or lymphatic obstruction
> -> exudative pleural fluid accumulation
> -> unilateral pleural effusion
> -> dyspnea and cough
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Cause of pleural effusion | Typical pattern | Why not this one |
> |---|---|---|
> | Malignancy | Large unilateral exudative effusion, weight loss | Best fit |
> | CHF | Usually bilateral transudates, volume overload | No JVD or edema; unilateral left is less typical |
> | Parapneumonic | Acute, fever, pleuritic pain, infiltrate | None present |
> | PE | Usually small painful effusion | This is large and subacute |
> | Hypothyroidism | Rare cause | Also usually causes weight gain, not loss |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Big one-sided effusion with cancer history is malignant until proved otherwise."**
> - Unilateral beats bilateral in diagnostic weight here.
> - Painless plus weight loss should raise cancer suspicion fast.
> - Thoracentesis confirms, but the diagnosis stem is already pointing hard.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Congestive heart failure**：通常 bilateral，且会有 volume overload signs；就算 unilateral 也更常见于右侧。
>
> **B - Hypothyroidism**：可致胸腔积液但很少见，而且更常伴 weight gain、fatigue、cold intolerance。
>
> **D - Parapneumonic effusion**：一般更急，常有 fever、pleuritic pain、肺部感染影像。
>
> **E - Pulmonary embolism**：可有小的疼痛性 exudative effusion，不符合 large painless 3-month course。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Congestive heart failure。因为题干有 CAD 和 orthopnea，但缺少典型容量负荷表现，而且 effusion 是 large unilateral left-sided。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q066 hematology oncology / sickle cell vaso-occlusive crisis
> ^Q066
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：African American child + acute back/thigh pain + anemia + indirect bilirubin/LDH 升高 + dehydration trigger，优先锁定 **sickle cell vaso-occlusive pain crisis**，本质是 **microvascular occlusion**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 3-year-old African American boy | High-pretest setting for sickle cell disease | 人群背景先把 SCD 拉进前排 |
> | Severe back and thigh pain for 3 hours | Typical musculoskeletal pain crisis distribution | VOC 常是多部位骨骼/肌肉剧痛 |
> | Viral illness last week with poor oral intake | Common crisis trigger | infection + dehydration 都会促发 sickling |
> | Hgb 8.5 g/dL with indirect bilirubin and LDH elevation | Hemolysis pattern | 支持 sickled RBC hemolysis，而不是单纯肌肉炎 |
> | Normal strength, no joint warmth | Less supportive of neurologic or septic joint process | 更像疼痛危象，不像关节或神经根病变 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Microvascular occlusion**
>
> **Core Concept**：sickle cell pain crisis 的直接机制不是 infection、DVT 或 AVN，而是 **sickled red blood cells 堵住 microvasculature**。局部组织缺氧后又进一步促进 sickling，形成恶性循环，所以会出现突发重度骨痛和 hemolysis labs。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：dehydration 和 infection 会增加 HbS polymerization，红细胞变形后卡在小血管里，造成局部 ischemia 和疼痛。
>
> ```text
> viral illness or dehydration
> -> more HbS sickling
> -> microvascular obstruction
> -> tissue hypoxia and acidosis
> -> more sickling and hemolysis
> -> acute severe musculoskeletal pain
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Choice | Typical pattern | Why not this one |
> |---|---|---|
> | Microvascular occlusion | Acute severe multifocal pain with hemolysis | Best fit |
> | Osteomyelitis | Fever, focal bone pain | 本题不发热，且疼痛多部位 |
> | Avascular necrosis | Chronic progressive joint pain | 不是 3 小时内急性爆发 |
> | DVT | Unilateral calf pain/swelling | 年龄和部位都不对 |
> | Viral myositis | Diffuse myalgia, high CK | 不能解释 hemolysis |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Sickle pain crisis = vessels blocked, not bone infected."**
> - Acute pain after dehydration: 先想 vaso-occlusion。
> - Indirect bilirubin + LDH 高: 这是 hemolysis 线索。
> - Multi-site bone pain in child: 比 osteomyelitis 更像 VOC。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Bone infection**：SCD 确实会增加 osteomyelitis 风险，但通常更偏单点、发热、持续性局灶骨痛。
>
> **B - Bone necrosis**：AVN 常见于 femoral head/humeral head，过程更慢，更像 chronic pain，不像本题急性多部位重痛。
>
> **C - Deep venous thrombosis**：典型是单侧下肢肿痛，且儿童少见。
>
> **E - Muscle inflammation**：viral myositis 更常见 diffuse myalgia 和 CK 升高，解释不了 hemolysis。
>
> **F - Nerve root compression**：应有神经根分布性 pain/paresthesia/weakness，本题 strength intact。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Bone infection。因为 leukocytosis 和骨痛会误导，但本题更关键的是 hemolysis 证据和 dehydration trigger。

> ^Q067
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：hemodynamically stable patient + ECG 指向 **atrial fibrillation with rapid ventricular response**，第一步不是 adenosine，也不是 rhythm drug，而是 **AV nodal rate control with metoprolol**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 2 months of fatigue and decreased exercise tolerance | Symptomatic tachyarrhythmia over time | 不是瞬时心悸，而是持续 rate-related symptoms |
> | No chest pain, syncope, or hypotension | Hemodynamically stable | 稳定 AF 先控率，不是立刻电复律 |
> | ECG exhibit | Rhythm identification drives therapy | 本题核心在先认出 AF with RVR |
> | Explanation describes irregularly irregular rhythm and absent P waves | Classic AF finding | 直接把 diagnosis 锁定到 AF |
> | Best initial pharmacotherapy | Asks first-line next step | 不是长期全套管理，只问起始用药 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Metoprolol**
>
> **Core Concept**：对 **stable atrial fibrillation with rapid ventricular response**，初始药物治疗是 **rate control**。常用 AV nodal blockers 包括 beta blocker（metoprolol）或 nondihydropyridine CCB（diltiazem/verapamil）。在这些选项里，metoprolol 最合适。
>
> **说明**：当前渲染页未显示 ECG tracing，本题节律依据 explanation 中明确写出的 irregularly irregular R-R intervals 和 absent P waves 来判定。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：AF 时 atria 电活动杂乱，AV node 不断接收到高频冲动。beta blocker 减慢 AV nodal conduction，降低传到 ventricles 的频率，从而改善症状并减少 tachycardia-mediated injury。
>
> ```text
> atrial fibrillation
> -> chaotic atrial impulses
> -> rapid transmission through AV node
> -> fast ventricular rate
> -> fatigue and exercise intolerance
> -> beta blocker slows AV nodal conduction
> -> rate control
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Drug/approach | Best use | Why not here |
> |---|---|---|
> | Metoprolol | First-line rate control in stable AF | Best fit |
> | Diltiazem/verapamil | Also first-line rate control | Not listed |
> | Adenosine | AVNRT diagnosis/termination | Not effective AF rate control |
> | Lidocaine | Ventricular arrhythmias | Wrong chamber |
> | No therapy | Only if no indication to treat | This patient is symptomatic |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Stable AF: slow the node before anything fancy."**
> - Irregularly irregular + no P waves = AF.
> - Stable patient = rate control first.
> - Beta blocker is the clean first answer if available.
>
> ---

> AV nodal conduction delay
> -> PR gets longer and longer
> -> one atrial impulse fails to conduct
> -> dropped QRS
> -> cycle repeats
> ```

> | Atrial fibrillation | No organized P waves | Irregularly irregular |
> | PACs | Early abnormal P waves | Palpitations, no Wenckebach pattern |

>

> **"Longer, longer, longer, drop."**

> - 然后漏掉一个 QRS。

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Teen with decreased exercise tolerance | Congenital lesion may stay subtle until later childhood | ASD 可拖到青少年才显出体力差 |

> **正确答案：D - Left-to-right intracardiac shunting**
>
> **Core Concept**：最典型的诊断是 **atrial septal defect with left-to-right shunt**。左心房血流持续流向右心房，导致右心容量负荷增加，右室排空时间延长，所以出现 **wide fixed split S2**；LUSB 的 systolic murmur 来自跨 pulmonic valve 的流量增加。

> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：ASD 本身常没有明显杂音，因为跨缺损血流速度不高；真正听到的是右心容量负荷增加后，经过 pulmonic valve 的流量性杂音。
>
> ```text
> atrial septal defect
> -> left-to-right shunt
> -> increased right heart volume
> -> delayed pulmonic valve closure
> -> wide fixed split S2
> -> increased flow across pulmonic valve
> -> midsystolic murmur at LUSB
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Finding | ASD | HCM | Aortic stenosis | Eisenmenger |
> |---|---|---|---|---|
> | Fixed split S2 | Yes | No | No | Late, not typical early clue |
> | Murmur location | LUSB flow murmur | Apex/LLSB | RUSB | Variable |
> | Cyanosis | No | No | No | Yes |
> | Mechanism | Left-to-right shunt | Dynamic LVOT obstruction | Fixed LVOT obstruction | Right-to-left shunt |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Fixed split S2 = think ASD first."**
> - Fixed beats wide alone.
> - LUSB flow murmur supports pulmonic overcirculation.
> - No cyanosis means still left-to-right.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破

>
> **C - Fixed obstruction of the left ventricular outflow tract**：aortic stenosis 杂音应在 RUSB，而不是 LUSB fixed split S2。
>
> **E - Right-to-left intracardiac shunting**：若 ASD 进展到 Eisenmenger 会有 cyanosis/clubbing，本题没有。

> | Hypotension, tachycardia, tachypnea | Shock physiology | 已经符合休克图景 |
> | Warm extremities and bounding pulses | Distributive hyperdynamic shock pattern | 这是 septic shock 的经典体征 |
> | 3 L normal saline already given | Adequate initial fluid resuscitation has been attempted | 题目在考 fluid 后的下一步 |
> | Broad-spectrum antibiotics already given | Source-directed early sepsis care already started | 抗生素也已经补上 |
> | MAP remains 50 mm Hg | Persistent hypotension defines septic shock requiring vasopressor | 关键决策点就在这里 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Norepinephrine infusion**
>
> **Core Concept**：sepsis 患者先做早期 **crystalloid resuscitation + broad-spectrum antibiotics**；如果之后仍然低灌注、MAP 上不去，就是 **septic shock**。一线 vasopressor 是 **norepinephrine**，靠强 **alpha-1 vasoconstriction** 把 systemic vascular resistance 拉回来，同时有适度 **beta-1** 支持 cardiac output。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：septic shock 的核心不是泵不动，而是外周血管扩张、毛细血管渗漏、有效循环量不足。补液后若 MAP 仍低，就要直接提高血管张力。
>
> ```text
> infection
> -> systemic inflammatory response
> -> peripheral vasodilation + capillary leak
> -> low SVR and hypotension
> -> fluids given
> -> MAP still < 60
> -> start norepinephrine
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Option | Typical use | 为什么不是本题最佳下一步 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Norepinephrine | First-line for septic shock | 本题正确答案 |
> | Dobutamine | Cardiogenic shock / low-output state | 本题是 warm shock，不是 cold cardiogenic shock |
> | Hydrocortisone | Refractory shock despite fluids and vasopressors | 不是第一步 |
> | Albumin | Alternative volume expander | 已经完成 initial crystalloids，关键矛盾变成 vasoplegia |
> | PRBC transfusion | Severe anemia or specific thresholds | Hb 9.8 g/dL 不到常规阈值 |
> | 3% saline | Severe symptomatic hyponatremia | Na 134 完全不支持 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Sepsis + 30 mL/kg done + MAP still low = norepi time."**
> - Warm hands、bounding pulses 提示 distributive shock。
> - 先 fluids，再 pressor；顺序要稳。
> - 记住 septic shock first-line pressor 是 norepinephrine。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Dobutamine infusion**：偏向 cardiogenic shock，需要 low-output、cold extremities、可能 elevated JVP；本题正相反。
>
> **B - Intravenous 3% saline**：只用于 severe symptomatic hyponatremia；本题 Na 134 mEq/L。
>
> **C - Intravenous albumin**：不是 septic shock 初始补液后的关键升级步骤，尤其当 MAP 仍明显不足时。
>
> **D - Intravenous hydrocortisone**：通常留给 fluids + vasopressors 后仍难纠正的 refractory shock。
>
> **F - Packed red blood cell transfusion**：Hb 9.8 g/dL 不到常规输血阈值。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Intravenous hydrocortisone。很多人记得“septic shock 可以用 steroids”，但那是 **after vasopressors are not enough**，不是当前 best next step。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：看到 **case-control study**，题目又问 exposure 在 cases vs controls 的比较，直接用 **OR = ad / bc**；本题算出来是 **13.54**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Case-control study | Odds ratio is the standard association measure | 先认题型，先决定公式 |
> | Cases: stroke, Controls: no stroke | Disease status defines rows/columns for setup | 谁是病例谁是对照要摆清楚 |
> | Exposure of interest is CHD | OR compares exposure odds in cases vs controls | 比较的是 CHD 暴露几率 |
> | 13, 287, 3, 897 provided | Full 2 x 2 table is available | 直接代公式即可 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - 13.54**
>
> **Core Concept**：在 case-control study 中，不能直接算 incidence 或 relative risk；要算 **odds ratio**。本题把表整理成：
>
> - `a = 13` (stroke +, CHD +)
> - `b = 3` (stroke -, CHD +)
> - `c = 287` (stroke +, CHD -)
> - `d = 897` (stroke -, CHD -)
>
> 所以：
>
> `OR = (a/c) / (b/d) = ad / bc = (13 x 897) / (287 x 3) = 13.54`
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：OR 本质上是在问“cases 暴露的 odds”相对于“controls 暴露的 odds”高多少。只要 2 x 2 表摆对，`ad/bc` 是最快算法。
>
> ```text
>            stroke+   stroke-
> CHD+          a         b
> CHD-          c         d
>
> odds ratio = (a/c) / (b/d)
>            = ad / bc
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Study design | Best measure | 高频提醒 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Case-control | Odds ratio | 先选 OR，别硬算 risk |
> | Cohort | Relative risk | 能从暴露追到结局 |
> | Cross-sectional | Prevalence / prevalence ratio | 同一时点看暴露和结局 |
> | RCT | Relative risk / ARR / NNT | 有随访就能算风险 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Case-control? OR takes control."**
> - 先辨 study design，再辨公式。
> - `ad/bc` 是最省时的考场算法。
> - OR > 1 代表暴露与疾病正相关。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - 0.003**：方向和数量级都错，通常来自错误地把某个单元格直接当概率。
>
> **B - 0.045**：常见于把 `13/287` 单独拿来当答案，或把比值倒置。
>
> **C - 0.067**：也是错误拼接分母分子后的产物。
>
> **D - 2.063**：说明知道要比较，但算式没有正确应用 `ad/bc`。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - 2.063。因为它看起来像“认真算过”的值，最容易骗到会做一半的人。

> ```text
> multiple myeloma
> -> excess monoclonal light chains
> -> filtration into renal tubules
> -> cast formation + tubular toxicity
> -> renal tubular damage
> -> kidney injury with often bland urinalysis
> ```
>

>

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|

> acute medical trigger (UTI)
> -> delirium
> -> fluctuating confusion and misperception
> -> severe agitation / line pulling / aggression
> -> immediate safety risk
> -> short-term low-dose haloperidol
> ```

> |---|---|---|
> | Shared vignette of violent acute psychosis | Still needs rapid emergency calming | 背景不是普通焦虑，而是可能伤人的 agitation |

>

> **机制说明**：很多 antipsychotics 有 quinidine-like effect，会进一步拉长 QTc，增加 **torsades de pointes** 风险。benzodiazepine 不靠这个通道起效，所以在 prolonged QT 情境下更合适。
>
> ```text
> acute agitation
> + QTc > 500 ms
> -> antipsychotic can worsen QT prolongation
> -> torsades risk rises
> -> choose benzodiazepine
> -> lorazepam for rapid sedation
> ```
>
> ---

> | Agent | Role in agitation | QT issue |
> |---|---|---|
> | Ziprasidone | Effective IM antipsychotic | Strong QT prolongation concern |

> | Lorazepam | Rapid sedation, IM available | No antipsychotic class QT burden |
> | Lithium | Long-term mood stabilizer | Not for immediate sedation |

> **"Agitated and QTc high? Reach for benzo, not more antipsychotic."**

> - `500 ms` 是转向点。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**MI 后第 3 天 + sudden shock + new holosystolic murmur at LSB + RV oxygen step-up**，直接锁定 **ventricular septal rupture causing VSD**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Day 3 after acute anterior MI | Classic timing for septal rupture | 机械并发症时间窗非常高频 |
> | Hypotension and tachycardia | Acute hemodynamic collapse | 说明不是稳定的慢性杂音 |
> | Holosystolic murmur at left sternal border | Classic murmur for post-MI VSD | LSB 是定位关键 |
> | Crackles at lung bases | Pulmonary congestion from sudden left-to-right shunt and low forward output | 合并心衰表现 |
> | RA saturation 65%, RV saturation 79% | Oxygen saturation step-up at ventricular level | 这是导管题最关键证据 |
> | Low cardiac output with elevated right-sided pressures | New shunt is overloading right heart and reducing effective systemic output | 血流动力学完全对上 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Ventricular septal defect**
>
> **Core Concept**：MI 后 3-5 天出现新杂音和休克，要优先想 **papillary muscle rupture** 或 **ventricular septal rupture**。如果 murmur 在 **left sternal border**，且 catheterization 出现 **RA -> RV oxygen saturation step-up**，就说明左心含氧血在 **ventricular level** 分流到右心，答案就是 **post-MI VSD**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：坏死的 interventricular septum 在几天后最脆弱，可能 rupture。左室高压血流冲进右室，造成 acute left-to-right shunt，既让右心压力升高，也让真正送到体循环的 forward output 下降。
>
> ```text
> myocardial infarction
> -> septal necrosis
> -> septal rupture (day 3-5)
> -> LV blood enters RV
> -> RV oxygen saturation step-up
> -> elevated right-sided pressures + shock
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：post-MI mechanical complication
> | Complication | Key clue | Cath or pressure clue |
> |---|---|---|
> | Ventricular septal rupture | LSB holosystolic murmur | RA -> RV O2 step-up |
> | Papillary muscle rupture -> acute MR | Apex holosystolic murmur, pulmonary edema | No RV O2 step-up, high wedge |
> | Free wall rupture | Tamponade / PEA | Pressure equalization |
> | LV pump failure | S3, pulmonary edema | High wedge, no shunt |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Step-up in the RV means hole in the septum."**
> - RA and RV saturation 本应接近。
> - 如果 RV 更高，说明氧合血刚在 RV 入口前被混入。
> - MI 后第 3-5 天，就是 septal rupture 的黄金考点。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Hypovolemic state**：wedge pressure 应该低，而且不会给你新 holosystolic murmur。
>
> **B - Septic shock, hyperdynamic phase**：虽然也会低血压，但 wedge pressure 倾向低，且没有 ventricular level O2 step-up。
>
> **C - Left ventricular pump failure**：可有 crackles 和低输出，但解释不了 RV saturation 升高。
>
> **D - Atrial septal defect**：如果是 ASD，step-up 应该发生在 **SVC/RA** 这一级，不是 RA/RV。
>
> **F - Acute mitral regurgitation**：也是 post-MI 高频机械并发症，但不会让 RV 含氧突然升高。
>
> **G - Right ventricular infarction**：会抬高右心压，但不造成分流。
>
> **H - Pulmonary embolism**：会让右心负荷高，但不造成 RA 到 RV 的 oxygen step-up。
>
> **I - Cardiac tamponade**：看的是 diastolic pressure equalization，这里没有。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Acute mitral regurgitation。因为同样是 MI 后几天、同样会 shock，但这题用 **murmur location + O2 saturation step-up** 把你拉回 VSD。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Sudden shortness of breath and chest discomfort | Acute cardiopulmonary event | 起病很急 |
> | Arterial oxygen saturation 82% | True hypoxemia | 不是单纯焦虑或心因性表现 |
> | Lungs are clear | Points away from left-sided pulmonary edema | 低氧但肺清，先想 PE |
> | RA/RV/PA pressures elevated | Acute right-heart strain | 栓子堵在肺循环前端 |
> | Pulmonary capillary wedge pressure 6 mm Hg | Left atrial pressure is normal | 不是左心衰、不是 acute MR |
> | No right-sided oxygen saturation step-up | Argues against ASD/VSD shunt | 排除 intracardiac shunt |
>

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：H - Pulmonary embolism**
>
> **Core Concept**：PE 会让肺动脉远端阻力突然升高，右心需要 against a blocked pulmonary circuit pumping，于是 **RA/RV/PA pressures 上升**、cardiac output 下降；但因为左心充盈并没有直接升高，**PCWP 仍可正常**。再结合 **hypoxia + clear lungs**，答案最稳。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：栓子阻塞 pulmonary vasculature 后，血流过不去，右心后负荷暴增；右心一旦撑不住，前向血流下降，出现 hypotension。与此同时，左房压力并不高，所以 wedge pressure 不会像 left-sided failure 那样升上去。
>
> ```text
> pulmonary embolus
> -> acute rise in pulmonary vascular resistance
> -> RV pressure overload
> -> elevated RA / RV / PA pressures
> -> low LV preload and low cardiac output
> -> hypotension with normal PCWP
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：右心导管鉴别
> | Diagnosis | Key hemodynamic clue | Why not here |
> |---|---|---|
> | Pulmonary embolism | High PA and right-sided pressures, normal PCWP | 本题正确 |
> | LV pump failure | High PCWP | 本题 wedge 正常 |
> | Acute MR | High PCWP with pulmonary edema | 本题肺清 |
> | VSD | RV oxygen saturation step-up | 本题没有 |
> | ASD | RA oxygen saturation step-up | 本题没有 |
> | RV infarction | Right-sided pressures up, but PA pressure not typically this pattern | 低氧和 PA-PCWP 梯度不如 PE 贴切 |
>

>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Clear lungs, low O2, high right pressure: think clot, not left heart."**
> - 低氧但肺不湿。
> - wedge 正常说明左房没堵。
> - PA 压高却 wedge 不高，卡点在肺血管之间。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Hypovolemic state**：不会把右心和肺动脉压力抬起来。
>
> **B - Septic shock, hyperdynamic phase**：可有低血压，但这种 **PA pressure 高而 wedge 正常** 的阻塞型图谱不对。
>
> **C - Left ventricular pump failure**：若是左心衰，PCWP 应升高，且肺部更可能有湿啰音。
>
> **D - Atrial septal defect**：会先看到 right atrium 氧饱和度升高。
>
> **E - Ventricular septal defect**：会看到 right ventricle 氧饱和度升高。
>
> **F - Acute mitral regurgitation**：同样会拉高 wedge pressure。
>
> **G - Right ventricular infarction**：虽然也会影响右心，但通常不造成这样明显的 pulmonary artery pressure elevation 与 hypoxemia 组合。
>
> **I - Cardiac tamponade**：更强调 diastolic pressure equalization，而非 isolated PA-PCWP gradient。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：G - Right ventricular infarction。因为也能低血压、右心压高，但本题的 **hypoxia + clear lungs + normal PCWP + elevated PA pressure** 更像 PE。
