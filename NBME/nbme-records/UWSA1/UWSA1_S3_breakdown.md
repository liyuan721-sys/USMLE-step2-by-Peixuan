> [!example]- UWSA1 Q081 骨科 / Septic arthritis in RA joint
> ^Q081
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：RA 患者出现 fever + acute monoarthritis + exquisitely tender MCP joint，不要先想 flare，先按 **septic arthritis due to gram-positive organisms** 处理。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Rheumatoid arthritis background | Abnormal joints are high risk for septic arthritis | 既往 RA 让关节本身异常，感染门槛更低 |
> | 1 day of severe monoarticular pain and swelling | Abrupt monoarthritis is more infectious than inflammatory flare | 起病很急，更像 septic arthritis 而不是慢一些的 RA flare |
> | Fever 38.2 C | Supports infectious joint process | 发热把思路推向 infection |
> | Exquisite tenderness with guarding | Typical hot, irritable joint | 触痛重到 guarding，符合 septic joint |
> | MCP involvement with chronic deformities elsewhere | Acute change is superimposed on chronic RA damage | 慢性畸形是背景，当前问题是新发急性感染 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Gram-positive bacterial infection**
>
> **Core Concept**：这题本质是在考 **RA is a risk factor for septic arthritis**。older patient、无 IV drug use 时，最常见致病菌是 **gram-positive cocci, especially Staphylococcus aureus**。真正需要立刻做的是 **arthrocentesis for Gram stain and culture**，因为关节破坏进展很快。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：已有结构异常的关节更容易被血行播散或局部接种后感染。感染进入关节腔后，中性粒细胞和炎症介质迅速升高，短时间内就能破坏 cartilage。
>
> ```text
> rheumatoid arthritis joint
> -> abnormal synovium and damaged joint surface
> -> easier bacterial seeding
> -> acute purulent synovitis
> -> severe pain, swelling, fever, limited ROM
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis / Cause | Typical Pattern | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Gram-positive septic arthritis | Acute monoarthritis + fever in abnormal joint | 本题最符合 |
> | RA flare | Often less abrupt, often polyarticular | 本题太急，而且伴发热 |
> | Gram-negative septic arthritis | More likely with IV drug use, severe immunosuppression, or specific exposures | 题干缺少这些线索 |
> | Mycobacterial arthritis | Indolent, persistent monoarthritis | 本题不是慢性拖着走 |
> | Osteoarthritis | Chronic mechanical pain, unusual at MCP | 解释不了急性炎症表现 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"RA joint + red-hot one joint = tap it, do not label it flare."**
> - RA 会增加 septic arthritis 风险。
> - Acute monoarthritis 比 polyarthritis 更要先排 infection。
> - 老年且无特殊暴露时，先想 gram-positive cocci。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Flare-up of rheumatoid arthritis**：RA flare 可以像 infection，但通常没有这么 abrupt，而且更常 polyarticular。
>
> **B - Gram-negative bacterial infection**：能导致 septic arthritis，但更常见于 IV drug use、严重免疫抑制或特定高风险人群。
>
> **D - Mycobacterial infection**：常是 indolent、culture-negative 的慢性 monoarthritis，不像这题的 1-day crash。
>
> **E - Osteoarthritis**：MCP 不是典型 OA 部位，也不该出现 fever 和强烈炎症体征。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Flare-up of rheumatoid arthritis。真正题眼是 **acute febrile monoarthritis**，不是单纯“RA 患者关节痛”。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q082 儿科 / Meckel diverticulum bleeding
> ^Q082
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：<2 岁、well-appearing、**painless maroon stools**、无腹痛无 fussiness，优先锁定 **Meckel diverticulum with ectopic gastric mucosa**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 18-month-old child | Classic age for symptomatic Meckel diverticulum | 小于 2 岁是高频年龄段 |
> | Large maroon stools | Lower GI bleeding pattern | 直接指向 painless intestinal bleeding |
> | No vomiting, no fussiness | Makes intussusception less likely | 没有 colicky pain 或 irritability |
> | Eating normally, normal vitals | Child is well appearing | 不是 toxic child，也不像 severe colitis |
> | Soft nontender abdomen, no fissure | Rules against common anorectal causes | 没有 anal source，也没有 peritoneal signs |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Ectopic gastric mucosa**
>
> **Core Concept**：Meckel diverticulum 是 persistent vitelline duct remnant。真正造成出血的不是“憩室本身”，而是其中的 **ectopic gastric mucosa** 分泌 acid，腐蚀邻近 ileal mucosa，导致 painless bleeding。诊断常用 **technetium-99m pertechnetate scan (Meckel scan)**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：异位胃黏膜会像胃一样分泌 hydrochloric acid，但回肠并没有相同的抗酸保护，因此周围小肠黏膜会 ulcerate and bleed。
>
> ```text
> persistent vitelline duct remnant
> -> Meckel diverticulum
> -> ectopic gastric mucosa
> -> acid secretion
> -> adjacent ileal ulceration
> -> painless maroon or red stool
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Cause of bleeding | Typical clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Meckel diverticulum | Age <2, painless bleeding, well appearing | 本题完全吻合 |
> | Intussusception | Colicky pain, fussiness, currant jelly stool | 本题无腹痛无烦躁 |
> | IgA vasculitis | Purpura, arthralgia, abdominal pain | 本题缺少 systemic vasculitic clues |
> | IBD | Chronic abdominal pain, diarrhea, weight loss | 年龄和病程都不对 |
> | Juvenile polyp | Painless bleeding but more often school-aged | 年龄偏大才更典型 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Quiet toddler, bloody diaper: think Meckel before intussusception."**
> - Meckel 出血常 painless。
> - Intussusception 常常哭闹、阵发腹痛。
> - 这题在考病因层面：是 **ectopic gastric mucosa**。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Adenomatous colon polyp**：更常见于成人，不是 toddler 的首选解释。
>
> **C - IgA vasculitis**：应有 palpable purpura、arthralgia 或 abdominal pain。
>
> **D - Inflammatory bowel disease**：非常早发虽可能，但通常伴 chronic symptoms、体重问题或腹痛。
>
> **E - Telescoping bowel segments**：就是 intussusception，典型是 colicky pain + currant jelly stools + fussiness。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Telescoping bowel segments。很多人看到“血便儿童”就冲去选 intussusception，但本题故意把腹痛和烦躁都拿掉了。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q083 hematology oncology / DIC with MAHA
> ^Q083
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：metastatic cancer + thrombocytopenia + low fibrinogen + prolonged INR + hemolysis labs，上来就要把 **DIC causing microangiopathic hemolytic anemia** 串起来。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Metastatic breast cancer | Malignancy is a classic trigger for DIC | 恶性肿瘤本身就是 DIC 高危背景 |
> | Platelets 42,000/mm3 + INR 3.2 + fibrinogen 125 mg/dL | Consumptive coagulopathy pattern | 这组就是 DIC 的核心实验室组合 |
> | Reticulocyte count 7.6%, bilirubin up, LDH 1200 U/L | Hemolysis is occurring | 说明不是单纯产量不足，而是 red cell destruction |
> | Hb 6.3 g/dL with normal MCV | Acute severe normocytic anemia | 更像 hemolysis / MAHA |
> | CBC normal one month ago | Abrupt process, not chronic marrow failure | 排除慢性 anemia of chronic disease 的思路 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Microangiopathic hemolytic anemia**
>
> **Core Concept**：DIC 会在微循环中形成 fibrin-rich thrombi，红细胞被剪碎后出现 **MAHA**。所以这题既要看到 **coagulopathy**，也要读出 **hemolysis**。真正问的是“anemia 原因”，不是“基础病是什么”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：malignancy 激活凝血级联后，体内到处铺 fibrin。RBC 经过这些 fibrin strands 时被机械性切割，形成 schistocyte pattern 的 nonimmune hemolysis。
>
> ```text
> metastatic malignancy
> -> systemic coagulation activation
> -> disseminated fibrin deposition
> -> consumption of platelets and fibrinogen
> -> RBC fragmentation in microvasculature
> -> MAHA with high LDH, indirect bilirubin, reticulocytosis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Cause of anemia | Typical lab pattern | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | MAHA from DIC | Hemolysis labs + thrombocytopenia + coagulopathy | 本题最符合 |
> | Bone marrow infiltration | Cytopenias from poor production | 不解释 reticulocytosis 和强烈 hemolysis |
> | Anemia of chronic disease | Mild-moderate anemia, low retic, chronic course | 本题起病太快 |
> | Autoimmune hemolysis | Hemolysis without low fibrinogen or prolonged INR | 缺少 DIC coagulation pattern |
> | Iron deficiency | Microcytosis, chronic blood loss | MCV 正常且伴 DIC |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Cancer + bleeding labs + breaking RBCs = DIC shredding red cells."**
> - 先认出 DIC。
> - 再问 anemia 为什么重，答案是 MAHA。
> - DIC 的 RBC 是被 fibrin 网切碎，不是 immune attack。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Bone marrow infiltration**：会让人想到 metastatic cancer，但通常是 production failure，不会给出这么明显的 hemolysis signature。
>
> **B - Anemia of chronic disease**：normocytic 会迷惑人，但 retic 不该高，且一个月前 CBC 正常不支持慢性过程。
>
> **C - Autoimmune hemolysis**：能解释黄疸和 LDH，但不能解释 low fibrinogen 和 INR 3.2。
>
> **E - Iron deficiency**：应偏 microcytic，也解释不了 consumptive coagulopathy。
>
> **F - Impaired folic acid metabolism**：应是 megaloblastic anemia，不会出现 DIC pattern。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Bone marrow infiltration。因为题干故意给 metastatic cancer 骨转移，但关键实验室 actually 在喊 **DIC + MAHA**。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q084 心内 / Rheumatic mitral stenosis murmur
> ^Q084
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：rheumatic history + commissural fusion + atrial fibrillation = **mitral stenosis**；症状已经明显时，最该听到的是 **mid-diastolic murmur at the apex**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Prior joint pains and heart murmur in adolescence | Suggests prior rheumatic fever | 提示 rheumatic valve disease 背景 |
> | Thickened mitral leaflets with commissural fusion | Classic echo description of rheumatic MS | 这是 mitral stenosis 的影像题眼 |
> | Progressive dyspnea and cough | Symptomatic elevated left atrial pressure | 说明狭窄已经影响肺循环 |
> | Irregularly irregular pulse, atrial fibrillation | Common complication of left atrial enlargement in MS | AF 常见于进展期 MS |
> | Asking for physical exam finding | Need to map severity to murmur timing | 在考杂音时相和听诊部位 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Mid-diastolic murmur at the cardiac apex**
>
> **Core Concept**：Mitral stenosis 的经典听诊是 **loud S1 + opening snap + low-pitched diastolic rumble at the apex**。随着 stenosis 变重，杂音在舒张期出现得更早，所以 symptomatic patient 会更像 **mid-diastolic murmur**。而 **presystolic accentuation** 需要 atrial contraction，在 AF 中会消失。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：mitral valve opening 受限后，左房到左室的血流在舒张期持续受阻，形成低频 rumble。狭窄越重，压力梯度存在得越早越久，所以 murmur 越早出现。
>
> ```text
> rheumatic scarring
> -> commissural fusion
> -> narrowed mitral valve orifice
> -> diastolic flow across stenotic valve
> -> low-pitched apical diastolic rumble
> -> earlier in diastole as stenosis worsens
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Auscultation finding | Best match | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Mid-diastolic apical murmur | Progressive mitral stenosis | 本题答案 |
> | Presystolic accentuation | MS with sinus rhythm | 本题有 AF，所以消失 |
> | Early diastolic LSB murmur | Aortic regurgitation | 听诊位置和疾病都不对 |
> | Late systolic apical murmur | Mitral valve prolapse | 是 systolic，不是 diastolic |
> | S4 | Stiff ventricle with organized atrial kick | AF 下不会有 S4 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"MS murmurs move earlier as stenosis worsens, but AF erases presystole."**
> - Apex 听低频 diastolic rumble。
> - Opening snap 支持 rheumatic MS。
> - AF 出现时，不要再选 presystolic accentuation。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Early diastolic murmur at the left sternal border**：这是 aortic regurgitation 的站位，不是 mitral stenosis。
>
> **B - Fourth heart sound at the cardiac apex**：S4 需要 atrial contraction；AF 已经把这个条件拿掉。
>
> **C - Late systolic murmur at the cardiac apex**：更像 mitral valve prolapse。
>
> **E - Presystolic murmur at the cardiac apex**：MS 在 sinus rhythm 时会有 presystolic accentuation，但 AF 时不成立。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Presystolic murmur at the cardiac apex。知道 MS 的人容易被这个术语吸走，但题干专门给了 **atrial fibrillation** 来否掉它。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q085 GYN / Screening mammogram interval
> ^Q085
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：average-risk woman age 68，**last mammogram 2 years ago**，现在最该补的是 **screening mammogram**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 68-year-old healthy woman | Falls in the 50-74 screening benefit window | 正在乳腺筛查获益年龄段内 |
> | Mammogram normal 2 years ago | Mammography is due now | 题眼就是筛查间隔 |
> | Colonoscopy normal 5 years ago | Colonoscopy interval is 10 years for average risk | 结肠镜还没到期 |
> | Pap with HPV cotest normal 3 years ago and age >65 | Cervical screening can stop after adequate prior screening | Pap 不是现在最需要的 |
> | Prior 10-year ASCVD risk 6.4% with good lipids | Lipid check can be spaced out | 不是眼下最优先 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Mammogram**
>
> **Core Concept**：对 **average-risk women age 50-74**，screening mammography 至少每 **2 years** 一次。她刚好 68 岁，且上次 mammogram 是 2 年前，所以此刻最应该做 mammogram。其余选项要么间隔未到，要么已可停止，要么没有常规推荐。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：预防医学题的关键不是死背所有项目，而是先看 **年龄窗 + 风险层级 + 上次筛查时间**。这三件事一拼，当前最到期的就是 breast cancer screening。
>
> ```text
> average-risk woman age 68
> + last mammogram 2 years ago
> -> within USPSTF benefit window
> -> repeat mammography now
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Screening test | Average-risk interval / rule | 本题判断 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Mammogram | Every 2 years age 50-74 | 现在该做 |
> | Colonoscopy | Every 10 years | 5 years ago normal, not due |
> | Pap / HPV | Stop after 65 if adequately screened | 已可停止 |
> | Lipid panel | Often every 3-5 years if low risk and no statin indication | 可做但不是最该现在做 |
> | TSH | No routine screening recommendation in asymptomatic adults | 不选 |
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q086 Biostats_Master / Type II error and effect size
> ^Q086
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：题干已经告诉你 `power = 80%`、`alpha = 0.05`、`no loss to follow-up`，最后却 `p = 0.18`；如果还想用 **type II error** 解释，最合理就是 **真实 effect size 比预期更小**，所以研究没抓到差异。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 80% power to detect `>=10%` difference | Power calculation was built around a prespecified effect size | 研究并不是“任何差异都能抓到”，只保证对足够大的差异有较高检出率 |
> | `p = 0.18` | Negative study result | 没有统计学显著性，不代表一定“完全没差异” |
> | no participants lost to follow-up | Final sample size stayed intact | 不能把锅甩给 sample size shrinkage |
> | randomized assignment | Reduces selection bias | selection bias 不是这里 type II error 的核心 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - The real difference between groups was less than expected at the beginning of the study**
>
> **Core Concept**：Type II error = **明明真有差异，却没检出来**。它常见于研究对“可检测差异”的预期过大。若真实差异小于最初 power calculation 假设的 `10%`，即使样本量没掉、alpha 也正常，研究仍可能因为 **power 不够覆盖较小 effect** 而得出“不显著”结果。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：power 不是固定万能值，而是绑定在 sample size、variability、effect size、significance level 这四个变量上。你把 effect size 设大了，真实世界差异若更小，就更难检出。
>
> ```text
> smaller true effect size
> -> groups look more similar
> -> harder to separate distributions
> -> lower effective power for that true difference
> -> higher risk of type II error
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Factor | 对 power 的影响 | USMLE 常见考法 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Larger sample size | increase power | 失访太多会降低 power |
> | Larger effect size | increase power | 真差异比预期小时更易 type II error |
> | Lower variability | increase power | 数据更集中更容易看出差异 |
> | Higher alpha | increase power | alpha 太低才会更难显著 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Study was built to catch a whale, but the true signal was only a fish."**
> - 设计时按 `>=10%` 大差异来算 power。
> - 真差异如果更小，统计网眼就太大。
> - 没抓到，不等于水里什么都没有。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - selection bias**：randomization 本来就是在降 selection bias，而且 selection bias 不是解释 type II error 的标准变量。
>
> **B - reduced sample size at study end**：题干明说 no loss to follow-up，这个选项被直接排掉。
>
> **D - significance level was too high**：方向反了。`alpha` 越高越容易显著、power 越高；真正会增加 type II error 的是 `alpha` 设得过低。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B。很多人一看到 “power 不够” 就条件反射选 sample size，但这题特意给了 `no loss to follow-up` 来封死这条路。

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |

> -> reduced capillary perfusion
> -> early tissue ischemia
> -> nonblanching erythema

> ```

> | Suspected infection | Consider appropriate antimicrobial approach | 不是先上 hydrogen peroxide |

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q088 pulmonary / Hemothorax after thoracentesis
> ^Q088
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：thoracentesis 后短时间内又出现 **dullness + absent breath sounds + shock**，先想 procedure complication，最像 **hemothorax**；血丢了，hemodynamics 的直接机制就是 **decreased left ventricular preload**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | thoracentesis 2 hours ago | Strong temporal relation to iatrogenic complication | 时间点非常关键 |
> | drained 2 liters, then hypotension/tachycardia | Suggests acute post-procedure deterioration | 不是原病自然缓慢进展 |
> | left-sided dullness + absent breath sounds again | Fluid has rapidly reaccumulated | 两小时内原 effusion 不该这么快自己回来 |
> | dizziness, diaphoresis, hypoxemia | Shock physiology plus respiratory compromise | 兼有失血和通气受限表现 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Decreased left ventricular preload**
>
> **Core Concept**：Thoracentesis 可损伤 **intercostal artery**，导致 **hemothorax**。血液积进 pleural space 一方面压肺造成 hypoxia，另一方面让有效循环血量下降，venous return 减少，所以左心室 preload 下降，表现为 hypotension 和 compensatory tachycardia。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题问的不是“并发症名称”本身，而是并发症引发低血压的血流动力学机制。
>
> ```text
> thoracentesis
> -> intercostal vessel injury
> -> hemothorax
> -> intravascular blood loss
> -> reduced venous return
> -> decreased LV preload
> -> hypotension + tachycardia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Option | 如果成立应有什么线索 | 为什么不如答案 |
> |---|---|---|
> | LV outflow obstruction | murmur, structural cardiac issue | 与 thoracentesis 时间关系差 |
> | Decreased LV preload | bleeding/volume loss | 完全符合 hemothorax shock |
> | Pulmonary artery obstruction | PE pattern | 解释不了单侧迅速积液体征 |
> | Increased LVEDP | heart failure/congestion | 方向与失血性低灌注不符 |
> | Decreased afterload | vasodilation physiology | 通常不会造成这种 unilateral chest finding |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"After a tap, shock plus dull chest means blood where fluid used to be."**
> - procedure 后立刻恶化，先想 iatrogenic injury。
> - dullness 回来了，不要先想 effusion 自己长回来。
> - 出血性并发症的 hemodynamic answer 常是 preload down。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Left ventricular outflow obstruction**：这类问题不会被一次 thoracentesis 突然诱发，也解释不了单侧胸部重新变 dull。
>
> **C - Obstruction of pulmonary artery blood flow**：PE 能低氧低压，但不该有这么明确的 unilateral pleural blood/fluid 体征。
>
> **D - Increased left ventricular end-diastolic pressure**：更像 pump failure / congestion，不像失血后的 preload depletion。
>
> **E - Decreased left ventricular afterload**：afterload 降低通常让心更容易 eject，不是这题低压主因。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C。因为它也能给你 hypoxia + hypotension，但胸部查体把方向拉回 pleural complication，而不是 pulmonary vascular obstruction。

> **提示**：platinum-based chemotherapy 后出现 **bilateral muffled hearing + high-pitched tinnitus + normal otoscopy**，最该想的是 **ototoxic medication side effect**，本质是 cochlear hair cell neurotoxicity。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | laryngeal cancer treated with platinum chemo | Classic exposure for ototoxicity | cisplatin 比 carboplatin 更常见，但两者都可致耳毒性 |

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Medication side effect**
>
> **Core Concept**：Platinum agents can cause **irreversible bilateral high-frequency sensorineural hearing loss** with tinnitus. Mechanistically, they damage **outer hair cells in the organ of Corti**, leading to cochlear neurotoxicity. 这类听力下降常是 dose-dependent，因此临床上会监测 hearing，尤其在儿童中更重视。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题虽然表面在问“hearing loss mechanism”，但本质是在考 chemo toxicity 的识别，而不是耳鼻喉局部感染或转移。
>
> ```text
> platinum chemotherapy
> -> cochlear outer hair cell injury
> -> sensorineural hearing loss
> -> bilateral high-frequency deficit
> -> tinnitus
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Cause | Hearing pattern | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Platinum ototoxicity | bilateral high-frequency sensorineural loss, tinnitus | 本题完全符合 |

> **"Platinum hits the ear hair, not the eardrum."**
> - tinnitus + normal otoscopy = think sensorineural.

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|

> **正确答案：F - Pulmonary contusion**
>

>
> ```text
> blunt thoracic trauma
> -> kinetic energy to chest wall
> -> alveolar capillary injury
> -> hemorrhage and edema
> -> impaired oxygen diffusion
> -> delayed hypoxia and patchy infiltrate
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Imaging/Timing | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Pulmonary contusion | delayed, irregular nonlobular infiltrate near injury | 本题完全符合 |
> | Hemothorax | pleural fluid, costophrenic blunting, often more acute | 本题更像肺实质浸润，不是胸膜腔积血 |
> | Aspiration pneumonitis | usually rapid after aspiration, often diffuse | 题干无 aspiration event |
> | Diaphragmatic rupture | bowel in chest or lost diaphragmatic contour | 影像不支持 |
> | Tracheobronchial rupture | pneumothorax/pneumomediastinum | 本题没有 air leak sign |

>

> **"Bruised chest, bruised lung."**
> - 伤后立刻片子可正常，数小时后才白起来。
> - `nonlobular` 是肺挫伤高频词。
> - 位置常紧贴受力一侧。

>
> **B - Aspiration pneumonitis**：需要明确 aspiration 背景，发作通常更快，也常更弥漫。
>
> **C - Diaphragmatic rupture**：应有 diaphragmatic contour 改变或腹腔脏器入胸。
>
> **D - Hemothorax**：会有 pleural fluid pattern 和 costophrenic angle blunting，不是这种肺内不规则浸润。

> -> easy bruising

> -> acne and irregular menses
> ```

>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Can explain | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Cushing syndrome | weakness, obesity, HTN, hyperglycemia, bruising, acne | 本题最完整匹配 |
> | Hypothyroidism | weight gain and myopathy possible | 常有 cold intolerance、delayed reflexes、CK 升高 |

> | Primary hyperaldosteronism | HTN, hypokalemia weakness | 不解释 hyperglycemia、bruising、androgen excess |
> | Statin myopathy | muscle symptoms | 通常 painful，CK 升高 |
>

>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Bruises + buffalo physiology beats simple obesity."**
> - 看到 proximal weakness，不要只想神经肌接头病。
> - 看到 bruising，要想到 cortisol 的 catabolic effect。
> - 看到 acne/irregular menses，说明 adrenal androgen 也被带起来了。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Hypothyroidism**：也会体重增加和乏力，但通常有 cold intolerance、反射异常，而且 hypothyroid myopathy 常伴 CK 升高。
>

>
> **D - Polycystic ovary syndrome**：能解释 obesity、acne、月经不规则，却解释不了 proximal muscle weakness 和 easy bruising。
>
> **E - Primary hyperaldosteronism**：可有 HTN 与低钾性无力，但没有 glucocorticoid excess 的皮肤和代谢特征。
>
> **F - Statin myopathy**：通常更痛，且 CK 会高，不符合本题 68 U/L。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Polycystic ovary syndrome。因为 obesity + acne + irregular menses 很像，但只要加上 bruising 和 proximal weakness，就应该跳出单纯 PCOS 框架。

> ^Q095
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：acute myocardial ischemia 问“**quickest way to decrease pain**”，不要先想 aspirin 或 coronary dilation，首选机制是 **nitrate-induced venodilation**，靠降 preload 降低 myocardial oxygen demand。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | retrosternal pain radiating to left arm | Classic ischemic chest pain | ACS 典型疼痛分布 |
> | nausea, diaphoresis, dyspnea | Autonomic ischemic symptoms | 强化 ongoing ischemia |
> | multiple CAD risk factors | High pretest probability | diabetes、smoking、HTN、HLD 全齐 |
> | ST depression in V2-V6 | Subendocardial ischemia / NSTEMI-UA pattern | 说明确实有 active myocardial ischemia |
> | asks quickest way to reduce pain | Mechanism-of-action question | 考 nitrate 的主要急性止痛机制 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Venous dilation**
>
> **Core Concept**：Organic nitrates 在 acute myocardial ischemia 中最先缓解疼痛，主要靠 **venodilation**。静脉扩张会减少 venous return，降低 **preload、LV volume、wall stress**，从而快速减少 **myocardial oxygen demand**。这比讨论 platelet inhibition 或轻度 coronary dilation 更直接对应“pain relief”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：很多人知道 nitrates 能“扩血管”，但考试更爱追问它到底先扩哪一端，以及为什么这会立刻止痛。
>
> ```text
> nitrate
> -> venodilation
> -> decreased preload
> -> lower LV wall stress
> -> decreased myocardial oxygen demand
> -> less ischemic chest pain
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Mechanism | Acute role | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Venodilation | fastest nitrate antianginal effect | 本题正确答案 |
> | Coronary artery dilation | minor contributor in typical ACS pain relief | 有作用，但不是主机制 |
> | Platelet inhibition | improves ACS outcomes | 不能立刻止痛 |
> | Afterload reduction | can help demand | 不是本题问的 quickest primary mechanism |
> | Higher diastolic pressure | increases workload | 会更糟 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Nitrates treat pain by unloading the heart before feeding the artery."**
> - 先减 preload，不是先开冠脉。
> - wall stress 降下来，耗氧量就先掉。
> - 问 acute pain relief 时，选 hemodynamics 最直接的那个。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Decrease in systolic blood pressure**：降低 afterload 的确可减耗氧，但不是 nitrate 最快、最主要的止痛机制。
>
> **B - Increase in diastolic blood pressure**：会增加 afterload，让 myocardium 更费力收缩，方向相反。
>
> **D - Coronary artery dilation**：nitrates 也可扩张冠脉，但 acute antianginal 的核心仍是 venodilation 降 preload。
>
> **E - Platelet aggregation inhibition**：aspirin 对 ACS 非常重要，但不会像 venodilation 那样立刻降低 ischemic pain。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Coronary artery dilation。因为大家容易把 nitrate 简化成“扩冠脉药”，但考试最常考的是它更偏静脉端的 hemodynamic effect。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q096 儿科 / Malrotation with volvulus
> ^Q096
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：`3-week-old + bilious vomiting + abdominal distension + corkscrew duodenum`，直接锁定 **intestinal malrotation with midgut volvulus**；被问 underlying cause 时，回到 **arrest of normal embryonic gut rotation**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 3-week-old infant | Fits classic age for malrotation with volvulus | 新生儿到 4 周内出现胆汁性呕吐要优先想 congenital obstruction |
> | sudden recurrent forceful bilious vomiting | Distal to ampulla obstruction | 有胆汁就不是 pyloric stenosis 那条线 |
> | abdominal distension and feeding refusal | Intestinal obstruction pattern | 说明不是单纯喂养问题 |
> | upper GI with corkscrew-shaped duodenum | Signature imaging finding | 这是 midgut volvulus 的高频影像题眼 |
> | small umbilical hernia is nontender | Incidental finding, not the driver | 脐疝存在但不红不痛，不支持 incarceration |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Arrest of normal embryonic gut rotation**
>
> **Core Concept**：正常 midgut 在胚胎期会 **270-degree counterclockwise rotation** 并固定到 posterior abdominal wall。若 rotation 中断，形成 **malrotation**，进而留下 **narrow mesenteric base**，让小肠容易绕 **SMA** 扭转，出现 **midgut volvulus** 和胆汁性呕吐。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是在背影像，而是在考你能否把影像表现反推回胚胎学根因。
>
> ```text
> arrested gut rotation
> -> abnormal bowel positioning
> -> narrow mesenteric base
> -> bowel twists around SMA
> -> midgut volvulus
> -> bilious vomiting and obstruction
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical Age / Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Malrotation with volvulus | any neonatal age, bilious emesis, corkscrew duodenum | 本题完全吻合 |
> | Duodenal atresia | first 1-2 days of life, double-bubble sign | 年龄更早，影像不同 |
> | Pyloric stenosis | 3-5 weeks, nonbilious projectile vomiting | 年龄可像，但呕吐应非胆汁性 |
> | Hirschsprung disease | delayed meconium passage, rectal exam explosive stool | 题干无排胎便异常 |
> | Incarcerated umbilical hernia | tender erythematous hernia with trapping | 本题脐疝无压痛发红 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Bilious baby + corkscrew = volvulus until proven otherwise."**
> - 看到 bilious vomiting，先把 gastric outlet causes 往后放。
> - `corkscrew` 基本就是在喊 midgut volvulus。
> - 被问根因时，答案常落在 embryology: failed normal rotation。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Failed recanalization of the duodenal lumen**：这是 duodenal atresia 的机制，通常出生后前 1-2 天就发作，影像是 `double bubble`。
>
> **C - Hypertrophic obstruction of the gastric outlet**：pyloric stenosis 年龄可接近，但关键错误点是它导致 **nonbilious** projectile vomiting。
>
> **D - Incarcerated umbilical hernia**：若真卡住，疝块应 tender 或 erythematous；本题脐疝无压痛，更像 incidental finding。
>
> **E - Wide-based mesentery affixed to small bowel**：正常固定反而会减少扭转风险；volvulus 的前提是 **narrow-based, abnormally mobile mesentery**。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Hypertrophic obstruction of the gastric outlet。因为题干有 `3-week-old` 和 forceful vomiting，但只要看到 **bilious** 就必须转向 distal obstruction。

>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题核心不是“尿酸 7.3 要不要治”，而是“什么情况下先改生活方式，什么情况下再上 urate-lowering therapy”。
>
> ```text
> first uncomplicated gout attack
> -> assess chronic urate-lowering indications
> -> none present
> -> choose lifestyle modification
> -> stop beer to lower recurrence risk
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Intervention | When it fits | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Alcohol cessation | first-line after first uncomplicated attack | 本题正确 |
> | Colchicine prophylaxis | short-term when starting urate-lowering therapy | 本题还没到那一步 |
> | Febuxostat / allopurinol | recurrent attacks, tophi, stones, CKD | 本题缺这些指征 |

> | Thiazide / niacin | can worsen hyperuricemia | 应避免 |

> **"First gout? Fix the trigger before lifelong pills."**
> - 一次发作后先找可逆诱因。
> - beer 比很多人想象中更像考试题眼。
> - chronic urate-lowering therapy 需要额外适应证，不看单次发作就启动。
>
> ---
>

>
> **E - Low-dose thiazide**：能升高 uric acid，可能诱发下一次 gout attack。
>
> **F - Niacin**：同样会加重 hyperuricemia。
>
> **G - Probenecid**：是 uricosuric 药，不是 first uncomplicated attack 的第一步。
>

> substantia nigra neuron loss

> -> basal ganglia motor dysfunction
> -> bradykinesia and rigidity

> ```

> | Cause of falls | Typical clue | 与本题区别 |

> | Peripheral sensory loss | wide-based gait, proprioceptive deficits | 本题是 parkinsonian small-step gait |
> | Alzheimer disease | cortical cognitive decline without classic tremor/rigidity | 本题有明确 extrapyramidal signs |
> | Seizure | episodic altered awareness or postictal state | 本题不符 |

> **"Small steps, stiff wrists, resting tremor: the dopamine system is failing."**

> - dementia 加 hallucinations 让你更接近 Lewy body spectrum。

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Tingling in toes followed by gait difficulty | Distal paresthesia can precede weakness in GBS | 先麻后无力，很典型 |
> | Decreased lower-extremity strength and diminished reflexes | Core neurologic pattern of GBS | 对称无力加反射减弱是主轴 |
> | No sensory loss | Relative sensory preservation fits GBS | 感觉可有轻度异常，但不会像脊髓病那样明确缺失 |
> | Bloody diarrheal illness | Suggests Campylobacter jejuni trigger | 最常考的前驱感染 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Normal leukocyte count**
>
> **Core Concept**：GBS 的 LP 不是看细胞升高，而是看 **albuminocytologic dissociation**。翻译成题目语言，就是 **CSF protein elevated, leukocyte count normal**。本题只给一个选项，就选正常白细胞数。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：前驱感染后，异常免疫反应攻击 peripheral nerve myelin 或 axon，导致神经根通透性改变，蛋白漏入 CSF，但并不产生明显炎性 pleocytosis。
>
> ```text
> recent infection
> -> autoimmune attack on peripheral nerves
> -> demyelination / nerve root inflammation
> -> weakness + hyporeflexia
> -> CSF protein rises
> -> CSF leukocyte count stays normal
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | CSF finding | 更像什么 | 与本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|
> | High protein + normal cells | Guillain-Barre syndrome | 本题标准答案 |
> | Low glucose | bacterial or fungal meningitis | 不符合周围神经病模式 |
> | Xanthochromia | subarachnoid hemorrhage | 临床图像完全不同 |
> | AFB positive | TB meningitis | 不是急性 ascending weakness |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"GBS: protein goes up, cells stay chill."**
> - 看到 Campylobacter 前驱史，要主动联想到 GBS
> - GBS 题里常把 CSF 当确认点来考
> - 最容易错在把“免疫病”误以为一定细胞也升高
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Normal protein level**：不对，GBS 的 protein 通常升高。
>
> **C - Low glucose content**：是 meningitis 方向，不是 peripheral neuropathy。
>
> **D - Xanthochromia**：提示 SAH，临床应是 thunderclap headache 或脑膜刺激征路线。
>
> **E - Acid-fast bacilli**：TB meningitis 会有亚急性脑膜炎表现，不是这种无力模式。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Normal protein level。因为题干只直接问一个 CSF finding，容易忘记 GBS 的完整经典组合。

> ^Q107
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：感染灶 + hypotension + tachycardia + AKI + elevated lactate，再加上 **bounding pulses**，这是典型 **septic shock / distributive shock**，hemodynamics 要选 **low SVR, high cardiac index, low-normal PCWP, high MvO2**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Infected, warm, erythematous foot | Source of sepsis | 感染源已经摆在题面上 |
> | BP 80/52, pulse 126, drowsy | Shock with end-organ hypoperfusion | 低血压加精神状态改变，不是单纯 cellulitis |
> | Leukocytosis, lactate elevation, creatinine 2.0 | Sepsis physiology with organ dysfunction | 脓毒性休克线索完整 |
> | Bounding peripheral pulses | Vasodilated hyperdynamic circulation | distributive shock 很爱给这个体征 |
> | Moist mucous membranes, no JVD | Against hypovolemic or tamponade physiology | 帮你排除低容量和某些阻塞性休克 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - PCWP normal/decreased, cardiac index increased, SVR decreased, MvO2 saturation increased**
>
> **Core Concept**：Septic shock 的核心是 **peripheral vasodilation + capillary leak + impaired oxygen extraction**。因此：
> - **SVR decreased**
> - **PCWP normal/decreased**
> - **cardiac index increased**（早期/高动力期）
> - **mixed venous O2 saturation increased**
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：外周血管扩张让 afterload 掉下去，心脏会以更高输出量代偿；但微循环与组织摄氧能力出问题，结果静脉端剩下的氧反而更高。
>
> ```text
> sepsis
> -> inflammatory vasodilation
> -> low SVR
> -> hyperdynamic circulation
> -> high cardiac index
> -> tissue oxygen extraction defect
> -> high mixed venous O2 saturation
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Shock type | PCWP | Cardiac index | SVR | MvO2 | 题眼 |
> |---|---|---|---|---|---|
> | Septic / distributive | low or low-normal | high | low | high | warm extremities, bounding pulses |
> | Hypovolemic | low | low | high | low | dry mucosa, low preload |
> | Cardiogenic/tamponade-like | high | low | high | low | congestion or elevated filling pressures |
> | PE / obstructive | normal-low | low-normal | high | often high | dyspnea/right heart strain |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Warm shock wastes pressure, not flow."**
> - 血压低是因为 vasodilation
> - 心脏输出常先升高，不是先降
> - 组织拿氧效率差，所以 venous oxygen 反而偏高
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - PCWP high, CI low, SVR high, MvO2 low**：更像 tamponade 一类的 obstructive/cardiac filling problem。
>
> **B - PCWP low, CI low, SVR high, MvO2 low**：典型 hypovolemic shock 组合。
>
> **C - PCWP normal/low, CI normal/low, SVR high, MvO2 high**：可往 pulmonary embolism 之类 obstructive shock 靠。
>
> **E - PCWP normal/high, CI high, SVR high, MvO2 normal/low**：SVR 方向先错，不能是 septic shock。
>
> **F - PCWP high, CI high, SVR low, MvO2 normal/low**：更像 high-output failure with congestion，不符合本题。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - 低容量性休克。因为题里有 hypotension 和 AKI，但 `bounding pulses + moist mucosa + infected foot` 把答案拉回 septic shock。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q108 免疫疫苗 / GPA vasculitic ulcer
> ^Q108
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：painful nonhealing ulcer 如果同时带着 **ENT symptoms + renal involvement**，不要只往 infection 想，要直接升维到 **granulomatosis with polyangiitis (GPA)**，病灶本质是 **necrotizing vasculitis** 导致局部缺血坏死。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Painful nodule that ulcerated | Suggests inflammatory/vasculitic skin lesion | 不是单纯慢性静脉溃疡 |
> | Otalgia and hearing loss | ENT involvement | GPA 很爱考 ear/nose/throat involvement |
> | Creatinine 1.9 with proteinuria and hematuria | Glomerulonephritis pattern | 形成 respiratory/ENT + kidney 组合拳 |
> | Fever without travel history | Systemic inflammatory disease | 发热不一定就是感染 |
> | White woman age 55 | Compatible demographic for GPA | 人群上不冲突 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Necrotizing vasculitis**
>
> **Core Concept**：GPA 是 **small- to medium-vessel necrotizing vasculitis**。血管壁炎症会造成 lumen occlusion、localized ischemia、tissue necrosis，因此皮肤病灶可以表现为 painful nonhealing ulcer 或 PG-like lesion。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题的关键不是背“GPA 也会有皮肤表现”，而是理解为什么 ulcer 会久不愈合。原因是 **vasculitic vessel damage** 让局部灌注差，伤口一直处在 ischemic/inflammatory state。
>
> ```text
> GPA
> -> necrotizing vasculitis
> -> vessel wall damage and occlusion
> -> localized ischemia
> -> tissue necrosis
> -> painful nonhealing ulcer
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Choice | Why It Can Seem Plausible | 为什么不是本题 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Bacterial infection | Ulcer + fever | 解释不了 ENT + glomerulonephritis |
> | Immunosuppression | Wound healing poor | 没有更强证据，而且系统表现更像 vasculitis |
> | Malnutrition | 慢性伤口可愈合差 | 不能把肾脏和耳部表现串起来 |
> | PAD | Ischemic ulcer | 缺少典型 atherosclerotic context，也解释不了全身表现 |
> | GPA vasculitis | ENT + renal + skin triad | 本题最佳整合 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"ENT + kidney + ulcer = think vessel, not germ."**
> - 上气道/耳鼻喉受累是 GPA 高频提示
> - 尿血 + proteinuria 把病变定位到 glomerulonephritis
> - 皮肤溃疡是 vasculitis downstream effect
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Bacterial infection**：有发热会诱导你选它，但无论如何都串不起 otalgia/hearing loss 和 glomerulonephritis。
>
> **B - Hemoglobinopathy**：sickle cell 可因 ischemia 影响愈合，但这个患者背景和病史都不支持。
>
> **C - Immunosuppression**：确实会让伤口好得慢，但不会自然解释 ENT + renal syndrome。
>
> **D - Malnutrition**：太泛，解释力不足。
>
> **F - Peripheral artery disease**：也会造成局部缺血，但患者整体更像 systemic vasculitis 而非 atherosclerotic limb ischemia。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Bacterial infection。因为 painful ulcer + fever 很像 infection，但这题真正考的是多系统线索整合。

> ^Q109
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：老年患者 wandering、短时记忆差、独居，再加上 ecchymoses、inflamed gums、edema、anemia，而且 platelet 正常，这是一道 **malnutrition due to dementia/self-neglect** 的整合题。

> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Found wandering, recalls 0/3 objects | Suggests underlying dementia | 先抓到认知障碍背景 |
> | Lives alone | High risk for poor intake and self-neglect | 社会背景帮助解释 malnutrition |
> | Ecchymoses with normal platelet count | Think coagulation/vitamin deficiency, not thrombocytopenia | 提示 vitamin K/C 问题而非血小板病 |
> | Inflamed gums | Classic vitamin C deficiency clue | 这是 scurvy 方向的高辨识度线索 |
> | Trace pedal edema | Hypoalbuminemia from poor nutrition | 营养不良能把 edema 串起来 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Malnutrition**
>
> **Core Concept**：Dementia 患者常因 self-care decline 出现 **protein-calorie malnutrition** 和多种 vitamin deficiency。于是会同时看到：
> - **hypoalbuminemia -> pedal edema**
> - **vitamin C deficiency -> inflamed gums**
> - **vitamin K deficiency -> ecchymoses despite normal platelets**
> - **folate deficiency -> anemia**
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是考单一 deficiency，而是考“认知障碍导致整体摄入差”，再把多个体征统一解释。能一条线串最多体征的就是 malnutrition。
>
> ```text
> dementia / self-neglect
> -> poor nutritional intake
> -> low albumin + vitamin deficiencies
> -> edema + gum inflammation + bruising + anemia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Finding | Nutritional link | 本题对应 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Inflamed gums | Vitamin C deficiency | 是 |
> | Ecchymoses with normal platelets | Vitamin K deficiency / fragile tissues | 是 |
> | Pedal edema | Hypoalbuminemia | 是 |
> | Anemia | Folate or broader deficiency | 是 |
> | Cognitive decline + living alone | Risk factor for self-neglect | 是 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Normal platelets, bleeding gums, edema: feed the patient before you chase zebras."**
> - 先看能否用一个 common geriatric problem 解释全部体征
> - dementia 是营养不良的上游原因
> - bruising 不等于一定是 abuse 或 platelet disorder
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Myelodysplastic syndrome**：可有 anemia，但常伴 leukopenia 或 thrombocytopenia，且不解释 gum inflammation 和 edema。
>
> **B - Adrenal insufficiency**：更常见 hypotension、electrolyte abnormalities，本题没有。
>
> **C - Chronic liver disease**：能解释 edema/coagulopathy，但病史和体征支持度不够。
>
> **D - Vasculitis**：常见 petechiae/purpura，不是这个营养缺乏组合。
>
> **F - Elder abuse**：必须纳入鉴别，但解释不了 gum inflammation、edema、anemia 这些系统性缺乏表现。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Elder abuse。因为 dementia + bruising 很容易触发这个联想，但出题人故意再加 gums、edema、normal platelets 来把答案推向 malnutrition。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q110 hematology oncology / MDS needs marrow biopsy
> ^Q110
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：older adult + macrocytic anemia + thrombocytopenia + leukopenia，再加上 smear 上 **ovalomacrocytosis + hyposegmented neutrophils**，这是 **myelodysplastic syndrome (MDS)** 指纹级组合，下一步就是 **bone marrow biopsy**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 66-year-old man | MDS is a disease of older adults | 年龄本身就提高先验概率 |
> | Hemoglobin 7.5, MCV 106 | Symptomatic macrocytic anemia | 不是单纯轻度实验室异常 |
> | Platelets 56,000 and leukocytes 4,000 | Pancytopenia / multilineage involvement | 支持 bone marrow stem cell disorder |
> | Ovalomacrocytosis | Dysplastic erythrocytes | MDS 高频 smear clue |
> | Neutrophils with reduced segmentation | Hyposegmented neutrophils | 与 B12 deficiency 的 hypersegmentation 相反 |
> | Normal vitamin B12 level | Makes megaloblastic deficiency less likely | 帮你排除 pernicious anemia 路线 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Bone marrow biopsy**
>
> **Core Concept**：MDS 是 **hematopoietic stem cell neoplasm**，会导致无效造血、细胞异型性和 cytopenias。外周血 smear 给你高度提示，但 **确诊必须靠 bone marrow biopsy**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：题目故意把宏细胞性贫血做成“像 B12 deficiency”，但关键分水岭在于 **hyposegmented** 而不是 **hypersegmented** neutrophils，而且还有多系细胞减少。
>
> ```text
> stem cell neoplasm
> -> ineffective hematopoiesis
> -> dysplastic RBCs and WBCs
> -> macrocytic anemia + leukopenia + thrombocytopenia
> -> confirm with bone marrow biopsy
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Condition | Smear / Labs | Next step |
> |---|---|---|
> | MDS | Macrocytosis + cytopenias + hyposegmented/hypogranular neutrophils | Bone marrow biopsy |
> | B12 deficiency | Macrocytosis + hypersegmented neutrophils +/- neuro findings | B12, methylmalonic acid, IF antibody |
> | Lead toxicity | Microcytic or normocytic anemia, basophilic stippling | Blood lead level |
> | Multiple myeloma | Normocytic anemia, rouleaux, monoclonal protein | SPEP/UPEP |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Macrocytosis plus low counts across lines: think marrow, not just vitamins."**
> - 多系细胞减少比单纯贫血更重要
> - `hyposegmented` 是这题最值钱的词
> - 正常 B12 只是辅助，核心还是 dysplasia pattern
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Anti-intrinsic factor antibody**：是 pernicious anemia 路线，但本题 B12 正常，而且 neutrophils 是 hyposegmented，不是 hypersegmented。
>
> **B - Blood lead level**：lead toxicity 不会这么典型地表现为 macrocytic pancytopenia with dysplasia。
>
> **D - Erythropoietin level**：治疗层面的信息晚于诊断确认。
>
> **E - Methylmalonic acid level**：还是在追 B12 deficiency，但题目已经给了反向线索。
>
> **F - Serum protein electrophoresis**：multiple myeloma 可致 anemia，但不典型造成这种 dysplastic pancytopenia。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Methylmalonic acid level。因为 `MCV 106` 很容易把人带去 megaloblastic anemia，但 smear 和 pancytopenia 才是决定性证据。

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q111 心内 / Hyperkalemic emergency after rhabdomyolysis
> ^Q111
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：seizure 后出现 **rhabdomyolysis + AKI**，随后从正常 ECG 迅速进展到 **low-amplitude P waves + high-degree AV block + hypotension/bradycardia**，先别被 pacing 带偏，核心是 **hyperkalemic emergency**，先打 **IV calcium gluconate**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Recurrent seizures with urinary incontinence | Seizure is the trigger | 为后面的 muscle breakdown 埋因果线 |
> | CPK 7,000 U/L + creatinine 2.4 mg/dL | Rhabdomyolysis with AKI | 说明 potassium 会继续从 damaged muscle 释放 |
> | Initial potassium 5.4 mEq/L | Already elevated early | 数值一开始不算极端，但趋势危险 |
> | ECG initially normal, 4 hours later low-amplitude P waves and AV block | Rapid progression of hyperkalemia | 动态恶化比单次化验更重要 |
> | Hypotension and pulse 40/min | Unstable arrhythmic complication | 已进入需要立刻 stabilizing membrane 的阶段 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Intravenous calcium gluconate**
>
> **Core Concept**：这题考的是 **treat the rhythm by treating the potassium**。Rhabdomyolysis 持续释放 intracellular potassium，导致 **hyperkalemic emergency**。一旦出现 ECG changes 或 bradyarrhythmia，先用 **IV calcium** 稳定 cardiac membrane，再用 insulin plus glucose 等措施把 potassium 暂时移回细胞内，最后再考虑 definitive removal。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：高钾不是单纯“数值高”，而是 extracellular potassium 升高后改变 resting membrane potential，逐步造成传导抑制和 arrhythmia。
>
> ```text
> seizure
> -> rhabdomyolysis
> -> potassium release + AKI
> -> rising serum potassium
> -> low-amplitude P waves / AV block / bradycardia
> -> give IV calcium first to stabilize myocardium
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Situation | Immediate priority | Why |
> |---|---|---|
> | Hyperkalemia with ECG changes | IV calcium | Stabilizes cardiac membrane right now |
> | Hyperkalemia without instability | Confirm and start shifting/removal plan | 不一定第一秒就需要 membrane stabilization |
> | Complete heart block from intrinsic conduction disease | Atropine then pacing pathway | 本题不是 primary conduction problem |
> | Torsade de pointes | Magnesium sulfate | 节律类型完全不同 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Tall K kills conduction before the lab repeats."**
> - ECG 从正常变坏，说明 potassium 正在动态上升
> - low P waves + AV block 是高钾传导抑制信号
> - 先稳 membrane，再谈清钾
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Insertion of a transvenous pacemaker**：看到 high-degree AV block 很容易冲去选它，但这里 block 是 **reversible metabolic toxicity**。先处理高钾，若仍不稳再走 pacing 路径。
>
> **C - Intravenous dobutamine**：可以升心排，但不能逆转 hyperkalemia 对 myocardium 的膜毒性。
>
> **D - Intravenous magnesium sulfate**：适用于 torsade de pointes，不适用于高钾导致的 bradycardic AV block。
>
> **E - Observation pending repeat laboratory studies**：有 ECG changes 就已经不是观察题。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Insertion of a transvenous pacemaker。因为题干给了 unstable AV block，但真正考点是识别 **underlying hyperkalemia as the first reversible cause**。

> |---|---|---|
> | Severe headaches, sweating, tachycardia | Classic triad | 嗜铬细胞瘤最常考的识别组合 |
> | Blood pressure 240/140 mm Hg | Catecholamine excess crisis | 不是普通 migraine 或 essential HTN |
> | 7-kg weight loss and fasting glucose 127 mg/dL | Catecholamine metabolic effects | 提示高代谢和高糖 |
> | Recently started propranolol | Beta blockade alone can worsen pheo HTN | unopposed alpha 是题眼 |
> | Pale and diaphoretic with normal TSH | Supports adrenergic state over thyrotoxicosis | 帮你排除 thyroid 路线 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Adrenal medullary disease**
>
> **Core Concept**：Pheochromocytoma 来源于 **adrenal medulla chromaffin cells**，分泌 catecholamines，导致 paroxysmal 或 sustained hypertension。处理上一定要记住顺序：**alpha blockade first, then beta blockade**，否则会因 unopposed alpha vasoconstriction 让血压更高。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：beta blocker 先上时，beta-mediated vasodilation 被拿掉，但 alpha-mediated vasoconstriction 还在，外周阻力会更高，所以血压反而飙升。
>
> ```text
> adrenal medullary tumor
> -> excess catecholamines
> -> episodic headache + sweating + tachycardia + HTN
> -> propranolol alone
> -> unopposed alpha vasoconstriction
> -> hypertensive crisis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Secondary HTN cause | Typical clue | Why not this one |
> |---|---|---|
> | Pheochromocytoma | Episodic headache, sweating, tachycardia | 本题完全匹配 |
> | Hyperaldosteronism | HTN + hypokalemia | 本题钾正常 |
> | Renal artery stenosis | Resistant HTN, abdominal bruit | 不解释 adrenergic spells |
> | Thyrotoxicosis | Weight loss, tremor, low TSH | 本题 TSH 正常 |
> | Coarctation | Young age, limb BP difference | 不符合症状谱 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Headache, sweat, race: think medulla, not migraine."**
> - triad 一出现就先升维到 catecholamine tumor
> - propranolol 加重是高频考试陷阱
> - 先 alpha 后 beta，顺序不能反
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Adrenal cortical disease**：cortex 走的是 aldosterone/cortisol 路线，常见 hypokalemia 或 Cushingoid features，不是这种 adrenergic triad。
>
> **C - Aortic disease**：coarctation 更偏年轻人和上下肢血压差，不会解释阵发性出汗心悸。
>
> **D - Parathyroid disease**：可有 constipation、stones、bones 等，但不造成这种 hypertensive adrenergic spells。
>
> **E - Renal parenchymal disease**：可有 secondary HTN，但 creatinine 正常且不解释 propranolol-triggered crisis。
>
> **F - Renal vascular disease**：能解释高血压，解释不了 headache-sweating-tachycardia triad。
>
> **G - Thyroid disease**：体重下降会迷惑你，但 TSH 正常，而且“beta blocker 单独加重”更典型指向 pheo。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Renal vascular disease。因为也是 secondary HTN，但缺乏典型的 paroxysmal catecholamine 症状。

> ^Q113
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：chronic **nonbloody diarrhea + abdominal pain + weight loss + fever** 已经很像 inflammatory bowel disease；再看到 **passing gas from the vagina**，直接锁定 **Crohn disease with enterovaginal fistula**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 2 months of crampy abdominal pain and nonbloody diarrhea | Chronic inflammatory bowel pattern | 不是急性感染性肠炎 |
> | Low-grade fever and 4-kg weight loss | Systemic inflammatory disease | 帮你往 IBD 而非功能性腹痛走 |
> | Symptoms wake her from sleep | Organic pathology | 夜间症状很重要 |
> | Gas and malodorous vaginal discharge | Enterovaginal fistula | 这是 Crohn 的高辨识度并发症 |
> | Age 29 | Typical age group for Crohn disease | 年龄匹配度高 |

>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Crohn disease**
>
> **Core Concept**：Crohn disease 的核心不是“腹泻”而是 **transmural inflammation**。一旦炎症穿透肠壁，就容易形成 sinus tract 和 fistula，连到 bladder、skin、vagina 等 hollow organs。题目里 **gas/feculent discharge from vagina** 基本就是 enterovaginal fistula 的翻译。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：UC 多数停留在 mucosa，而 Crohn 可以全层发炎，所以更容易出 **fistula、stricture、abscess** 这类穿透性并发症。
>
> ```text
> Crohn disease
> -> transmural bowel inflammation
> -> sinus tract formation
> -> penetration into adjacent organ
> -> enterovaginal fistula
> -> gas / malodorous discharge via vagina
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Bowel pattern | Fistula tendency | Key distinction |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Crohn disease | Often nonbloody diarrhea | Common | Transmural inflammation |
> | Ulcerative colitis | Often bloody diarrhea | Rare | Mucosal disease, toxic megacolon |
> | PID | No chronic bowel inflammatory pattern | No | More gynecologic pain/bleeding |
> | Diverticulitis | Usually acute in older adults | Can occur | Age and tempo do not fit |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"If stool finds a tunnel, think Crohn."**
> - fistula = 先想 transmural disease
> - nonbloody diarrhea 比 bloody diarrhea 更贴 Crohn
> - 夜间症状和体重下降都支持 organic inflammation
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Diverticulitis**：也能并发 fistula，但通常更急性、年龄更大，而且 chronic weight loss/nonbloody diarrhea 不典型。
>
> **C - Endometriosis**：可有 cyclical pelvic pain 或 dyspareunia，但不解释 chronic inflammatory diarrhea 和 fistula。
>
> **D - Ovarian cancer**：可有腹痛和体重下降，但常见 bloating、early satiety、urinary symptoms，不是这条 GI-fistula 路线。
>
> **E - Pelvic inflammatory disease**：新性伴会诱导你选它，但 PID 不会造成 enterovaginal fistula 和慢性非血性腹泻。
>
> **F - Tuberculosis**：能 mimic Crohn，但通常有 pulmonary TB 背景、ascites 或腹块支持。
>
> **G - Ulcerative colitis**：最容易和 IBD 混淆，但 fistula 是 Crohn 强项，不是 UC。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Pelvic inflammatory disease。因为题干故意给了 new sexual partner 和 vaginal discharge，但真正决定性的是 **gas from vagina**，那是 fistula 线索，不是 cervicitis 线索。

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |

> -> glomerular deposition
> -> poststreptococcal glomerulonephritis
> -> hematuria / edema / hypertension
> ```
>

>

> | Parvovirus B19 | Myocarditis occasionally | Rash is slapped cheek/lacy |
>

> **"Honey on the face, urine a week later."**
> - honey crust = impetigo
> - skin GAS -> PSGN
> - treatment impetigo 也不能完全阻止 PSGN

>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：heavy smoker + baseline bronchodilators + acute **dyspnea, cough, yellow sputum** + hyperinflation on chest x-ray，这是标准 **COPD exacerbation**；题目问谁能缩短住院时间，答案就是 **systemic glucocorticoids**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 40-pack-year smoker on albuterol and ipratropium | Established COPD background | 基础病先定住 |
> | 2 days of worsening dyspnea, cough, yellow sputum | Cardinal exacerbation symptoms | COPD exacerbation 三联线索 |

> | Chest x-ray with flattened diaphragms | Confirms hyperinflation | 进一步排除 pulmonary edema |

>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Glucocorticoids**
>
> **Core Concept**：Acute COPD exacerbation 的核心住院获益药物之一是 **systemic glucocorticoids**，因为它们能减轻 airway inflammation、改善 lung function，并 **shorten length of hospitalization**。Bronchodilators 要继续用，但题目问的是“最可能缩短住院天数”的干预。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：感染或其他刺激导致 airway inflammation 加重、mucus 增多和 airflow obstruction 恶化。Steroids 不是立刻打开气道的“救火喷雾”，但能更快把炎症压下来，所以住院恢复更快。
>
> ```text
> COPD
> -> acute airway inflammation and mucus increase
> -> worse airflow obstruction
> -> dyspnea / cough / sputum
> -> systemic glucocorticoids
> -> improved lung function and shorter hospitalization
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Therapy | Role in COPD exacerbation | Exam-relevant benefit |
> |---|---|---|
> | Short-acting bronchodilators | Immediate symptom relief | Standard acute treatment |
> | Systemic glucocorticoids | Reduce airway inflammation | Shorter hospital stay |
> | Antibiotics | If >=2 cardinal symptoms, especially purulent sputum | Reduce infectious burden |
> | NPPV | Hypercapnic ventilatory failure | Avoid intubation |
> | Diuretics | For pulmonary edema, not COPD itself | Wrong disease process here |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"COPD flare: puff, purge infection, and prednisone."**
> - dyspnea + cough + sputum = classic flare
> - x-ray hyperinflation keeps you out of CHF lane
> - steroids are the hospitalization-shortening pearl
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - ACE inhibitor**：能治高血压，但和当前 COPD flare 恢复速度无关。
>
> **B - Alpha-adrenergic antagonist**：不是 COPD exacerbation 治疗药。
>
> **C - Beta-adrenergic antagonist**：还有可能加重 bronchospasm，更不该选。
>
> **E - Leukotriene receptor antagonist**：偏 asthma/allergic pathway，不用于 COPD exacerbation。
>
> **F - Loop diuretics**：如果是 pulmonary edema 可能有用，但本题没有 crackles、vascular congestion，反而有明显 hyperinflation。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Loop diuretics。因为 elderly dyspnea 常让人想到 heart failure，但 scaphoid abdomen、prolonged expiration、hyperinflated CXR 都把方向拉回 COPD。

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Chemotherapy last week | Major trigger for neutropenia | 时间轴非常贴合 chemo-induced neutropenia |
> | WBC count 600/mm3 | Severe neutropenia | 这题真正的题眼，不是单纯发热 |

> **正确答案：D - Bacterial infection**
>

>
> ---

> **机制说明**：chemotherapy 把中性粒细胞压低后，机体对细菌的早期控制能力明显下降，感染可迅速进展为 sepsis，而且炎症信号可能不典型。
>
> ```text
> chemotherapy
> -> neutropenia
> -> impaired innate defense
> -> bacterial invasion spreads easily
> -> sepsis / encephalopathy
> -> confusion + fever + tachycardia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | 选项 | 何时更像 | 本题为什么不是 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Tumor lysis syndrome | HyperK, hyperphos, hypocalcemia, uric acid up, AKI | 题干没给典型 electrolyte pattern |
> | Tumor expansion | Progressive local or mass-effect symptoms | 1 month after debulking 且有高热/中性粒细胞低，不符合 |
> | Delayed transfusion reaction | Hemolysis, jaundice, renal injury | 不解释 profound neutropenia |
> | Drug fever | Isolated fever | 不该有这么强的 septic picture |
>
> ---
>

> **"Chemo + ANC crash + fever = infect first, explain later."**
> - 肿瘤病人发热，不先把 recent transfusion 当主线。
> - 看到 `WBC 600/mm3`，思路要立刻切到 febrile neutropenia。
> - altered mental status 在这里常是 systemic infection 的结果。
>
> ---
>

>
> **B - Tumor expansion**：肿瘤进展不会这么好地解释 fever、tachycardia 和 severe leukopenia。

> **C - Delayed transfusion reaction**：更像 hemolytic anemia + jaundice + renal dysfunction，不是 neutropenic sepsis。
>
> **E - Drug fever**：通常是 isolated fever，不足以解释 confusion 和整体感染图景。
>

> [!example]- UWSA1 Q119 endocrine / DKA ketones come from hepatic fatty acid breakdown
> ^Q119
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：青少年 + polyuria/polydipsia + hyperglycemia + anion gap metabolic acidosis + Kussmaul respirations，就是 **DKA**；问“症状由什么过程造成”时，落点是 **fatty acid breakdown in the liver -> ketone production**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Several weeks of thirst and urination | New type 1 diabetes pattern | DKA 前驱线索 |
> | Glucose 382 mg/dL | Hyperglycemia | 满足 DKA 诊断要件之一 |
> | pH 7.2 and bicarbonate 10 mEq/L | Metabolic acidosis | 严重程度已经很明确 |
> | Deep, rapid breathing | Kussmaul respirations | 酸中毒代偿 |
> | Vomiting and abdominal pain | Ketosis manifestations | DKA 常见“像腹症”表现 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Fatty acid breakdown in the liver**
>
> **Core Concept**：在 insulin deficiency 下，peripheral fat stores 先被动员，fatty acids 被送到 **liver**，再被转成 ketones（beta-hydroxybutyrate、acetoacetate）。真正造成 vomiting、abdominal pain、Kussmaul respirations 的，是 **ketone-driven high anion gap metabolic acidosis**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题常见误区是把“脂解发生在外周脂肪”与“症状由哪里产生”混成一件事。外周 lipolysis 提供原料，但临床症状的核心病理产物来自 **hepatic ketogenesis**。
>
> ```text
> insulin deficiency
> -> increased counterregulatory hormones
> -> lipolysis in adipose tissue
> -> free fatty acids to liver
> -> ketone production
> -> anion gap metabolic acidosis
> -> abdominal pain / vomiting / Kussmaul breathing
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | 过程 | 会不会解释本题核心症状 | 说明 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Hepatic fatty acid breakdown to ketones | Yes | 正解 |
> | Skeletal muscle glycogenolysis | No | 可伴随发生，但不制造 ketone acidosis |
> | Proximal RTA | No | 是 non-anion gap acidosis |
> | Distal RTA | No | 也是 non-anion gap acidosis |
> | Renal ketoacid excretion | No | 是治疗后帮助清除，不是起病机制 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"DKA symptoms are made in the liver."**
> - 脂肪在外周被拆，ketone 在肝里被做出来。
> - Step 2 喜欢考“原料来源” vs “症状真正来源”。
> - Kussmaul breathing 是在为 ketone acidosis 擦屁股。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Glycogenolysis in the skeletal muscles**：不是导致 ketone accumulation 的关键步骤。
>
> **C - Impaired bicarbonate reabsorption in the proximal renal tubules**：RTA type 2 是 non-anion gap acidosis，本题 anion gap 明显升高。
>
> **D - Impaired urine acidification in the distal renal tubules**：RTA type 1 也不符合这个高血糖酮症场景。
>
> **E - Increased renal excretion of ketoacids**：更多是补液后的“清酸”方向，不是起病原因。
>
> **F - Peripheral lipolysis due to decreased catecholamine levels**：文本层这一项/解释有轻微错位；本题 keyed mechanism 仍是 hepatic ketone production，不按该项作答。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Peripheral lipolysis ...。因为很多人知道 DKA 有 lipolysis，但题目要的是更直接导致症状的 **liver ketone generation**。

>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：pediatric DKA 在完成首个 `10 mL/kg` isotonic fluid bolus 后，下一步是 **regular IV insulin drip + potassium-containing isotonic fluids**；glucose 还没降到 `<=200 mg/dL`，不要急着加 dextrose。

>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Already received 10 mL/kg normal saline over 1 hour | First step completed | 现在考的是下一步，不是起始补液 |
> | Glucose 360 mg/dL | Still too high for dextrose fluids | 还没到加 D5 的节点 |
> | pH 7.2, bicarbonate 12 mEq/L, large ketones | DKA persists | 酸中毒和 ketosis 仍在 |
> | Potassium 4.4 mEq/L | Serum K looks normal but total body K depleted | 开 insulin 时必须同步补钾 |
> | Hemodynamics improved | Safe to move to insulin phase | 补液后才开始 insulin，可降低 cerebral edema 风险 |
>

>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Regular insulin drip with potassium-containing fluids**
>
> **Core Concept**：DKA 的标准顺序是先 gradual isotonic fluid resuscitation，再上 **continuous IV regular insulin**。与此同时要补 potassium，因为总钾储备其实已经亏空；一旦 insulin 开始，K 会迅速回到细胞内，血钾可快速下跌。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：为什么不一开始就猛打 insulin？因为 insulin 会迅速降 glucose 和 plasma osmolality，增加水进入脑内的风险。先补液、后 insulin，是为了降低 **cerebral edema** 风险。
>
> ```text
> DKA
> -> dehydration + hyperosmolar state
> -> initial slow isotonic fluid bolus
> -> then IV insulin
> -> potassium shifts into cells
> -> concurrent potassium replacement needed
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | 管理节点 | 何时做 | 本题状态 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Isotonic fluid bolus | First step | 已完成 |
> | IV insulin drip + K fluids | After initial bolus if K not severely low | 现在就该做 |
> | Add dextrose | When glucose <=200 mg/dL | 还没到 |
> | Bicarbonate | Usually only if pH <=6.9 | 本题不需要 |
> | Transition to SQ insulin | After gap closes and acidosis resolves | 还太早 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Fluids first, insulin second, potassium alongside."**
> - DKA 的顺序题是 Step 2 高频。
> - 正常血钾不等于正常总钾。
> - dextrose 不是和 insulin 永远一起上，而是 glucose 降到阈值后再加。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Continuation of normal saline at a maintenance rate**：单纯补液不够，不能纠正 ongoing insulin deficiency 与 ketogenesis。
>
> **B - Intravenous sodium bicarbonate**：通常只在 `pH <= 6.9` 才考虑，否则获益有限且副作用更多。
>
> **C - Regular insulin drip with dextrose-containing fluids**：要等 serum glucose `<=200 mg/dL` 再加 dextrose，本题还是 `360 mg/dL`。
>
> **E - Subcutaneous long-acting insulin**：initial DKA management 不用 basal SQ insulin。
>
> **F - Subcutaneous short-acting insulin with potassium supplementation**：SQ 起效慢且不好滴定，急性 DKA 首选 IV infusion。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Regular insulin drip with dextrose-containing fluids。因为很多人记住“DKA treatment 要 insulin + dextrose”，却忘了 glucose threshold。
