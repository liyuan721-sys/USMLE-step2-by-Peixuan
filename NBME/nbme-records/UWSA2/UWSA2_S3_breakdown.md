###### Q081

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q081 GI / Focal nodular hyperplasia
> ^Q081
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**solitary liver lesion + arterial enhancement + central stellate scar**，先锁定 **focal nodular hyperplasia (FNH)**；影像典型时 **no intervention indicated**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 33-year-old woman | Fits typical demographic for FNH | FNH 更常见于女性 |
> | Solitary 4-cm hepatic lesion | Matches common benign presentation | 小而孤立的肝占位更像 benign lesion |
> | Arterial-phase enhancement | Hypervascular lesion pattern | FNH 常见 selective arterial enhancement |
> | Central stellate scar | Classic imaging clue | 这是本题最关键的影像题眼 |
> | No cirrhosis or hepatitis risk factors | Argues against HCC workup | 没有肝癌背景时，不要被 liver mass 三个字带偏 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - No intervention indicated**
>
> **Core Concept**：FNH 是 **most common benign nonvascular liver lesion**。当 CT 或 MRI 出现 **arterial enhancement + central stellate scar** 的经典组合时，通常不需要 biopsy、AFP、或 surgery。管理以 **conservative observation** 为主。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：FNH 不是肿瘤性增殖，而是对局部异常动脉灌注的 **hyperplastic response**。所以它有中心异常血供，影像上才会出现 arterial enhancement 和 stellate scar。
>
> ```text
> anomalous central artery
> -> local hyperperfusion
> -> hyperplastic hepatocyte response
> -> focal nodular hyperplasia
> -> arterial enhancement + central scar
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：常见 solitary liver lesion
> | Lesion | Typical Clue | Management Hint |
> |---|---|---|
> | FNH | Arterial enhancement + central stellate scar | Typical imaging -> observe |
> | Hepatic adenoma | OCP association, hemorrhage risk, no central scar | Often needs different follow-up or resection |
> | HCC | Cirrhosis/hepatitis risk, elevated AFP possible | Cancer workup/treatment |
> | Echinococcal cyst | Smooth cystic lesion | Serology if cystic exposure pattern |
> | Hemangioma | Peripheral nodular enhancement | Usually incidental benign lesion |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Scar in the center, leave it alone if classic."**
> - central stellate scar = 先想 FNH
> - young woman with small solitary lesion = 更像 benign
> - typical FNH imaging = no biopsy, no AFP, no surgery
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Echinococcus serology**：包虫更像 smooth cystic mass，不是 arterial-enhancing solid lesion with stellate scar。
>
> **B - Fine-needle biopsy**：若影像不典型可考虑，但这题影像非常典型，不必多做侵入性检查。
>
> **D - Serum alpha-fetoprotein**：AFP 主要用于怀疑 HCC 时辅助判断；本题没有 cirrhosis、viral hepatitis 或恶性影像线索。
>
> **E - Surgical resection**：通常留给 symptomatic 或 large lesion，不适用于典型 4-cm FNH。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Fine-needle biopsy。很多人看到 liver mass 会条件反射要取病理，但 **central stellate scar** 就是在提醒你影像已足够。
>

###### Q082

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q082 感染 / Listeria meningitis source
> ^Q082
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**older or immunocompromised patient + gastroenteritis followed by meningitis + gram-positive bacilli**，优先想 **Listeria monocytogenes**，预防点是 **avoid unpasteurized milk products**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Age 64 with chronic lymphocytic leukemia | High-risk host for invasive Listeria | 年龄 >50 且有 hematologic malignancy，风险显著上升 |
> | Nausea, vomiting, watery diarrhea first | Suggests foodborne entry | Listeria 可先表现为 febrile gastroenteritis |
> | Fever, confusion, photophobia, neck stiffness | Meningoencephalitis syndrome | 后续进展到 CNS infection |
> | CSF neutrophilic pleocytosis | Bacterial meningitis pattern | 支持细菌性脑膜炎 |
> | Blood culture grows gram-positive bacilli | Organism clue points strongly to Listeria | 这是本题定锚线索 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Avoiding unpasteurized milk products**
>
> **Core Concept**：Listeria 是 **gram-positive bacillus**，通过 **unpasteurized dairy, raw meats/vegetables, prepackaged meats** 传播。健康成年人常只有轻度胃肠症状，但 **pregnant, extremes of age, immunocompromised** 人群可发展成 bacteremia 或 meningoencephalitis。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是在问“哪种 meningitis 最常见”，而是在问“这位特定高危宿主最像哪种食源性病原体”。先用宿主背景缩小范围，再用 **gram-positive bacilli** 完成锁定。
>
> ```text
> contaminated food exposure
> -> febrile gastroenteritis
> -> bloodstream invasion in high-risk host
> -> CNS seeding
> -> Listeria meningoencephalitis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：食源性病原体对比
> | Pathogen | Gram stain | Classic Source | Classic Syndrome |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Listeria monocytogenes | Gram-positive bacillus | Unpasteurized dairy, deli meats | Gastroenteritis then bacteremia/meningitis |
> | Legionella | Gram-negative | Contaminated water | Atypical pneumonia, GI symptoms |
> | Vibrio vulnificus | Gram-negative | Raw shellfish | Wound infection or septicemia |
> | Campylobacter | Gram-negative | Undercooked poultry | Enteritis, bloody diarrhea |
> | Streptococcus pneumoniae | Gram-positive cocci | Not foodborne | Most common community meningitis |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Listeria likes dairy, deli, and damaged defenses."**
> - dairy/deli = exposure clue
> - damaged defenses = age, pregnancy, immunocompromise
> - diarrhea first, meningitis later = classic progression
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Adequately disinfecting water supplies**：更像 Legionella 暴露路径，而且 Legionella 是 gram-negative，不符合 blood culture。
>
> **B - Avoiding raw shellfish and seafood**：指向 Vibrio vulnificus，常见 septicemia 或 wound infection，不是典型 meningitis with gram-positive bacilli。
>
> **D - Cooking poultry products completely**：更像 Campylobacter 暴露来源。
>
> **E - Receiving age-appropriate vaccinations**：pneumococcal vaccine 当然重要，但题干已经给出 gram-positive **bacilli**，不是 cocci。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Receiving age-appropriate vaccinations。因为年龄大又有 meningitis，容易反射到 pneumococcus，但 **gram-positive bacilli** 明确把你拉回 Listeria。
>

###### Q083

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q083 pulmonary / Pulmonary cachexia
> ^Q083
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**severe COPD + low BMI + poor appetite + muscle wasting + no mass on CXR**，最该想到的是 **pulmonary cachexia from advanced COPD**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Severe COPD on home oxygen | Signals advanced chronic disease | 病情已经足够重，能解释全身消耗 |
> | Lost 7 kg in 1 year, BMI 18 kg/m2 | Meets cachexia-type pattern | 明显体重下降加低 BMI |
> | Thin extremities with decreased muscle mass | Lean body mass loss | 不是单纯“吃得少”，而是肌肉消耗 |
> | Poor appetite | Worsens energy imbalance | COPD cachexia 常有 intake 下降 |
> | Chest x-ray without lung mass | Makes malignancy less likely | 这是用来挡 lung cancer 的关键线索 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Advanced COPD**
>
> **Core Concept**：Severe COPD 可导致 **pulmonary cachexia**。核心机制是 **increased work of breathing + decreased caloric intake + systemic inflammation + muscle hypoxia**，结果是 lean muscle mass 流失、感染风险上升、死亡率增加。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题考的是 COPD 的全身效应，不是肺部局部并发症。患者即使没有急性加重，也会因长期呼吸功耗太高而处于负能量平衡。
>
> ```text
> severe COPD
> -> increased work of breathing
> + poor appetite and low intake
> + systemic inflammation
> -> energy deficit
> -> skeletal muscle wasting
> -> pulmonary cachexia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：COPD患者体重下降鉴别
> | Cause | Typical Clue | Why Not Best Here |
> |---|---|---|
> | Pulmonary cachexia | Severe COPD, low BMI, muscle loss | 本题最符合 |
> | Lung cancer | Mass lesion, more obvious malignant features | CXR 没有 mass |
> | Adrenal insufficiency | Hypotension, fatigue, hypoglycemia | 单次短疗程 prednisone 不太支持 |
> | Right heart failure | Edema and volume overload | 更常体重增加，不是消瘦 |
> | Hyperthyroidism | Heat intolerance, increased appetite | 本题 appetite 差，线索不对 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"COPD can burn muscle even when the lungs are stable."**
> - stable symptoms does not mean no systemic cost
> - home oxygen + low BMI = advanced disease clue
> - no lung mass = do not overcall cancer
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Adrenal insufficiency**：若真有 AI，通常会看到 hypotension、fatigue、甚至 hypoglycemia；一次 2-week prednisone taper 很难造成这种程度问题。
>
> **C - Lung cancer**：吸烟史确实让它成为干扰项，但题干故意给了 **no mass on chest x-ray**，同时整体更像典型 pulmonary cachexia。
>
> **D - Right ventricular failure**：会有下肢水肿、hepatomegaly、容量负荷增加，体重趋势通常不是下降。
>
> **E - Thyroid disease**：hyperthyroidism 常见 appetite increased 和 heat intolerance，本题都不支持。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Lung cancer。因为 smoker + weight loss 很诱人，但本题真正想考的是 **advanced COPD 的 systemic wasting**。
>

###### Q084

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q084 心内 / Femoral artery pseudoaneurysm
> ^Q084
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**post-catheterization groin swelling + pulsatile mass + systolic bruit**，最经典就是 **femoral artery pseudoaneurysm**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Cardiac catheterization 3 days ago | Establishes vascular access complication window | 时间轴非常典型 |
> | Pain and swelling at femoral access site | Local postprocedural complication | 指向穿刺点相关问题 |
> | Pulsatile mass | Suggests arterial communication | 不是普通血肿或静脉问题 |
> | Systolic bruit | High-yield clue for pseudoaneurysm | 收缩期血流冲入假性腔隙产生 bruit |
> | No hypotension or flank pain | Argues against major retroperitoneal bleed | 说明更像局限性并发症 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Femoral artery pseudoaneurysm**
>
> **Core Concept**：Pseudoaneurysm 是 **arterial wall defect** 后，血液漏到周围组织内形成 **contained hematoma**，但仍与动脉腔持续相通。结果就是 **pulsatile mass + systolic bruit**。确诊首选 **ultrasound**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：和 true aneurysm 不同，pseudoaneurysm 的壁不是完整血管壁，而是周围结缔组织把血液“包住”。只要还和动脉腔交通，每次收缩期就会往里灌血。
>
> ```text
> femoral arterial puncture
> -> incomplete seal of arterial wall
> -> blood leaks into periarterial tissue
> -> contained hematoma with arterial communication
> -> pulsatile mass + systolic bruit
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：导管穿刺点并发症
> | Complication | Key Finding | Distinguishing Feature |
> |---|---|---|
> | Pseudoaneurysm | Pulsatile mass, systolic bruit | 本题最典型 |
> | Arteriovenous fistula | Continuous bruit, palpable thrill | Usually no discrete pulsatile mass |
> | Hematoma | Pain, bruising, nonpulsatile mass | No bruit |
> | Arterial thrombosis | 5 Ps of limb ischemia | Distal ischemia dominates |
> | Femoral DVT | Leg swelling/erythema | Venous pattern, no bruit |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Pulses and bruit after puncture = pseudoaneurysm until proven otherwise."**
> - puncture site complication
> - arterial communication
> - ultrasound confirms
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Arteriovenous fistula**：也会有 bruit，但通常是 **continuous bruit + palpable thrill**，而且没有这种典型 pulsatile mass。
>
> **B - Deep femoral artery thrombosis**：应该看到急性 limb ischemia 的 5 Ps，而不是局部搏动性肿块。
>
> **D - Femoral vein thrombosis**：更像下肢 swelling、pain、erythema 的静脉问题，不会听到 bruit。
>
> **E - Hematoma with retroperitoneal extension**：可有疼痛和肿胀，但一般 **不搏动、无 bruit**；严重时还会低血压和 flank pain。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Arteriovenous fistula。因为 catheterization 后也常考这个，但要抓住 **continuous bruit/thrill** 和 **pulsatile mass** 的差别。
>

###### Q085

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q085 GI / Esophageal perforation
> ^Q085
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**sudden severe chest pain + fever + pneumomediastinum/widened mediastinum**，先按 **esophageal perforation** 处理；下一步是 **water-soluble contrast esophagography**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Sudden severe retrosternal chest pain | High-acuity thoracic emergency | 不是普通 reflux pain |
> | Fever and tachycardia | Systemic inflammatory response | perforation 后可迅速引发 mediastinitis |
> | Mediastinal air on chest x-ray | Pneumomediastinum clue | 对食管穿孔非常关键 |
> | Prior mild chest pain and epigastric burning | Suggests antecedent esophagitis | 给出病因背景 |
> | Potassium chloride pills | Classic medication-induced esophagitis trigger | 药物性食管炎进一步进展到 perforation |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Water-soluble contrast esophagography**
>
> **Core Concept**：Esophageal perforation 常表现为 **severe chest pain, fever, tachycardia, pneumomediastinum**。诊断确认用 **water-soluble contrast esophagography** 或 CT with water-soluble contrast。确认后需要 **NPO, IV antibiotics, IV PPI, emergent surgical consultation**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：食管内容物和空气漏到纵隔，会快速造成炎症和感染。因为已经怀疑 perforation，所以任何进一步的 **esophageal instrumentation** 都可能让破口更大。
>
> ```text
> pill-induced esophagitis
> -> mucosal injury
> -> transmural perforation
> -> air and GI contents leak into mediastinum
> -> chest pain + fever + pneumomediastinum
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：胸痛 + widened mediastinum
> | Diagnosis | Key Clue | Next Step |
> |---|---|---|
> | Esophageal perforation | Pneumomediastinum, fever, severe pain | Water-soluble contrast study |
> | Aortic dissection | Tearing pain, pulse deficits, no mediastinal air | CTA or TEE if appropriate |
> | ACS | Ischemic ECG/troponin pattern | Antiplatelet/ACS pathway |
> | Reflux/esophagitis | Burning pain without mediastinal air | Medical therapy |
> | Boerhaave syndrome | Often after forceful vomiting | Same perforation workup and urgent surgery |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Air in the mediastinum means keep the esophagus closed, not instrumented."**
> - no oral meds
> - no endoscopy first
> - no TEE if perforation suspected
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Aspirin, clopidogrel, and repeat ECG in 30 minutes**：ACS 永远要想，但本题 ECG 只有 sinus tachycardia，而影像给出了更定向的 pneumomediastinum。
>
> **B - Oral omeprazole and antacids**：即使 underlying esophagitis 存在，一旦怀疑 perforation 就必须 **NPO**，不能 oral treatment。
>
> **C - Transesophageal echocardiography**：TEE 会进一步 instrument the esophagus，在 suspected perforation 时应避免。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q086 儿科 / DiGeorge T-cell deficiency

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**cleft palate + micrognathia + neonatal hypocalcemic seizure/tetany + new congenital murmur**，直接锁定 **DiGeorge syndrome**，因此最该担心的是 **cellular immune deficiency**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 14-day-old with seizure and muscle spasms | Neonatal hypocalcemia clue | 新生儿抽搐不是先想感染，这里更像低钙 tetany |
> | Cleft palate, low-set ears, small jaw | Abnormal facies pattern | craniofacial anomalies 指向 22q11 deletion |

> | Full-term infant, no fever or URI symptoms | Makes infection less likely as seizure cause | 题干在帮你把 febrile seizure/meningitis 排掉 |
> | Syndromic triad with hypocalcemia | Points to DiGeorge syndrome | cleft palate + heart defect + hypocalcemia 很经典 |
>

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Cellular immune deficiency**
>
> **Core Concept**：DiGeorge syndrome 是 **22q11.2 deletion**，导致 **pharyngeal pouch** 发育异常。副甲状腺发育不良造成 **hypocalcemia**，胸腺发育不良造成 **T-cell deficiency**，所以核心免疫缺陷是 **cell-mediated immunity** 受损。

>
> ```text
> 22q11.2 deletion
> -> abnormal pharyngeal pouch development
> -> thymic hypoplasia/aplasia + parathyroid hypoplasia
> -> T-cell deficiency + hypocalcemia
> -> viral/fungal infection risk + neonatal tetany/seizure
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：DiGeorge 的三条主线
> | Finding | Source | Exam Meaning |
> |---|---|---|
> | Cleft palate / abnormal facies | Craniofacial maldevelopment | 帮你认出综合征 |
> | Hypocalcemia, spasms, seizure | Parathyroid hypoplasia | 新生儿 tetany clue |
> | Cellular immune deficiency | Thymic hypoplasia | T-cell low, viral/fungal infection risk |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"No thymus, no T cells; no parathyroids, low calcium."**
> - palate 有问题，不要只停在面容异常
> - seizure 在这里是 **low Ca**, 不是 CNS infection
> - murmur 说明这不是 isolated cleft palate
>
> ---
>

>
> **C - Hypogammaglobulinemia**：DiGeorge 的 B-cell 数量通常正常，主要问题是 **T-cell development**。
>
> **D - Phagocyte dysfunction**：neutrophil defect 来自骨髓系问题，不是 pharyngeal pouch 发育异常。
>
> **E - Splenic dysfunction**：常见于 sickle cell disease 等，不是本综合征特征。

> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Hypogammaglobulinemia。很多人看到免疫缺陷会泛化成 antibody problem，但这题真正题眼是 **thymic hypoplasia**。

> -> parietal pleura friction with respiration
> -> sharp pleuritic pain

> ```text
> chronic hypertension
> -> concentric LV hypertrophy
> -> stiff LV / diastolic dysfunction
> -> elevated LV filling pressure
> -> elevated LA pressure
> -> pulmonary venous hypertension
> -> increased pulmonary arterial pressure
> ```
>

> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：pulmonary hypertension 分组
> | Cause | Typical Pattern | 与本题区别 |

> | Group 3 lung disease | Severe COPD/ILD, hypoxemia, right-heart strain | 本题无明显肺病 |
> | Group 4 CTEPH | Chronic thromboembolic obstruction, usually normal left heart | 本题左房大不支持 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Big LA means pressure backed up from the left."**

> - preserved EF 不等于没有 heart failure
> - PH can be downstream of left-heart disease
>

>
> **C - Primary pulmonary arterial hypertension**：多为 pulmonary arteriolar remodeling，常见于较年轻患者，且左心通常正常。
>
> **E - Thromboembolic disease**：**group 4 PH** 更像慢性反复 PE，左心大小和功能多正常。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q096 OB / Hyperemesis gravidarum with enlarged uterus
> ^Q096
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**8 weeks pregnant + severe persistent vomiting + ketonuria + hypokalemic hypochloremic metabolic alkalosis + uterus larger than dates**，先诊断 **hyperemesis gravidarum**，下一步别只补液，还要做 **pelvic ultrasound** 排查 **molar pregnancy** 或 **multifetal gestation**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 8 weeks gestation with 2 weeks of severe vomiting | First-trimester hyperemesis window | 妊娠早期严重呕吐首先想 HG |
> | Unable to tolerate intake, orthostasis, dry mucosa, ketonuria | True volume depletion and starvation | 不是普通 morning sickness，而是已到脱水和饥饿酮症 |
> | Potassium 3.1, chloride 85, HCO3 34 | Hypochloremic metabolic alkalosis | 呕吐丢失 gastric acid 的典型实验室组合 |
> | 12-week-size uterus at 8 weeks | Excess beta-hCG clue | 子宫大于孕周要想到 mole 或 multifetal gestation |
> | No abdominal pain, normal AST/lipase | Less support for biliary or pancreatic disease | 把 hepatobiliary distractor 排掉 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Pelvic ultrasound**
>
> **Core Concept**：这题不是在问你会不会认 **hyperemesis gravidarum**，而是在问你会不会继续追它的高危病因。**Hydatidiform mole** 和 **multifetal gestation** 都会让 **beta-hCG** 明显升高，从而提高 HG 风险；再加上 **uterus larger than dates**，因此除 supportive therapy 外必须做 **pelvic ultrasound**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：高水平 **beta-hCG** 与妊娠期恶心呕吐加重相关。若背后是 **molar pregnancy** 或 **multiple gestation**，单纯止吐补液只能处理表面症状，不能完成病因评估。
>
> ```text
> elevated beta-hCG state
> -> severe nausea/vomiting
> -> dehydration + ketonuria + metabolic alkalosis
> -> hyperemesis gravidarum
> -> evaluate pregnancy with pelvic ultrasound
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：pregnancy vomiting 鉴别
> | Scenario | Key Clue | Best Next Thought |
> |---|---|---|
> | Normal nausea/vomiting of pregnancy | Mild, still tolerates PO | Conservative outpatient care |
> | Hyperemesis gravidarum | Weight loss, ketonuria, dehydration, alkalosis | IV fluids + antiemetics |
> | HG + uterus larger than dates | Suspect high beta-hCG state | Pelvic ultrasound |
> | Biliary disease | RUQ pain, transaminitis | Hepatobiliary ultrasound |
> | Refractory upper GI symptoms | Fails medical therapy | Consider EGD later |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"HG plus big uterus = scan the pregnancy."**
> - severe vomiting tells you HG
> - large uterus tells you why
> - answer is not "supportive therapy only"
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Evaluation of insulin and C-peptide levels**：低血糖更像短期饥饿所致，不像 insulinoma 或外源性胰岛素问题。
>
> **B - Evaluation of serum cortisol levels**：adrenal crisis 会更偏 **hyperkalemia** 和 **metabolic acidosis**，与本题不符。
>
> **C - Evaluation of stool for ova and parasites**：这是 chronic diarrhea 方向，不是 isolated vomiting。
>
> **D - Hepatobiliary ultrasound**：若有 RUQ pain 或 transaminitis 才更像胆囊病变。
>
> **F - Supportive therapy only**：补液止吐当然要做，但题眼是 **larger-than-dates uterus**，还必须查病因。
>
> **G - Upper endoscopy**：应留给对规范内科治疗无反应的上消化道症状。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Supportive therapy only。很多人看到典型 HG 就停住，但这题专门加了 **12-week-size uterus at 8 weeks** 来逼你进一步评估。

> **机制说明**：pathologic **FGR** 的核心是胎盘灌注或供氧异常，因此 Doppler 会变差、增长会落后；而 constitutionally small fetus 的胎盘功能正常，所以虽然体重百分位低，但没有长期缺氧或营养不足造成的后遗症。
>
> ```text
> small estimated fetal weight
> -> ask: placenta normal or abnormal?
> -> normal Doppler + normal interval growth
> -> constitutional smallness
> -> normal long-term outcome
> ```

> | Feature | Constitutional smallness | Pathologic FGR |
> |---|---|---|
> | EFW percentile | <10th percentile | <10th percentile |
> | Umbilical artery Doppler | Normal | Often abnormal |
> | Serial growth | Appropriate | Poor interval growth |
> | Placental function | Normal | Impaired |
> | Long-term outcome | Usually normal | Higher neurodevelopmental/metabolic risk |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Small but flowing and growing."**
> - flowing = Doppler normal
> - growing = interval growth normal
> - then long-term outcome is usually fine
>

>
> **B - Childhood obesity**：同样更偏 FGR 的 later metabolic programming 风险。
>
> **C - Delayed cognitive development**：提示宫内病理过程导致的长期神经发育影响，但本题 Doppler 正常。
>
> **D - Delayed puberty**：无论 FGR 还是 constitutional smallness 都不是经典关联。

###### Q099

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q099 psych / SSRI sexual dysfunction
> ^Q099
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**decreased libido + erectile dysfunction + no morning erections**，再看到药单里有 **sertraline**，而 diabetes、hypertension、depression 都控制得不错，最该锁定 **SSRI-induced sexual dysfunction**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Decreased libido and difficulty maintaining erection | Broad sexual dysfunction pattern | 不只是 performance issue，而是欲望和 arousal 都受影响 |
> | No morning erections | Points away from purely psychogenic ED | 缺少 spontaneous erection，支持 organic or medication cause |
> | Depression history but currently stable | Less likely active MDD symptom | 不能简单把锅甩给 depression 本身 |
> | HbA1c 6.5%, normal exam, controlled BP | Comorbidities are present but controlled | 题目在帮你排除 diabetes/HTN 主导病因 |
> | Taking sertraline | High-yield offending medication | SSRI 是最常考性功能副作用药物 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：G - Sertraline**
>
> **Core Concept**：**SSRIs**，尤其 **sertraline**, **fluoxetine**, **paroxetine**，常见副作用就是 **decreased libido**, **erectile dysfunction**, **anorgasmia**, **delayed ejaculation**。有多种潜在病因并存时，要找“最符合题目强调的、最经典的可解释因素”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：增强 serotonergic signaling 会抑制 sexual desire、arousal 和 orgasm pathways，因此患者可能同时出现 libido 下降、勃起困难、射精延迟或高潮困难。
>
> ```text
> SSRI exposure
> -> increased serotonin signaling
> -> reduced libido/arousal/orgasm
> -> sexual dysfunction
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：male sexual dysfunction 常见病因
> | Cause | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | SSRI adverse effect | Started or taking SSRI; libido and orgasm changes | 本题最符合 |
> | Active depression | Low mood, anhedonia, neurovegetative symptoms | 本题未提示当前抑郁 |
> | Diabetes neuropathy/vasculopathy | Poor glycemic control, chronic complications | 本题 A1c 控制尚可 |
> | Performance anxiety | Situational ED with preserved morning erections | 本题晨勃也缺失 |
> | Antihypertensive effect | Often beta blocker or thiazide | enalapril 不是高频元凶 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Sertraline steals sex drive."**
> - libido down
> - erection weaker
> - ejaculation/orgasm can also be delayed
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Depression**：重度抑郁当然会影响性功能，但题目明确提示当前状态稳定，而且过去治疗有效。
>
> **B - Diabetes**：长期可致 ED，但本题血糖控制好，且 UWorld 更想考药物副作用。
>
> **C - Enalapril**：ACE inhibitor 不是典型 cause。
>
> **D - Hypertension**：高血压本身可有关联，但强度不如经典 SSRI adverse effect。
>
> **E - Metformin**：不是性功能障碍常考药。
>
> **F - Performance anxiety**：常为情境性、突发性，而且通常不影响 REM-related spontaneous erections。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Performance anxiety。题干专门提到他担心 sexual performance 来诱导你选心理因素，但这更像结果，不是最主要病因。
>

###### Q100

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q100 OB / Post-cesarean incisional hematoma
> ^Q100
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**1 week after cesarean + increasing incisional pain + dark red sanguineous drainage + obesity + enoxaparin + fascia intact**，最符合 **incisional hematoma**，不是 fascial dehiscence，也不像 infection。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Dark red sanguineous drainage | Blood collection clue | 暗红血性渗出比脓液更像 hematoma |
> | Increasing pain around incision | Expanding collection | 血肿变大时疼痛会加重 |
> | BMI 40 and enoxaparin use | Major risk factors | obesity 和 anticoagulation 都支持 bleeding complication |
> | No erythema or induration | Against cellulitis/abscess | 缺少感染体征 |
> | Swab meets resistance at rectus fascia | Fascial layer intact | 把 fascial dehiscence 关键性排除 |
> | Firm uterus, moderate lochia | Against uterine dehiscence | 子宫切口裂开会更重、更不稳 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Incisional hematoma**
>
> **Core Concept**：**Incisional hematoma** 是术后切口潜在间隙内积血，常表现为 **pain + sanguineous drainage**，风险因素包括 **inadequate hemostasis**, **obesity**, **anticoagulation**。题目里的 swab probing 到 intact fascia 非常重要，说明问题主要在浅层切口，而不是 fascial disruption。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：手术止血不充分或术后凝血能力下降时，切口内血液逐渐聚集，越积越多后会把原本贴合的组织平面重新撑开，于是出现小裂口和血性渗出。
>
> ```text
> surgery + bleeding risk
> -> blood collects at incision
> -> expanding hematoma
> -> pain + sanguineous drainage
> -> small wound opening but intact fascia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：post-cesarean wound complication
> | Diagnosis | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Incisional hematoma | Painful sanguineous drainage, bleeding risk | 本题最符合 |
> | Fascial dehiscence | Popping sensation, bulge, copious serous drainage | 本题 fascia intact |
> | Incisional cellulitis | Erythema, warmth, tenderness | 本题没有 redness/induration |
> | Abscess | Fever, purulent drainage | 本题是血性而非脓性 |
> | Necrotizing fasciitis | Toxic patient, severe pain out of proportion, crepitus/bullae | 本题生命体征稳定 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Red blood from the wound? Think hematoma before infection."**
> - sanguineous, not purulent
> - risk factors for bleeding present
> - fascia still intact
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Fascial dehiscence**：更像切口深层散开，常有 bulge、copious serous drainage，且 probing 不会轻易停在完整 fascia。
>
> **B - Incisional cellulitis**：应该看到 redness、warmth、induration 或脓性改变。
>
> **D - Necrotizing fasciitis**：患者通常 toxic，疼痛失衡明显，可有 crepitus 或 bullae。
>
> **E - Postoperative abscess**：常伴 fever 或 purulent discharge。
>
> **F - Uterine dehiscence**：会更偏 severe abdominal pain、hemodynamic instability、heavy vaginal bleeding，本题 uterus firm 且 lochia moderate。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Fascial dehiscence。因为也会出现切口开口，但真正决定性信息是 **resistance at rectus fascia**，说明 fascia 没开。
>

###### Q101

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q101 GYN / New postmenopausal breast mass
> ^Q101
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**postmenopausal woman + new palpable breast mass + age >30**，即使 **6 months ago mammogram normal**，下一步仍是 **diagnostic mammography**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 56-year-old, postmenopausal | Baseline breast cancer risk is higher | 绝经后新发乳房肿块不能用“先观察”带过 |
> | New palpable 2-cm breast mass | New mass requires workup | 这不是筛查题，是有症状/体征后的诊断题 |
> | Normal mammogram 6 months ago | Recent normal screening does not rule out interval cancer | 近期阴性 screening 不能排除新出现的 malignancy |
> | Upper outer quadrant mass | Common location for breast cancer | 外上象限本身就常见恶性病变 |
> | No nipple discharge | Helps narrow differential, but does not reduce need for imaging | 没有分泌物不代表能跳过影像评估 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Mammography**
>
> **Core Concept**：对 **age >=30** 的女性，出现 **new palpable breast mass** 时，首选 **diagnostic mammography**。它比 ultrasound 更能看出 **calcifications** 等恶性征象，而且本题还能直接和 6 个月前的 screening mammogram 对比。若 mammography 结果正常，再补做 breast ultrasound。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：筛查阴性只说明当时没看出异常，不代表之后不会出现新的肿瘤。对绝经后女性来说，出现新的可触及肿块时，思路要从“screening”切换到“diagnostic evaluation”。
>
> ```text
> postmenopausal woman
> -> new palpable breast mass
> -> malignancy risk is meaningful
> -> diagnostic mammography first
> -> compare with prior screening study
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：palpable breast mass initial test
> | Patient group | Preferred first imaging | 关键理由 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Age <30 | Ultrasound | 乳腺更致密，恶性概率更低 |
> | Age >=30 | Diagnostic mammography | 更擅长识别 calcifications，可与旧片比较 |
> | Mammography normal but mass persists | Add ultrasound | 补充区分 cystic vs solid lesion |
> | Adolescent with low-risk mass | Short-term observation may be considered | 仅限低癌症风险人群 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"New lump after 30? Mammogram the mass, not just the memory of a normal screen."**
> - 新肿块比旧 screening 更重要
> - postmenopausal = baseline risk up
> - recent normal mammogram != no cancer now
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Breast ultrasound**：会用到，但不是本题第一步。对 >=30 岁新发 palpable mass，先做 diagnostic mammography。
>
> **B - Discontinue estrogen therapy**：本题是 **estrogen-only therapy**，而且在肿块性质未定前不需要因为评估本身立刻停药。
>
> **C - Fine-needle aspiration**：适合 symptomatic painful cyst 的处理，不替代初始影像学评估。
>
> **E - Repeat breast examination in 1 month**：这是低风险青少年思路，不适用于绝经后新发肿块。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Breast ultrasound。很多人记得“摸到肿块先 ultrasound”，但真正要分年龄；本题 **age 56** 把首选拉到 diagnostic mammography。
>

###### Q102

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q102 GYN / Female sexual interest/arousal disorder
> ^Q102
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**想维持关系、觉得伴侣有吸引力，但长期缺乏 sexual desire 且因此 distress**，又已排除 dyspareunia、hypothyroidism、active depression、medication effect，最符合 **female sexual interest/arousal disorder**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Loss of libido for 1 year | Persistent low desire | 不是短暂波动，而是持续性问题 |
> | Distressed and wants intimacy | Required clinical significance | DSM 里“本人困扰”很关键 |
> | Finds partner attractive | Against relationship aversion | 不是关系差或没兴趣这个伴侣 |
> | Vaginal estrogen use, no pain with intercourse | Makes dyspareunia less likely | 已经在处理 vaginal atrophy，且无性交痛 |
> | Bupropion, not SSRI | Medication clue | bupropion 通常不致 sexual dysfunction，反而常被拿来改善它 |
> | Normal TSH, neutral mood, no neurovegetative symptoms | Rules out medical/depressive explanation | 排除了 hypothyroidism 和 active major depression |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Female sexual interest/arousal disorder**
>
> **Core Concept**：**female sexual interest/arousal disorder** 指 clinically significant 的性兴趣/唤起下降，前提是不能被 **medical illness**, **substance/medication**, **relationship problem**, 或其他精神疾病更好解释。本题核心不在 menopause 本身，而在“她确实困扰且已排除更常见可逆原因”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：女性性功能障碍常受生理、心理、关系和药物共同影响。题目要求你做的是排除诊断思维: 先排 pain、内分泌、抑郁、药物，再落回原发性的低 desire/arousal 问题。
>
> ```text
> low libido
> + distress
> + wants intimacy
> - dyspareunia
> - thyroid disorder
> - active depression
> - serotonergic medication effect
> = female sexual interest/arousal disorder
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：low libido differential
> | Cause | Typical clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Female sexual interest/arousal disorder | Low desire with distress after exclusion of other causes | 本题最符合 |
> | Dyspareunia | Pain with intercourse, often from vaginal atrophy | 本题无疼痛且已用 vaginal estrogen |
> | SSRI-related dysfunction | Started/worsened after serotonergic antidepressant | 本题用 bupropion |
> | Active major depression | Low mood, anhedonia, sleep/energy/concentration changes | 本题 mental status 不支持 |
> | Performance anxiety | Wants sex but fears poor performance | 本题是 desire 本身低 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"She wants the relationship, not the sex."**
> - attraction still there
> - distress is real
> - rule out pain, pills, physiology
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Adjustment disorder**：有更具体的 sexual dysfunction 诊断时，不再用 adjustment disorder 兜底。
>
> **B - Dyspareunia**：如果是疼痛导致回避，题干会给 pain with intercourse；这里恰恰没有。
>
> **D - Medication-induced sexual dysfunction**：典型要想到 SSRIs，而不是 bupropion。
>
> **E - Normal aging**：postmenopausal 不能直接等于“正常衰老”，尤其她因此明确 distress。
>
> **F - Performance anxiety**：这种患者通常有 desire，但担心表现不好；本题是 desire 减退。
>
> **G - Sexual dysfunction due to a medical condition**：TSH 正常，查体和情绪状态也没有支持性证据。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Normal aging。考点就是不要把绝经后女性的 distressing symptom 轻率归因于年龄。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q104 Biostats_Master / Selection bias by severity
> ^Q104
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：observational study 里如果 **sicker patients are more likely to receive the aggressive treatment**, 后面出现更差结局时，先想 **selection bias / susceptibility bias**，不是治疗本身一定更差。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Observational study | No randomization | 没有随机分配，先天更容易有 baseline imbalance |
> | Early PCI group older | Higher baseline risk | 年龄更大本身就可能结局更差 |
> | More vasopressor use on admission | Marker of greater illness severity | 这是最关键的“更重病”证据 |
> | Early PCI group had higher mortality | Apparent treatment harm | 这个差异未必来自 PCI 本身 |
> | Comparing 2 chosen treatment paths | Risk of confounding by indication | 医生按病情选治疗，天然会把重症患者推向更激进方案 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Selection bias**
>
> **Core Concept**：当治疗方案的选择受到患者初始病情严重程度影响时，比较两组结局就容易出现 **selection bias**。本题里更 sick 的患者更可能被送去 early PCI，因此更高死亡率可能反映的是 **baseline severity**, 不是 PCI 导致死亡增加。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这类题本质是 **confounding by indication**。临床上不会把所有 NSTEMI 患者随机送去同一种方案，真正更危重的人更容易接受更积极治疗，所以未校正分析会把“病更重”错看成“治疗更差”。
>
> ```text
> sicker patient
> -> clinician chooses early PCI
> -> patient already has higher mortality risk
> -> observational comparison shows worse outcome
> -> false impression that PCI caused the harm
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：常见 bias 对比
> | Bias | Core idea | 本题为什么不是它 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Selection bias | Groups differ at baseline before outcome occurs | 本题正确答案 |
> | Hawthorne effect | Subjects change behavior because they know they are studied | 本题不是行为改变问题 |
> | Observer bias | Investigator's knowledge affects outcome assessment | 本题不是结局测量被观察者影响 |
> | Recall bias | Exposure history recalled inaccurately | 本题不是回忆暴露 |
> | Ascertainment bias | Atypical sampled population misrepresents source population | 本题重点不是抽样外推 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"The sickest patients get the strongest treatment."**
> - not randomized
> - sicker at baseline
> - treatment looks guilty, severity is the real confounder
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Ascertainment bias**：更强调抽到的样本人群不代表总体，而不是治疗组在起点就严重程度不同。
>
> **B - Hawthorne effect**：受试者因“知道被研究”而改变行为；本题完全不是这个方向。
>
> **C - Observer bias**：研究者知道暴露状态后影响 outcome assessment，本题并未描述这种测量偏差。
>
> **D - Recall bias**：常见于 case-control，对既往暴露的过度或不足回忆；本题是住院结局比较，不靠患者回忆暴露。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Ascertainment bias。因为它也属于 selection-related error，但本题最精准的点是 **treatment chosen based on severity**。
>

> -> high cortisol
> -> pituitary ACTH suppression
> -> low DHEAS
> -> dexamethasone cannot suppress tumor cortisol production
> ```

> | Ectopic ACTH | Nonsuppressed | High | High |
> | Exogenous glucocorticoid use | Suppressed endogenous cortisol axis | Low | Low |
> | Normal | Suppressed | Normal | Normal |
>
> ---
>

> **"Adrenal makes cortisol alone, so the pituitary goes silent."**

>
> **D - Suppressed / Normal / High**：更偏 androgen excess 或其他非 cortisol 主导图景。
>
> **E - Suppressed / Low / Low**：低 ACTH、低 DHEAS 可以对，但如果 cortisol 还能被 dexamethasone 压下，更像 exogenous steroid effect，不像 autonomous adenoma。

> | Asked for greatest risk "given mother's condition" | Wants infant-of-diabetic-mother physiology | 题目在考 diabetic pregnancy 的新生儿并发症图谱 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Polycythemia**
>
> **Core Concept**：母体 hyperglycemia 导致胎儿 **hyperglycemia + hyperinsulinemia**。胎儿代谢需求上升，而 diabetic placental vasculopathy 又会让氧输送跟不上，形成 **chronic fetal hypoxemia**。胎儿因此分泌更多 **erythropoietin**，最终出现 **polycythemia**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题容易只背“infant of diabetic mother = hypoglycemia”。但本题问的是更上游的长期宫内适应。胎儿长期处在高代谢、高耗氧环境里，所以会通过增加红细胞来补氧。
>
> ```text
> maternal hyperglycemia
> -> fetal hyperglycemia
> -> fetal hyperinsulinemia
> -> increased fetal metabolic demand
> -> relative chronic hypoxemia
> -> increased fetal erythropoietin
> -> polycythemia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：infant of diabetic mother complications
> | Complication | Why It Happens | 与本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Polycythemia | Chronic fetal hypoxemia raises erythropoietin | 本题最佳答案 |
> | Hypoglycemia | Persistent neonatal hyperinsulinemia after cord clamping | 也常见，但不在选项里对应为最强 |
> | Hypocalcemia | Transient low PTH in newborn | 常见，但不是 hypercalcemia |
> | Brachial plexus injury | Shoulder dystocia from macrosomia | cesarean 已降低风险 |
> | HIE | Intrapartum oxygen interruption | 正常 Apgar，不支持 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Big baby, thick blood."**
> - diabetic mother -> macrosomia
> - fetal insulin high -> oxygen demand high
> - chronic hypoxemia -> erythropoietin rises
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Brachial plexus injury**：巨大儿确实会让人想到 shoulder dystocia，但题目特地写了 **scheduled cesarean delivery**，就是在替你排这个。
>
> **B - Cardiac arrhythmia**：先天 heart block 更像 maternal lupus；diabetic mother 更常见的是 **hypertrophic cardiomyopathy**。
>
> **C - Hypercalcemia**：方向相反，infants of diabetic mothers 常见的是 **hypocalcemia**。
>
> **D - Hyperglycemia**：脐带一夹，母体葡萄糖供应结束，但胎儿高 insulin 还在，所以更该担心 **hypoglycemia**。
>
> **E - Hypoxic-ischemic encephalopathy**：通常伴低 Apgar、poor tone、差的呼吸努力，本题没有。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Brachial plexus injury。因为大家一看到 `5.4 kg` 会先冲向 macrosomia trauma，但本题用 `scheduled cesarean` 把这个陷阱提前拆掉了。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q108 心内 / Amaurosis fugax from carotid plaque
> ^Q108
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**painless transient monocular vision loss for minutes** 就是 **amaurosis fugax**，最常见病因是 **ipsilateral internal carotid atherosclerotic plaque embolization**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Sudden darkness in one eye | Monocular event localizes anterior to chiasm | 单眼发作先想 retina/optic nerve circulation |
> | Resolved in about 5 minutes | Transient ischemic pattern | 持续几分钟又恢复，非常像 embolus 短暂堵塞后再通 |
> | Painless and no focal neurologic deficits | Favors retinal ischemia over stroke syndrome | 没有 MCA/PCA 那套神经定位体征 |
> | Recurrent episode months earlier | Ongoing embolic source | 说明上游病灶一直在，最常见就是 carotid plaque |
> | Diabetes, hypertension, hyperlipidemia, smoking | Strong atherosclerotic risk profile | 这些危险因素把答案推向 carotid atheroembolism |
> | Regular pulse | Makes atrial fibrillation less likely | 减少 left atrial appendage thrombus 可能 |
>

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Ulcerated plaque in the internal carotid artery**
>
> **Core Concept**：**amaurosis fugax** 是短暂、无痛、单眼的视力丧失，通常由 **ipsilateral carotid plaque** 脱落的小 emboli 造成 retinal ischemia。它既是眼部症状，也是 **stroke warning sign**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题本质上是把 transient ischemic attack 的概念放到 retina 上。embolus 暂时堵住 ophthalmic artery 或 central retinal artery 的分支，随后碎裂或移位，所以视力恢复。
>
> ```text
> carotid atherosclerotic plaque
> -> cholesterol or platelet embolus
> -> transient retinal arterial occlusion
> -> brief monocular ischemia
> -> amaurosis fugax
> -> future stroke risk if source not treated
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：短暂视力丧失定位
> | Pattern | Typical Cause | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Transient monocular blindness | Carotid atheroembolism | 本题完全符合 |
> | Sudden persistent monocular blindness | Central retinal artery occlusion | 本题会自行恢复 |
> | Homonymous hemianopsia | PCA stroke | 是双眼同向视野缺损，不是单眼 |
> | Major focal deficits with visual loss | MCA stroke | 应有 hemiparesis 或 aphasia 等 |
> | Progressive bilateral loss | Diabetic retinopathy | 不会是 5 分钟自行恢复的单眼事件 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Curtain down in one eye = carotid until proven otherwise."**
> - monocular
> - transient
> - vascular risk factors heavy
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Dot and blot hemorrhage of the retina**：这是 diabetic retinopathy 体征，通常造成慢性进行性视力问题，不是几分钟的单眼 blackout。
>
> **B - Inflammation of the ophthalmic artery**：指向 giant cell arteritis，但题里没有 headache、jaw claudication、polymyalgia rheumatica 等支持点。
>
> **C - Mural thrombus in the left atrial appendage**：需要先有 atrial fibrillation 之类的 cardioembolic 背景，本题 pulse regular。
>
> **D - Thromboembolism in the posterior cerebral artery**：PCA 病变造成的是 **contralateral homonymous hemianopsia**，不是一只眼突然全黑。
>
> **E - Thrombotic occlusion of the middle cerebral artery**：会有明显 neurologic deficits，不会只剩 isolated transient monocular vision loss。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Inflammation of the ophthalmic artery。因为 giant cell arteritis 也会导致 transient monocular visual symptoms，但本题年龄虽够，系统炎症线索完全不够。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**allopurinol 6 weeks later + diffuse morbilliform rash + facial edema + eosinophilia + transaminitis**，就是 **DRESS syndrome**，下一步先 **stop the offending drug**。

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Allopurinol started 6 weeks ago | Classic DRESS latency | DRESS 和普通药疹不同，常延迟 2-8 周出现 |

> | Eosinophils 15% | "E" in DRESS | 明确把病变拉向 hypersensitivity syndrome |

> | No mucosal involvement or skin blistering | Less consistent with SJS/TEN | 排除另一类严重药物反应 |

> **机制说明**：DRESS 的核心不是某一种皮损，而是“延迟出现的药物反应 + eosinophilia + systemic involvement”。看到 rash 之外还有肝酶升高和 lymphadenopathy，就不能把它当作单纯 viral exanthem 或普通 allergy。
>
> ```text
> high-risk drug exposure
> -> delayed hypersensitivity reaction
> -> diffuse morbilliform eruption
> + eosinophilia
> + lymphadenopathy
> + organ injury such as hepatitis
> -> DRESS syndrome
> -> immediate drug withdrawal
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：严重 rash 鉴别
> | Diagnosis | Key Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | DRESS | 2-8 week latency, eosinophilia, facial edema, organ injury | 本题完全符合 |
> | Simple drug eruption | Rash only, minimal systemic findings | 本题已有 transaminitis 和 nodes |
> | SJS/TEN | Mucosal involvement and blistering | 本题明确没有 |
> | EBV mononucleosis | Fever, pharyngitis, lymphocytosis | 本题是 eosinophilia，不是 pharyngitis 主导 |
> | Secondary syphilis | Diffuse rash, often palms/soles | 解释不了 allopurinol latency 与 eosinophilia |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"DRESS gets dressed slowly."**
> - delayed after drug start
> - eosinophils rise
> - systemic organs join in
>
> ---

> **正确答案：D - Colonic pseudo-obstruction**
>
> **Core Concept**：**Ogilvie syndrome** 是 postoperative 或严重应激状态下的 **functional colonic obstruction**。影像看到 **large-bowel dilation without a mechanical lesion**，临床上要警惕继续扩张后出现 **colonic ischemia 或 perforation**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：本题不是“术后任何不排便都叫 ileus”。ileus 更早、更弥漫；Ogilvie 是 **autonomic dysregulation** 导致的 **isolated colonic dysmotility**，所以会出现明显 colonic dilation。
>
> ```text
> surgery/illness + opioids + electrolyte abnormality
> -> autonomic imbalance in colon
> -> impaired colonic motility
> -> progressive colonic dilation without blockage
> -> risk of ischemia and perforation
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Key Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Ogilvie syndrome | Postop, colonic dilation, no anatomic obstruction | 本题完全符合 |
> | Postoperative ileus | Usually earlier, diffuse bowel hypomotility | 不强调 isolated severe colonic dilation，且常在肠功能尚未恢复前发生 |
> | Cecal volvulus | Mechanical torsion | imaging 应该提示明确 obstruction pattern |
> | Toxic megacolon | Colitis + systemic toxicity | 前面应有 diarrhea / inflammatory colitis 线索 |
> | Opioid constipation | Constipation after analgesics | 通常不足以解释显著 colonic dilation 到 splenic flexure |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Big colon after big surgery, but no block on CT."**
> - postop
> - colon huge
> - no true obstruction
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Acute infectious colitis**：应该更像 diarrhea +/- fever/leukocytosis，不是 obstipation。
>
> **B - Acute ischemic colitis**：典型是 crampy pain + hematochezia，不是单纯巨大结肠扩张。
>
> **C - Cecal volvulus**：是机械性扭转，影像学不该让 contrast 这么顺利走到 distal colon。
>
> **E - Opioid-induced constipation**：能解释便秘，但解释不了这种明显 colonic dilation pattern。
>
> **F - Postoperative ileus**：很像，但通常更早、范围更弥漫，也不太会在术后已经恢复 bowel function 后再这样出现。
>
> **G - Toxic megacolon**：需要 colitis 背景和 systemic toxicity。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Postoperative ileus。两者都可术后腹胀不排便，但本题决定点是 **severe colonic dilation without anatomic obstruction**。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q116 肾脏 / Hypertonic hyponatremia in HHS
> ^Q116
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：**glucose 986 mg/dL + sodium 125 mEq/L + lethargy/myoclonic jerks**，先把这题识别成 **hyperosmolar hyperglycemic state**，其低钠本质是 **translocational hyponatremia from high serum osmolality**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Missed antidiabetic medications for 3 days | Sets up severe hyperglycemia | 停药后最容易出现 HHS 失控 |
> | Glucose 986 mg/dL | Extreme hyperglycemia | 这个数值足以先想 hypertonic state |
> | Sodium 125 mEq/L | Apparent hyponatremia | 不是先补“低钠病因分类”，而是要问它是真低钠还是 translocational |
> | Lethargy, occasional myoclonic jerks | Hyperosmolar neurologic dysfunction | 高渗状态可直接引起神经症状 |
> | Urine sodium 10 mEq/L with hemoconcentration | Volume depletion from osmotic diuresis | 提示肾脏在保钠，不支持 renal tubular dysfunction |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - High blood osmolality**
>
> **Core Concept**：这不是 classic hypotonic hyponatremia，而是 **hypertonic hyponatremia**。严重 **hyperglycemia** 让 extracellular compartment 变得高渗，水从 intracellular space 被拉出来，结果 serum sodium 被稀释，形成 **translocational hyponatremia**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：题眼不是“低钠”本身，而是“什么东西把水从细胞里拖出来”。这里的 osmotically active solute 就是 glucose。
>
> ```text
> severe hyperglycemia
> -> high extracellular osmolality
> -> water shifts out of cells
> -> extracellular sodium gets diluted
> -> hypertonic hyponatremia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：hyponatremia 先看 tonicity
> | Pattern | Typical Setting | 本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Hypotonic hyponatremia | SIADH, polydipsia, volume depletion | 不是本题核心 |
> | Hypertonic hyponatremia | Hyperglycemia, mannitol | 本题答案 |
> | Isotonic pseudohyponatremia | Hyperlipidemia, paraproteinemia | 与极高 glucose 不符 |
> | Low urine sodium in hypovolemia | Appropriate renal sodium retention | 说明肾小管在正常反应，不是 tubular dysfunction |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Very high sugar makes sodium look low."**
> - 先看 glucose，再解读 sodium。
> - 高渗把水拉出细胞，不是总钠真的先丢掉。
> - insulin 纠正 glucose 后，sodium 往往会回升。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Nonosmotic release of antidiuretic hormone**：volume depletion 的确可刺激 ADH，但那更偏向 **hypotonic** hyponatremia，不是本题的高渗机制。
>
> **C - Renal tubular dysfunction**：BUN/Cr ratio 升高且 urine sodium 低，说明肾脏在积极保钠，反而支持 prerenal volume depletion。
>
> **D - Secondary hyperaldosteronism**：RAAS activation 会发生，但不是这道题低钠的直接解释。
>
> **E - Volume depletion with excessive water intake**：喝水和脱水可让低钠更复杂，但题目最强信号是 glucose 986 导致的 translocational shift。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B。因为题干确实有 poor intake 和 volume depletion，但如果忽略 **glucose 986 mg/dL**，就会把“低钠”误判成普通 ADH 相关低钠。

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Echocardiography**
>
> **Core Concept**：**coarctation of the aorta** 会造成 **proximal hypertension** 和 **decreased distal perfusion**。成年患者常因高血压或头痛被发现，体检最重要的是 **brachial-femoral pulse delay**。确诊首选 **echocardiography**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：缩窄通常位于 **distal to the left subclavian artery**。缩窄近端压高，远端流量低，所以出现“上面高压、下面脉慢”的分布性线索。
>
> ```text
> aortic narrowing distal to left subclavian
> -> high pressure proximal to lesion
> -> reduced flow distal to lesion
> -> arm hypertension + delayed femoral pulses
> -> LV pressure overload
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：年轻高血压的常见二级病因线索
> | Condition | Signature Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Coarctation of the aorta | Brachial-femoral pulse delay | 本题答案 |
> | Primary hyperaldosteronism | HTN + hypokalemia | 本题无低钾，且 pulse delay 不支持 |
> | Pheochromocytoma | Episodic HTN, palpitations, sweating | 本题是持续性高压 |
> | Fibromuscular dysplasia | Young woman, renovascular HTN | 不解释 delayed femoral pulses |
> | Peripheral arterial disease | Leg ischemia in older atherosclerotic patient | 年龄和高血压模式都不符 |
>

> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Arm pressure up, leg pulse late: think aortic coarctate."**
> - young hypertensive patient
> - femoral delay
> - echo next

> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Ambulatory blood pressure monitoring**：这题不是“有没有高血压不确定”，而是已经有 repeated elevated readings 加上体征指向具体病因。
>
> **B - CT scan of the adrenal glands**：适合怀疑 aldosterone-producing adenoma，但本题的 pulse delay 完全不搭。
>
> **D - Lower extremity duplex imaging**：PAD 会让下肢脉弱，但不该出现在这个年龄，也解释不了上半身高压分布。
>
> **E - Plasma and urine catecholamine measurement**：pheochromocytoma 更像 paroxysmal headache, flushing, sweating, tachycardia。
>
> **F - Renovascular imaging**：fibromuscular dysplasia 常见于年轻女性，且不应导致 brachial-femoral pulse delay。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F。因为“young patient with secondary HTN”很容易让人想到 fibromuscular dysplasia，但 **delayed femoral pulses** 这一条直接把定位拉回 aorta。
