> [!example]- UWSA2 Q001 OB / Galactocele after breastfeeding
> ^Q001
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：产后 7 个月、刚停止 breastfeeding 后出现 soft, mobile, nontender subareolar breast mass，优先锁定 **galactocele（乳汁潴留囊肿）**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 7 months postpartum, breastfed for 6 months | Timing is weeks to months after breastfeeding cessation | 断奶后乳管堵塞，乳汁滞留形成囊肿 |
> | Soft, mobile, nontender 4-cm subareolar mass | Classic benign milk retention cyst exam | 软、可移动、无痛、乳晕下，是 galactocele 的典型组合 |
> | No fever, erythema, skin change, or lymphadenopathy | Makes infection and malignancy less likely | 没有乳腺脓肿或炎性/恶性乳腺病变的红旗 |
> | No bloody nipple discharge | Makes intraductal papilloma less likely | 乳管内乳头状瘤常见小肿块加血性溢液 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Galactocele（乳汁潴留囊肿）**
>
> **Core Concept**：Galactocele 是 benign milk retention cyst，常在停止哺乳后数周到数月发生。乳管阻塞导致 milk accumulation，体检常见 soft, mobile, nontender subareolar mass。首选 ultrasound，needle aspiration 可确认诊断，也可治疗有症状的囊肿。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：断奶后乳汁仍可短期产生，如果乳管出口被堵，乳汁停在导管/腺泡内，形成无菌性、可移动的囊性肿块。
>
> ```text
> cessation of breastfeeding
> -> milk stasis
> -> ductal blockage
> -> retained milk cyst
> -> soft mobile nontender subareolar mass
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：产后/哺乳相关 breast mass
> | Diagnosis | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Galactocele | After breastfeeding cessation; soft, mobile, nontender | 本题完全符合 |
> | Breast abscess | Fever, tender fluctuant mass, erythema | 本题 afebrile, nontender, no erythema |
> | Fat necrosis | Trauma, ecchymosis, firm irregular mass | 本题无 trauma/ecchymosis |
> | Fibroadenoma | Teen/young woman, estrogen-responsive mobile mass | 可移动无痛会迷惑，但断奶后新发更支持 galactocele |
> | Intraductal papilloma | Small subareolar lesion with bloody nipple discharge | 本题无血性溢液 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"断奶后软包 = milk trapped in duct"**
> - After breastfeeding stops：先想 galactocele。
> - Soft + mobile + nontender：偏 benign cyst。
> - Subareolar：乳管区域，更贴合 milk retention。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Abscess**：需要 fever、tender/fluctuant mass、surrounding erythema；本题都没有。
>
> **B - Fat necrosis**：常有 trauma 或 ecchymosis，可无痛但通常不是断奶后乳晕下软性囊肿。
>
> **C - Fibroadenoma**：也是 mobile/nontender，所以是强干扰；但 fibroadenoma estrogen-responsive，多见青春期/年轻女性，断奶后新发乳晕下大软包更像 galactocele。
>
> **D - Fibrocystic changes**：通常 diffuse, bilateral, cyclic breast pain/nodularity，而非单侧 4-cm subareolar mass。
>
> **F - Intraductal papilloma**：多为小的乳头附近肿块，典型伴 bloody nipple discharge。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Fibroadenoma。看到 mobile/nontender 容易选它，但题眼是 recently stopped breastfeeding + subareolar mass。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q002 外科 / Strangulated inguinal hernia
> ^Q002
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：older man + acute painful inguinal mass + bilious vomiting/abdominal distension + fever/erythema = **strangulated inguinal hernia**，手术中最可能见到 ischemic bowel segment。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Acute painful right groin swelling | Hernia complication rather than chronic benign bulge | 急性痛性腹股沟包块提示 incarceration/strangulation |
> | Bilious vomiting, distension, hyperactive bowel sounds | Mechanical bowel obstruction | 肠袢卡住后出现梗阻表现 |
> | Fever, tachycardia, erythema, marked tenderness | Compromised blood supply and inflammation | 发热、红、压痛重提示 strangulation，而不仅是可复性疝 |
> | Urgent exploratory groin surgery | Strangulated hernia needs immediate operation | 题干已经把处理推向急诊手术 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Ischemic bowel segment（缺血性肠段）**
>
> **Core Concept**：Incarcerated hernia 是肠管被卡住；如果血供被压断，就变成 strangulated hernia，导致 ischemic necrosis。急性腹股沟痛性肿块合并 bowel obstruction signs 和 systemic inflammatory signs 时，不要停在“疝”这个诊断，要进一步想到 **肠段缺血坏死**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：疝囊颈部卡住肠袢，先造成 venous congestion 和肠梗阻；持续受压后 arterial inflow 也下降，最终形成 ischemic bowel segment。
>
> ```text
> inguinal hernia
> -> trapped bowel loop
> -> incarceration
> -> venous congestion and obstruction
> -> impaired arterial flow
> -> strangulation
> -> ischemic bowel segment
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：腹股沟包块的手术所见
> | Finding | Best Match | 关键排除点 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Ischemic bowel segment | Strangulated inguinal hernia | 本题有梗阻 + 发热 + 红痛 |
> | Keratin-filled cyst | Epidermoid cyst | 皮肤结节，不造成 bilious vomiting |
> | Dilated tortuous veins | Varicocele | 慢性阴囊/腹股沟坠胀，不造成肠梗阻 |
> | Infarcted testis | Testicular torsion | 高位横卧睾丸、急性阴囊痛 |
> | Localized pus | Abscess | 可红肿痛，但不解释 hyperactive bowel sounds 和 bilious vomiting |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Painful groin bulge + vomiting = bowel is trapped, not just bulging."**
> - Painful mass：疝内容物被卡。
> - Vomiting/distension：肠腔被堵。
> - Fever/erythema：血供出问题，进入 strangulation。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Arteriovenous fistula**：PCI 后 femoral vessel complication 可有 thrill、distal ischemia 或 high-output heart failure；不会造成急性肠梗阻。
>
> **B - Cyst containing keratinous material**：epidermoid cyst 是皮肤内角蛋白结节，可感染发炎，但不导致 bilious vomiting。
>
> **C - Dilated, tortuous blood vessels**：varicocele 是 pampiniform plexus 扩张，通常慢性钝痛/坠胀。
>
> **D - Infarcted testes**：testicular torsion 多见儿童青少年，表现为急性阴囊痛、高位横卧睾丸。
>
> **F - Localized pus collection**：inguinal abscess 可红肿痛，但 bowel sounds 应正常或低下，不能解释 obstructive symptoms。
>
> **G - Malignant deposits in lymph nodes**：恶性腹股沟淋巴结多为亚急性/慢性，无数小时内梗阻表现。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Localized pus collection。红、热、痛会像 abscess，但 bilious vomiting + hyperactive bowel sounds 把答案拉回 bowel obstruction/strangulation。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q003 肾脏 / Prerenal AKI after overdiuresis
> ^Q003
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：COPD/right-sided heart failure 住院后 furosemide 好转，但随后 flat neck veins + BUN/Cr >20 + bland urine = 过度利尿导致 **decreased intravascular volume -> prerenal AKI**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | COPD on home oxygen, loud P2, JVD, edema, ascites | Pulmonary hypertension with right-sided heart failure | COPD 造成 pulmonary HTN/cor pulmonale，右心衰表现 |
> | Improved with IV furosemide | Diuresis reduced preload/congestion | 初期利尿有效说明容量过多被纠正 |
> | Now flat neck veins despite residual edema | Intravascular volume has fallen too much | 外周仍水肿不等于血管内容量足够 |
> | Urine output falls 4.2 -> 3.1 -> 1.5 L/day | New AKI with oliguria trend | 利尿后尿量反降，提示肾灌注不足 |
> | BUN 52, Cr 2.1, bland urinalysis | Prerenal pattern | BUN/Cr >20 且尿沉渣干净，是 prerenal AKI |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Decreased intravascular volume（血管内容量下降）**
>
> **Core Concept**：Right ventricular dysfunction 患者 often preload-dependent。利尿过猛时，即使腿肿和 ascites 还没完全消失，真正供肾脏灌注的 intravascular volume 可能已经不足，导致 prerenal AKI。典型表现是 decreased urine output、BUN/Cr ratio >20:1、urine sediment bland。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：右心衰既会造成静脉系统淤血，也会让心输出依赖适当 preload；过度利尿会把有效循环容量抽低，肾脏感知到低灌注后加强钠水和尿素重吸收。
>
> ```text
> right-sided heart failure
> -> congestion and edema
> -> IV furosemide
> -> excessive intravascular volume loss
> -> decreased renal perfusion
> -> increased urea reabsorption
> -> BUN/Cr ratio >20 with bland urine
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：AKI 线索定位
> | Pattern | Key Finding | 本题对应 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Prerenal AKI | BUN/Cr >20, FeNa <1%, bland sediment | BUN 52 / Cr 2.1，尿检无 cells/casts/protein |
> | Intrinsic AKI | Casts, protein, inflammatory urine findings | 本题尿沉渣干净 |
> | Postrenal AKI | Obstruction, hydronephrosis, anuria/retention | 前列腺稍大但仍有尿量，不支持显著梗阻 |
> | Hepatorenal syndrome | Cirrhosis + splanchnic vasodilation | 本题是 COPD/pulmonary HTN/right HF |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Edema outside, dry inside."**
> - 右心衰患者可以一边外周水肿，一边血管内容量不足。
> - Flat neck veins 是本题反转点。
> - 利尿后 BUN/Cr 拉高：先想 prerenal。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Bilateral renal artery stenosis**：也可低肾灌注，但本题 AKI 紧随利尿和 flat neck veins 出现，更像容量不足。
>
> **B - Bladder outlet obstruction**：老年男性和前列腺轻大是诱饵；但持续尿量存在，且题干没有 retention/hydronephrosis，不能解释 prerenal labs。
>
> **C - Decreased afferent arteriolar dilation**：典型为 NSAID 抑制 prostaglandin 后 afferent arteriole constriction；本题无 NSAID。
>
> **E - Impaired cardiac contractility**：初期利尿后临床好转，不像新的泵衰；现在 flat neck veins 更支持血管内容量不足。
>
> **F - Renal parenchymal inflammation**：interstitial nephritis/内源性 AKI 应有白细胞、casts 或 protein 等尿检异常。
>
> **G - Splanchnic arterial dilation**：见于 cirrhosis 的 hepatorenal syndrome；本题 ascites 来自右心衰而非肝硬化。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Bladder outlet obstruction。题干给 enlarged prostate，但 bland urine + ongoing urine output + overdiuresis 时间轴才是核心。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q004 endocrine / Primary adrenal insufficiency needs mineralocorticoid
> ^Q004
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：primary adrenal insufficiency 用 hydrocortisone 后仍 orthostatic hypotension + dizziness = aldosterone replacement 不足，最可能 **high plasma renin activity**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Known primary adrenal insufficiency | Whole adrenal cortex affected | 原发性肾上腺皮质功能不全会影响三层皮质 |
> | Hydrocortisone improved fatigue/weight loss | Glucocorticoid replacement helped | cortisol 缺乏症状改善 |
> | Persistent dizziness and BP 100/80 seated -> 75/50 standing | Ongoing mineralocorticoid deficiency | aldosterone 不足导致盐丢失、血容量低、体位性低血压 |
> | Hyperpigmentation | High ACTH supports primary disease | ACTH 高刺激 melanocortin receptor |
> | Autoimmune background | Autoimmune adrenalitis likely | celiac + family thyroid disease 指向 autoimmune polyglandular tendency |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - High plasma renin activity（血浆肾素活性升高）**
>
> **Core Concept**：Primary adrenal insufficiency 破坏 zona glomerulosa、fasciculata、reticularis，所以既缺 cortisol，也缺 aldosterone 和 adrenal androgens。Hydrocortisone 只有部分 mineralocorticoid activity，生理剂量通常不足以替代 aldosterone；持续 orthostatic hypotension 提示需要 fludrocortisone。Aldosterone 低时 RAAS 被激活，因此 plasma renin activity 升高。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：原发性肾上腺皮质受损时，aldosterone 下降导致 sodium wasting、volume depletion、hyperkalemia/hyponatremia；肾脏感知低有效循环量后提高 renin。
>
> ```text
> primary adrenal cortex destruction
> -> low aldosterone
> -> sodium wasting and volume depletion
> -> orthostatic hypotension
> -> RAAS activation
> -> high plasma renin activity
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：Primary vs Secondary adrenal insufficiency
> | Feature | Primary adrenal insufficiency | Secondary/central adrenal insufficiency |
> |---|---|---|
> | Cortisol | Low | Low |
> | Aldosterone | Low | Usually normal |
> | ACTH | High | Low/inappropriately normal |
> | Pigmentation | Hyperpigmentation | No hyperpigmentation |
> | Electrolytes | Hyponatremia, hyperkalemia | Usually minimal electrolyte disturbance |
> | Renin | High if mineralocorticoid under-replaced | Usually not the defining issue |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Primary = all cortex layers are down."**
> - Glomerulosa down：low aldosterone -> high renin。
> - Fasciculata down：low cortisol -> high ACTH。
> - Reticularis down：low adrenal androgens。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - High plasma DHEA-S**：DHEA-S 来自 zona reticularis；primary adrenal insufficiency 会降低 adrenal androgens。
>
> **C - Hypernatremia**：aldosterone 缺乏导致 sodium wasting，更可能 hyponatremia。
>
> **D - Hypokalemia**：aldosterone 缺乏使 potassium 排泄下降，更可能 hyperkalemia。
>
> **E - Low circulating norepinephrine**：adrenal medulla 不直接被 primary adrenal insufficiency 破坏；norepinephrine 通常正常。可下降的是 epinephrine conversion 相关部分，但不是本题选项。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Low circulating norepinephrine。看到 adrenal 会想到 catecholamine，但本题核心是 cortex 的 mineralocorticoid replacement 不足。
>

> |---|---|---|
> | 20-kg weight loss, BMI 19 | Energy deficit suppresses hypothalamic GnRH | 体重显著下降是 FHA 的强题眼 |
> | Low FSH with normal prolactin and TSH | Hypothalamic-pituitary-ovarian suppression | 不是高泌乳素，也不是甲状腺导致 |
> | Decreased vaginal rugation | Low estrogen state | 阴道皱襞减少提示 hypoestrogenemia |
> | Progesterone challenge: no bleeding | Endometrium is not estrogen-primed | 内膜太薄，给孕激素也撤退不出血 |
> | Copper IUD | Nonhormonal contraception | copper IUD 不应导致闭经 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Functional hypothalamic amenorrhea（功能性下丘脑性闭经）**
>
> **Core Concept**：Functional hypothalamic amenorrhea 由 caloric deficiency、significant weight loss、strenuous exercise、chronic illness 或 stress 触发。能量不足和压力信号改变下丘脑调控，GnRH pulsatility 下降，LH/FSH 下降，卵巢 estrogen 下降。因为 endogenous estrogen 不足，endometrium 没有被增生，progesterone challenge test 不会出现 withdrawal bleeding。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：FHA 是 HPO axis 被“节能模式”关闭，不是卵巢坏了。低 estrogen 会造成 amenorrhea、vaginal dryness/decreased rugation、骨量下降/应力性骨折风险。
>
> ```text
> weight loss or low energy availability
> -> decreased leptin and altered stress signals
> -> decreased GnRH pulsatility
> -> decreased LH and FSH
> -> decreased ovarian estrogen
> -> thin endometrium
> -> no withdrawal bleeding after progesterone
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：Secondary amenorrhea + progesterone challenge
> | Result/Clue | Interpretation | 本题对应 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Positive pregnancy test | Pregnancy | 本题 negative |
> | High prolactin | Hyperprolactinemia | 本题 prolactin normal |
> | Abnormal TSH | Thyroid disease | 本题 TSH normal |
> | High FSH | Primary ovarian insufficiency | 本题 FSH low |
> | No progesterone withdrawal bleeding + low FSH | Low estrogen due central suppression | 本题 functional hypothalamic amenorrhea |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Low weight -> low GnRH -> low FSH -> low estrogen -> no bleed."**
> - Copper IUD 是非激素避孕，不该让月经停掉。
> - Progestin challenge 没血：先问有没有 estrogen 打底。
> - Low FSH：问题在上游，不在卵巢本身。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Copper intrauterine device use**：progestin IUD 可闭经；copper IUD 非激素，通常仍有月经，甚至可能经量增多。
>
> **C - Lactational amenorrhea**：常发生在正在 breastfeeding 时；她已经停止哺乳一年。
>
> **D - Postpartum thyroiditis**：通常发生在产后一年内，可有 fatigue、heat intolerance、tachycardia，且 TSH 异常；本题 TSH 正常。
>
> **E - Primary ovarian insufficiency**：卵巢衰竭时 FSH 应升高；本题 FSH low，说明中枢驱动不足。
>
> **F - Sheehan syndrome**：典型与严重 postpartum hemorrhage、产后不能哺乳、多种 anterior pituitary hormone 低有关；本题能哺乳一年且 TSH 正常。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Sheehan syndrome。它也可 low FSH 和 postpartum 语境，但“能哺乳 + TSH 正常 + 显著减重”把答案推向 FHA。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q007 Biostats_Master / Confidence interval width
> ^Q007
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：三组 sample size 都是 100 时，谁的 **SD 更大，95% CI 就更宽**；本题 drug Z 的 interval 最宽，所以它的 effects range 最宽。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 3 groups of 100 each | Same N removes sample-size confounding | 每组样本量相同，比较 CI 宽度时主要看 SD |
> | Drug Z SD = 3.32, highest | Larger SD means larger standard error | SD 越大，数据分散越大 |
> | CI for Z = 82.3-83.7 | Widest interval among the 3 groups | Z 的 95% CI 宽度是 1.4，最大 |
> | No dose-response information | Potency cannot be inferred | 没给 dose-response，不能谈 potency |
> | No mortality outcome | Mortality conclusion impossible | 终点只有 glucose，不是生存结局 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Drug Z likely has the widest range of effects on serum glucose values**
>
> **Core Concept**：95% confidence interval 反映总体真实均值可能落入的范围。公式是 `mean +/- 1.96 x standard error`，而 `SE = SD / sqrt(N)`。本题三组 `N` 相同，因此 **CI 谁更宽，本质上就是谁的 SD 更大**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：当样本量固定时，individual values 越分散，sample mean 的不确定性越大，所以 confidence interval 会变宽。
>
> ```text
> same N
> -> compare SD
> -> larger SD
> -> larger standard error
> -> wider confidence interval
> -> wider likely range of population effect
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：CI 题常见判断
> | Statement Type | Can You Infer It? | 原因 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Wider CI = more variable estimated effect | Yes | 直接来自 CI 定义 |
> | Potency of a drug | No | 需要 dose-response curve |
> | Mortality benefit/harm | No | 需要 mortality outcome data |
> | Statistical difference between groups | Sometimes | 看 CI overlap 或直接看 hypothesis test |
> | Precision of estimate | Yes | Narrower CI = more precise |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Same N? CI width follows SD."**
> - `SE = SD / sqrt(N)`。
> - N 不变时，SD 是主导。
> - 宽 CI = less precise estimate。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Drug X is likely the most potent**：potency 需要 log-dose response curve，不是看哪个均值更低。
>
> **B - Drug Y increases mortality**：题目根本没测 mortality，不能瞎外推。
>
> **D - Sample selection is improper**：同一医院招募并随机分组，题干反而是在提示 sampling/assignment 基本合理。
>
> **E - No statistically significant difference between X and Z**：两组 CI 不重叠，反而支持有 statistically significant difference。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E。很多人把 “均值接近” 错当成 “无显著差异”，但 interval 不重叠时通常说明差异存在。
>

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：同事已经出现 prescribing error 且拒绝进一步评估时，优先上报 **hospital physician health program (PHP)**，不是继续观察，也不是直接跳 state board。

>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Progressive forgetfulness over months | Suggests impaired physician, not one-time mistake | 是持续性功能受损，不是偶发疏忽 |
> | Misplaced prescription pad and dose error | Patient safety is already affected | 已经出现处方安全问题，不能再拖 |

> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：医学伦理并不是“等造成真实伤害再处理”，而是当安全风险已经显现时，启动最合适、最规范、同时最有助于患者和同事的报告渠道。

> ```text

> -> colleague denies problem

> - PHP 是首选，不是 state board first。
> - “建议再看看” usually means you missed the harm threshold。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：出生后数周出现、3 个月内越长越红越凸、单发且界清的 bright red scalp nodule，是典型 **superficial infantile hemangioma**；无 high-risk features 时处理是 **serial observation**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | First noticed at age 3 weeks | Hemangioma appears days to weeks after birth | 不是 congenital lesion，这点很关键 |
> | More raised and brighter red over time | Proliferative phase in first 6 months | 符合 infantile hemangioma 的 natural history |
> | Solitary well-circumscribed scalp lesion on image | Typical superficial lesion, not segmental | 图像支持单发、表浅、边界清楚 |

> | No periorbital, airway, large facial, or multiple lesions | No indication for urgent treatment/workup | 缺少 propranolol 或 PHACE workup 的触发点 |
>

>

> **正确答案：D - Serial observation**
>
> **Core Concept**：Infantile hemangioma 是 benign vascular tumor，通常在出生后几天到几周出现，前 6 个月进入 proliferative phase，之后逐渐 involute。对于 **simple superficial hemangioma**，诊断是 clinical，管理以 reassurance + serial observation 为主。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：早期以 vasculogenesis 驱动增殖，所以 lesion 会在婴儿前几个月变得更亮红、更隆起；随后 apoptosis 增加，病灶在数年内逐步退化。
>
> ```text
> appears weeks after birth
> -> proliferates during age 0-6 months
> -> bright red raised nodule/plaque
> -> then slowly involutes over years
> -> most uncomplicated lesions only need observation
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：什么时候不只是观察
> | Feature | Why It Matters | Next Step |
> |---|---|---|
> | Periorbital lesion | Risk of visual impairment | Beta-blocker therapy |
> | Large facial/segmental lesion | PHACE association | MRI head + echocardiography |
> | Beard distribution | Subglottic involvement risk | Laryngoscopy |
> | >=5 cutaneous lesions | Hepatic hemangiomas | Liver ultrasound |
> | Simple solitary superficial lesion | Usually benign course | Serial observation |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Weeks after birth, bright red growth, then watch it fade."**
> - Not present at birth。
> - Grows first, regresses later。
> - Treat only if location/pattern makes it high risk。
>
> ---
>

> **C - MRI of the head**：用于 facial/segmental hemangioma concern for PHACE，不是这种 solitary well-circumscribed nodule。
>
> **E - Surgical excision**：通常避免，因 bleeding/scarring 风险高，而且大多数会自然 involute。

> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C。病灶在 scalp 上容易让人过度联想到 PHACE，但题图并不是 large segmental geographic hemangioma。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q011 外科 / Testicular cancer
> ^Q011
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：年轻男性出现 **firm, painless intratesticular mass that does not transilluminate**，哪怕前面有 trauma，也先按 **testicular germ cell tumor** 想。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 26-year-old man | Peak age for testicular cancer | 15-35 岁是高发年龄段 |
> | Firm, nontender nodule in the testis | Classic malignant testicular mass | 实质性、无痛、睾丸内结节比感染更像肿瘤 |
> | Does not transilluminate | Solid lesion, not fluid collection | 不透光说明不是 hydrocele 这类液体病变 |
> | Prior football injury | Common distractor; trauma may reveal tumor | trauma 常只是让患者更早注意到原本已存在的肿块 |
> | No fever, dysuria, urethral discharge | Argues against epididymitis/orchitis | 缺少感染线索 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Malignant proliferation of germinal epithelium**
>
> **Core Concept**：绝大多数 testicular cancers 都是 **germ cell tumors**，来源于 seminiferous germinal epithelium。临床上常见 **painless unilateral testicular mass**，查体 firm、ovoid、**不透光**。支持诊断用 scrotal ultrasound 和 tumor markers，但 definitive diagnosis/initial treatment 是 **radical inguinal orchiectomy**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：题目在考你先区分 **intratesticular solid mass** 和各种囊性/血管性/外源性包块。只要是年轻男性、无痛、实性、睾丸内病变，就应把 germ cell tumor 放到最前面。
>
> ```text
> young man
> -> intratesticular firm mass
> -> no transillumination
> -> solid tumor, not fluid
> -> think testicular germ cell tumor
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Testicular cancer | Painless firm intratesticular mass | 本题完全符合 |
> | Spermatocele | Cystic epididymal mass superior to testis | 不在睾丸实质内 |
> | Varicocele | "Bag of worms," positional | 是静脉团，不是 firm nodule |
> | Hydrocele | Fluid around testis, transilluminates | 本题明确不透光 |
> | Hematocele | Painful after trauma | 本题现在无痛，且持续 2 个月 |
> | Inguinal hernia | Groin bulge | 位置和触感都不对 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Trauma explains notice, not the tumor."**
> - 受伤后才发现，不代表病因就是 trauma。
> - 睾丸癌题眼是 **painless + firm + intratesticular + no transillumination**。
> - 年轻男性看到这组词，优先锁定 germ cell tumor。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Cystic epididymal accumulation of sperm**：spermatocele 在 epididymis，通常位于 testis 上方，偏囊性而非 firm intratesticular mass。
>
> **B - Dilation of pampiniform venous plexus**：varicocele 是 "bag of worms"，且与体位相关。
>
> **C - Herniation of abdominal content**：inguinal hernia 更像 groin bulge，不是睾丸内实性结节。
>
> **E - Serous fluid collection outside the testes**：hydrocele 会 transilluminate。
>
> **F - Vascular injury and blood accumulation**：hematocele 常伴明显疼痛；本题后续变成无痛 firm mass，更支持潜在肿瘤被 trauma 暴露。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Vascular injury and blood accumulation。因为题干故意给了 football injury，但真正该抓的是现在的 **painless nontransilluminating testicular nodule**。

> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是单纯考诊断，而是在诊断成立后追问药物。关键是先区分 bulimia nervosa 和 anorexia nervosa，再记住 bulimia 有明确药物证据，首选 fluoxetine。

> + compensatory purging signs
> + normal BMI
> -> bulimia nervosa
> -> CBT + nutrition rehab + fluoxetine
> ```
>
> ---
>

> | Disorder | Weight | Binge/Purge | Medication Pearls |
> |---|---|---|---|

> | Binge-eating disorder | Often overweight | No compensatory behavior | SSRIs or lisdexamfetamine may help |

> **"Bulimia likes fluoxetine; bupropion bites back."**

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q013 ethics / Intimate partner violence
> ^Q013
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：患者刚披露 **intimate partner violence** 时，第一步不是下命令、不是 confront perpetrator，而是 **support + validate + offer help**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Husband threatened to throw out medication and hurt her | Threatened abuse counts as IPV | 不是非得先有肢体伤害才算 abuse |
> | Controlling behavior about friends | Escalating control pattern | 社交隔离是经典危险信号 |
> | "He has never hurt me and I don't think he ever would" | Minimization/ambivalence | 患者在 disclosure 初期常会淡化风险 |
> | Physician asks what is wrong and encourages her to continue | Opportunity for disclosure | 现在考的是 disclosure 后第一句话怎么回 |
> | Best initial response | Prioritizes first step, not full management plan | 先支持，再评估 safety 和做 safety plan |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - "You are brave to tell me; being threatened is abusive and I will help you in any way I can."**
>
> **Core Concept**：IPV disclosure 后的首要动作是 **nonjudgmental validation**。要明确告诉患者：她的经历是 abuse，不是她的错，你会支持她。随后才进入 **safety assessment** 和 **safety planning**。不要逼她立刻离开，也不要去联系施暴者。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这类题常用 stages of change 思路。患者此刻刚开始说出口，还在犹豫和否认之间。如果医生一上来就指挥、施压或替她对抗配偶，反而会破坏信任并增加风险。
>
> ```text
> IPV disclosure
> -> validate and support
> -> assess immediate safety
> -> create safety plan
> -> offer resources when patient is ready
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Response Type | Should You Do It First? | Why |
> |---|---|---|
> | Validate and support | Yes | 建立信任，承认 abuse，减少羞耻感 |
> | Pressure immediate action | No | 可能引发防御或增加危险 |
> | Confront partner | No | 可能升级 violence |
> | Immediate referral only | No | 太快，患者未必会接受 |
> | Minimize threat | No | 会错过真实风险 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"First, hold the door open; do not push the patient through it."**
> - 先接住 disclosure。
> - 先说 abuse is wrong and undeserved。
> - 之后才谈 plan，不要代替患者冲出去对抗。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Encourage her to stand up for herself**：直接 confrontation 可能升级 violence。
>
> **B - Tell her she is in danger and needs to act now**：风险判断可以存在，但强压立即行动不是最好的第一句。
>
> **C - Offer to speak with partner**：绝对不应直接 confront perpetrator。
>
> **E - Say husband may feel threatened and ask her to tell you later**：把 abuse 轻描淡写了，而且她已经表达了威胁。
>
> **F - Immediate counseling-group referral**：资源可以提供，但这不是 disclosure 后最优先的第一反应。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Immediate referral。很多人知道 IPV 需要资源支持，但题目问的是 **most appropriate response right now**，先 validate。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |

> **正确答案：B - MECP2 gene analysis**
>

>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：本题不是单纯 autism spectrum disorder，因为它强调了 **先正常发育，后退化**，再加上 hand stereotypies，这个组合比 isolated autism 更指向 Rett syndrome。
>
> ```text
> normal infancy
> -> developmental regression at 12-18 months
> -> loss of speech and purposeful hand use
> -> hand stereotypies
> -> Rett syndrome
> -> test MECP2
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Disorder | Typical Patient | Key Clue | Test |

> | Rett syndrome | Young girl | Regression + hand wringing | MECP2 analysis |

> | Tay-Sachs disease | Infant | Neurodegeneration + cherry-red macula | Hexosaminidase A |
> | PKU | Either sex | Musty odor, seizures, eczema | Phenylalanine level |
> | Isolated autism | No required regression pattern | Social communication deficits without classic Rett hand findings | Clinical diagnosis |
>
> ---

> **"Rett regresses and wrings."**
> - Regresses after normal start.
> - Wringing/clapping hands.

>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q015 hematology oncology / Cancer pain control
> ^Q015
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：癌痛患者已经按时吃 **short-acting morphine**，但药效撑不到下次且夜里痛醒，说明需要 **add a long-acting opioid**，不是把短效药越开越密。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Metastatic breast cancer with bone and liver metastases | Severe chronic cancer pain context | 晚期转移癌，疼痛负担大 |
> | Short-acting oral morphine every 4 hours | Already on scheduled short-acting opioid | 不是还没上 opioid 的阶段 |
> | Effects wear off before next dose | Breakthrough/end-of-dose failure | 典型提示基础镇痛不稳 |
> | Awakens frequently due to pain | Poor overnight coverage | 夜间控制差很支持需要长效制剂 |
> | Tearful and frustrated | Affective symptoms may be pain-driven | 先把 pain control 做好，再评估情绪干预 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Add extended-release morphine**
>
> **Core Concept**：中重度 cancer-related pain 的核心是稳定、连续的 analgesia。若患者已在 fixed-schedule short-acting opioid 上，仍有 **persistent breakthrough pain** 或 **inadequate overnight relief**，下一步应加 **long-acting opioid**，同时保留 short-acting opioid 作为 breakthrough rescue。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：短效 morphine 每 4 小时可以起步，但对持续性癌痛常不够平稳。长效制剂能提供 steadier drug levels，减少夜间反复痛醒和 dose-end failure。
>
> ```text
> scheduled short-acting opioid
> + pain returns before next dose
> + nighttime breakthrough pain
> -> baseline analgesia inadequate
> -> add long-acting opioid
> -> keep short-acting rescue PRN
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Situation | Best Next Step | Why |
> |---|---|---|
> | Moderate-severe cancer pain, opioid naive | Start opioid regimen | Need baseline analgesia |
> | Scheduled short-acting opioid works poorly between doses | Add long-acting opioid | Smoother control |
> | Breakthrough pain on long-acting opioid | Keep short-acting rescue | Handles episodic flares |
> | Distress from uncontrolled pain | Improve analgesia first | Mood may improve once pain improves |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"If pain breaks through the schedule, build a better schedule."**
> - Dose-end failure -> think long-acting backbone.
> - Short-acting PRN stays for rescue.
> - Supportive care matters, but analgesia comes first here.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Increase short-acting morphine frequency to every 2 hours**：长期不现实，pill burden 太高，夜间也难维持。
>
> **C - Refer to support group**：可作为辅助，但不能替代立即优化镇痛。
>
> **D - Start antidepressant therapy**：被动 suicidal thoughts 可能来自失控疼痛；而且 antidepressants 起效慢，不是 initial intervention。
>
> **E - Start stimulant therapy**：不能解决核心问题，还可能加重体重下降。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Start antidepressant therapy。因为题干有 tearful 和 "worth living" 这句，很容易被拉去做情绪题，但优先级仍是 uncontrolled cancer pain。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q016 心内 / Cardiogenic shock hemodynamics
> ^Q016
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：急性 MI 线索 + hypotension + JVD + crackles + cold clammy extremities，就是 **cardiogenic shock**；血流动力学组合应为 **preload up, cardiac output down, SVR up**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | CAD risk factors + intermittent chest pain | Strongly supports acute MI | 高血压、糖尿病、吸烟、PVD 都把底层病因推向冠脉事件 |
> | New left bundle branch block | Can indicate acute MI | 新发 LBBB 在这类场景里要按急性缺血看 |
> | BP 80/50, pulse 120, lactate elevated | True shock physiology | 已经不是单纯心衰，而是低灌注休克 |
> | JVD and bilateral crackles | High right- and left-sided filling pressures | 静脉淤血加肺水肿，说明泵不出去 |
> | Cold, clammy extremities | Peripheral vasoconstriction | 机体正靠升高 SVR 来硬撑血压 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Left ventricular preload up, cardiac output down, systemic vascular resistance up**
>
> **Core Concept**：acute MI 导致 left ventricular systolic dysfunction，stroke volume 掉下去，所以 **cardiac output 下降**。血射不出去，左室舒张末容量上升，所以 **LV preload 升高**。机体为维持灌注会外周血管收缩，因此 **SVR 升高**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：cardiogenic shock 的本质是 pump failure，不是没血，也不是血管太松。
>
> ```text
> acute MI
> -> LV contractility down
> -> stroke volume down
> -> cardiac output down
> -> blood backs up in LV/lungs
> -> preload up
> -> compensatory vasoconstriction
> -> SVR up
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：常见 shock 血流动力学
> | Shock type | Preload | Cardiac output | SVR | 题眼 |
> |---|---|---|---|---|
> | Cardiogenic | Up | Down | Up | JVD + crackles + cold extremities |
> | Hypovolemic | Down | Down | Up | volume loss, flat neck veins |
> | Obstructive | Usually up on right side | Down | Up | tamponade/PE/tension physiology |
> | Distributive (early sepsis) | Down | Up | Down | warm extremities, vasodilation |
> | Neurogenic | Down | Down | Down | loss of sympathetic tone |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Weak pump, wet lungs, tight vessels."**
> - Weak pump: CO down
> - Wet lungs: preload up
> - Tight vessels: SVR up
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A**：高输出状态才会是 CO up + SVR down，不符合冷湿型休克。
>
> **C**：这是 hypovolemic shock 模板，题里没有失血或脱水，反而有 JVD 和 crackles。
>
> **D**：像早期 distributive shock，但本题不是 warm shock。
>
> **E**：像 neurogenic shock；这里患者是代偿性 tachycardia 和 vasoconstriction，不是 sympathetic failure。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C。很多人一看到 hypotension 就先套低 preload，但 JVD + pulmonary edema 明确告诉你 preload 是高的。
>

> urate crystals in joint

> -> intense inflammatory monoarthritis

> -> microtubule polymerization inhibited
> -> neutrophil activity down
> -> flare improves
> ```
>

>

> | Drug class | When useful | 何时别选 |

> | Probenecid | Chronic uricosuric therapy | 不是 acute flare first step |

> **"Crystal proven, blood thinners on board: skip indomethacin."**
> - Gout confirmed
> - NSAID risky
> - Colchicine wins

> -> closed cervix + fetal cardiac activity

> -> reassure and follow with repeat ultrasound
> ```

> |---|---|---|

> | Inevitable/incomplete abortion | Open cervix or tissue passage | Misoprostol or uterine evacuation |
> | Unstable or septic abortion | Heavy bleeding, anemia, sepsis | Urgent suction curettage |
> | Pregnancy of unknown location | Nondiagnostic ultrasound | Serial beta-hCG |
>

> **"Closed cervix, living fetus: do less."**

> **机制说明**：膀胱在还没该收缩的时候就自己收缩，患者就会先感觉强烈 urgency，随后来不及到厕所即漏尿。核心不是“尿道关不住”，而是“膀胱乱收缩”。
>
> ```text
> involuntary detrusor contraction
> -> sudden urgency
> -> cannot defer voiding
> -> immediate urine leakage
> -> frequency/nocturia/pad use
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Incontinence type | Core clue | PVR |
> |---|---|---|
> | Urgency | Urgency, frequency, nocturia | Normal |
> | Stress | Leakage with cough/laugh/exertion | Normal |
> | Overflow | Retention, dribbling, weak stream | High |
> | Functional | Mobility/cognition barrier | Variable |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Urge = bladder jumps first."**
> - 先有 urgency，再漏。
> - Stress 是腹压一上来立刻漏，不会拖到跑了 20 分钟才发作。
> - Overflow 要看残余尿，题目给 normal PVR 就是在排它。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Bladder outlet obstruction**：会导致 urinary retention 和 overflow incontinence，应见 elevated postvoid residual。
>
> **B - Detrusor underactivity**：本质也是排不空，表现偏 overflow，不是强烈 urgency。
>
> **C - Estrogen deficiency**：可伴 vulvovaginal atrophy，但本题 well-rugated vagina 说明 estrogenization 还可以。
>
> **E - Peripheral neuropathy**：神经源性膀胱通常造成 retention/overflow，而不是典型 urgency syndrome。
>
> **F - Urethral hypermobility**：stress urinary incontinence 应在 cough、laugh、sneeze、running 当下立刻漏，且常可被 Valsalva 诱发。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Urethral hypermobility。因为她跑步会漏尿，但真正细节是“跑到 20 分钟后才漏”且伴 urgency/frequency/nocturia，不是单纯 stress pattern。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q024 GI / Acute liver failure
> ^Q024
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：acute jaundice + encephalopathy + asterixis in a patient without chronic liver disease，最能定义 **acute liver failure** 的实验室指标是 **prolonged prothrombin time**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Progressive confusion, somnolence, asterixis | Hepatic encephalopathy | 说明已不是单纯 hepatitis，而是 liver failure |
> | Jaundice and tender hepatomegaly | Acute hepatic injury | 支持急性肝损伤背景 |
> | No stigmata of chronic liver disease | Distinguishes ALF from cirrhosis | acute liver failure 需无既往 chronic liver disease |
> | Viral hepatitis/acetaminophen risk | Common ALF etiologies | 题目在提示 ALF 病因背景 |
> | Asked for lab most consistent with ALF | Need defining feature, not just associated abnormality | 要选“定义性”实验室异常 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Prolonged prothrombin time**
>
> **Core Concept**：Acute liver failure 的诊断三联是 acute liver injury、hepatic encephalopathy、synthetic dysfunction。最特异、最定义性的化验就是 PT prolongation / INR >1.5，因为它直接体现肝脏合成 clotting factors 的能力下降。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：肝细胞大面积急性损伤后，先出问题的不只是转氨酶升高，更重要的是“做不出凝血因子”。因此 PT/INR 比 bilirubin 或 ALP 更能抓住 ALF 的本质。
>
> ```text
> acute hepatocyte injury
> -> impaired hepatic synthesis
> -> decreased clotting factor production
> -> prolonged PT / increased INR
> -> defines acute liver failure
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Lab abnormality | Can occur in ALF? | Why it is not the best answer |
> |---|---|---|
> | Prolonged PT/INR | Yes, defining feature | Best marker of synthetic failure |
> | Elevated bilirubin | Yes | Common but less specific |
> | Elevated ALP or GGT | Yes | Nonspecific cholestatic markers |
> | Elevated creatinine | Often | Reflects renal injury, not liver definition |
> | Low albumin | Sometimes later | More typical of chronic disease or later stage |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Acute liver failure is not just yellow, it cannot clot."**
> - Bilirubin tells you liver is sick.
> - PT/INR tells you liver is failing.
> - Encephalopathy seals the diagnosis.
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Decreased serum albumin**：albumin 半衰期长，早期 ALF 不一定马上下降，更常见于 chronic liver disease。
>
> **B - Elevated alkaline phosphatase**：可以升高，但太不特异，抓不到 ALF 定义。
>
> **C - Elevated serum creatinine**：ALF 常并发 AKI，但 creatinine 升高不是 ALF 的核心诊断条件。
>
> **D - Increased gamma-glutamyl transpeptidase**：同样属于 nonspecific hepatobiliary marker。
>
> **E - Increased serum bilirubin**：很多人会选，因为患者 jaundice 明显；但 bilirubin 升高只说明代谢/排泄异常，不如 PT 直接代表 synthetic failure。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Increased serum bilirubin。临床表现最显眼的是 jaundice，但考试真正要你抓的是 acute liver failure 的 defining lab feature。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q027 neuro / Basal ganglia hemorrhage
> ^Q027
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：uncontrolled HTN + cocaine + coma + **ipsilateral blown pupil** + **contralateral extensor posturing**，先想 **intracranial hemorrhage with uncal herniation**，最常见源头是 **basal ganglia hemorrhage**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Uncontrolled hypertension and crack cocaine | Major risk factors for hypertensive intracerebral hemorrhage | 先把病因框到 hemorrhagic stroke |
> | Found unresponsive | Large bleed or herniation syndrome | 单纯 cortical infarct 通常不至于这么深度昏迷 |

> | Left extensor posturing | Midbrain compression with contralateral long-tract findings | 说明 mass effect 已经严重 |
> | Left hyperreflexia and Babinski | Contralateral upper motor neuron signs | 支持右侧病变 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Right basal ganglia hemorrhage**
>
> **Core Concept**：Hypertensive vasculopathy 最常见 spontaneous intracerebral hemorrhage 部位是 **basal ganglia**。当血肿产生 mass effect，可出现 **uncal herniation**，导致 ipsilateral CN III palsy（fixed dilated pupil）、contralateral extensor posturing、coma，甚至 respiratory compromise。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是单考“哪种 stroke”，而是考你把 **hemorrhage location** 和 **herniation signs** 连起来。
>
> ```text
> chronic HTN / cocaine
> -> small vessel damage
> -> basal ganglia hemorrhage
> -> expanding mass effect
> -> uncal herniation
> -> CN III compression + midbrain compression
> -> blown pupil + extensor posturing + coma
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Lesion | Typical findings | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Basal ganglia hemorrhage | Acute coma, focal deficits, possible herniation | 本题最佳匹配 |
> | Basis pontis infarction | Motor syndromes, dysarthria, weakness | 不典型 fixed blown pupil + posturing |
> | Cerebellar hemorrhage | Ataxia, vomiting, gait issues | 不典型 CN III compression pattern |
> | Tonsillar herniation | Flaccidity, cardiorespiratory collapse | 不是本题 extensor posturing 主轴 |
> | Motor cortex infarction | Focal UMN deficits with preserved consciousness | 太轻，解释不了 coma |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"HTN bleed loves the basal ganglia; herniation blows the pupil."**
> - HTN hemorrhage 最常考 basal ganglia。
> - Uncal herniation 看 ipsilateral pupil。
> - Posturing 说明脑干已经被压到了。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Basis pontis infarction**：会有 motor deficits，但 coma with blown pupil and extensor posturing 不典型。
>
> **B - Cerebellar hemorrhage**：也能来自 HTN，但更偏 headache, vomiting, ataxia，不是 CN III compression 体征。
>
> **C - Cerebellar tonsillar herniation**：常见 flaccid paralysis、respiratory arrest、hemodynamic instability，不是本题的典型组合。
>
> **E - Right motor cortex infarction**：可有左侧 Babinski/hyperreflexia，但通常 consciousness 保留、pupils 正常。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C。看到 herniation 容易选，但题目给的是 **uncal herniation pattern**，而不是 tonsillar herniation pattern。

> **提示**：白天反复睡着 + **laughing 时 knees buckle** + 入睡/醒来时 vivid dreamlike scenes，这就是 **narcolepsy with cataplexy**；polysomnography 最该看到 **decreased REM latency**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Excessive daytime sleepiness for months to years | Core symptom of narcolepsy | 白天无法维持清醒是主轴 |
> | Falls asleep in meetings | Functional impairment from hypersomnolence | 不是单纯“困”，而是 pathological sleep attacks |
> | Knees buckle while laughing | Cataplexy is highly specific | 强烈情绪诱发短暂失张力，几乎直接点名 narcolepsy |
> | Vivid scenes while falling asleep and waking up | Hypnagogic/hypnopompic hallucinations | 也是经典伴随特征 |
> | Sleeps 8 hours without awakening | Makes chronic sleep deprivation less likely | 睡眠时长够，但白天仍异常困 |
> | Wife notes only light snoring, no witnessed apneas | OSA becomes less likely | 帮你排除最常见干扰项 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Decreased REM latency**
>
> **Core Concept**：Narcolepsy 的关键生理异常之一是 **REM sleep intrudes too early**，所以患者可在入睡后很快进入 REM。考试最常考的是 **cataplexy + hallucinations + decreased REM latency** 这一组。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：正常人 REM 大约在入睡后 90 分钟出现；narcolepsy 患者 REM gate 失控，REM 特征提前闯入 wake-sleep transition，所以会看到早发 REM、cataplexy、sleep-related hallucinations。
>
> ```text
> narcolepsy
> -> abnormal REM regulation
> -> REM intrudes near sleep onset
> -> decreased REM latency
> + cataplexy
> + hypnagogic/hypnopompic hallucinations
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Finding | More Consistent With | 与本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Decreased REM latency | Narcolepsy | 本题答案 |
> | >=15 obstructive respiratory events/hour | Obstructive sleep apnea | 本题缺乏 apneas/snorting/gasping，且 cataplexy 更关键 |
> | Decreased REM density | Not narcolepsy | narcolepsy 反而可有 increased REM density |
> | Increased sleep latency | Insomnia or difficulty initiating sleep | 与 daytime sleep attacks 不符 |
> | Generalized 3-Hz spike-wave | Absence seizure | 年龄和表现都不对 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Laugh -> collapse -> think cataplexy, then think early REM."**
> - 情绪触发失张力 = cataplexy。
> - Cataplexy 几乎总要把你拉回 narcolepsy。
> - Narcolepsy 的睡眠实验室高频考点就是 **decreased REM latency**。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - >=15 obstructive respiratory events/hour**：如果是 OSA，bed partner 常描述 apneas、gasping、snorting；本题更像 central hypersomnia，而且有 cataplexy。
>
> **B - Decreased REM density**：narcolepsy 不是 REM 变少，而是 REM 来得太早、表现更突出。
>
> **D - Generalized 3-Hz spike-wave activity on electroencephalogram**：absence seizure 多起病于儿童，表现为 staring spells，且不会被 laughter 诱发肌张力丧失。
>
> **E - Increased sleep latency**：narcolepsy 患者恰恰是更容易睡着，sleep latency 会降低。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A。因为患者 overweight、轻微打鼾，很容易被带去 OSA；但真正高价值线索是 cataplexy 和 hypnagogic/hypnopompic hallucinations。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：eczema-like dry cheeks + recurrent infections + **microthrombocytopenia with small platelets**，这是 Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome 的标准三联征，答案锁定 **Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 21-month-old boy | X-linked immunodeficiency often presents in male infants/toddlers | 年龄和性别都贴合 WAS |
> | Gross hematuria and petechiae | Clinically significant thrombocytopenic bleeding | 出血表现提示 platelet problem |
> | Platelets 20,000/mm3 | Severe thrombocytopenia | 数量明显低 |
> | Small platelet size on smear | Microthrombocytopenia is the buzzword | 这是把 ITP 排开的最高价值线索 |
> | Recurrent otitis media and pneumonias | Combined immune dysfunction | leukocyte cytoskeletal defect 带来反复感染 |
> | Dry skin on cheeks | Eczema clue | 和感染、血小板问题组成经典 triad |
>

>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：G - Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome**
>
> **Core Concept**：Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome 是 **X-linked recessive WAS gene defect**，导致 leukocytes 和 platelets 的 cytoskeletal remodeling 异常。考试固定抓三件事：**eczema + recurrent infections + microthrombocytopenia**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：WAS protein 异常会影响免疫细胞与血小板的骨架动态，结果一边是免疫功能差、反复感染，另一边是 platelets 又小又少，导致 petechiae、hematuria 甚至更严重出血。
>
> ```text
> WAS gene defect
> -> abnormal cytoskeleton in leukocytes and platelets
> -> immune dysfunction + microthrombocytopenia
> -> recurrent infections + eczema + bleeding
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Disorder | Key Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome | Eczema + recurrent infections + small platelets | 本题答案 |
> | ITP | Isolated thrombocytopenia after viral illness | platelet size usually normal or large |
> | HUS | Hemolytic anemia + thrombocytopenia + AKI | 本题无 anemia/renal failure |
> | Ataxia-telangiectasia | Ataxia, telangiectasia, recurrent infections | 无 microthrombocytopenia |
> | Job syndrome | Recurrent infections + eczema-like rash | 不会有小血小板和明显出血 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"WAS = Wet skin, Absent immunity, Small platelets."**
> - Wet skin: eczema。
> - Absent immunity: recurrent infections。
> - Small platelets: 最关键实验室点。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Ataxia-telangiectasia**：会有 cerebellar ataxia、oculomotor abnormalities、telangiectasias，不是以小血小板出血为主。
>
> **B - Chediak-Higashi syndrome**：关键是 partial albinism、neuropathy、recurrent pyogenic infections，本题都没有。
>
> **C - Hemolytic-uremic syndrome**：需要 hemolytic anemia 和 acute kidney injury，这里 hemoglobin 正常，也没有 oliguria。
>
> **D - Henoch-Schonlein purpura**：可有 hematuria，但 rash 是 palpable purpura，且 platelet count 通常正常。
>
> **E - Immune thrombocytopenic purpura**：最像，因为也能 viral illness 后出血，但 ITP 是 isolated thrombocytopenia，platelets 通常 normal to large，不是 **small**。
>
> **F - Job syndrome**：有 eczema-like rash 和肺部感染会误导，但不合并 microthrombocytopenia。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E。题干故意放了“recent URI”让你想到 ITP，但 smear 上 **small platelets** 一出，方向就该立刻改成 Wiskott-Aldrich。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Thromboangiitis obliterans**
>
> **Core Concept**：Thromboangiitis obliterans 是 **nonatherosclerotic inflammatory vaso-occlusive disease**，累及 small and medium-sized vessels，典型见于吸烟者，导致 distal ischemic pain、ulcers、gangrene。**Complete smoking cessation** 是唯一真正能阻止进展的治疗。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题的高分点在于分清 **small/medium vessel distal ischemia** 和 **large-vessel PAD**。Buerger 病可以数字端已经坏疽，但 proximal pulses 依然正常。
>
> ```text
> tobacco exposure
> -> inflammatory thrombosis of small/medium vessels
> -> distal ischemia
> -> toe pain/discoloration
> -> ulceration and gangrene
> with preserved proximal pulses
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Thromboangiitis obliterans | Young smoker, distal ulcers/gangrene, normal pulses possible | 本题答案 |
> | Atherosclerotic PAD | Older patient, diabetes, diminished pulses, claudication | 本题年龄和体征都不典型 |
> | Raynaud phenomenon | Episodic color change, often symmetric fingers | 可有 digital pain，但通常不是这种单侧足趾坏疽图景 |
> | Acrocyanosis | Painless bluish discoloration | 不会造成 ulcer or gangrene |
> | Antiphospholipid syndrome | Arterial/venous thrombosis, pregnancy loss | 本题缺少高凝病史线索 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Smoker's toes die while pulses survive."**
> - 年轻吸烟者出现 distal gangrene，先想 Buerger。
> - 看到 pulses normal，不要被误导成“血供就没问题”。
> - 戒烟不是建议，是 definitive treatment。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Acrocyanosis**：会发青，但通常 painless，也不会出现 ulcers 或 gangrene。
>
> **B - Antiphospholipid syndrome**：可导致 arterial thrombosis，但更常见的是 DVT/PE、pregnancy morbidity、TIA/stroke；题干没有这些高凝背景。
>
> **C - Atherosclerotic peripheral artery disease**：最像“肢体缺血”大方向，但这位患者年轻、无糖尿病、体重正常，而且 pedal pulses 正常，不像 large-vessel atherosclerotic PAD。
>
> **D - CREST syndrome**：可伴 secondary Raynaud 和 digital ulcers，但应有 sclerodactyly、skin thickening 或其他 limited scleroderma 线索。
>
> **E - Raynaud phenomenon**：通常是 episodic vasospasm，常累及手指且更对称；本题是持续进展到 dry gangrene 的 distal occlusive disease。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C。因为“足趾发黑坏疽”非常容易让人先跳到 PAD，但题目用 **young smoker + normal pulses** 明确在提示 Buerger disease。

> **提示**：asthma 控制越来越差 + eosinophilia + brownish sputum/hemoptysis + **central bronchiectasis**，直接锁定 **allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis (ABPA)**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Poorly controlled asthma with repeated exacerbations | ABPA 常发生在 asthma 或 CF 背景上 | 题干先给宿主土壤，再给并发症线索 |
> | Brownish sputum with occasional blood flecks | Suggests mucus plugging and inflammatory airway injury | 棕色痰是 ABPA 很经典的提醒词 |
> | Eosinophils 12% | Supports allergic/hypersensitivity process | 把单纯细菌性肺炎往后放 |
> | Recurrent infiltrate and fever | ABPA 可反复出现 fleeting infiltrates | 不是一次性感染就结束 |
> | Bilateral central bronchiectasis on CT | High-yield imaging clue for ABPA | central bronchiectasis 是定向提示 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis**
>
> **Core Concept**：ABPA 是对 inhaled *Aspergillus fumigatus* 的 hypersensitivity reaction，通常见于 **asthma** 或 **cystic fibrosis** 患者。临床上表现为 asthma 难控制、反复肺部浸润、eosinophilia、brown sputum、hemoptysis，以及 **central bronchiectasis**。治疗以 **oral corticosteroids + itraconazole** 为主。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：关键不是把它当普通感染，而是识别成“真菌暴露触发的过敏性气道炎症”。反复炎症和 mucus plugging 会逐渐破坏中央气道，形成 bronchiectasis。
>
> ```text
> asthma / CF background
> -> hypersensitivity to Aspergillus
> -> recurrent airway inflammation + mucus plugging
> -> poorly controlled asthma
> -> recurrent infiltrates + eosinophilia
> -> central bronchiectasis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | ABPA | Asthma, eosinophilia, brown sputum, central bronchiectasis | 本题完全贴合 |
> | Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency | Early basilar emphysema | 不解释 eosinophilia 和 recurrent infiltrates |
> | Cystic fibrosis | Childhood disease with GI/pulmonary manifestations | 可是 ABPA 背景病，但本题主线是 asthma |
> | IgA deficiency / immotile cilia | Recurrent infections, possible bronchiectasis | 都缺少 uncontrolled asthma + eosinophilia |
> | Sarcoidosis | Reticular opacities, hilar adenopathy | 不典型 focal infiltrate + brown sputum |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Asthma gets worse, sputum turns brown, bronchi widen in the center."**
> - asthma 题里看到 eosinophilia，要提高对 ABPA 的警觉。
> - brown sputum 是比“咳痰”更有方向性的词。
> - **central** bronchiectasis 比单纯 bronchiectasis 更关键。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency**：会想到年轻肺病，但其核心是 basilar emphysema，不是 eosinophilic asthma pattern。
>
> **C - Cystic fibrosis**：CF 确实也是 ABPA 背景病，但成年才首次以这组线索出现 CF 很不自然；本题早已给出 asthma 主线。
>
> **D - IgA deficiency**：可反复 respiratory infection，但不会解释 central bronchiectasis 合并 eosinophilia 和 asthma worsening。
>
> **E - Immotile cilia syndrome**：也是 recurrent infection/bronchiectasis 框架，但缺 asthma 和 eosinophilia。
>
> **F - Legionella infection**：更像 severe atypical pneumonia，常伴高热和 GI 症状，不是慢性失控 asthma 图景。
>
> **G - Mycobacterium tuberculosis infection**：可咯血，但影像和病史都不如 ABPA 贴切。
>
> **H - Pulmonary sarcoidosis**：常见 hilar adenopathy，不会把题目导向 brown sputum + central bronchiectasis。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Cystic fibrosis。因为 CF 也能有 bronchiectasis，但本题更想考的是 **ABPA 的宿主背景与影像组合**，不是 CF 本身。

###### Q037

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q037 psych / TCA overdose toxidrome
> ^Q037
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：seizure + anticholinergic signs + hypotension + **QRS 130 msec**，在 antidepressant 线索背景下优先锁定 **tricyclic antidepressant (TCA) overdose**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | New-onset seizure | TCA overdose 常见 severe CNS toxicity | 不是单纯“喝断酒”那么简单 |
> | Sedated and disoriented | TCA 可致 sedation/delirium | 和 serotonin syndrome 的 agitation 相反 |
> | Flushed, febrile, dilated pupils | Anticholinergic toxidrome | 直接把题往 TCA 拉 |
> | Decreased bowel sounds | Ileus from muscarinic blockade | 比 tachycardia 更有区分度 |
> | BP 90/50 mm Hg | Alpha-1 blockade causes hypotension | 帮你排除 cocaine/withdrawal 这类高血压图景 |
> | QRS 130 msec | Sodium channel blockade | TCA 题最要命的 ECG 标志 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Tricyclic antidepressant overdose**
>
> **Core Concept**：TCA overdose 会同时造成 **CNS toxicity**、**anticholinergic toxicity**、以及 **cardiac fast sodium channel blockade**。因此同一题里会出现 seizure、dilated pupils、flushed skin、decreased bowel sounds、hypotension、和 **QRS widening**。真正致命点通常是心血管毒性。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是背一个单一 toxidrome，而是要把 TCA 的“多受体打击”拼起来。muscarinic blockade 解释 anticholinergic 表现，alpha-1 blockade 解释 hypotension，fast sodium channel blockade 解释 QRS widening。
>
> ```text
> TCA overdose
> -> muscarinic blockade
> -> flushed skin + mydriasis + ileus
> -> alpha-1 blockade
> -> hypotension
> -> cardiac fast sodium channel blockade
> -> QRS widening + arrhythmia risk
> -> CNS toxicity
> -> seizure / sedation
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | TCA overdose | Anticholinergic signs + seizure + QRS widening + hypotension | 本题答案 |
> | Alcohol withdrawal | Seizure, tremor, autonomic hyperactivity | 更常见 hypertension，且无 QRS widening |
> | Bupropion overdose | Seizure, tachycardia, QTc issues | 不会有明显 anticholinergic toxidrome |
> | Cocaine overdose | Hyperthermia, seizure, tachycardia | 常为 hypertension 和 sympathomimetic picture |
> | Serotonin syndrome | Agitation, clonus, hyperreflexia | 不应有 QRS widening 或 ileus |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Wide QRS + dry hot bowel-silent patient = think TCA."**
> - tox 题里看到 **QRS >100 msec**，先想 sodium channel blockade。
> - anticholinergic signs 配 hypotension，比单纯 sympathomimetic 更像 TCA。
> - seizure 只是其中一部分，别忽略 ECG。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Alcohol withdrawal**：能抽搐、心率快，但通常是 **hypertension**，而且没有 anticholinergic picture 或 QRS widening。
>
> **B - Bupropion overdose**：也会 seizures，是很强的迷惑项；但它不解释 flushed skin、dilated pupils、decreased bowel sounds 这组 anticholinergic 表现。
>
> **C - Cocaine overdose**：sympathomimetic 会导致 hyperthermia 和 seizures，但更该看到 hypertension，而不是 hypotension。
>
> **D - Opioid withdrawal**：会散瞳和自主神经过强，但常见 diarrhea/cramps，不会 bowel sounds 下降，也不该 QRS widening。
>
> **E - Serotonin syndrome**：要点是 agitation、clonus、hyperreflexia，不是 sedation + ileus。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Bupropion overdose。因为 seizure + antidepressant history 很容易先选它，但 **QRS widening + anticholinergic signs** 是把答案钉死到 TCA 的关键。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q038 endocrine / severe hypercalcemia initial step
> ^Q038
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：confusion + dehydration signs + **calcium 14.1 mg/dL** + lung mass，先别急着想降钙药，**best initial therapy 是 0.9% saline**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Progressive confusion and weakness | Symptomatic hypercalcemia | 神经精神症状已经出来了 |
> | Calcium 14.1 mg/dL | Severe hypercalcemia | 数值本身就达到重度阈值 |
> | Dry mucous membranes, tachycardia, high BUN | Volume depletion | 高钙常导致 polyuria 和脱水 |
> | Smoker with lung mass | Suggests humoral hypercalcemia of malignancy | 常考 PTHrP 机制 |
> | Sodium 131 mEq/L only mildly low | Hyponatremia is not the main problem | 避免被 3% saline/SIADH 带偏 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - 0.9% saline**
>
> **Core Concept**：重度症状性 hypercalcemia 的第一步是 **aggressive isotonic saline hydration**，目的是恢复 intravascular volume 并促进 urinary calcium excretion。随后常合并 **calcitonin** 快速降钙，再加 **bisphosphonate** 维持。题目问的是 **best initial therapy**，所以先选 saline。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：高钙会造成 nephrogenic diabetes insipidus 样表现，病人容易 polyuria、口服减少、进一步脱水。若不先补液，后续很多降钙手段效果和安全性都受限。
>
> ```text
> malignancy
> -> severe hypercalcemia
> -> polyuria + decreased intake
> -> volume depletion
> -> reduced renal calcium clearance
> -> worsening hypercalcemia
> initial fix: isotonic saline
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Therapy | When Used | 与本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 0.9% saline | Initial treatment for severe symptomatic hypercalcemia | 本题答案 |
> | Calcitonin | Rapid adjunct after hydration begins | 题目没给这个选项 |
> | Bisphosphonate | Sustained calcium lowering over 2-4 days | 不是最初一步 |
> | Furosemide | Only if significant volume overload | 本题反而是脱水 |
> | 3% saline / fluid restriction | Severe symptomatic hyponatremia / SIADH | 被 mild hyponatremia 诱导的错误选项 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"High calcium dries the patient out, so start by filling the tank."**
> - 重度高钙先补液，不是先限水。
> - 看到 dry mucosa 和 tachycardia，要主动识别 volume depletion。
> - mild hyponatremia 在这里是噪音，不是主战场。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - 3% saline**：只在 acute severe symptomatic hyponatremia 更合适；本题 Na 131 不足以解释整套表现。
>
> **C - 5% dextrose in 0.45% saline**：hypotonic，不如 isotonic saline 适合纠正 intravascular depletion。
>
> **D - Dexamethasone**：适用于 calcitriol-mediated hypercalcemia，如 lymphoma 或 granulomatous disease，不是这里最可能的 PTHrP 图景。
>
> **E - Fluid restriction**：SIADH 才考虑；本题是 dry，不是 euvolemic。
>
> **F - Furosemide**：以前常被误背成“高钙就利尿”，但没有 volume overload 时会让脱水更糟。
>
> **G - Potassium supplementation**：K 正常。
>
> **H - Regular insulin**：用于 hyperkalemia，不处理高钙主问题。
>
> **I - Sodium bicarbonate infusion**：适用于严重 acidosis 或 hyperkalemia，本题都没有。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Fluid restriction。因为 lung mass 容易让人联想到 SIADH，但题干体征和实验室真正指向的是 **severe hypercalcemia with dehydration**。

>
> ```text
> vitamin K exposure
> -> warfarin effect decreases
> -> subtherapeutic INR
> -> thrombus on mechanical mitral valve
> -> obstructed transmitral flow
> -> pulmonary congestion / dyspnea
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Typical Clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Prosthetic valve thrombosis | Mechanical valve + low INR + acute HF symptoms | 本题答案 |
> | Calcific degeneration | Bioprosthetic valve after years | 机械瓣不匹配 |
> | Fragmentation hemolysis | Mechanical valve + anemia/hemolysis labs | 本题 Hb 正常 |
> | LV systolic dysfunction | Cardiomyopathy/myocarditis pattern | 不如低 INR 的线索强 |
> | Pulmonary embolism | Dyspnea with pleuritic pain or right-heart strain | 本题 crackles 更偏左心淤血 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Mechanical mitral + low INR = thrombus until proven otherwise."**
> - mitral mechanical valve 比 aortic 更容易 thrombose。
> - warfarin 病人一旦补充 vitamin K，先看 INR。
> - 突然 dyspnea 像 valve stenosis 复发时，要想 prosthetic obstruction。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破

>
> **B - Coronary artery disease**：年轻、危险因素少，且题目刻意给了机械瓣和 INR 线索，不该绕远路。
>

>
> **D - Fragmentation hemolysis**：机械瓣确实可溶血，但应看到 anemia 或 hemolysis 证据，本题没有。
>
> **E - Left ventricular systolic dysfunction**：可有 crackles 和 dyspnea，但本题的 subtherapeutic INR 和 mitral prosthesis 更具决定性。

> **G - Pulmonary embolism**：会呼吸困难，但 absence of chest pain 加上 crackles 使之不如左侧瓣膜阻塞合理。

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Permanent destruction and dilation of the airways**
>
> **Core Concept**：Bronchiectasis 的定义不是“有点慢性咳嗽”，而是 **bronchial wall 被永久性破坏并扩张**。严重感染后如果气道清除能力下降，就会进入 bacterial overgrowth、neutrophilic inflammation、tissue damage 的恶性循环，导致 chronic purulent sputum、hemoptysis、反复感染和 weight loss。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题的核心是把临床表现回扣到结构改变。重症肺炎造成初始伤害，之后因清除差而持续感染，最后形成不可逆的 airway destruction/dilation。
>
> ```text
> severe pneumonia
> -> structural airway injury
> -> impaired bacterial clearance
> -> recurrent infection
> -> neutrophilic inflammation
> -> tissue damage
> -> permanent airway dilation
> ```

> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Bad infection breaks the bronchi, then bacteria keep coming back."**
> - 支扩看见的是“反复脓痰感染”，本质是永久结构破坏。
> - hemoptysis 在 chronic productive cough 题里很支持 bronchiectasis。
> - definitive diagnosis 常要靠 **high-resolution CT**。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Airway infiltration by eosinophils**：这更像 asthma、ABPA、EGPA 等 eosinophilic airway disease，不是 postinfectious purulent cough。
>
> **B - Interstitial lung fibrosis**：ILD 以干咳和渐进性呼吸困难更常见，反复细菌感染并不典型。
>
> **D - Permanent enlargement of the airspaces distal to the terminal bronchioles**：这是 emphysema 的定义，不是 bronchiectasis。
>
> **E - Squamous cell lung carcinoma**：典型强相关 tobacco，而且本题年龄和病程都更支持 postinfectious structural lung disease。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Permanent enlargement of the airspaces distal to the terminal bronchioles。因为它也是“永久性结构改变”的表述，但部位是 **alveoli**，不是 **airways/bronchi**。
