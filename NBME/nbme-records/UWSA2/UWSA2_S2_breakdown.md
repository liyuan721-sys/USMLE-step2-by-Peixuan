> [!example]- UWSA2 Q041 骨科 / Paget disease of bone
> ^Q041
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：older adult + increased hat size + hearing loss + isolated elevated alkaline phosphatase + mixed lytic/sclerotic skull lesions，先锁定 **Paget disease of bone**，机制是 **disorganized bone remodeling**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 68-year-old man | Paget disease usually occurs after age 40 | 年龄层非常对，先把儿童骨病和先天病往后放 |
> | Increased hat size over 2 years | Skull enlargement is classic | 颅骨增厚会让帽围变大，是高频题眼 |
> | Decreased hearing, headaches | Temporal bone involvement can compress nearby structures | 颞骨受累可导致 hearing loss，也常伴 headache |
> | Elevated alkaline phosphatase with otherwise normal labs | High bone turnover with normal calcium/phosphorus | 骨转换增加，但不是全身性钙磷紊乱 |
> | Bone thickening with lytic and sclerotic lesions | Classic imaging pattern of Paget disease | 混合溶骨和硬化最支持异常重塑，而不是单纯骨质流失 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Disorganized bone remodeling**
>
> **Core Concept**：Paget disease of bone 的核心不是“骨变少”或“骨没矿化”，而是 **osteoclast dysfunction -> increased bone turnover -> disordered remodeling**。结果是新骨虽然多，但结构乱、质量差，所以会出现骨增大、疼痛、畸形、听力下降和病理骨折风险。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：先有异常 osteoclast activity，后续 osteoblast 被动跟着快速补骨，最终造成“做得很快但搭得很乱”的骨。
>
> ```text
> osteoclast dysfunction
> -> increased bone resorption
> -> compensatory osteoblast activity
> -> high-turnover but disorganized bone
> -> enlarged weak bone with mixed lytic/sclerotic lesions
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Disease | Key Mechanism | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Paget disease | Disordered remodeling | 本题完全符合 |
> | Osteomalacia | Poorly mineralized osteoid | 更偏 diffuse bone pain/fragility，不是局灶 skull enlargement |
> | Primary hyperparathyroidism | Cortical bone loss | 常有 hypercalcemia，不会是帽围变大 |
> | Fibrous dysplasia | Fibrous replacement of bone | 更常见于 younger patients |
> | Acromegaly | Excess IGF-1 | 可帽围变大，但不会有 mixed lytic/sclerotic skull lesions |
> | Multiple myeloma | Plasma cell infiltration | lytic lesions 可见，但 head enlargement 不是典型点 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Big head, bad hearing, busy bone turnover."**
> - Hat size bigger：想 skull expansion。
> - Hearing loss：想 temporal bone involvement。
> - ALP up, Ca/P normal：想 Paget 高转换骨病。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Abundant mineralization of the periosteum**：这是 periosteal reaction 的方向，常见于 osteomyelitis、tumor、vitamin A excess，不解释典型 Paget 影像。
>
> **B - Cortical bone loss**：更像 primary hyperparathyroidism，题干没有 hypercalcemia。
>
> **D - Fibrous replacement of the bone**：对应 fibrous dysplasia，年龄和影像都不对。
>
> **E - Increased deposition of poorly mineralized osteoid**：对应 osteomalacia，应该是矿化不足，不是骨头变厚又混合硬化/溶骨。
>
> **F - Increased insulinlike growth factor 1 production**：acromegaly 也会帽围变大，但本题的 ALP 和 skull x-ray pattern 更支持 Paget。
>
> **G - Monoclonal plasma cell infiltration**：multiple myeloma 主打 lytic lesions、贫血、肾损伤等，不会典型表现为 increased hat size。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：F - Increased insulinlike growth factor 1 production。因为“hat size increased”很像 acromegaly，但影像和 isolated ALP elevation 把题拉回 Paget disease。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q042 ethics / Disclosure of medical error
> ^Q042
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：题目一旦明确是 **preventable medical error**，对病人的最佳回应必须包含 3 件事: **直接承认错误、清楚解释发生了什么、真诚道歉**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Guidewire entirely left in femoral vein | Clear preventable procedural error | 这不是并发症的自然风险，而是实打实的操作失误 |
> | Error recognized after catheter placement | Disclosure should be prompt | 发现后不能等病人自己知道，次日就应完整说明 |
> | Patient stabilized and extubated | Now he can personally receive disclosure | 前面找配偶 consent 是急救情境，后面要回到病人本人 |
> | Question asks for best statement | Ethics communication question | 重点不是技术补救，而是 disclosure wording |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - "There was a medical error ... we apologize ..."**
>
> **Core Concept**：disclosure of medical error 的标准动作是:
> 1. 明说发生了 medical error。
> 2. 用非行话解释具体出了什么错。
> 3. 对造成的伤害或风险道歉。
>
> 不能模糊处理，也不能把事实包装成“只是有点困难”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这里考的是 professionalism 和 patient safety，不是法律躲闪术。透明 disclosure 反而更符合伦理，也有助于维持 trust 和后续治疗合作。
>
> ```text
> medical error occurs
> -> disclose promptly and clearly
> -> explain facts in simple language
> -> apologize and answer questions
> -> preserve trust and ethical care
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Good disclosure element | Should it appear? | 本题要点 |
> |---|---|---|
> | State that an error occurred | Yes | 必须直说 medical error |
> | Use simple language | Yes | 避免 technical jargon |
> | Apologize | Yes | 不能只说 unfortunate |
> | Invite questions | Yes | 让病人理解并可追问 |
> | Distort facts to sound safer | No | 绝对不行 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Own it, explain it, apologize."**
> - Own it：明确说 error。
> - Explain it：病人听得懂。
> - Apologize：承担责任，不甩锅给 procedure risk。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B**：只说“有困难”，没有明确承认错误，也没解释 wire 被遗留。
>
> **C**：内容方向接近，但用了 lay patient 不易理解的 jargon，比如 vessel lumen、extraction。
>
> **D**：把错误淡化成不可预测事件，既不承担责任，也没有道歉。
>
> **E**：不诚实。IR 不是因为“更擅长放 catheter”，而是为了处理 guidewire retention 这个错误后果。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C。它看起来信息量足，但沟通对象是病人，不是同行；ethical disclosure 需要 plain language。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q043 endocrine / Hereditary hemochromatosis
> ^Q043
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：middle-aged man + fatigue + MCP arthropathy + hepatomegaly + low libido/ED，这组跨系统组合优先想 **hereditary hemochromatosis (iron overload)**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Fatigue for >1 year | Chronic systemic process | 慢性疲劳本身不特异，但要和其他器官线索拼起来看 |
> | Erectile dysfunction and loss of sexual desire | Suggests hypogonadism | 男性 libido 下降和 ED 提示 pituitary/gonadal 轴受影响 |
> | Mild hepatomegaly | Points to liver iron deposition | 肝脏是 HH 的高频受累器官 |
> | MCP joint tenderness and swelling | Classic arthropathy clue | MCP arthropathy 很有 hemochromatosis 味道 |
> | No heavy alcohol use, no jaundice/splenomegaly | Makes cirrhosis from alcohol less likely | 排除最顺手的酒精性肝病解释 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Iron overload**
>
> **Core Concept**：Hereditary hemochromatosis 由 **HFE mutation -> increased intestinal iron absorption** 引起。铁沉积到 liver、pituitary、joints、heart 等多个器官，所以会把看似分散的症状串起来: fatigue、hepatomegaly、arthropathy、hypogonadism。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：本题最值钱的不是记住 bronze diabetes，而是记住早期更常见的“非经典组合”: 肝大 + 关节痛 + 性功能下降。
>
> ```text
> HFE mutation
> -> increased intestinal iron absorption
> -> iron deposition in tissues
> -> liver injury + arthropathy + pituitary dysfunction
> -> hepatomegaly + MCP pain + decreased libido/ED
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Can cause fatigue? | 为什么不如本题 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Hemochromatosis | Yes | 还能统一解释 hepatomegaly + MCP arthropathy + hypogonadism |
> | Alcoholism | Yes | 但酒量不大，也缺 cirrhosis stigmata |
> | Depression | Yes | 不解释体征异常 |
> | Chronic fatigue syndrome | Yes | 不解释 hepatomegaly 和关节肿痛 |
> | Hypothyroidism | Yes | 常见体重增加、怕冷、便秘、bradycardia，不典型 |
> | Primary biliary cholangitis | Yes | 更常见 pruritus、cholestatic pattern，且多见女性 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Iron hits liver, joints, and libido."**
> - Liver：hepatomegaly。
> - Joints：MCP arthropathy。
> - Libido：secondary hypogonadism。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Alcoholism**：会让人想到 fatigue + hepatomegaly，但题干酒精暴露不重，而且 hypogonadism 通常见于更晚期 cirrhosis。
>
> **B - Chronic fatigue syndrome**：只能解释 fatigue，解释不了 hepatomegaly 和关节体征。
>
> **C - Depression**：decreased libido 可见，但没有理由出现 MCP swelling 和 hepatomegaly。
>
> **D - Hypothyroidism**：也可能 fatigue + low libido，但 arthropathy uncommon，hepatomegaly rare，且缺少 bradycardia/weight change。
>
> **F - Primary biliary cholangitis**：fatigue 和肝大可以像，但 absence of pruritus and icterus 不支持。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Alcoholism。因为看到 hepatomegaly 容易往肝病和性功能下降联想，但 MCP arthropathy 是把题翻回 iron overload 的关键线索。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q044 GI / Acute pancreatitis initial management
> ^Q044
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：constant epigastric pain + vomiting + leaning forward relief + alcohol history = **acute pancreatitis**；问 initial management 时，先给 **aggressive isotonic crystalloids**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Constant upper abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting | Classic pancreatitis symptom cluster | 持续性上腹痛配恶心呕吐很典型 |
> | Pain relieved by sitting up or bending forward | High-yield clue for pancreatitis | 前倾缓解是非常常考的提示语 |
> | Alcohol use | Major risk factor | 酒精暴露让诊断更顺 |
> | Left basilar dullness and decreased breath sounds | Pleural effusion can accompany pancreatitis | 左侧胸腔积液是加分线索 |
> | Hypocalcemia, hyperglycemia, leukocytosis | Supports systemic inflammatory pancreatitis | 说明已经有全身炎症反应，不是单纯 dyspepsia |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Isotonic crystalloids**
>
> **Core Concept**：acute pancreatitis 初始处理的核心是 **early aggressive volume resuscitation**。原因是炎症介质会导致第三间隙丢失和低灌注，若前 48 小时补液不够，坏死性胰腺炎、AKI 和其他器官损伤风险都会上升。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：胰酶和 cytokines 释放后，会出现类似 sepsis 的 systemic inflammatory state，所以“先补液”不是配角，而是初始 management 的主轴。
>
> ```text
> pancreatic inflammation
> -> enzyme/cytokine release
> -> third spacing and hypovolemia
> -> organ hypoperfusion
> -> higher risk of necrosis and AKI
> -> early isotonic fluids improve outcomes
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Intervention | In acute pancreatitis, when? | 本题为什么不是它 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Isotonic crystalloids | Immediately | 初始最重要 |
> | Pain control | Also early | 重要但题目问 principal role |
> | Antibiotics | Only with infected necrosis or another infection | 不能 routine empiric 给 |
> | Calcium gluconate | Only if severe/symptomatic hypocalcemia | 本题还没到这一步 |
> | Insulin infusion | If marked hyperglycemia persists/severe | 先补液常可先改善 |
> | Laparotomy | If another surgical abdomen exists | 本题没有 peritoneal signs |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Pancreatitis dries the patient before it kills the pancreas."**
> - 炎症先让血管内容量掉下去。
> - 所以最先补的是 volume，不是 calcium、antibiotics 或 insulin。
> - Lean forward pain relief 是快速识别点。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Antiplatelet agents**：inferior MI 可伪装成 epigastric pain，但 fever 和 leaning-forward relief 不支持。
>
> **B - Broad-spectrum antibiotics**：leukocytosis 和 fever 在 pancreatitis 本身就能出现，不能因此 routine 抗感染。
>
> **C - Calcium gluconate**：hypocalcemia 常见，但多数随病情改善而纠正，只有 severe/symptomatic 才积极补。
>
> **D - Exploratory laparotomy**：如果是 perforated viscus，应该更像 peritoneal signs。
>
> **E - Insulin infusion**：250 mg/dL 不是这题的主轴，volume resuscitation 优先级更高。
>
> **G - Proton pump inhibitors**：PUD 不解释 systemic inflammatory picture。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Broad-spectrum antibiotics。因为 fever + leukocytosis 很容易让人机械性联想到感染，但 acute pancreatitis 初始并不常规给 empiric antibiotics。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q045 OB / Preeclampsia with severe features
> ^Q045
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：35 weeks pregnant + new HTN + proteinuria + creatinine 1.5 mg/dL = **preeclampsia with severe features**；一旦已经到 `>=34 weeks`，最佳下一步就是 **delivery**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 35 weeks gestation | Delivery threshold matters | gestational age 直接决定管理 |
> | BP 148/98 then 152/106 | New-onset hypertension after 20 weeks | 已满足 preeclampsia 的高血压框架 |
> | Protein 2+ | Supports preeclampsia | 有蛋白尿，但这题关键甚至不是它 |
> | Creatinine 1.5 mg/dL | Severe feature | 肾功能受损就是 severe feature，即使血压没到 160/110 |
> | Reactive nonstress test | Fetal testing is reassuring but does not override maternal indication | NST 好看不改变 severe preeclampsia 的分娩指征 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Immediate delivery**
>
> **Core Concept**：preeclampsia with severe features 的定义不只看 severe-range BP，也包括 **end-organ damage**，这里就是 creatinine `>1.1 mg/dL`。当孕周达到 **34 weeks or more**，继续妊娠的 maternal-fetal risk 高于 prematurity risk，因此应 delivery。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：很多人会被“没有 headache / vision change / RUQ pain”误导，但 severe feature 不是必须有神经症状；肾脏受累本身就够了。
>
> ```text
> >=20 weeks pregnancy
> -> new hypertension
> + proteinuria or end-organ damage
> -> preeclampsia
> + creatinine >1.1
> -> severe features
> + gestational age >=34 weeks
> -> delivery indicated
> ```
>

> | Scenario | Best next step |

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q050 neuro / Lambert-Eaton syndrome
> ^Q050
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：older smoker + **proximal leg-predominant weakness + absent reflexes + no ocular/bulbar findings**，优先锁定 **Lambert-Eaton myasthenic syndrome (LEMS)**，下一步别忘了找 **small cell lung cancer**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 10 weeks of progressive proximal weakness | Subacute neuromuscular process | 不是瞬间发作，也不是多年慢病 |
> | Trouble climbing stairs and rising from a chair | Classic proximal lower-extremity pattern | LEMS 常先累及腿近端 |
> | Absent deep tendon reflexes | Strong clue against myasthenia gravis | MG reflexes 通常保留 |
> | No ocular or bulbar symptoms | Makes MG less likely | 没有 ptosis、diplopia、dysphagia |
> | 40-pack-year smoking history | Raises concern for paraneoplastic syndrome from SCLC | 抽烟史是整题最关键病因线索 |
> | Normal sensation and plantar reflexes | Less supportive of polyneuropathy or upper motor neuron lesion | 更像 NMJ disorder |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - CT scan of the chest**
>
> **Core Concept**：LEMS 由 **autoantibodies against presynaptic voltage-gated calcium channels** 引起，典型表现是 **proximal weakness + diminished/absent reflexes**，ocular/bulbar symptoms 比 MG 少见。因为大多数病例与 **small cell lung cancer** 相关，所以 workup 里必须做 **chest imaging**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：肿瘤细胞表达 voltage-gated calcium channel antigen，诱发自身抗体；抗体干扰 presynaptic calcium entry，导致 acetylcholine release 下降，所以出现 NMJ transmission failure。
>
> ```text
> small cell lung cancer
> -> antibodies to presynaptic VGCC
> -> decreased calcium-mediated ACh release
> -> proximal weakness and reduced reflexes
> -> evaluate chest for underlying tumor
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Disorder | Typical clues | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | LEMS | Proximal weakness, absent reflexes, smoker, paraneoplastic SCLC | 本题完全符合 |
> | Myasthenia gravis | Ocular/bulbar symptoms, normal reflexes, thymoma | 本题反而缺这些 |
> | Guillain-Barre syndrome | Rapid progression over about 2 weeks, often postinfectious | 本题是 10 周，且无感染前驱 |
> | Hypothyroid myopathy | Proximal weakness plus hypothyroid symptoms | 本题没有 cold intolerance or weight gain |
> | Polymyalgia rheumatica | Proximal pain/stiffness more than true weakness | 本题是神经肌接头型 weakness |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Smoker + weak thighs + weak reflexes = scan the chest."**
> - 腿近端无力先于眼肌症状，想 LEMS。
> - Reflexes 低下是和 MG 的高价值分界点。
> - 一旦想到 LEMS，就要反射性联想 SCLC。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Anti-acetylcholine receptor antibody testing**：这是 MG 方向；MG 更常见 ocular/bulbar symptoms，且 reflexes 通常保留。
>
> **B - Cerebrospinal fluid testing for albuminocytologic dissociation**：GBS 会有 weakness + areflexia，但一般更急，常在约 2 周内进展并有感染前驱。
>
> **D - Serum TSH level**：hypothyroidism 可致 proximal myopathy，但常伴 weight gain、cold intolerance、bradycardia 或 myalgias。
>
> **E - Trial of prednisone therapy**：polymyalgia rheumatica 更强调 proximal pain/stiffness 和 inflammatory markers 升高，不是典型 NMJ syndrome。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Anti-acetylcholine receptor antibody testing。因为很多人会先把“proximal weakness”套进 MG，但 **areflexia + smoker + no ocular/bulbar symptoms** 更支持 LEMS。
>

###### Q051

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q051 neuro / Dementia with Lewy bodies
> ^Q051
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：认知波动 + frequent falls + bradykinesia/slow speech + 早期 visuospatial deficit，比起 Alzheimer disease 更要先想 **dementia with Lewy bodies**，查体对应 **postural instability**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | "good days and bad days" | Fluctuating cognition is a core DLB clue | 认知状态波动，不是单纯稳定缓慢下降 |
> | Frequent falls | Supportive parkinsonian feature | 跌倒常来自 postural instability |
> | Slow movements and speech | Spontaneous parkinsonism | bradykinesia/parkinsonism 已经在题干出现 |
> | Gets lost, poor clock drawing | Early visuospatial dysfunction | DLB 常早期先伤 attention/visuospatial，而不是先伤短时记忆 |
> | Recalls 3/3 after 1 minute | Relative preservation of memory early | 这点帮助你别被 Alzheimer 锚定 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Postural instability**
>
> **Core Concept**：Dementia with Lewy bodies 的核心是 progressive dementia 合并 fluctuating cognition、visual hallucinations、spontaneous parkinsonism。查体最匹配的是 parkinsonian gait/postural instability，因此也解释她反复跌倒。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：alpha-synuclein deposition 影响 brainstem、limbic system 与 neocortex，所以既有 cognition 波动，也有 extrapyramidal motor signs。
>
> ```text
> Lewy bodies
> -> cortical + extrapyramidal involvement
> -> fluctuating cognition + visuospatial deficits
> -> bradykinesia and postural instability
> -> repeated falls
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Disease | Key Cognitive Pattern | Motor/Exam Clue |
> |---|---|---|
> | Dementia with Lewy bodies | Fluctuating attention, early visuospatial deficit | Parkinsonism, postural instability |
> | Alzheimer disease | Early memory impairment | No prominent early parkinsonism |
> | Vascular dementia | Stepwise decline | Focal UMN signs such as Babinski may appear |
> | Frontotemporal dementia | Early disinhibition/language change | Primitive reflexes may be present |
> | Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease | Rapidly progressive dementia | Startle myoclonus |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Lewy = looks around poorly, thinks variably, moves like Parkinson."**
> - 导航差、clock drawing 差，提示 visuospatial deficit。
> - 反复跌倒，不是 cerebellum first，而是 postural instability。
> - 记忆早期还行，是区分点。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Babinski sign**：提示 pyramidal tract lesion，更像 vascular dementia 或其他 UMN 病变，不是 DLB 的典型查体。
>
> **B - Limb ataxia**：指向 cerebellar lesion。题干没有 dysmetria、broad-based gait 或 cerebellar signs。
>
> **D - Primitive reflexes**：frontal release signs 更常见于 frontotemporal dementia。
>
> **E - Startle myoclonus**：更像 prion disease，尤其 Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease 的快速进展型 dementia。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Babinski sign。因为题干有 dementia + falls，容易想到“脑血管病”，但认知波动和 parkinsonism 把方向拉回 DLB。
>

###### Q052

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q052 OB / Preterm PROM with unknown GBS
> ^Q052
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：`<37 weeks` + rupture of membranes + labor + unknown GBS status，先补 **intrapartum penicillin prophylaxis**；不要再做 tocolysis。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 35 weeks gestation | Preterm delivery risk factor for neonatal GBS disease | `<37 weeks` 本身就是 unknown GBS 时的给药指征 |
> | Nitrazine-positive clear fluid | Membranes are ruptured | 已经是 PROM，不是单纯 contractions |
> | Contractions every 2-3 minutes, cervix 1 cm | She is in labor | 已进入 intrapartum management 逻辑 |
> | Last prenatal visit 2 weeks ago | GBS culture result not available | 题目明说 unknown GBS status |
> | Reassuring tracing, vertex presentation | No indication for urgent cesarean | 没有把你引向手术分娩 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Penicillin**
>
> **Core Concept**：Unknown GBS status 时，看 intrapartum risk factors。只要是 `<37 weeks` delivery，或 fever，或 rupture of membranes `>=18 hours`，就要给 intrapartum penicillin。本题 35 weeks preterm PROM，答案直接锁定。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：GBS 常无症状定植于 maternal genital tract；preterm neonate 免疫更不成熟，所以 unknown status 且早产时，阈值更低，直接做 prophylaxis。
>
> ```text
> unknown maternal GBS status
> + delivery before 37 weeks
> -> high neonatal transmission risk
> -> intrapartum IV penicillin
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Scenario | Best Step | Why |
> |---|---|---|
> | Unknown GBS + <37 weeks | Penicillin | Preterm is prophylaxis indication |
> | Unknown GBS + ROM >=18 hr | Penicillin | Prolonged exposure risk |
> | Unknown GBS + intrapartum fever | Penicillin | Suggests infection risk |
> | PROM >=34 weeks | Delivery rather than tocolysis | Infection risk outweighs prolongation benefit |
> | Threatening very preterm delivery <32 weeks | Magnesium sulfate | Fetal neuroprotection, not GBS coverage |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Unknown GBS? Think 3 triggers: preterm, fever, long ROM."**
> - 这题最容易被 labor management 细节带偏。
> - 真正的出题点不是 PROM 本身，而是 GBS prophylaxis 指征。
> - 34 周后 PROM 不再主张拖产保胎。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Amnioinfusion**：用于 variable decelerations 提示 cord compression；本题 fetal tracing 正常。
>
> **B - Cesarean delivery**：需要 fetal distress 或 malpresentation，本题 vertex 且 tracing reassuring。
>
> **C - Magnesium sulfate**：fetal neuroprotection 的门槛通常是预计 `<32 weeks` 分娩。
>
> **D - Nifedipine**：tocolysis 不适合 `>=34 weeks` 的 preterm rupture of membranes。
>
> **F - Terbutaline**：同样是 tocolytic，逻辑上和 D 一样不对。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Nifedipine。很多人见到 35 周 contractions 会先想“抑制宫缩”，但 PROM `>=34 weeks` 应该走 delivery 路线，同时别漏掉 GBS prophylaxis。
>

###### Q053

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q053 心内 / LV aneurysm embolus
> ^Q053
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：2 个月前 anterior STEMI 后仍有 persistent ST elevation + deep Q waves，现在突然 acute limb ischemia，优先锁定 **left ventricular aneurysm with mural thrombus embolization**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Severe leg pain, numbness, cold limb | Acute arterial occlusion | 先识别这是 acute limb ischemia |
> | Femoral pulse present, distal pulses absent | Embolus lodged distally | 局灶远端动脉阻塞，支持 embolic event |
> | Anterior wall MI 2 months ago | Classic timing for LV aneurysm | LV aneurysm 是 STEMI 的晚期并发症 |
> | Persistent ST elevation with deep Q waves | ECG hallmark of LV aneurysm | 这是最关键的题眼，不是 reinfarction |
> | Apical holosystolic murmur | LV remodeling causing secondary MR | 进一步支持 post-MI structural change |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Left ventricular aneurysm**
>
> **Core Concept**：Transmural MI 后坏死区瘢痕化、变薄、dyskinetic，形成 LV aneurysm。腔内血流淤滞可形成 mural thrombus，随后 systemic embolization，造成 stroke、mesenteric ischemia 或 acute limb ischemia。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题把 ECG 和并发症串在一起考。看到 persistent ST elevation after old MI，不要自动以为“又梗了”，而要想到 dyskinetic scarred ventricle。
>
> ```text
> transmural anterior MI
> -> scarred thin dyskinetic LV wall
> -> LV aneurysm
> -> stagnant blood flow
> -> mural thrombus
> -> systemic embolus
> -> acute limb ischemia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Post-MI complication | Time Course | Signature Clue |
> |---|---|---|
> | Papillary muscle rupture | 3-5 days | Acute pulmonary edema, severe MR, shock |
> | Free wall rupture | 5 days-2 weeks | Tamponade, sudden death |
> | Ventricular septal rupture | 3-5 days | New harsh murmur, biventricular failure |
> | LV aneurysm | Weeks to months | Persistent ST elevation, HF, thromboembolism |
> | Reinfarction | Any time | New ischemic symptoms and new ECG/troponin change |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Old STEMI + ST still up = aneurysm until proven otherwise."**
> - 旧 MI 后 ST 还高，不等于新梗死。
> - Aneurysm 最爱考 thrombus 和 embolus。
> - Distal pulse loss 是 embolic occlusion 的落点。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Aortic dissection**：也能造成 limb ischemia，但通常伴突发胸背痛，且题干给了强烈 post-MI ECG 线索。
>
> **C - Nonbacterial thrombotic endocarditis**：可 embolize，但常见于 malignancy/hypercoagulability，没有 persistent ST elevation 这个特征。
>
> **D - Papillary muscle rupture**：发生在 MI 后几天内，表现为 acute severe MR、hypotension、pulmonary edema；时间轴不对。
>
> **E - Recurrent myocardial infarction**：应有新胸痛、呼吸困难或 ECG 新变化；题干明确 ECG unchanged。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Recurrent myocardial infarction。因为看到 persistent ST elevation 容易误判成 ongoing infarction，但“2 months later + unchanged ECG” 是典型 LV aneurysm。
>

###### Q054

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q054 GI / Vitamin C deficiency
> ^Q054
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：malnutrition + poor dentition + perifollicular hemorrhage/coiled hair，先想 **scurvy (vitamin C deficiency)**，相关联的典型后果是 **delayed wound healing**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Eats mostly breads and canned foods | Low fresh fruit/vegetable intake | 缺少 vitamin C 的饮食背景 |
> | Poor dentition, thin, weak | Malnutrition clue | 老年营养缺乏题常先看整体营养状态 |
> | Rough follicular papules with surrounding hemorrhage | Classic scurvy skin finding | perifollicular hemorrhage 很高频 |
> | Coiled hairs in explanation | Corkscrew hair strongly points to vitamin C deficiency | 基本是 scurvy 的指纹线索 |
> | Hip fracture after fall | Frailty context, but not the primary tested concept | 骨折是背景，不是考点核心 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Delayed wound healing**
>
> **Core Concept**：Vitamin C 是 collagen synthesis 中 proline/lysine hydroxylation 所必需。缺乏后 collagen cross-linking 变差，connective tissue strength 下降，出现 perifollicular hemorrhage、gingival disease、mucosal bleeding，以及 delayed wound healing。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：scurvy 不是“凝血因子不够”，而是“胶原质量不够”。血管壁和结缔组织变脆，所以会出血、伤口不易愈合。
>
> ```text
> vitamin C deficiency
> -> impaired proline/lysine hydroxylation
> -> weak collagen cross-linking
> -> fragile capillaries and poor connective tissue strength
> -> perifollicular hemorrhage + delayed wound healing
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Vitamin deficiency | Signature Clue | Associated Finding |
> |---|---|---|
> | Vitamin C | Corkscrew hair, perifollicular hemorrhage | Delayed wound healing |
> | Vitamin B1 | Neuropathy, cardiomyopathy | High-output heart failure |
> | Vitamin K | Easy bruising, bleeding | Impaired coagulation factors |
> | Vitamin B12 | Ataxia, paresthesia | Loss of proprioception |
> | Vitamin A | Xerophthalmia | Night blindness |
> | Niacin | Dermatitis, diarrhea, dementia | Progressive dementia |
> | Vitamin B6 | Isoniazid-related neuropathy | Sideroblastic anemia |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"C for collagen, corkscrew hair, capillary fragility."**
> - 只要看到 perifollicular hemorrhage，就先把 scurvy 放最前。
> - 记住这是 connective tissue problem，不是 primary coagulopathy。
> - 题目常用 poor dentition 和极差饮食来加固诊断。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - High-output cardiac failure**：对应 thiamine deficiency (beriberi)，不是 scurvy。
>
> **C - Impaired coagulation**：容易被“出血”误导，但 vitamin C deficiency 的本质是 collagen defect，不是 coagulation factor deficiency。
>
> **D - Loss of proprioception**：指向 vitamin B12 deficiency 的 posterior column involvement。
>
> **E - Night blindness**：典型 vitamin A deficiency。
>
> **F - Progressive dementia**：更符合 niacin deficiency 的 pellagra。
>
> **G - Sideroblastic anemia**：更常联系 vitamin B6 deficiency 或 copper deficiency。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Impaired coagulation。因为题干有 hemorrhage，但出血分布在毛囊周围且合并 corkscrew hair，更像 collagen fragility 而不是 clotting factor problem。

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |

> pregnancy
> -> urinary stasis and hydronephrosis
> -> ascending UTI
> -> acute pyelonephritis
> -> systemic inflammation
> -> ARDS + uterine irritability/preterm labor
> ```

> | Peripartum cardiomyopathy | HF signs, JVD, S3, edema | 本题有感染源和 flank pain |
> | Abruptio placentae | Painful/tender uterus, fetal distress | 本题无 uterine tenderness or decelerations |
> | Severe preeclampsia | Hypertension + end-organ signs | 本题 hypotensive |
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q062 Biostats_Master / CI crosses null
> ^Q062
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：题干真正考点不是 hepatitis C treatment 本身，而是 **confidence interval crosses the null value = no statistically significant difference**，所以 8 weeks 已达到相近 SVR12，且 adverse effects 更少。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | HCV genotype 1a, treatment-naive, no cirrhosis | Matches the ad's target population | 先确认这名患者是否能外推研究结果 |
> | Baseline HCV RNA 2 million IU/mL | Falls in the <4 million subgroup | 对应广告里给出的 subgroup |
> | 8-week course had 96% SVR12 | High efficacy in matching subgroup | 说明短疗程已经很好 |
> | CI for 8 vs 12 weeks crosses 0% | No statistically significant difference | 统计学核心句 |
> | Longer treatment has more adverse effects | Tips decision toward shorter regimen | 疗效差不多时选副作用更低的方案 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - The patient should receive 8 weeks of treatment for optimal results.**
>
> **Core Concept**：当 **8-week regimen** 与 **12-week regimen** 的疗效差异 **not statistically significant**，而 longer exposure 增加 adverse reactions 时，最佳结论就是选择 **更短但同样有效** 的方案。这里判断统计学显著性的抓手是 **confidence interval includes the null value**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：对连续或比例差值的比较，如果 CI 跨过“无差异点”，就说明不能排除真差异为 0，因此不能说 12 weeks 更有效；临床上就会回到 risk-benefit 权衡。
>
> ```text
> compare 8 weeks vs 12 weeks
> -> confidence interval includes 0
> -> no statistically significant efficacy difference
> -> compare harms
> -> choose shorter course if adverse effects are lower
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：这题在考什么
> | Finding | Interpretation | Exam Decision |
> |---|---|---|
> | CI excludes null | Statistically significant difference | Can claim a real difference |
> | CI includes null | No statistically significant difference | Do not claim superiority |
> | Similar efficacy, fewer adverse effects | Better net benefit | Prefer shorter/safer option |
> | Patient matches trial subgroup | Results generalizable | No need for extra subgroup fishing |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"If the interval touches zero, superiority disappears."**
> - CI 过 null，不要硬说更长疗程更好。
> - 疗效打平时，副作用更少的一方赢。
> - 本题借 HCV 包装，其实在考 biostats decision-making。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Additional subgroup analysis required**：患者已经非常贴合现有 subgroup，不需要再继续切更小亚组。
>
> **B - Study exclusion criteria**：题干没有 cirrhosis、renal failure、prior treatment 等排除线索，反而与研究人群高度匹配。
>

> ### 💡 一句话锁定

> | Placebo-controlled RCT | Randomized intervention vs placebo | 有标准治疗存在时通常不合伦理 |

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q064 Biostats_Master / Statistical vs clinical significance
> ^Q064
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：作者不展示某个 subgroup bar graph，最常见不是“有问题”，而是 **结果没带来新的 clinically useful information**。本题对应选项就是 **lack of significant difference in primary endpoint**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 8-week low viral load subgroup had 96% SVR12 | Already excellent outcome shown | 已经知道短疗程 subgroup 结果很好 |
> | Overall 8-week arm had 95% SVR12 | Subgroup is similar to overall arm | 差异很小，提示再多画一根柱子意义不大 |
> | No significant difference between 8-week and 12-week groups | Longer treatment not superior | 关键统计背景 |
> | Omitted result is 12-week low viral load subgroup | Likely also similar and unremarkable | 不展示往往因为“不增加信息” |
> | Question asks most likely reason for omission | Focus is interpretation, not trial misconduct | 别被 bias/randomization/ITT 这些术语诱走 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Lack of significant difference in primary endpoint**
>
> **Core Concept**：如果缺失的那组结果大概率与已展示结果 **差不多**，而且不会改变对 primary endpoint 的判断，作者就可能不展示它，因为临床信息增量太低。USMLE 要你理解：**不是所有“没差异”的结果都值得单独成图**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：统计显著性回答“差异是否超过随机波动”，临床显著性回答“这差异有没有实际决策价值”。本题里即使再补一个 12-week low-viral-load bar，结论仍是差不多，因此图被省略。
>
> ```text
> subgroup result likely similar
> + no significant efficacy difference
> -> no new message about primary endpoint
> -> low clinical added value
> -> omitted from ad
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：常见统计术语误导
> | Choice Pattern | What It Means | Why Not Here |
> |---|---|---|
> | Post-hoc analysis bias | Unplanned subgroup testing after seeing data | 不是解释“为什么没画这根柱子”的最佳答案 |
> | Failed randomization | Baseline imbalance due to bad allocation | 题干没有这种证据 |
> | Insufficient power | Sample too small to detect true difference | 解释页反而暗示 subgroup size should be adequate |
> | No significant difference | Similar outcome, little new value | 本题正确逻辑 |
> | ITT violation | Patients analyzed outside assigned groups | 解释明确说 ITT was performed |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"No new message, no new graph."**
> - 结果若不改结论，广告可直接不展示。
> - 统计学题常把“没显著差异”包装成“为什么作者这样写”。
> - 先选最直接解释，不要过度阴谋论。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Concern about post-hoc analysis bias**：这是一个真实统计风险，但不能最好解释本题这项结果为何被省略。
>
> **B - Failure of proper randomization**：若随机化失败，通常会看到 baseline imbalance 或方法学质疑，本题没有。
>
> **C - Insufficient power**：解释页指出 8-week subgroup 已有 235 人，按随机化推断 12-week arm 低病毒载量 subgroup 也不至于小到完全没法判断。
>
> **E - Violation of intention-to-treat analysis**：原文明确写了 ITT 分析包含 12-week treatment patients。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Insufficient power。因为“没展示结果”很容易让人脑补样本太少，但题干和解释反而在暗示该 subgroup 规模足够，而且真正问题是“即便展示也没新信息”。

###### Q066

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q066 免疫疫苗 / Antiphospholipid syndrome
> ^Q066
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：acute DVT pattern + recurrent miscarriages + livedo reticularis，直接锁定 **antiphospholipid syndrome (APS)**；实验室最经典的反常点是 **prolonged PTT**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 3-day unilateral leg pain and swelling | Suggests acute deep venous thrombosis | 先建立“正在血栓”这个主线 |
> | Recurrent miscarriages | Classic obstetric clue for APS | 反复流产是 APS 高频题眼 |
> | Purplish blotchy rash worsened by cold | Matches livedo reticularis | 不是单纯皮疹，而是 vaso-occlusive skin clue |
> | Rendered image shows mottled, lacy rash on legs | Visually confirms livedo reticularis | 图像是加分确认，不是另一个诊断 |
> | Asked what is found on additional evaluation | Pushes toward APS lab pattern | 题目不是问诊断，而是问下一步还能看到什么 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Prolongation of partial thromboplastin time**
>
> **Core Concept**：APS 虽然是 **hypercoagulable state**，但实验室上常见 **PTT prolongation**，因为 **lupus anticoagulant** 会干扰体外 phospholipid-dependent clotting assay。临床上血栓更多，实验室上 PTT 却更长，这是经典“反直觉考点”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：APS 抗体在体内促进 thrombosis，但在试管里会结合检测所用 phospholipid reagent，导致 aPTT 看起来延长；所以它是“体内促凝、体外抗凝”的典型反差。
>
> ```text
> antiphospholipid antibodies
> -> endothelial and coagulation activation in vivo
> -> thrombosis and pregnancy loss
> -> bind phospholipid reagent in vitro
> -> prolonged PTT
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：Livedo reticularis 联想
> | Finding | Most Useful Association | Why It Fits or Not |
> |---|---|---|
> | Livedo reticularis + DVT + miscarriages | Antiphospholipid syndrome | 本题标准组合 |
> | Livedo reticularis alone in cold weather | Benign physiologic LR | 若无血栓/流产线索可偏良性 |
> | Elevated beta-2 microglobulin | Lymphoproliferative disease | 不解释 obstetric thrombosis pattern |
> | Increased transferrin saturation | Hemochromatosis | 与皮疹和流产线索不匹配 |
> | Prolonged PT | Extrinsic pathway problem | 不是 APS 经典化验模式 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"APS clots in the body but prolongs PTT in the tube."**
> - Miscarriage + thrombosis：先想 APS。
> - Livedo reticularis：皮肤在帮你点题。
> - PTT 延长不等于出血病，这题正好相反。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Elevated beta-2 microglobulin level**：更像 multiple myeloma 或其他高细胞周转状态，不解释 miscarriages + DVT。
>
> **B - Increased transferrin saturation**：指向 hemochromatosis，和这道 hypercoagulable/obstetric 题不在一个轨道。
>
> **D - Prolongation of prothrombin time**：APS 经典是 **PTT prolonged, PT usually normal**。
>
> **E - Vitamin B12 deficiency**：可导致 anemia 和 neurologic symptoms，但不能串起 livedo reticularis、DVT、反复流产。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Prolongation of prothrombin time。因为很多人只记得“凝血时间会异常”，却忘了 APS 特异性更强的是 **aPTT 延长**。
>

>
> ```text
> thyroid surgery
> -> parathyroid injury or removal
> -> low PTH
> -> low calcium + high phosphorus
> -> high calcium-phosphorus product
> -> basal ganglia calcification
> -> extrapyramidal symptoms
> ```
>
> ---
>

> | Basal ganglia calcification | Chronic complication | Cannabis/vitamin toxicities 不解释 |
> | Dry coarse skin | Can accompany chronic hypocalcemia | 不是 vitamin A toxicity 专属 |
> | Thyroglobulin <0.1 and anti-thyroglobulin Ab negative | Argues against recurrent thyroid cancer | recurrence 证据不足 |
> | Hypercalcemia | Vitamin D toxicity pattern | 与本题相反 |
>

>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"No parathyroids, no calcium control."**
> - Thyroid surgery 后看到 low Ca/high P，先想 parathyroid injury。

>

>

>
> **E - Vitamin D toxicity**：应见 **hypercalcemia**，与题面方向相反。

> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Vitamin A toxicity。因为 dry skin、headache、blurry vision 容易让人飘过去，但真正决定题目的还是 **post-thyroidectomy + low Ca/high P + basal ganglia calcification**。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q075 心内 / Anthracycline cardiotoxicity
> ^Q075
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：化疗史里看到 **doxorubicin**，再配 **S3 + displaced apical impulse + cardiomegaly**，就是 anthracycline 引起的 dilated cardiomyopathy。病理机制选 **cardiomyocyte necrosis with replacement fibrosis**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | ABVD includes doxorubicin | Anthracycline exposure | 题目的真正毒性抓手 |
> | Progressive exertional dyspnea | Heart failure symptom | 慢性 LV systolic dysfunction 线索 |
> | S3 and displaced apical impulse | Dilated cardiomyopathy findings | 比 pulmonary exam 更有指向性 |
> | Clear lungs with cardiomegaly | Cardiac cause over primary lung toxicity | 虽然 bleomycin 也会考，但这里不是肺纤维化画风 |
> | No chest radiation history | Makes constrictive pericardial disease less likely | 排除 pericardial fibrosis 选项 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Cardiomyocyte replacement by fibrous tissue**
>
> **Core Concept**：anthracyclines 如 **doxorubicin** 会造成 **type I chemotherapy-induced cardiotoxicity**，本质是 **cardiac myocyte necrosis and death**，后续被 fibrous tissue 替代，因此往往 **less reversible**，并与 **cumulative dose** 相关。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：anthracycline 通过 oxidative membrane injury 以及 **topoisomerase II-anthracycline-DNA complex** 诱导 myocyte death，不是单纯功能性抑制，所以与 trastuzumab 那种可逆的 myocardial stunning 不同。
>
> ```text
> doxorubicin exposure
> -> oxidative injury + topo II mediated damage
> -> cardiomyocyte necrosis
> -> replacement fibrosis
> -> dilated cardiomyopathy / HFrEF
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：化疗相关心毒性
> | Toxicity type | Drug | Mechanism | Reversibility |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | Type I | Anthracyclines | Myocyte necrosis, replacement fibrosis | Less reversible |
> | Type II | Trastuzumab | Myocardial stunning/dysfunction without cell death | More reversible |
> | Radiation-related | Chest radiation | Pericardial thickening/fibrosis | Structural, not anthracycline pattern |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Doxorubicin destroys; trastuzumab stuns."**
> - doxorubicin: dead myocytes, scar
> - trastuzumab: stunned myocardium, often reversible
> - exam clue: S3 + cardiomegaly after chemo
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Cardiomyocyte hypertrophy and disarray**：这是 hypertrophic cardiomyopathy 的病理，不是 chemo toxicity。
>
> **C - Ischemic myocardial necrosis**：如果是 MI 后心衰，通常要有 coronary disease or infarct context。
>
> **D - Myocardial stunning**：更符合 trastuzumab 的 type II cardiotoxicity，而不是 doxorubicin。
>

> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |

>

> -> abrupt psychotic symptoms
> -> duration >=1 day and <1 month
> -> return to premorbid functioning
> = brief psychotic disorder
> ```

> | Disorder | Core Feature | Why Not This One |

> | Schizophreniform disorder | >=1 month and <6 months | 本题不满足时长 |
> | Schizophrenia | >=6 months with functional decline | 本题很快恢复工作 |
> | Acute stress disorder | Trauma-related intrusion/avoidance/hyperarousal | 不是 psychosis 主导 |

>
> ```text
> trauma with major blood loss
> -> decreased intravascular volume
> -> decreased tissue perfusion
> -> hypotension and tachycardia
> -> immediate uncrossmatched type O blood
> -> activate MTP if instability persists
> ```

> ---

> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Step | When to Do It | Why |

> | Retrograde urethrogram | Suspected urethral injury after stabilization | Important but not before resuscitation |
> | CT abdomen/pelvis | Hemodynamically stable enough for transport | Diagnostic, not first in shock |
> | Vasopressor | Refractory shock after volume correction or nonhemorrhagic etiologies | Contraindicated in isolated hemorrhagic shock |
>

> **"Shock first, scans later."**

>

>
> **D - Retrograde urethrogram**：blood at urethral meatus 和 pelvic fracture 确实提示 urethral injury，但要在 hemodynamic stabilization 之后。
>
> **E - Tranexamic acid**：可以考虑早期辅助，但不能替代立刻恢复 circulating volume 的 blood transfusion。
>

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Normal behavior**
>
> **Core Concept**：Children younger than 6 often do **not fully understand permanence, inevitability, or universality of death**. 他们会把 fantasy、media、自己的愿望混进对死亡的理解里。只要 **school/social functioning 正常**，这类反复提问、night-light、和死去宠物“保持联系”都可属于发育正常范围。
>

> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是在考 anxiety diagnosis，而是在考 **developmental psychology**。先问自己“这个行为是否超出该年龄层的认知特点，并造成 impairment”。
>
> ```text
> age < 6
> -> incomplete concept of death
> -> magical thinking and reversibility beliefs
> -> questions and rituals after a loss
> + preserved functioning
> = normal behavior
> ```

> **"Preschoolers think death is part fantasy, not finality."**

> - 功能是否受损是第二线索。
> - Magical thinking 在这个年龄段常是 normal development。

> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Adjustment disorder with anxiety**：如果反应明显过度并影响家庭或学校功能才更像 adjustment disorder。
>
> **B - Generalized anxiety disorder**：GAD 是多个领域长期过度担忧，不是围绕近期 loss 的发展期特异问题。
>
> **D - Post-traumatic stress disorder**：她没有 intrusive reexperiencing、flashbacks、nightmares 或 hyperarousal syndrome。
>
> **E - Separation anxiety disorder**：虽然她担心父母死亡，但没有 school refusal、clinging 到无法分离或明显 separation-triggered meltdown。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Separation anxiety disorder。题干故意给了担心父亲晚归、坐在父亲腿上，但 **能上学、能交朋友、无明显分离障碍** 把答案拉回正常发育。
