> [!example]- UWSA2 Q121 derm / Psoriasis nail disease
> ^Q121
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：extensor plaques + nail pitting/onycholysis + recent beta blocker，先锁定 **psoriasis**；nail involvement 最该联想到后续 **psoriatic arthritis**，这里对应 symmetric polyarthritis。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | erythematous plaques on extremities | Classic psoriatic skin pattern | 四肢斑块先把方向拉向 psoriasis，而不是单纯湿疹 |
> | nail pitting and distal onycholysis | Strong psoriasis-associated nail findings | nail pitting 是题眼，也提示 arthritis 风险更高 |
> | beta blocker recently added | Can worsen psoriasis | propranolol 类药物是经典加重因素 |
> | asking about greatest future risk | Wants association/complication, not rash diagnosis | 题目不是问皮疹本身，而是问并发症联想 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Symmetric polyarthritis**
>
> **Core Concept**：Psoriasis 不只累及皮肤。合并 **nail disease** 的患者，发生 **psoriatic arthritis** 的风险更高。其关节表现可以是 DIP arthritis、asymmetric oligoarthritis、axial disease，也可以是 **symmetric polyarthritis**，外观上很像 rheumatoid arthritis。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：psoriasis 是 immune-mediated inflammatory disease。皮肤和甲板角化异常只是表面表现，真正高频考点是它和关节炎的联动，尤其在 nail involvement 时更明显。
>
> ```text
> psoriasis
> -> nail pitting / onycholysis
> -> higher risk of psoriatic arthritis
> -> may present as symmetric polyarthritis
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Condition | 典型线索 | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Psoriasis | extensor plaques, silvery scale, nail pitting | 本题完全吻合 |
> | Systemic sclerosis | sclerodactyly, Raynaud, telangiectasia, dysmotility | 没有皮肤变硬，也不是甲点凹 |
> | Sjogren syndrome | dry eyes, dry mouth, parotid issues | 和甲板改变不匹配 |
> | Vitiligo | depigmented patches | 不是 erythematous plaques |
> | IPF / clubbing | clubbing, cyanosis | clubbing 不是 nail pitting |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Psoriasis hits skin, nails, then joints."**
> - 看到 **nail pitting**，别只停在皮肤题。
> - nail disease 是 psoriatic arthritis 的加分线索。
> - beta blocker 是常见出题诱因。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Autoimmune thyroiditis**：更像 vitiligo 相关自身免疫联动，不是 psoriasis 的代表并发症。
>
> **B - Esophageal dysmotility**：这是 systemic sclerosis / CREST 的味道。
>
> **C - Exocrine gland dysfunction**：提示 Sjogren syndrome，不符合本题皮损和甲损。
>
> **D - Fungal endophthalmitis**：需要 candidemia/immunocompromise 场景，不由 psoriasis 本身导出。
>
> **E - Progressive lung fibrosis**：如果是 clubbing 才会更靠近肺病；本题是 pitting/onycholysis。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Esophageal dysmotility。因为它也是经典“皮肤病联想系统并发症”题型，但 systemic sclerosis 的皮肤体征和甲板表现完全不同。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q122 GI / Primary sclerosing cholangitis
> ^Q122
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：male + pruritus + jaundice + dark urine/pale stools + markedly cholestatic labs + MRCP strictures/dilations，直接锁定 **primary sclerosing cholangitis (PSC)**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | fatigue and pruritus | Common cholestatic presentation | 胆汁淤积题先看 pruritus |
> | scleral icterus, dark urine, pale stools | Conjugated hyperbilirubinemia | 说明胆汁排泄受阻 |
> | alkaline phosphatase 580 U/L | Cholestatic liver injury pattern | ALP 明显升高比 AST/ALT 更关键 |
> | middle-aged man with MRCP multifocal stricturing/dilation | Classic PSC imaging pattern | 这是和 PBC、pancreatic cancer 拉开的核心线索 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：E - Primary sclerosing cholangitis**
>
> **Core Concept**：PSC 是 intrahepatic 和 extrahepatic bile ducts 的慢性炎症纤维化狭窄病，典型表现是 **cholestatic labs**、fatigue、pruritus、jaundice，以及 MRCP 上的 **multifocal strictures with segmental dilations**。考试里它和 **ulcerative colitis** 的关联也要顺手想到。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：胆管壁持续炎症和纤维化，导致多发狭窄；未狭窄段相对扩张，所以成像上出现串珠样不规则改变。
>
> ```text
> bile duct inflammation
> -> fibrosis and stricturing
> -> impaired bile flow
> -> cholestasis
> -> pruritus / jaundice / elevated ALP
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | 典型线索 | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | PSC | male, cholestasis, MRCP multifocal strictures | 本题最匹配 |
> | PBC | usually women, AMA positive, small ducts | MRCP 往往不典型 |
> | Autoimmune hepatitis | hepatocellular injury, high AST/ALT | 不是以 ALP 为主 |
> | Choledocholithiasis | RUQ pain, obstructing stone | 会看到 stone/CBD dilation |
> | Pancreatic head cancer | pancreatic mass compressing bile duct | 不是多发胆管狭窄 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"PSC = beaded ducts in a pruritic man."**
> - **P**ruritus
> - **S**trictures on cholangiography
> - **C**holestatic labs
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Autoimmune hepatitis**：核心是 hepatocellular injury，不是这种以 ALP 为主的胆汁淤积模式。
>
> **B - Choledocholithiasis**：也会 cholestatic，但更常有 RUQ pain，而且成像应见 stone 或单点梗阻。
>
> **C - Pancreatic head cancer**：会导致梗阻性黄疸，但影像重点应是 pancreatic mass，不是 multifocal stricturing。
>
> **D - Primary biliary cholangitis**：很爱考混淆，但 PBC 更常见于女性，且 MRCP 通常不出现 PSC 这种典型大中胆管改变。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Primary biliary cholangitis。两者都能出现 pruritus + cholestatic labs，但性别和 cholangiography pattern 把答案定到 PSC。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q123 外科 / Oral squamous cell carcinoma
> ^Q123
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：nonhealing tongue ulcer + indurated margins + tobacco history，下一步不是经验治疗，而是 **biopsy of the lesion**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | ulcer persists for 1 month and does not heal | Persistent ulcer is malignant until proven otherwise | 反复不愈合比“疼不疼”更关键 |
> | indurated margins | Red flag for squamous cell carcinoma | 硬边是肿瘤侵袭感的典型提示 |
> | lateral tongue lesion | Common oral SCC location | 舌侧缘是经典高发部位 |
> | former 30-pack-year smoker, HIV | Strong risk context | 烟草暴露风险远大于普通复发性口疮 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Biopsy of the lesion**
>
> **Core Concept**：oral squamous cell carcinoma 常表现为 **nonhealing ulcer with indurated edges**。对这类病灶，最重要的 first step 是 **obtain tissue diagnosis**。只有在明确病理后，才进入 staging 和 definitive treatment。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：口腔鳞癌来自 squamous epithelium 的逐步恶变。早期可先是 leukoplakia/erythroplakia，继续进展后形成不愈合溃疡并向深部组织侵袭。
>
> ```text
> tobacco exposure
> -> epithelial dysplasia
> -> carcinoma in situ
> -> invasive squamous cell carcinoma
> -> persistent indurated oral ulcer
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Lesion | Typical clue | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Oral SCC | nonhealing indurated ulcer | 本题最典型 |
> | HSV | painful vesicles/gingivostomatitis | 不是单个持久硬边溃疡 |
> | Aphthous ulcer | painful, shallow, self-limited | 通常 <2 weeks，常反复但会自愈 |
> | Oral candidiasis | white plaques that scrape off | 不是溃疡型硬边病灶 |
> | Syphilitic chancre | painless ulcer | 本题有疼痛且总体更像肿瘤 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"If it does not heal, biopsy the edge."**
> - 口腔 persistent ulcer 不要先用药试错。
> - **induration** 是关键危险信号。
> - smoker + lateral tongue = oral SCC 高频场景。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Acyclovir therapy**：HSV 更像 vesicles/gingivostomatitis，不是单个持续 1 个月的硬边溃疡。
>
> **B - Benzathine penicillin**：primary syphilis 的口腔 chancre 通常 painless。
>
> **D - Fluconazole therapy**：candidiasis 是 white patches/plaques，不会是 indurated ulcer。
>
> **E - Pathergy test**：Behcet syndrome 走 recurrent aphthous ulcer 路线，不是这种单发持续硬边病灶。
>
> **F - Supplementation with B vitamins**：B-vitamin deficiency 更像 glossitis with loss of papillae。
>
> **G - Topical glucocorticoid**：适合 aphthous ulcer，不适合 malignancy warning lesion。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：G - Topical glucocorticoid。因为题干提到既往有 oral ulcers，容易让人误判为 aphthous ulcer；但这次的题眼是 **persistent + indurated**。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q124 psych / Lithium-induced hypothyroidism
> ^Q124
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：bipolar patient on chronic lithium 出现 fatigue, weight gain, poor concentration, decreased libido，先别急着改精神科药，先 **obtain thyroid function tests**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | chronic lithium therapy | Classic endocrine adverse effect risk | lithium 高频副作用就是 hypothyroidism |
> | fatigue, weight gain, impaired concentration | Can be hypothyroid symptoms | 和 depression 重叠，所以最容易误判 |
> | decreased libido | Also compatible with hypothyroidism | 不一定只是 SSRI 副作用 |
> | therapeutic lithium level and previously stable mood | Makes toxicity or clear bipolar relapse less likely | 提示先查 medical cause |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Obtain thyroid function tests**
>
> **Core Concept**：Lithium 可抑制 thyroid hormone synthesis/release，导致 **hypothyroidism**。当 lithium 患者出现“像 depression”的新症状时，USMLE 常考你先排除 **可逆的内分泌原因**，而不是马上换 antidepressant、加 psychotherapy 或加新的 bipolar medication。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：lithium 干扰 T3/T4 的形成和释放，甲状腺激素下降后，TSH 代偿性升高，临床上就会出现乏力、体重增加、注意力差、性欲下降等表现。
>
> ```text
> chronic lithium use
> -> impaired T3/T4 production/release
> -> hypothyroidism
> -> fatigue / weight gain / low libido / poor concentration
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Option | 为什么现在不选 | 正确思路 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Add sildenafil | Treats symptom only | 先找性欲下降的病因 |
> | Switch to bupropion | SSRI adverse effect is possible but not first | 先排除 lithium-induced hypothyroidism |
> | CBT referral | Useful later if needed | 不能跳过 medical workup |
> | Start lurasidone | Bipolar depression drug | 先确认是否真是 mood relapse |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Lithium slows the thyroid before you blame the mood."**
> - lithium + fatigue/weight gain = check TSH/T4
> - psychiatric symptoms can be endocrine mimic
> - first step = evaluate, not escalate psych meds
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Add sildenafil**：对 erectile dysfunction 有用，但这里的主诉是 low libido，且可能是 hypothyroidism 驱动。
>
> **B - Discontinue escitalopram and start bupropion**：SSRI 的确会影响性功能，但本题全套症状更像 hypothyroidism，先查 thyroid。
>
> **D - Refer for cognitive behavioral therapy**：不是错方向，只是不是 next best step。
>
> **E - Start lurasidone**：只有在确定是 bipolar depressive episode 后才考虑加药；当前先排除 lithium adverse effect。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：B - Discontinue escitalopram and start bupropion。因为 decreased libido 很容易让人直接联想到 SSRI sexual dysfunction，但 weight gain + fatigue + poor concentration 把题干重新拉回 hypothyroidism。
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q125 心内 / Ventricular septal defect
> ^Q125
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：young asymptomatic adult + loud harsh holosystolic murmur + thrill at left lower sternal border，最典型就是 **ventricular septal defect (VSD)**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | murmur since childhood | Congenital lesion likely persisted into adulthood | 先想 congenital heart disease |
> | no symptoms, good exercise tolerance | Suggests small restrictive lesion | 小缺损常可长期无症状 |
> | loud holosystolic murmur | Signature VSD sound | holosystolic 是第一步定位 |
> | fourth intercostal space, left sternal border, thrill | Classic location and intensity for restrictive VSD | 左胸骨缘第 3-4 肋间最经典 |
> | normal upper and lower extremity pulses | Argues against coarctation | 顺手排掉主动脉缩窄 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：G - Ventricular septal defect**
>
> **Core Concept**：small restrictive VSD 因为跨缺损压差大，所以往往产生 **loud harsh holosystolic murmur**，并可伴 **palpable thrill**。这类患者右心压力和整体血流动力学可以长期正常，因此成人仍可能无症状。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：左心室收缩压高于右心室，收缩期血液持续从左向右分流，所以产生整个收缩期都在的杂音。缺损越小，阻力越大，声音反而越响。
>
> ```text
> small VSD
> -> high pressure LV-to-RV shunt during systole
> -> harsh holosystolic murmur
> -> palpable thrill at LLSB
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Diagnosis | Murmur pattern | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | VSD | harsh holosystolic at LLSB | 本题完全符合 |
> | ASD | fixed split S2, flow murmur | 不是 holosystolic |
> | PDA | continuous machinery murmur | 不是“全收缩期” |
> | HOCM | crescendo-decrescendo, dynamic | 会受 Valsalva/standing 影响 |
> | MR/flail leaflet | holosystolic at apex | 听诊位置不对 |
> | Severe AS | systolic ejection murmur at RUSB | 也不是 LLSB thrill 型 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Small hole, loud noise."**
> - restrictive VSD 常常 **更响**
> - location: **LLSB**
> - thrill makes VSD 更像 textbook stem
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破

> |---|---|---|

>

> ```text
> prochlorperazine
> -> dopamine D2 blockade
> -> nigrostriatal dysfunction
> -> extrapyramidal symptoms
> -> akathisia
> -> cannot sit still / needs to pace
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Medication | Common notable adverse effect | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Prochlorperazine | EPS, including akathisia | 本题正确答案 |

> **"Need to walk after dopamine block = akathisia."**
> - antiemetic can act like antipsychotic
> - akathisia is a movement side effect, not just anxiety
> - pacing > tremor for this diagnosis
>

> ```text
> aging or prior chemo/radiation

> ```

> ```

> **正确答案：E - Referral for valve replacement**
>
> **Core Concept**：AS 治疗决策看两件事: **severity** 和 **symptoms**。本题两者都满足，所以应转介做 **aortic valve replacement**。对 symptomatic severe AS，继续随访会错过最佳干预窗口。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：AS 是 fixed outflow obstruction。左心室必须长期对抗高后负荷，先形成 concentric hypertrophy；一旦代偿失败或灌注不足，患者开始出现 dyspnea, angina, syncope，之后死亡风险明显上升。
>
> ```text
> calcified narrowed aortic valve
> -> fixed LV outflow obstruction
> -> chronic pressure overload
> -> concentric LV hypertrophy
> -> exertional symptoms develop
> -> valve replacement indicated
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Scenario | Best Step |
> |---|---|
> | Severe AS + symptoms | Valve replacement |
> | Severe AS + no symptoms | Close follow-up, consider exercise testing |
> | Mild/moderate AS | Serial echocardiography based on severity |
> | Severe AS + active CAD evaluation before surgery | Coronary angiography as pre-op planning |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Severe plus symptomatic equals surgery."**
> - AS 题先找 **AVA number**。
> - 再问自己有没有 **dyspnea, angina, syncope/presyncope**。
> - 两者都在，就不要被“再观察一下”诱导。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Addition of beta blocker**：对 symptomatic severe AS 不解决固定梗阻，反而可能降低收缩力、使 hemodynamics 更差。
>
> **B - Addition of isosorbide mononitrate**：nitrates 可能导致显著 hypotension，尤其在 fixed outflow obstruction 背景下。
>
> **C - Exercise stress testing**：适用于 **asymptomatic** severe AS，用来挖出潜在症状或异常血压反应；本题已经有症状。
>
> **D - Follow-up echocardiogram in 6 months**：仍属于 surveillance 思路，只适合无症状患者。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：D - Follow-up echocardiogram in 6 months。因为不少人记得 severe AS 要每 6 to 12 months follow-up，但那是 **asymptomatic severe AS**，本题已经跨过那个门槛。

> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：看到 RR/OR 和 95% CI，先做两步: **看点估计是否大于或小于 1，再看 CI 是否跨 1**。本题 smoking 对 endometrial cancer 是 **0.81 (0.74-0.88)**，所以是 **statistically significant decreased risk**。

>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Relative risks or odds ratios | Null value is 1 | RR/OR 题第一反应就是盯住 1 |
> | 95% confidence intervals provided | Determines statistical significance | CI 包不包含 1 比“感觉大小”更重要 |
> | Smoking and endometrial cancer: 0.81 (0.74-0.88) | Less than 1 and CI excludes 1 | 明确提示显著负相关 |
> | Vitamin D/calcium: 0.62 (0.39-1.09) | CI crosses 1 | 点估计看似保护，但统计学上不能下结论 |
> | Diabetes around 2.0 | About 100 percent increase, not 20 percent | 常见换算误区 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：A - Cigarette smoking is associated with a decreased risk of endometrial cancer**
>
> **Core Concept**：对 RR/OR，**<1 = negative association**, **>1 = positive association**；只有当 **95% CI 不跨 1** 时，才能说这种关联达到统计学显著。本题 smoking 对 endometrial cancer 的 CI 全部小于 1，因此该表述最稳。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题核心不在机制本身，而在统计解释。先判方向，再判显著性，不要把点估计和显著性混为一谈。
>
> ```text
> RR or OR
> -> compare with 1
> -> less than 1 means decreased association
> -> greater than 1 means increased association
>
> then check 95% CI
> -> if CI crosses 1: not statistically significant
> -> if CI excludes 1: statistically significant
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Result pattern | Interpretation |
> |---|---|
> | 0.81 (0.74-0.88) | Significant decreased association |
> | 1.10 (1.02-1.14) | Significant increased association |
> | 1.01 (0.98-1.04) | No significant association |
> | 2.01 (1.10-3.63) | About doubled risk/odds, significant |
> | 0.62 (0.39-1.09) | Point estimate suggests protection, but not significant |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"For RR/OR questions: direction first, significance second."**
> - 数字在 1 左边还是右边，决定方向。
> - CI 是否跨 1，决定能不能下结论。
> - 点估计很好看，但 CI 跨 1 就不能吹。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **B - Diabetes mellitus increases the risk of breast and endometrial cancer by ~20%**：风险比接近 2 代表大约 **100% increase**，不是 20%。
>
> **C - Effect modification explains the differential effects of oral contraceptive pills**：题目只给汇总风险估计，没有分层变量或交互信息，不能直接推 effect modification。
>
> **D - There is no statistically significant difference between the risks ... among caffeine consumers**：breast cancer 那一列不显著，但 endometrial cancer 那一列是显著降低；题干给的数据不足以支持这个结论。
>
> **E - Vitamin D/calcium supplementation is protective against endometrial cancer**：CI 为 0.39-1.09，跨 1，所以不能说有统计学保护作用。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：E - Vitamin D/calcium supplementation is protective against endometrial cancer。因为点估计 0.62 看起来很诱人，但 USMLE 这里专门考你 **CI crossing 1 means not significant**。

>
> ```text
> wants highest reliability
> -> compare typical-use efficacy
> -> user-dependent methods lose effectiveness
> -> LARC maintains >99 percent effectiveness
> -> choose IUD or implant
> ```
>

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q140 OB / Normal postpartum lochia
> ^Q140
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：8 weeks postpartum、已停止 breastfeeding、生命体征正常、宫颈关闭、仅有 pink lochia 加一次恢复月经样出血，最合理的是 **正常 postpartum bleeding pattern**，处理就是 **reassurance**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 8 weeks postpartum | Still near the end of normal postpartum window | 仍可见 lochia 与恢复排卵月经 |
> | Pink discharge since delivery | Fits lochia serosa/alba transition | 粉色持续分泌物本身不异常 |
> | Formula feeding after stopping breastfeeding | Nonlactating women resume ovulation/menses earlier | 题目在提示“月经可能已恢复” |
> | Brief heavier bleeding, now minimal | Likely first menses rather than ongoing pathology | 一阵重一点又自行减轻，更像月经启动 |
> | Normal vitals, no lesions, closed cervix | No signs of infection or major structural disease | 没有 endometritis 或 retained products 线索 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：F - Provide reassurance**
>
> **Core Concept**：**Lochia** 是产后正常子宫蜕膜和血液排出，可持续数周甚至 2 到 3 个月。未哺乳或停止哺乳后，**ovulation 通常 6-12 weeks postpartum 恢复，menses 通常 8-14 weeks postpartum 恢复**。因此本题的长期 pink discharge 加短暂 heavier bleeding，最符合正常 lochia + 初次月经恢复。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：产后两条时间线同时在跑。第一条是 endometrial regeneration 伴 lochia 颜色变化；第二条是 hypothalamic-pituitary-ovarian axis 恢复后重新 ovulation/menses。停止 breastfeeding 会让第二条更早回来。
>
> ```text
> delivery
> -> lochia for weeks
> -> endometrium regenerates
>
> no breastfeeding or breastfeeding stops
> -> ovulation returns by 6 to 12 weeks
> -> menses returns by 8 to 14 weeks
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：产后出血线索
> | Pattern | Most likely meaning |
> |---|---|
> | Red-brown to pink to white discharge over weeks | Normal lochia evolution |
> | 8 weeks postpartum after formula feeding, transient heavier bleeding | Return of menses |
> | Fever, uterine tenderness, purulent lochia | Postpartum endometritis |
> | Persistent heavy abnormal bleeding with concerning findings | Consider structural or retained tissue workup |
> | Excessive hemorrhage from delivery onward | Consider postpartum hemorrhage or bleeding disorder |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Pink lochia plus period comeback is still postpartum normal."**
> - 先看时间轴。
> - 再看 breastfeeding status。
> - 最后看 infection or instability red flags 有没有出现。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Check FSH, TSH, and prolactin levels**：本题不是 amenorrhea，也没有 endocrine dysfunction 线索，反而像正常月经恢复。
>
> **B - Measure von Willebrand factor activity**：vWD 常见于 heavy regular menses 或异常产后 hemorrhage；本题分泌物长期偏轻，不匹配。
>
> **C - Order a pelvic ultrasound**：若真是 abnormal uterine bleeding 或结构异常才考虑；这里临床图景仍在正常范围内。
>
> **D - Perform an endometrial biopsy**：24 岁、典型 postpartum 时间轴、无内膜病变风险特征，不需要。
>
> **E - Prescribe broad spectrum antibiotics**：没有 fever、purulent lochia、uterine tenderness，不像 endometritis。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Order a pelvic ultrasound。因为“产后一直出血”容易让人过度检查，但题干反复用正常体征和哺乳时间轴提醒你这是 physiology。

> [!example]- UWSA2 Q141 肾脏 / Carbamazepine-induced SIADH
> ^Q141
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：hyponatremia + euvolemia + carbamazepine，先锁定 **SIADH**，实验室组合就是 **low serum osmolality + high urine osmolality + high urine sodium**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Serum sodium 125 mEq/L | True hyponatremia with symptoms | fatigue、nausea、forgetfulness 都能由慢性低钠解释 |
> | Carbamazepine use | Classic drug cause of SIADH | 这是题眼，不要被 trigeminal neuralgia 分散注意 |
> | Occasional ibuprofen use | NSAIDs can worsen ADH effect | 进一步强化 drug-induced SIADH 场景 |
> | Moist mucous membranes, no orthostasis, no edema | Euvolemic state | 既不像 hypovolemia，也不像 hypervolemia |
> | Asked for osmolality and urine sodium pattern | Diagnostic physiology question | USMLE 常考 SIADH 的实验室三联征 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：B - Low serum osmolality (<275), high urine osmolality (>100), high urine sodium (>40)**
>
> **Core Concept**：**SIADH** 会造成 **euvolemic hypotonic hyponatremia**。虽然血已经偏稀，但 ADH 让 collecting duct 继续保水，所以尿液仍然 **inappropriately concentrated**；同时因为总体是 euvolemic，肾脏不会拼命保钠，因此 **urine sodium 通常 >40 mEq/L**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：carbamazepine 可增强肾脏对 ADH 的反应，导致 free water retention 多于 sodium retention。结果是血钠被稀释，但尿却不够稀。
>
> ```text
> carbamazepine
> -> increased ADH effect at collecting duct
> -> free water retention
> -> dilutional hyponatremia
> -> low serum osmolality
> -> urine remains concentrated
> -> urine sodium stays high in euvolemia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：低钠实验室模式
> | Pattern | Typical Diagnosis | 关键区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Low serum osm + high urine osm + high urine Na | SIADH | 本题答案 |
> | Low serum osm + low urine osm | Primary polydipsia | 肾脏能正常排稀尿 |
> | Low serum osm + high urine osm + low urine Na | Hypovolemic hyponatremia | 会出现 dry mucosa、orthostasis |
> | High serum osm + low Na | Hyperglycemia or other osmoles | 不是 hypotonic hyponatremia |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"SIADH = serum diluted, urine disobediently concentrated."**
> - 低钠先问是不是 hypotonic。
> - euvolemia 再把方向拉向 SIADH。
> - SIADH 的尿不该稀却偏浓，这是最爱考的反常点。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Low serum osmolality + low urine osmolality + high urine sodium**：更像 primary polydipsia，问题在喝太多水，不是 ADH 过强。
>
> **C - Low serum osmolality + high urine osmolality + low urine sodium**：更像 hypovolemia；此时肾脏会积极保钠，但本题没有脱水体征。
>
> **D - High serum osmolality + low urine osmolality + low urine sodium**：对应其他渗透活性物质导致的高渗性低钠，不符合药物性 SIADH。
>
> **E - High serum osmolality + high urine osmolality + high urine sodium**：仍然错在 serum osmolality，不符合 SIADH 的 hypotonic picture。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Low serum osmolality, high urine osmolality, low urine sodium。因为很多人只记得“尿浓”，却忘了 **euvolemia 下 urine sodium 应该高**。

> ^Q142
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：heavy menstrual bleeding + anemia + irregularly enlarged uterus，先想 **leiomyomata uteri**；fibroids 是 **myometrium** 的 benign proliferation。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Menses with 4 days requiring a pad every hour | Pathologic heavy menstrual bleeding | 已经超过普通经量范围 |
> | Diagnosed anemia and on iron | Confirms chronic blood loss impact | 症状层面也支持 menorrhagia |
> | Copper IUD removed with no improvement | Bleeding is not just an IUD side effect | 题目主动排除最表面的解释 |
> | Slightly enlarged uterus with irregular contour | Classic fibroid exam finding | irregular contour 是 fibroids 高频词 |
> | Reproductive-age woman | Typical leiomyoma demographic | 年龄和表现都很贴合 |
>

>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Myometrium**
>
> **Core Concept**：**Leiomyomata uteri (uterine fibroids)** 是来源于 **uterine smooth muscle** 的良性肿瘤，也就是 **myometrium**。其中 **submucosal fibroids** 最容易造成 heavy menstrual bleeding，**intramural fibroids** 也可导致出血和 uterine enlargement，**subserosal fibroids** 更容易让子宫外形不规则。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：fibroid 虽然位置可分 submucosal、intramural、subserosal，但“母组织”都还是 myometrium。出血多少主要取决于 fibroid 是否扭曲 endometrial cavity。
>
> ```text
> myometrial smooth muscle proliferation
> -> uterine fibroid
> -> enlarged irregular uterus
> -> if submucosal or intramural
> -> heavy prolonged menstrual bleeding
> -> iron deficiency anemia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：异常子宫出血来源对比
> | Tissue / Condition | Typical Bleeding Pattern | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Myometrium / leiomyoma | Heavy cyclic menses, enlarged irregular uterus | 本题完全符合 |
> | Endometrium / hyperplasia or polyp | Abnormal bleeding, often not irregular bulky uterus | 缺少子宫轮廓不规则 |
> | Cervical transformation zone | Postcoital bleeding or abnormal Pap context | 不是 bulky irregular uterus |
> | Ovarian or tubal epithelium | Pelvic mass, bloating, ascites | 不是长期 heavy cyclic menses |
> | Uterine serosa | Not the true proliferative origin of fibroids | 只是 fibroid 可向外突出 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Fibroids are muscle balls."**
> - fibroid = leiomyo-ma，名字里就带 `myo`。
> - heavy bleeding 想 submucosal。
> - irregular contour 想 subserosal/intramural contributions。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Cervical transformation zone**：更关联 cervical dysplasia/cancer，常见 abnormal Pap 或 postcoital bleeding，不是 irregularly enlarged uterus。
>
> **B - Endometrium**：endometrial hyperplasia 或 polyp 也会出血，但不会把子宫摸成 irregular contour。
>

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|

> -> small bowel obstruction
> -> intraluminal pressure rises
> -> bowel perfusion falls
> -> ischemia and necrosis
> -> urgent laparotomy
> ```

> chronic diarrhea

> -> acidic concentrated urine
> -> uric acid stays less soluble
> -> uric acid crystal precipitation
> -> recurrent uric acid nephrolithiasis
> ```

> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Headaches, dizziness, blurry vision | Classic hyperviscosity symptoms | 这些是微循环变差的高价值线索 |

>

>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：这题不是在考长期肿瘤治疗，而是在考先后顺序。只要已经出现 hyperviscosity symptoms，就先把血液黏稠度降下来，再谈后续 oncologic workup 和 definitive therapy。
>
> ```text
> lymphoplasmacytic clone
> -> excess monoclonal IgM
> -> increased plasma viscosity
> -> impaired capillary circulation
> -> headache / dizziness / blurry vision
> -> urgent plasma exchange
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查
> | Finding | Best Interpretation | 本题意义 |
> |---|---|---|
> | High IgM + neuro/visual symptoms | Hyperviscosity syndrome | 先做 plasma exchange |
> | Mild edema + S3 | Possible concurrent cardiomyopathy/volume issue | 不是当前第一优先 |
> | Hgb 8.2 g/dL | Chronic anemia/cytopenia | 还不到先输血的逻辑 |
> | Platelets 63,000/mm3 with ecchymoses | Platelet/coagulation dysfunction from IgM | 不等于先输 platelets |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"WM makes thick blood; thick blood needs exchange."**
> - IgM 最大，最会拖慢微循环。
> - vision change + headache 是 hyperviscosity 典型组合。
> - 先把 IgM 拿掉，再谈别的支持治疗。
>
> ---
>

> **A - Loop diuretic and ACE inhibitor**：S3 和 edema 会把人带去 heart failure，但这些不是当前最危险的问题。hyperviscosity syndrome 是急症。
>
> **B - Packed red blood cell transfusion**：Hgb 虽低，但题干没有 acute bleeding、acute coronary ischemia 或典型 symptomatic anemia。反而贸然输血可能进一步增加 viscosity。

>

> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Decreased cardiac output due to right ventricular dysfunction**
>
> **Core Concept**：**massive PE** 会突然阻断大块 pulmonary arterial circulation，使 **pulmonary vascular resistance** 急升，右心室立刻面临巨大 afterload。急性 RV 扩张和收缩失败会让 forward flow 掉下来，造成 **hypotension、syncope、shock**。所以本题 syncope 最关键的直接机制是 **acute RV dysfunction**。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：很多人会笼统记成“PE 让 preload 降低”，但那是更后面的继发现象。第一步真正出问题的是 **RV afterload 暴增**，于是 RV pump 不动了，LV filling 才跟着下降。
>
> ```text
> massive pulmonary embolism
> -> sudden rise in pulmonary vascular resistance
> -> acute RV pressure overload
> -> RV dysfunction
> -> reduced forward flow to LV
> -> low cardiac output
> -> syncope and hypotension
> ```
>
> ---
>

> | Mechanism | Typical Setting | 与本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|

> | Reduced RV preload | Severe hypovolemia/hemorrhage | 不是本题主线 |

> | Senior year transfer after family move | Psychosocial stressor is present | 有诱因不等于只能诊断 adjustment disorder，关键看症状是否达到 MDD 门槛 |
> | Grades dropped, stopped caring about college, no social effort | Functional impairment + loss of motivation | 学业和社交双下降，支持临床抑郁而非普通青春期波动 |
> | "Too tired", lies in bed, sleeps until 2 PM | Neurovegetative symptoms | fatigue + marked hypersomnia 是 depression 的硬线索 |
> | Lost 6.8 kg, poor concentration, hopelessness | DSM-style depressive features | 体重下降、注意力差、无望感都把诊断推向 MDD |
> | No suicidal thoughts, occasional marijuana only | Helps narrow but does not rule out depression | 没有 suicidal ideation 不排除 MDD；偶尔 marijuana 不足以解释全貌 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：D - Major depressive disorder**
>
> **Core Concept**：MDD 可以被 psychosocial stress precipitate，但只要症状的数量、持续时间和功能损害已经满足 major depressive episode，就不能退回写成 adjustment disorder。adolescent depression 常表现为 **irritable mood**，不一定是典型的“悲伤哭泣”。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：考试重点不是神经递质细节，而是诊断层级。先看有没有 stressor，再看症状是否已经“升级”到 full MDD criteria。
>
> ```text
> psychosocial stressor
> -> mood symptoms begin
> -> symptoms persist and intensify
> -> neurovegetative changes + hopelessness + impairment
> -> meets major depressive disorder criteria
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：MDD vs adjustment disorder
> | Diagnosis | Typical Pattern | 与本题关系 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Major depressive disorder | >=2 weeks of depressive syndrome with functional impairment | 本题符合 |
> | Adjustment disorder | Stress-related symptoms that do not meet criteria for another disorder | 本题症状太重，不能停在这里 |
> | Normal adolescent behavior | Moodiness without sustained vegetative symptoms or major impairment | 本题有 weight loss, hypersomnia, decline in function |
> | Substance-induced depressive disorder | Heavy or persistent substance exposure temporally driving symptoms | 本题仅 occasional marijuana，不够解释 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"有 stressor，不代表只能选 adjustment disorder。"**
> - Step 2 常见陷阱：看到搬家、失恋、考试失败，就机械选 adjustment disorder。
> - 真正分水岭：症状是否已经完整像一个 depressive episode。
> - adolescent 可用 irritability 代替明显 sad mood。
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Adjustment disorder**：这是最常见误选。题干虽然有 stressor，但一旦症状强度和功能损害足以满足 MDD，就应升级诊断。
>
> **B - Borderline personality disorder**：要看到长期的人际关系不稳、冲动、自伤/suicidal threats 等人格模式，这里没有。
>
> **C - Cyclothymic disorder**：需要长期波动性的轻躁狂样和抑郁样症状，不是单次明确 depressive syndrome。
>
> **E - Normal adolescent behavior**：普通青春期情绪起伏不会伴随显著 weight loss、hypersomnia 与学业社交双受损。
>
> **F - Parent-child relational problem**：母女张力更像 depression 的后果，不是本题主要诊断焦点。
>
> **G - Substance-induced depressive disorder**：题干特意写 only several occasions of marijuana，就是告诉你不要把主诊断推给 substance use。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：A - Adjustment disorder。真正题眼是 full depressive syndrome，而不是 stressor 本身。

> ```

> | Diagnosis | Key Pattern | 与本题区别 |

> ^Q155
>
> ### 💡 一句话锁定
> **提示**：first trimester pregnancy + hyperemesis gravidarum + low TSH with normal-high free T4 + no goiter/tenderness，最该想到 **hCG stimulation of TSH receptor**，不是 Graves disease。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🔑 Stem 关键词识别
> | Key Clue | Why It Matters | 中文解析 |
> |---|---|---|
> | 11 weeks gestation | Peak hCG period | 妊娠早期正是 hCG 最高、最容易压低 TSH 的阶段 |
> | Severe nausea and vomiting | Suggests hyperemesis gravidarum | 高 hCG 常与 hyperemesis 并行出现 |
> | TSH 2.5 -> 0.2 uU/mL, free T4 1.1 -> 1.7 ng/dL | Physiologic gestational thyroid stimulation | 化验模式像轻度/暂时性 hyperthyroid pattern |
> | Free T4 still within first-trimester range | Supports physiologic rather than overt pathology | 仍在孕早期参考范围内，不像明显病理性甲亢 |
> | Normal, nontender thyroid and no Graves signs | Helps exclude autoimmune or inflammatory disease | 没有 goiter、眼征、压痛 |
>
> ---
>
> ### ✅ 答案 + Core Concept
> **正确答案：C - Elevated levels of hCG**
>
> **Core Concept**：hCG 与 TSH 共享 alpha subunit，beta subunit 也很相似，因此在 pregnancy early first trimester 可直接刺激 **TSH receptor**，导致 thyroid hormone production 增加、pituitary TSH feedback suppression。这种实验室改变常见于正常妊娠，也可在 **hyperemesis gravidarum** 时更明显。
>
> ---
>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：本题同时考两个孕期甲状腺生理变化，但真正让 TSH 下降的是 hCG，不是 TBG。
>
> ```text
> early pregnancy
> -> hCG rises
> -> TSH receptor stimulation
> -> increased thyroid hormone production
> -> pituitary TSH suppression
> -> low TSH with normal/high-normal free T4
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：pregnancy thyroid physiology
> | Mechanism | Hormone Effect | 考试怎么问 |
> |---|---|---|
> | hCG stimulates TSH receptor | Lowers TSH, may slightly raise free T4 | 本题正确答案 |
> | Estrogen raises TBG | Raises total T4/T3 more than free hormone | 常作为干扰项出现 |
> | Graves disease | Overt hyperthyroidism, goiter, autoimmune signs | 本题缺少典型临床表现 |
> | Hashimoto thyroiditis / iodine deficiency | Hypothyroid pattern | 与本题方向相反 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"hCG high, TSH shy."**

>
> ### 🧠 机制理解
> **机制说明**：血液在异常机械瓣或瓣周漏的高剪切环境中通过时，RBC 被打碎，形成 intravascular hemolysis；platelets 也可能被一起机械损伤。
>
> ```text
> prosthetic valve dysfunction
> -> turbulent high-shear flow / paravalvular leak
> -> RBC fragmentation
> -> intravascular hemolysis
> -> anemia + high LDH + dark urine
> -> fatigue, dyspnea, tachycardia
> ```
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 High-Yield 速查：贫血来源怎么分
> | Cause | 典型线索 | 与本题区别 |
> |---|---|---|
> | Prosthetic valve hemolysis | Mechanical valve, high LDH, hemoglobinuria, murmur | 本题完全吻合 |
> | Occult bleeding | Iron deficiency pattern, no hemolysis labs | 不能解释高 LDH 和 dark urine |
> | Myelodysplasia | Marrow production failure, often pancytopenia | 不该有 hemoglobinuria |
> | Interstitial nephritis | Kidney injury signs | creatinine 应上升 |
> | Ventricular tachycardia | Tachyarrhythmia instability | ECG 不支持 |
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎣 Memory Hook
> **"Mechanical valve + dark urine = think shredded RBCs."**
> - valve patient 贫血先分 hemolysis vs bleeding
> - LDH 高是 hemolysis 强提示
> - new murmur 说明不只是“有机械瓣”，而是“机械瓣出问题”
>
> ---
>
> ### ❌ 干扰项击破
> **A - Interstitial nephritis**：应有 renal dysfunction 或药物诱因，本题 creatinine 正常。
>
> **B - Myelodysplasia**：会走向低生成性 cytopenia，不会解释 dark urine 和高 LDH。
>
> **C - Occult bleeding**：warfarin 确实容易让人想到出血，这是最强干扰；但失血不会把 LDH 顶这么高，也不该出现 hemoglobinuria。
>
> **E - Ventricular tachycardia**：ECG 只是 sinus tachycardia with occasional ventricular premature beats，不是 VT。
>
> **⚡ 最强干扰项**：C - Occult bleeding。看到 warfarin 容易顺手选出血，但本题真正抓手是 **hemolysis pattern + prosthetic valve murmur**。
